SUMMARY

The 2024 Oscars Best Picture race is becoming more diverse in genre and style thanks to an expanded and more diverse voting body. Front-runner titles for Best Picture include Oppenheimer and Killers of the Flower Moon, but awards season is still in its early stages and campaigning will play a role in the final decision. Other potential Best Picture nominees to watch for include American Fiction, Maestro, The Holdovers, and Poor Things, among others.

Literature Review

The Best Picture race is already heating up for the 2024 Oscars. Here’s a prediction for the 10 nominees and which movie will be the winner.

The Oscars 2024 race is heating up, as a growing list of movies hope to compete for a coveted Best Picture nomination with hopes of winning the Academy Awards’ top prize. Hollywood’s biggest awards ceremony has undergone significant changes in recent years that have brought in an expanded and more diverse voting body. The effects of this have been seen across the board, but the Best Picture nominees have become more varied in genre and style than before. This has also been aided by the decision to expand the category to always nominate 10 movies, which is perfect for a year like 2024 with several strong contenders. Predictions for Best Picture have long included a few front-runner titles among the expected 2024 Oscars nominees, such as Christopher Nolan’s Oppenheimer and Martin Scorsese’s Killers of the Flower Moon, and that has not changed after their releases. While they might seem like the strongest contenders to earn the biggest Academy Award, awards season is still in its relative infancy, with plenty of jockeying, campaigning, and shrewd tactics sure to factor into the final decision. With that in mind, this list of the 2024 Oscars’ Best Picture nominations and predicted winner will be updated as the January 2024 nominations announcement date approaches. 10 May December

May December
8 / 10

Release Date December 1, 2023 Director Todd Haynes Cast Natalie Portman , Julianne Moore , Charles Melton Distributor(s) Netflix

Netflix has a contender for Best Picture it appears in Todd Haynes’ movie May December. The film started to gain recognition following its debut at the Cannes Film Festival, which is when Netflix came aboard to distribute the drama. Natalie Portman stars as an actress researching a woman, played by Julianne Moore, who she is about to play in a movie. The meta-movie story could hit well with the Academy just as it has with critics at large. This would be the first Best Picture Oscar nomination for any movie Todd Haynes has directed. Its case looks stronger after a Golden Globes Best Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy nomination.

9 American Fiction

American Fiction
8 / 10

Release Date September 8, 2023 Director Cord Jefferson Cast Jeffrey Wright , Tracee Ellis Ross , John Ortiz , erika alexander , Leslie Uggams , Adam Brody​ , Keith David , Issa Rae , Sterling K. Brown Distributor(s) Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer

Amazon Studios and MGM have hope of hearing American Fiction earn a Best Picture Oscar nomination in 2024 based on current trends. The directorial debut of Cord Jefferson looks to be among the biggest crowd-pleasers of the year, one that is only bolstered by the strong performances of Jeffrey Wright and Sterling K. Brown. Its most notable awards season victory so far is winning the People’s Choice Award at TIFF following its premiere. The praise and adoration for American Fiction has only grown since then, including earning a Golden Globe nomination for Best Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy.

8 Past Lives

Past Lives

Release Date January 21, 2023 Director Celine Song Cast Greta Lee , Teo Yoo , John Magaro

Past Lives has been pinpointed as a likely 2024 Oscars contender since before 2023’s ceremony even took place. The emotional romantic drama had its debut at the Sundance Film Festival in January 2023, which is where the first wave of rave reviews came from. The movie had a slow rollout as it was taken to many other film festivals ahead of its June theatrical release. Past Lives is among the best-reviewed movies of 2023, and the 98% Rotten Tomatoes score has started bringing in some recognition ahead of the Oscars, including a Best Motion Picture - Drama nomination from the Golden Globes.

7 The Zone of Interest

Director: Jonathan Glazer Cast: Christian Friedel, Sandra Hüller The return of Jonathan Glazer over a decade after his last movie has helped position The Zone of Interest firmly in the Best Picture race. The historical drama focused on an Auschwitz commandant starting a life near a concentration camp has been heralded by critics for tackling difficult subjects and re-examining horrific events. The Zone of Interest had its world premiere at Cannes, where it lost the Palme d’Or to Anatomy of a Fall, but continued to earn high praise at subsequent showings at Telluride and the Toronto International Film Festival. The fact that it earned a Golden Globes nomination for Best Motion Picture - Drama bolsters its chances.

6 The Holdovers

The Holdovers
7 / 10

Release Date November 10, 2023 Director Alexander Payne Cast Paul Giamatti , Da’Vine Joy Randolph , Dominic Sessa , Carrie Preston Distributor(s) Focus Features

The Holdovers is heading toward a Best Picture Oscars nomination in 2024 barring any major setbacks it seems. Alexander Payne is no stranger to such potential success, as he delivered his first Best Picture nominee in 2012 with The Descendants. He has also won two Oscars for his screenplays, and The Holdovers looks to be a contender for that award in 2024, along with Paul Giamatti thought of as in the running for a Best Actor nomination too. From the initial reviews following the movie’s debut at Telluride to its Golden Globes nomination for Best Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy, The Holdovers is contending.

5 Maestro

Maestro
7 / 10

Release Date December 20, 2023 Director Bradley Cooper Cast Bradley Cooper , Carey Mulligan , Maya Hawke , Sarah Silverman , Matt Bomer Distributor(s) Netflix

Five years after A Star Is Born received a Best Picture nomination, Bradley Cooper’s biopic about the legendary composer Leonard Bernstein is poised to repeat the awards success of his directorial debut - as long as Maestro’s controversy does not stop it. While it was always thought to be an awards contender, Maestro’s likelihood of receiving a Best Picture nomination at the 2024 Oscars increased with its debut at the Venice International Film Festival, where it was nominated for the top prize, the Golden Lion. Now that Bradley Cooper’s movie is out and a Golden Globe nominee for Best Motion Picture - Drama, its position has strengthened.

4 Poor Things

Poor Things
9 / 10

Release Date September 8, 2023 Director Yorgos Lanthimos Cast Emma Stone , Willem Dafoe , Mark Ruffalo , Ramy Youssef , Jerrod Carmichael , Christopher Abbott Distributor(s) Searchlight Pictures

Yorgos Lanthimos and Emma Stone have joined forces again to create one of the biggest contenders to win the 2024 Oscars’ Best Picture. Following their shared success with The Favourite in 2018, Poor Things has been lauded for its creativity and uniqueness ever since it premiered at Venice, where it won the prestigious Golden Lion award. The result greatly increased the chance of a nomination, and possibly even a win, for Best Picture. Recent Golden Lion winners that became Best Picture winners include Nomadland and The Shape of Water. Adding a Golden Globes nomination for Best Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy should help too.

3 Barbie

Barbie
9 / 10

Release Date July 21, 2023 Director Greta Gerwig Cast Margot Robbie , Ryan Gosling , Simu Liu , Ariana Greenblatt , Helen Mirren , Nicola Coughlan , John Cena , Will Ferrell , Ritu Arya , Michael Cera , America Ferrera , Alexandra Shipp , Kate McKinnon Distributor(s) Warner Bros. Pictures

The overall success of Barbie is expected to translate to a 2024 Oscars nomination for Best Picture. The movie surpassed all box office expectations on its way to becoming the biggest movie of the year, and it now is on track to continue Greta Gerwig’s Oscars streak. Her previous two movies, Little Women and Lady Bird, both received Best Picture nominations under the older format. The support for Barbie’s 2024 Oscars campaign can be seen with its various love from critics circles to dominating the 2024 Golden Globes nominations, where it tied for the second most nominations by a single film in history.

2 Killers of the Flower Moon

Killers of the Flower Moon

Director Martin Scorsese Cast Leonardo DiCaprio , Louis Cancelmi , Jillian Dion , Scott Shepherd , Jesse Plemons , JaNae Collins , Robert De Niro , Jason Isbell , Cara Jade Myers , Lily Gladstone Studio(s) Paramount Pictures Distributor(s) Apple , Paramount Pictures

Martin Scorsese has one of the strongest Best Picture contenders for the 2024 Oscars with Killers of the Flower Moon. Its premiere at Cannes led to a rapturous response heralding the true story as the latest Scorsese masterpiece, bringing all three of its leads to be viewed as locks for their own Oscar nominations. Martin Scorsese’s Oscars nomination history is significant, as he’s had nine movies nominated in the biggest category, but only The Departed won. The career legacy angle and a Golden Globes Best Motion Picture - Drama nomination could be used to the movie’s benefit, giving Killers of the Flower Moon a strong narrative to win.

1 Oppenheimer (Winner)

Oppenheimer
9 / 10

Release Date July 21, 2023 Director Christopher Nolan Cast Cillian Murphy , Emily Blunt , Matt Damon , Robert Downey Jr. , Rami Malek , Florence Pugh Distributor(s) Universal Pictures

Oppenheimer winning Best Picture at the 2024 Oscars appears to be the most likely outcome at this point. Christopher Nolan’s biopic about J. Robert Oppenheimer is arguably his best movie to date. Like Scorsese, a Nolan movie has never won Best Picture, but his movies have only received nominations twice before. Oppenheimer is guaranteed to at least be among the list of nominees announced in January 2024. Its case to win the award comes through the overall acclaim and success of the movie, which now includes a Best Motion Picture - Drama nomination from the Golden Globes.
