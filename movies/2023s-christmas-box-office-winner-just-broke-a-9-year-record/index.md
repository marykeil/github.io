# 2023s Christmas Box Office Winner Just Broke A 9 Year Record


## SUMMARY 


 

The Color Purple broke a nine-year record by winning the Christmas Day box office, becoming the first non-franchise film to do so since 2014.
            

It exceeded expectations by making $18 million on its opening day, surpassing other 2023 releases in just two days.
            

The Color Purple is on track to potentially break even with at least $200 million, making it a refreshing box office success.
