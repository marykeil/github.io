Andrew Garfield s Spider-Man Dons A Black Suit & Battles Venom In The Amazing Spider-Man 3 Art

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 700 words 3 minutes 
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Andrew Garfield bonds with the Venom symbiote in new fan art for The Amazing Spider-Man 3. Fans are campaigning for The Amazing Spider-Man 3 to be made, allowing Garfield to complete his own trilogy. Garfield’s Spider-Man could potentially appear in future live-action or animated projects, either in the Spider-Verse or through collaboration between Marvel Studios and Sony Pictures.

Literature Review

As the world still wants to see The Amazing Spider-Man 3 happen, new fan art imagines Andrew Garfield’s hero getting the Venom symbiote suit.

Andrew Garfield’s version of the iconic Marvel hero bonds with the Venom symbiote as he gets his own version of the black suit in new The Amazing Spider-Man 3 fan art. While The Amazing Spider-Man 3 got canceled, 2014 wouldn’t be the last time Garfield got to play the web-slinger. In 2021, Garfield entered the MCU timeline via Spider-Man: No Way Home, which saw him join forces with Tobey Maguire and Tom Holland’s respective hero iterations in what would become one of the biggest superhero movies of all time.

Following his appearance in the Spider-Man: No Way Home cast, fans are actively campaigning for The Amazing Spider-Man 3 to get made, allowing Garfield to complete his own trilogy. One popular idea for The Amazing Spider-Man 3 is seeing Peter explore the symbiote, as Pablo Ruiz & Erathrim shared fan art of what that story could look like for him.

Ruiz’s The Amazing Spider-Man 3 fan art sees Garfield’s Peter beginning to bond with the symbiote as it slowly consumes him. While it never appeared in the theatrical cut, The Amazing Spider-Man 2 marketing did at one point include the symbiote.

The Amazing Spider-Man 3 fan art by Erathrim sees Garfield’s web-crawler not only donning the black suit, but also being pitted against Tom Hardy’s Venom. Erathrim also cleverly added the caption “I want to fight an alien!” from Spider-Man: No Way Home, when Garfield’s Peter expressed that he wanted to take on an extraterrestrial being like his Spider-Man variants.

Will Andrew Garfield’s Spider-Man Ever Appear On-Screen Again?

The question that remains is whether or not The Amazing Spider-Man 3 will ever happen, or if there are other ways for Garfield’s hero to return in future live-action projects. For the time being, The Amazing Spider-Man 3’s chances appear slim to none, as Spider-Man’s movie endeavors are split between Holland’s Spider-Man 4 and Spider-Man: Beyond The Spider-Verse. Even though Garfield has expressed being open to another movie, Sony hasn’t currently shown any hints that they are thinking of pursuing The Amazing Spider-Man 3.

However, Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse did include a brief cameo by Garfield, through archival footage, showing his franchise being tied to the animated property. This paves the way for Spider-Man: Beyond the Spider-Verse to feature a proper appearance by his Spider-Man, as the Spider-Verse movies are confirmed to be able to tap between live-action and animation. There is also the option for Marvel Studios to collaborate with Sony Pictures again and have Garfield’s Spider-Man show up in something like Avengers: Secret Wars as they get to the end of The Multiverse Saga.

While Garfield is not getting a sequel, there have been rumors about Maguire’s Spider-Man 4 possibly happening at Sony.

Given the massive love for Garfield’s Spider-Man, it would be genuinely shocking if the character was never brought back in some capacity, even if it takes a few years. Whether that is a Marvel Studios multiverse movie or The Amazing Spider-Man 3, Garfield will hopefully suit up as the beloved Marvel superhero sooner rather than later.
