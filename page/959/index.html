Killers Of The Flower Moon Ending Explained The Osage Murders Trial & Outcome

 Ella published on 2020-10-16
SUMMARYKillers of the Flower Moon depicts the devastating consequences of the murders, theft, and mistreatment suffered by the Osage Nation, as they finally attain justice. William King Hale’s plan involved killing Mollie Burkhart’s entire family in order to inherit their headrights, which represented their wealth. Hale was able to carry out his crimes due to his deceptive nature and influence within the community. Ernest Burkhart’s decision to testify against his uncle stemmed from being backed into a corner and realizing that his uncle did not care about the consequences of their actions.
