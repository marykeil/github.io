10 Apocalyptic Manhwa To Fill The Void Of Solo Leveling

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 2000 words 10 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Level Up with the Gods offers a thrilling adventure in a tower filled with mythology and treacherous games. Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint takes readers on a journey where the protagonist must change the course of a novel to save the world. The Esper’s Game combines action and fantasy elements in a unique manhwa with a focus on dungeons and superpowers.

Literature Review

Readers trying to fill the void left by Solo Leveling will find the following manhwa teeming with monsters, powers, and the fall of civilization.

Warning: MASSIVE spoilers for Solo Leveling ahead Sung Jinwoo and his treacherous journey in the apocalyptic world of Solo Leveling has left readers in a void, now that the manhwa has ended (even if the anime adaptation has only just started). From a revolutionary incident to ridding the world of monsters, Solo Leveling is a shining example of how to effectively use tropes common to the fantasy/adventure genre within manhwa. Thankfully, plenty of others within the medium possess the qualities that make it a fan favorite: weak to strong protagonist, hunters, leveling system, dungeons or gates, guilds, gods or higher beings, and to up the stakes, the impending doom of civilization.

10 Best Manhwa Every Fan of Isekai Anime Needs to Read
While isekai has been popular with anime and manga for years, there are a surprising number of manhwa that pose as formidable competition.

10 Level Up with the Gods (2022) Based on the novels by Black Ajin & illustrated by Ohyeon

Status Ongoing Number of Chapters 98+ Where to Read Tapas

With ancient evils threatening to destroy the world, Kim Yuwon, with the help of his comrades, decides to climb the tower once more with the hopes of becoming stronger to overpower his enemies. Yuwon’s knowledge helps him level up, along with his understanding of how the tower operates, including its floor managers. His lone-wolf personality is similar to Jinwoo, yet he allows himself to cautiously guide Zeus’ son. Lovers of mythology will delight in the God of Olympus characters and their treacherous games spread throughout the tower, as well as the world of Murim, as the tower possesses different dimensions within its structure. Like Sung Jinwoo, Kim Yuwon must oppose a greater force after leveling up in order to secure a future for both himself and the tower. READ ON TAPAS

9 Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint (2020) Based on the novels by Sing-Shong, written by UMI, & illustrated by Sleepy-C

Status Ongoing Number of Chapters 187+ Where to Read Webtoon

Kim Dokja escapes from his mundane life as an office worker by reading the web novel ‘Three Ways to Survive the Apocalypse’ by TLS123. When the unimaginable happens, Dokja’s world becomes the setting of the novel, and he is the only one who knows what will happen next. In order to save the world, he will use his knowledge and wits to change the course of the story. Besides being produced by Redice Studio, Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint is also similar to Solo Leveling in regard to its world building. Dokja is now part of a system that caters to higher beings, such as Constellations, Demons, and Outer-Gods. He also possesses the power to glimpse at the thoughts of others, which steadily levels up as he completes plot points and bonds with the main characters. Fans of Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint may enjoy Sing-Shong’s other notable apocalyptic work, The World After the Fall, also available to read on Webtoon.

READ ON WEBTOON

8 The Esper’s Game (2022) Based on the novels by Niccolo, written by Two-zero, & illustrated by Plan A

Status Ongoing Number of Chapters 85+ Where to Read Tappytoon

Seomun Yeop, one of the world’s seven heroes, was lost after saving the world. Now that the fate of the world is secured, the Espers no longer have a need for their powers; cue the Virtual Reality game Battle Field. This VR recreates dungeons of defeated monsters for a heated competition between Esper teams. Yeop finally takes an interest in the games, until he uncovers the evil force of the subterranean burrowers at play behind the scenes. The Esper’s Game is a unique action manhwa with fantasy elements that alternate between the physical and virtual worlds. In a setting similar to SoloLeveling, clubs are the norm rather than guilds, and dungeons remain the same; however, the superpowers differ, and items are more commonplace. READ ON TAPPYTOON

7 Return of the Disaster Class Hero (2022) Based on the novels by San. G, written by HEATS, & illustrated by BGman

Status Hiatus Number of Chapters 74 Where to Read Tapas

The Zodiacs chose 12 Saints to combat what humanity called “calamities”, or monsters that found their way to Earth. Geon Lee was an anomaly, another chosen, and the most powerful Saint of them all. Betrayed by the Saints, Geon falls in the demon tower to a zone where calamities are known to endlessly spawn. However, 20 years later, he finds himself finally free and on the hunt for revenge. The setting is similar to Solo Leveling, in which a greater understanding of the world and its politics come into play, as well as the continual threat of monsters attempting to take over the planet. The protagonist is also similar in that he becomes overpowered through continual training and has a set goal that he’s working toward, the same as Jinwoo in his hopes of defeating the double dungeon. From the studio that produced Solo Leveling: Redice Studios.

READ ON TAPAS

Solo Leveling Took a Big Risk By Rejecting the Big New Anime Trend
Instead of following in the footsteps of Oshi no Ko and Frieren, Solo Leveling’s prologue is split into one okay debut and one spectacular follow up.

6 My S-Class Hunters (2022) Based on the novels by Geunseo, written by Seri, & illustrated by Biwan

Status Ongoing Number of Chapters 98+ Where to Read Webtoon

When a monster outbreak occurs and gates appear, Yoojin Han finds himself at the bottom of the food chain, an F-rank, while his younger brother Yoohyun Han is awakened as an S-rank. Used to taking care of his brother, Yoojin tries various means to increase his rank; but ultimately, his attempts end in Yoohyun’s demise. Given another opportunity, Yoojin decides to change his future by keeping a low profile. However, his new skill makes this an impossible feat, since he can see the max level-up grade potential of a hunter. Like Jinwoo, Yoojin holds fast to his values, prioritizing his family, while also operating within an intricate political system consisting of multiple guilds. The setting also features gates and an outside influence over the system, which Yoojin tries his best to cooperate with as he surrounds himself with overpowered hunters. READ ON WEBTOON

5 The Hero Returns (2022) Based on the novels by B. Ain, written by Antstudio, & illustrated by Antstudio and Yungun

Status Hiatus Number of Chapters 70 Where to Read Tapas

Sungin Kim has a hero complex that does not abate even when he regresses to before the emergence of dungeons as high school student Soohyun Kim. With his memories intact, Soohyun challenges the tower of trials in the hopes of becoming an overpowered hero once more to prevent the destruction of Earth. To this end, he acquires items and grows closer to influential figures as he prevents disasters before they have a chance to strike. While Jinwoo in Solo Leveling has a prescribed power, Soohyun acquires skills through trading points, which he earns through tower trials, along with leveling up his base stats. The worlds are also similar in that they have awakeners, which are like hunters, as well as guilds. Both feature the lone-wolf archetype protagonist who is eventually surrounded by people who share a synergetic relationship throughout their journey. READ ON TAPAS

4 SSS – Class Revival Hunter Based on the novels by No-Ah Shin, written by Neida, & illustrated by Bill K

Status Ongoing Number of Chapters 105+ Where to Read Tapas

One day, a Tower appeared and granted people skills, effectively turning them into hunters. At rock bottom, protagonist Gong-Ja Kim is envious of the celebrated hunter known as the Flame Emperor. Through his greed, the Tower grants him an S-rank skill. However, in order to use this skill, he must die. Not only that, but each death sends him 24-hours back in time. Now at the beginning of the Tower’s reign, Gong-Ja prepares to alter his fate and that of those around him. Guilds form, trials are set, and Gong-Ja strategizes the best ways in which to conquer the Tower based on his previous experience. While similar to Solo Leveling in that the main character gains skills by overcoming death, it includes a system to acquire unique skills as well as a vast setting, where each level of the tower holds a different dimension, offering readers unique storylines as the plot progresses. READ ON TAPAS

10 Best Long Manhwa Every Fan Needs to Binge
Thanks to their high number of chapters, these long manhwa feature some of the medium’s most complex stories and immersive, intricate worlds.

3 Second Life Ranker Based on the novels by Sadoyeon & written and illustrated by Nongnong

Status Ongoing Number of Chapters 154+ Where to Read Tapas

Yeonwoo, known as Cain in the Tower of the Sun God, targets those who betrayed his brother. With the knowledge that his brother left behind, Yeonwoo contacts members his brother was associated with, forming bonds, gaining power, and acquiring items. The ecosystem of the tower is made up of multiple species, from humanoid demon folk to the Gods, specifically those within Greek mythology. As Yeonwoo climbs the tower, his dark attribute grows. Like Jinwoo, Yeonwoo acquires the epitaph “one who leads death,” which allows him to command the dead. He also prefers solitude and secrecy against his enemies, only allowing a select few into his inner circle. Unlike Solo Leveling, the manhwa is revenge-based rather than about securing the fate of the world. READ ON TAPAS

2 The Lone Necromancer Based on the novels by JJJ, written by Kim Kyoung-Yeol, & illustrated by Kim Dong-Jun

Status Ongoing Number of Chapters 106+ Where to Read Webtoon

Seongwu’s life as a college student is turned upside-down when a holographic screen asks him to “select a role.” He receives the power of a necromancer, now able to control the dead. The world is on the verge of collapse while game-type simulations commence. To survive, Seongwu must learn to use his powers and navigate a world overrun with monsters. In a state of emergency, factions are created, items are acquired, and barriers are made. Seongwu’s power is similar to Jinwoo, in which his bloodkin and base stats level up with experience. , Seongwu also has a reckoning with Death just as Jinwoo does, both expanding their authority over the domain of death. READ ON WEBTOON

1 All-Master Necromancer Based on the novels by Cheongsong, written by blue-jaengyee, & illustrated by JiBaekHo

Status Ongoing Number of Chapters 34+ Where to Read Tapas

Cha Jinhyuk was a formidable force within the Ninestar guild, but unfortunately they betrayed him, resulting in his death. However, he won’t go down without a fight. Using his last resort of soul-transference, Jinhyuk wakes up in a new body a decade later. While originally powerful, Jinhyuk now possesses an even greater skill, enabling him to become a master of all occupations within the tower. Cha Jinhyuk embarks on a quest for vengeance, mastering his new skill as he uses his knowledge to climb the tower once again. Not only that, but one distinguishing characteristic that links the two manhwa is that Jinhyuk is a necromancer who also says “arise” to summon souls, yet a key difference is that he can also consume those souls to raise his stats. READ ON TAPAS
