Wolverine Officially Debuts New ADAMANTIUM ARMOR to Finally Kill His Nemesis

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 700 words 4 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Wolverine will use Forge’s Adamantium Armor to battle Sabretooth in their escalating war, which has been fueled by Victor’s recent heinous attacks on Wolverine’s loved ones. Logan is an incredibly prideful man, and his donning this armor is proof of how desperate he is to defeat Sabretooth. The synopsis for Wolverine #49 hints that Sabretooth may have depowered Wolverine, making the Adamantium Armor necessary for his survival.

Literature Review

Covers for the final issues of Benjamin Percy’s Wolverine see Logan donning an Adamantium Armor created by Forge for his ultimate Sabretooth battle.

As the horrific war between Wolverine and Sabretooth escalates in the pages of Wolverine, Marvel has unveiled a powerful new weapon Logan will use to try and take down Victor Creed in Sabretooth War. Wolverine #49 will see the debut of Logan’s new “Adamantium Armor,” crafted by legendary mutant inventor Forge, which an apparently powerless Logan will be forced to don if he ever hopes to defeat his psychopathic rival Sabretooth.

Wolverine and Sabretooth have been archenemies for literal decades, but Victor’s recent attacks against Logan’s loved ones - brutally murdering Quentin Quire and Akihiro - are truly heinous, prompting Wolverine to go full berserker against the villain, amidst Orchis’ continued assault on mutantkind.

Marvel has revealed that Forge’s Adamantium Armor will debut in Wolverine #49 and will play a major role in Logan’s final battle against Sabretooth in Wolverine #50, from writers Victor LaValle and Benjamin Percy with art from Geoff Shaw and Alex Sinclair:

Armor up for the penultimate part of ‘Sabretooth War!’ Wolverine is powerless—but not defenseless. Forge made Logan one last invention, and with the lives of the X-Men and all mutants on the line, it’s time to unleash the ultimate weapon! COLLECTORS’ NOTE: Featuring the full debut of the Adamantium Armor!

Forge Gifted Wolverine With A Powerful Adamantium Armor

The mutant inventor Forge is known for creating fantastical new devices seemingly pulled straight from his mind… which is exactly what his powerful ability enables him to do. For much of the Krakoan Age Forge was the mutant who revolutionized Krakoan technology by utilizing biotechnology farmed from Krakoa’s impressive flora. Many of Wolverine’s X-Force technologies were developed by Forge, and the impressive mutant also once iconically made Logan an adamantium-dipped surfboard that could support Wolverine’s increased weight.

Marvel’s tease of the Adamantium Armor reveals there is a chance that Forge made the armor before the war with Orchis ever began, hinting that Forge knew Logan might need this ultimate weapon at some point in the future. Interestingly, Iron Man - who recently teamed up with Forge to create mysterium battleships - created the Mark 31, an Adamantium Iron Man armor, years ago in 2006’s Iron Man Vol. 4 #11 although the armor was quickly decommissioned. Victor Creed is a fierce and unrepentant murderer, and while he would not be able to shatter Wolverine’s adamantium bones, he could easily tear Logan from limb to limb, effectively killing him.

Wolverine May Be Depowered By Sabretooth

The synopsis for Wolverine #49 teases that Logan is “powerless,” hinting that Sabretooth may have found a way to depower Wolverine, potentially taking away his healing factor or somehow removing the adamantium from his bones. While this would be incredibly difficult, it has happened to Wolverine before, and it only makes sense that he would need Forge’s Adamantium Armor if he was unable to survive a direct assault by Creed. Wolverine is an incredibly prideful man, so the fact that he would even don an Adamantium Armor shows how desperate and determined he is to defeat Victor and avenge his loved ones.

While Wolverine has defeated Sabretooth in the past, Victor more commonly has the upper hand on Logan, meaning the Adamantium Armor could be the exact weapon Wolverine needs to finally put Sabretooth down for good. Wolverine #50 is the final issue of the Sabretooth War and also Benjamin Percy’s final Wolverine issue of the Krakoan Era, so the showdown between Wolverine and his lifelong archnemesis Sabretooth will surely be brutal, bloody, and covered in adamantium.

Wolverine #49 from Marvel Comics debuts May 8th, 2024.
