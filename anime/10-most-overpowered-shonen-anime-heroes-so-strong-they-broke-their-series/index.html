10 Most Overpowered Shonen Anime Heroes So Strong They Broke Their Series

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 2600 words 13 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Shonen anime heroes possess remarkable strength, extreme powers, and unshakable moral beliefs. Many of their feats seem truly impossible to achieve. Gojo Satoru from Jujutsu Kaisen , All Might from My Hero Academia , and Gyomei Himejima from Demon Slayer are a few of the most powerful and impressive shonen heroes. Popular shonen heroes like Eren Jaeger, Giorno Giovanna, and Monkey D. Luffy have achieved remarkable feats, constantly captivate viewers, and, at times, redefine what is possible in the series they feature in.

Literature Review

These 10 shonen heroes possess such impossible levels of power and skill that they often left fans speechless with their amazing actions.

Shonen anime series are known for their vivid animation, action-packed fight scenes, and larger-than-life characters that gave life to some of the most overpowered heroes in anime. These series are often characterized by themes like good vs. evil and protecting the weak. Some popular shonen anime include One Piece, Jojo’s Bizarre Adventure, Jujutsu Kaisen, Death Note, Attack on Titan, and Demon Slayer. One of the most important aspects of any good shonen anime is the brave heroes that serve as role models for fans to adore. These characters often spend their lives fighting to protect those who are in danger, have unshakable moral beliefs, and detest what they perceive to be evil or harmful to society. These 10 heroes possess all of those qualities and more, reigning supreme compared to all other characters. The powerhouses in this list are not only incredibly talented, but so overwhelmingly powerful that the series they are featured in are unable to handle the sheer magnitude of their strength.

10 All Might/Toshinori Yagi (My Hero Academia) Produced by bones inc., based on the original manga by Kōhei Horikoshi Toshinori Yagi, most popularly known as All Might, is widely lauded as the most impressive hero in My Hero Academia. He possesses an amazing Quirk called One For All that gives him impressive strength, agility, and speed. All Might’s incredible talent in battle defined what it means to be a true hero in the My Hero Academia universe, for Deku and for all other successors. His sheer, unmatched strength and muscle impress everyone around him and even just the mention of his name sends villains running. At times, his physical abilities seem truly impossible compared to other heroes. All Might has numerous skills that make him unstoppable enough to beat anyone and to redefine what the title “strongest hero” means. The techniques given to him by his Quirk One For All, the smashes Texas Smash and Detroit Smash, for example, help him to take down enemies with brute force and calculated attacks. Amazingly, he can even control the weather with his punches by producing wind and rain, something almost no other Quirk users are able to do. His unique and oftentimes surprising physical abilities make him an immensely overpowered character in comparison to his comrades in My Hero Academia. Watch on Crunchyroll

9 Kyojuro Rengoku (Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba) Produced by Ufotable, based on the original manga by Koyoharu Gotouge Kyojuro Rengoku from Demon Slayer was incredibly well-developed in his fighting and combat tactics. He employed a unique and powerful Breathing Technique that incorporated flames. This technique gave him the ability to fight off even the most powerful of demons with walls of fire. He was commonly regarded as one of the strongest of all the Hashira in Demon Slayer and his physical power and skilled use of his technique was almost unmatched by any other character in the series. Furthermore, his willpower and drive to fight is unbreakable and in battle, he simply refused to give up. Rengoku was taken out by Akaza, the Upper Rank Three Demon, in the Mugen Train arc of Demon Slayer, but his unfortunate demise does not negate his accomplishments. Shockingly, Rengoku earned the respect of Akaza himself, causing the demon to praise his developed abilities and offer to turn him into a demon, so he could continue utilizing his skills. The fact that he earned the regard of a demon is incredible and not seen often in Demon Slayer. He was one of the most overpowered Flame Hashiras to ever wield the Breathing Technique and well-deserving of his spot on this list. Watch on Crunchyroll

8 Monkey D. Luffy (One Piece) Produced by Toei Animation, based on the original manga by Eiichiro Oda As the One Piece anime and manga series have progressed, Monkey D. Luffy has only grown as an accomplished and proficient fighter. Even though he is fairly young, only 19 years old, he possesses an astonishing level of skill that most other characters in the series are unable to compete with. A few different abilities in Luffy’s arsenal to use against enemies are Gomu Gomu no Storm, Gomu Gomu no Cannon, and Gomu Gomu no Bullet. He has unleashed some overwhelming attacks on his enemies and has definitely proven himself an intimidating force to be reckoned with in battle. Luffy has become the master hero. He continually unleashes new and never before seen Devil Fruit powers that help him destroy any opponents in his way. He even develops a range of different Gears that allow him to use his abilities more successfully than anyone else can, shattering previous precedents One Piece established for how Devil Fruit can be used. Although some other characters can use similar abilities of control over their powers, no one has quite the advanced repertoire of techniques Luffy does. This special advantage makes him One Piece’s most groundbreaking and overpowered hero in comparison to all other characters. Watch on Crunchyroll

7 Izuku Midoriya/Deku (My Hero Academia) Produced by bones inc., based on the original manga by Kōhei Horikoshi One of My Hero Academia’s most marvelous characters is Izuku Midoriya, otherwise known as Deku. Deku started his hero journey completely Quirkless, with nothing but the dream of being a hero to inspire him to achieve this lofty goal. With valuable training from his hero, All Might, and after inheriting his remarkable Quirk, One For All, Deku became a forceful hero who defeats his enemies with ease. Deku is able to use One For All better and more effectively than All Might in one unique way: the past eight One for All Wielders’ Quirks are accessible for him to use. These past Quirks were not able to be utilized by All Might, but Deku has shockingly surpassed his predecessor and is even continuing to master new techniques. At the power level Deku is currently at, he has surpassed even his mentor All Might. This development is one of the series’ most important milestones. It seems incomprehensible that a previously Quirkless individual has grown to such unbelievable heights that he could beat the greatest hero of all time, but Deku achieved this and shattered everyone’s belief that to be a successful hero you must be born with a Quirk. Watch on Crunchyroll

6 Sasuke Uchiha (Naruto) Produced by Pierrot and Aniplex, based on the original manga by Masashi Kishimoto Naruto’s Sasuke Uchiha is one of the most exceptionally overpowered heroes in all of Naruto and is often viewed as one of the strongest characters in anime. Sasuke continually develops his abilities but also possesses a natural-born inclination to be incredible at his techniques, as if he was simply destined to be a master fighter from birth. He was called a “genius” multiple times throughout the course of the series, and he proves his powers time and time again in battle. His relentless mission to kill Itachi who murdered his clan drove him to push himself to impossible heights of power. Perhaps one of his only near equals is Naruto, who is on a similar level. This caused him to resent Naruto. Eventually, he helped convert Sasuke to better motives and aided him in ending his revenge-seeking tirade on Itachi. Sasuke is consistently one of the series’ most unbelievable and advanced characters, continually stretching the limits of power in the Naruto universe. His impressive physical magnitude, expert use of his chakra, and incorporation of other advanced techniques like Ninjutsu and Sharingan, shocked fans and earned him his rightful spot as one of the most groundbreakingly impactful heroes in his series. Watch on Crunchyroll

5 Gojo Satoru (Jujutsu Kaisen) Produced by MAPPA, based on the original manga by Gege Akutami

Gojo Satoru is Jujutsu Kaisen’s most complex hero. He utilizes many incredible abilities that are completely unique to him, including Hollow Purple, Six Eyes, and Limitless. Limitless makes enemy attacks have no effect on Gojo; the attacks slow down consistently and are never able to touch him. Hollow Purple melds his Red and Blue abilities into a terrifying blend that can wipe anything in its path out of existence altogether. Gojo’s Six Eyes help him “read” others’ levels of cursed energy, infer how they plan to attack, and predict the enemy’s next move with ease. Satoru’s techniques truly broke the Jujutsu Kaisen universe; no other sorcerer compares to him. He is the only holder of Six Eyes technique, and only members of the Gojo clan can utilize Limitless. In the Shibuya Incident arc, Satoru annihilated 1000 curses in a span of only 299 seconds. He was so powerful that his enemies could only devise a plan to trap him inside the Prison Realm, not beat him. Although Gojo lost to Ryomen Sukuna in chapter #236, fans hold out hope that Gojo’s overpowering strength will prevail like always and he will return. Watch on Crunchyroll

4 Giorno Giovanna (Jojo’s Bizarre Adventure) Produced by David Production, based on the original manga by Hirohiko Araki User of the Stand Golden Experience, Giorno Giovanna, is the strongest, most overpowered, and most intelligent Jojo in the series Jojo’s Bizarre Adventure. Golden Experience is one of the most powerful Stands featured in the anime, with the unique ability of creation at his disposal. Towards the end of Part 5: Golden Wind, Golden Experience levels up into Golden Experience Requiem after being hit by a Requiem Arrow and becomes unbeatable. This version of Giorno’s stand caused fans to rethink their previous beliefs on which Stand is strongest and accept Golden Experience Requiem as the most remarkable stand in the franchise. Golden Experience/Golden Experience Requiem possesses the ability to create new life and Giorno often uses it to change objects into completely new creations, like plants and frogs. He has used the skill effectively against enemies in numerous instances throughout the series, and it seems the Stand always has the perfect solution to ensure Giorno’s victory in literally every circumstance he comes across. Golden Experience Requiem can also stop enemy actions from ever being completed, thus allowing the Stand User to win every time. This virtually unconquerable ability allows him to overpower enemies who stand in his way without fail. Watch on Crunchyroll

3 Eren Jaeger (Attack on Titan) Produced by Wit Studio and MAPPA, based on the original manga by Hajime Isayama

Few Attack on Titan characters possess the sheer motivation, drive, endurance, and power that Eren Jaeger did. Eren was willing to sacrifice anything imaginable to achieve his goal of fully eradicating the man-eating Titans from the face of the earth and achieving victory and acceptance for him and his comrades on the island of Paradis. Eren did not simply have his Attack Titan powers, he had Founding Titan powers as well, doubling his abilities and making him virtually impossible to even dream of beating. With Eren’s Founding Titan power, he could control every other Titan as well as his own. Eren was strong enough simply with his Attack Titan powers, but his Founding Titan powers truly pushed him over the top and made him a terrifying adversary, capable of breaking the Attack on Titan universe. He effectively started The Rumbling, sent every Colossal Titan on a stomping rampage, and nearly succeeded in wiping out everyone on the entire planet. This was a horrific moment that left fans in complete awe at Eren’s sheer capacities. Whether one loves or hates Eren Jaeger, it is undeniable that few anime heroes or villains possess the intense, unbelievable power and unshakable will that he did. Watch on Crunchyroll

2 Gyomei Himejima (Demon Slayer) Produced by Ufotable, based on the original manga by Koyoharu Gotouge Gyomei Himejima, the Stone Hashira of Demon Slayer, is a character that is unfortunately often overlooked and forgotten about when considering all the intriguing Hashira in the series. One aspect that cannot be overlooked is Gyomei’s strength and brawn. He is confirmed to be the strongest Hashira, with Tanjiro and Inosuke both backing up this indisputable fact. Additionally, Kokushibo, an Upper Rank One demon, amazingly states that in a span of three hundred years, he had not ever come across anyone nearly as advanced in fighting and truly strong on a physical level as Gyomei proves to be. Although he is mostly rather soft-spoken and quiet, his physical skills make him a stand-out fighter against demons, and he has trained dutifully to reach this peak level. His shocking strength is unmatched by almost any other character in the series, hero or villain. Some of his most noteworthy traits are his fast speed, his incredibly muscled physique, his seemingly endless supply of stamina, and his expert use of Stone Breathing. Gyomei’s accomplishments are immensely noteworthy and continually push the limits of what a Demon Slayer Hashira is capable of. He certainly deserves a spot among shonen’s most overpowered heroes. Watch on Crunchyroll

1 Jotaro Kujo (Jojo’s Bizarre Adventure) Produced by David Production, based on the original manga by Hirohiko Araki

Jotaro Kujo is one of the most unforgettable Jojos in Jojo’s Bizarre Adventure. Amazingly, he was able to take down Dio Brando, the threatening vampire who wreaked havoc on humanity in Part 3: Stardust Crusaders. His Stand, Star Platinum, boasts immense physical strength and lightning speed, making him unstoppable. In order to vanquish his enemy, Jotaro stopped time with Star Platinum, a distinctive skill previously believed to only be available to Dio’s Stand, The World. This was one of the most surprising moments in anime history, redefining how his Stand could be used and allowing him to end the vampire’s villainous tirade. Star Platinum was difficult to beat even before Jotaro discovered how to stop time, but once he learned, he broke the Jojo’s Bizarre Adventure universe up to that point, becoming nearly impossible to achieve victory over. He goes on to help his daughter, Jolyne Kujo, face off against the terrifying Enrico Pucci in Part 6: Stone Ocean. Although his abilities decreased in strength with age, he was able to succeed against many menacing villains and save his daughter. Jotaro is one of the series’ most famous characters for good reason; his inconceivable strength makes him one of shonen’s most overpowered heroes. Watch on Crunchyroll It is quite unsurprising that shonen anime continues to be such a popular genre throughout the years. Shonen anime seems to have it all: eye-catching animation, intense action and gripping fight scenes, and most importantly of all, tough and steadfast heroes who are capable of destroying and redefining the universe of the series they are featured in. These characters vanquish any villains who dare to stand in their path with advanced physical force and calculated attacks. They never stop causing viewers’ jaws to drop with their overpowering actions, constantly changing what fans previously believed to be true with stronger evolutions of power.
