Starscream vs Soundwave Decides the Decepticons Leader in Major Transformers Lore Change

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 700 words 4 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Soundwave challenges Starscream for Decepticon leadership, potentially changing the continuity and resulting in an epic fight. The issue introduces a new story arc and artist, promising major changes for the Transformers series. The fallout from the previous issue’s shocking ending and the return of Megatron in the Energon Universe add to the suspense and uncertainty surrounding the characters’ fate.

Literature Review

The solicits for April’s Transformers #7 promise a fight between Starscream and Soundwave to determine once and for all who leads the Decepticons…

WARNING! Potential spoilers ahead for Transformers #7Starscream is currently the leader of the Decepticons in the new Transformers continuity, but a new challenger might be coming for the throne. According to the solicitations for this April’s Transformers #7, none other than Soundwave will be taking on Starscream for the leadership of the Decepticons, leading to an epic fight that is sure to have major ramifications going forward.

Releasing on April 10, the issue is notable for starting a new story arc and introducing a new artist in Jorge Corona. The solicitation promises more major changes for the new Transformers series, as Starscream takes on Soundwave to fill the leadership vacuum left by Megatron’s absence.

Transformers #7 (2024) Release Date: April 10, 2024 Writer: Daniel Warren Johnson Artist: Jorge Corona Colorist: Mike Spicer Letterer: Rus Wooten Cover Artists: Daniel Warren Johnson & Mike Spicer NEW STORY ARC! WHO WILL LEAD THE DECEPTICONS? After last issue’s shocking ending, Starscream and Soundwave battle for leadership—and the result will change the Decepticons forever. And the Autobots learn they may have more allies than they imagined… JORGE CORONA joins DANIEL WARREN JOHNSON to begin the Energon Universe story that will have everyone talking!

In addition to Starscream and Soundwave duking it out, the issue will also deal with the fallout of the previous issue, in which a major character is supposed to die. With the return of Megatron imminent in the Energon Universe, anything is possible, and no one is safe.

Will Soundwave Defeat Starscream To Become Leader of the Decepticons?

Soundwave challenging Starscream shouldn’t come as much of a surprise, as the current Decepticon leader has resorted to more brutal methods to gain Energon since reawakening on Earth. Soundwave has tolerated Starscream in the series up to this moment, but whatever will go down in Transformers #6 must be enough to push him to make a bid for the Decepticon leadership. Daniel Warren Johnson’s cover shows Starscream tearing Soundwave to bits, but the variant by Casper Wijngaard depicts Soundwave coming out on top. The two will have to figure something out quickly, as there are only a handful of Decepticons active in the current series.

Aside from the Decepticon smackdown, the issue promises other major revelations to kick off the second story arc. The solicitations for issue #6 state that a major character won’t survive, and the cover seems to be hinting that Optimus Prime might be the one to bite the bullet. Whether or not Prime falls in the course of the series, the Autobots are going to need all the help they can get. According to the solicits for Transformers #7, “the Autobots learn they may have more allies than they imagined…” It looks like new Autobots Elita-1 and Arcee will be joining the fight, but there could be some Real American Heroes around the corner.

The Energon Universe Promises Big Changes for Transformers In 2024

With the release of the first issues of Duke and Cobra Commander, the G.I. Joe corner of the Energon Universe is growing. While G.I. Joe and Cobra don’t yet exist in this continuity, fans have learned that M.A.R.S. Industries is developing weapons with Energon, and Cobra Commander is holding none other than Megatron himself prisoner. The Energon Universe began with a shocking reveal when Jetfire appeared at the end of Void Rivals #1, and it sounds like the surprises will keep coming well into 2024. Whether or not Starscream or Soundwave winds up as leader of the Decepticons, fans won’t want to miss a single issue of Transformers.

Transformers #7 is available April 10 from Skybound Entertainment
