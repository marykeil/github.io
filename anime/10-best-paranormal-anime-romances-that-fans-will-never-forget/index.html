10 Best Paranormal Anime Romances That Fans Will Never Forget

2020-10-16 About 2300 words 11 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Paranormal romance anime combines otherworldly elements with unforgettable love stories. Some couples, such as Jonathan Joestar and Erina Pendleton, have tragic romances that leave a major impact on the story. Like Inuyasha and Kagome, many iconic couples have disliked each other before ultimately falling in love.

Literature Review

While anime often blends genres, its combination of paranormal elements with romance has led to some memorable relationships over the years.

Anime is excellent at depicting the paranormal and combining it with some unforgettable romances over the years. The word “paranormal” refers to anything that is beyond the current understanding of science. While it’s often used interchangeably with “supernatural,” there may be slight differences between the two terms. Regardless, otherworldly creatures have become increasingly popular in the media, especially in stories involving romance. Due to their curiosity about the unknown, humans have always been fascinated by strange and unexplainable phenomena. This led to many stories about mysterious occurrences that were passed down through generations. As the Gothic influence also grew in the 19th century, some authors combined paranormal elements with romance and created a blend of genres that has since become a standard in fiction, including anime.

10 Hotaru & Gin Perfectly Represent Tragic Love Hotarubi no Mori e, produced by Brain’s Base and based on the manga by Yuki Midorikawa

Number of Episodes 1 (Film) Where to Stream Not available for streaming

As a child, Hotaru first meets Gin after the mysterious boy guides her out of the forest. When she tries to hug him, he quickly moves away and reveals that he will die if he touches a human. As the young girl continues to visit him every summer, the two develop a lasting friendship that turns to love as Hotaru enters high school. As the protagonists struggle with their feelings and inability to make physical contact, Gin takes Hotaru to a festival for spirits. When a human boy sneaks into the celebration, the young man accidentally touches him. Before he disappears, however, the two friends embrace for the first time and confess their feelings for each other. While their love never has a chance to blossom, Gin and Hotaru’s sentimental tale makes their romance unforgettable. Buy on Apply TV+

9 Chise Hatori & Elias Ainsworth Bond Over Shared Insecurities The Ancient Magus’ Bride, produced by Wit Studio/Studio Kafka and based on the manga by Kore Yamazaki

Number of Episodes 48 + 6 OAD Where to Stream Crunchyroll

After being shunned for her strange abilities and abandoned by her family, Chise sells herself at an auction. She is bought by Elias, a humanoid being with the head of an animal skull, who hopes to find a way to prevent her early death. Though their relationship starts on uneven ground, their master-servant relationship quickly turns into a mutual affection for each other. While Chise’s loyalty towards Elias stems from her dependence on him, the two stand on more equal ground as the story progresses. The Magus takes her as an apprentice and teaches her about her powers. At the same time, she teaches him about human emotions and empathy. Overall, Elias and Chise bond over their shared insecurities while helping each other appreciate the importance of relationships. Watch on Crunchyroll

8 Hiyori & Yato Fight Against Destiny Noragami, produced by Bones and based on the manga by Adachitoka

Number of Episodes 25 + 4 OVA Where to Stream Crunchyroll, Funimation, Hulu

After Hiyori’s soul leaves her body, she becomes stuck between the human world and the afterlife as a Half-Phantom. She makes a deal with the Delivery God, Yato, who claims he can fix her problem. As the two create one of the best fond families in anime with Yato’s weapon, Yukine, they also form a bond that is strengthened by Hiyori’s devotion to remembering the god. Throughout the series, the pair’s romance is hinted at through their exchanges. Several events foreshadow their union, such as Daikokuten, a god of matchmaking, blessing their plaques. However, many forces also keep them apart. If Hiyori returns to her human life, she will forget Yato, who will eventually outlive her. While Noragami’s recent conclusion offers little closure to their relationship, fans can remain hopeful about the pair’s future. Watch on Crunchyroll

7 Yusuke Urameshi & Keiko Yukimura Prove Odd Couples Can Also Love Each Other Yu Yu Hakusho, produced by Pierrot and based on the manga by Yoshihiro Togashi

Number of Episodes 112 + 8 OVA Where to Stream Crunchyroll, Funimation, Tubi TV, Hulu

For his first major work, Yoshihiro Togashi brought to life this iconic delinquent and his childhood friend. After Yusuke passes away while saving a child, Keiko rushes into a burning building to protect his body. When all hope seems to be lost, she revives him by transferring her life energy through a kiss. Based on his willingness to sacrifice himself for her safety, the spirit detective also cares deeply about his friend. Keiko is often absent from Yusuke’s adventures, but she remains one of his main connections to the human world. The young woman serves as a voice of reason for her reckless companion and is one of the few people who can scare him. While they often bicker, Yusuke and Keiko show affection for each other. Before leaving for Demon World, he even proposes to her, signifying his desire to stay by her side.

Yu Yu Hakusho
Yu Yu Hakusho is a multimedia franchise that began as a Japanese manga series in 1992 by author and illustrator Yoshihiro Togashi. The franchise ventured into anime in 1992 from Fuji Television, and there have also been two animated films. In 2023, Netflix released a live-action TV series starring Takumi Kitamura, Shuhei Uesugi, Jun Shison, and Kanata Hongō.

Watch on Crunchyroll

6 Jonathan Joestar & Erina Pendleton Suffer A Tragic Fate JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure, produced by David Production and based on the manga by Hirohiko Araki

Number of Episodes 190 total (9 episodes in Phantom Blood) Where to Stream Netflix, Hulu, Tubi TV, Crunchyroll, Pluto TV, Funimation

Despite their short presence in the series, Jonathan and Erina’s relationship has a lasting impact on the story. The two first meet as children and become inseparable until Dio’s actions force them apart. When they are reunited as adults, Erina tends to Jonathan’s injuries from his fight. This eventually brings them closer, and they soon marry. Unfortunately, the newlywed couple’s honeymoon is marred by tragedy after Dio appears and fatally wounds Jonathan, who dies one of the most shocking deaths in anime. Erina chooses to die by her husband’s side, but he persuades her to save an orphaned baby and escape in the vampire’s coffin. The young woman survives and gives birth to their son, George Joestar II, who grows up and marries the baby his mother saved. While their romance is ultimately short-lived, Jonathan and Erina’s union becomes the progenitor of the Joestar legacy, which will persevere through time and dimensions. Watch on Netflix

5 Nanami Momozono & Tomoe Fight For Forbidden Love Kamisama Kiss, produced by TMS Entertainment and based on the manga by Julietta Suzuki

Number of Episodes 25 + 5 OVA Where to Stream Crunchyroll, Hulu, Funimation

When Nanami first becomes a Land God, Tomoe shows irritation at having to serve a human girl. However, he slowly warms up to her cheerful personality and supports her in her duties as a new god. After Nanami first confesses to him, Tomoe rejects her since humans and Yokai are forbidden from falling in love. Despite this, he finds himself falling in love with her as the story progresses. Since Nanami and Tomoe are equally stubborn, their frequent clashes result in some hilarious moments. The fox demon’s initial mockery of the god turns to respect, and he comes to have faith in her abilities. This sentiment is returned by the young woman, who strives to work harder so she doesn’t hold him back. Despite their master-familiar contract, Nanami and Tomoe’s interactions resemble those of friends and equals. Watch on Crunchyroll

4 San & Ashitaka Prove That Love Can Overcome Hatred Princess Mononoke, written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki and produced by Studio Ghibli

Number of Episodes 1 (Film) Where to Stream HBO Max, Netflix

While San and Ashitaka represent different sides of the war, they are united by a common goal. The former prince first sees the wolf princess in the forest and is immediately drawn to her. The two have a rocky start to their relationship due to San’s hatred of humans and Ashitaka’s curse, but they quickly grow to trust each other. After the war ends, San returns to the forest while Ashitaka stays behind to help rebuild Iron Town and promises to visit her. There is a silent acknowledgment of their feelings for each other, but the two protagonists decide to part ways and stay in their respective worlds. Their relationship bridges the gap between the human and spirit worlds by showing how the two entities can peacefully co-exist.

Princess Mononoke
From Studio Ghibli and Hayao Miyazaki, Princess Mononoke is set in ancient Japan and follows Prince Ashitaka who, after being wounded by a corrupted boar spirit, ventures out on a quest to lift the curse placed on him by the wound. Originally written and performed in Japanese, the English-dubbed version of the film stars Billy Crudup, Claire Danes, and Minnie Driver. 

Watch on HBO Max

3 Kiyoka Kudo & Miyo Saimori Are A Truly Wholesome Couple My Happy Marriage, produced by Kinema Citrus and based on the light novel series by Akumi Agitogi and Tsukiho Tsukioka

Number of Episodes 12 Where to Stream Netflix

Due to her lack of supernatural power, Miyo is abused by her family and treated as a servant. Her father orders her to marry Kiyoka Kudo, who is rumored to be ruthless towards his prospective marriage partners. When she goes to live with her new fiancée, he notices her low self-esteem and submissive behavior, which he believes is strange for a woman from a distinguished family. Once he learns about her traumatic past, he does his best to help her realize her self-worth. Miyo’s appearance in the Kudo household breaks down Kiyoka’s walls. His initial pity for her situation turns into love, and he meets with her family to formally propose marriage only if they apologize to their eldest daughter. Miyo’s strong feelings allow her to stand up to her stepmother after she threatens the young woman into leaving her fiancée. Through their engagement, the couple bond from their experiences and learn to trust each other. This culminates in a wholesome relationship between two supposedly different individuals. Watch on Netflix

Romance anime had a fantastic year in 2023, with a variety of series that push the stereotypes of the genre while providing great entertainment.

2 Tohru Honda & Kyo Sohma Overcome their Flaws Together Fruits Basket, produced by TMS Entertainment and based on the manga by Natsuki Takaya

Number of Episodes 63 (2019 series) Where to Stream Crunchyroll, Hulu, Funimation

Tohru’s timid and patient nature is balanced by Kyo’s brash and short-tempered personality. While he’s initially reluctant to be her friend, her kindness and acceptance win him over, and he slowly opens up to her. Tohru also begins to see Kyo as her closest confidant and the only person with whom she can share her vulnerabilities. As they spend time together, their feelings of friendship slowly blossom into love. Tohru and Kyo’s interactions help them overcome their insecurities, with Kyo learning to communicate his feelings and Tohru learning to speak up for herself. After the young woman falls off a cliff, the redhead takes responsibility for his mistakes and confesses his love for her. Her reciprocation results in them breaking the Zodiac Curse and finally starting to date. In the epilogue, it’s shown that the couple will continue to maintain a loving relationship in the future. Watch on Crunchyroll

1 Inuyasha & Kagome Higurashi Inuyasha, produced by Sunrise and based on the manga by Rumiko Takahashi

Number of Episodes 193 Where to Stream Netflix, The Roku Channel, Pluto TV, Hulu, Freevee

What starts as a series of hostile encounters soon turns into a heartfelt romance for the protagonists. After learning that Kagome is Kikyo’s reincarnation, Inuyasha projects his anger onto her. However, he eventually recognizes the teenager as a different person and falls in love with her quirks. While their petty bickering often leads to full-blown fights, the duo always finds ways to reconcile. Inuyasha’s lingering feelings for Kikyo frequently cause a rift between the couple. However, as they strengthen their bond, the time traveler makes peace with her insecurities, and the demon learns to move on from his past. After the final battle for the Shikon Jewel, Kagome returns to the Feudal Era after three years and marries Inuyasha. They later have a daughter named Moroha, who grows up to be one of the protagonists in Yashahime: Princess Half-Demon. In addition to having one of the best paranormal romance, Inuyasha and Kagome also share one of the longest-lasting relationships in anime. Watch on Netflix
