Naruto s Only Two Canon Movies Create a Big Problem For Fans of the Series

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1100 words 5 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

The two canon movies in the Naruto franchise, The Last: Naruto the Movie and Boruto: Naruto the Movie, are currently unavailable on streaming platforms. The Last movie showcases strong character writing for Naruto and Hinata, and arguably the franchise’s most epic fight against Otsutsuki Toneri. Boruto: Naruto the Movie effectively introduces the next generation of ninjas and provides emotional moments while passing the torch to Boruto.

Literature Review

Two of the best Naruto movies that feature pivotal plot details and incredible fight scenes are currently more challenging to watch than ever.

People interested in experiencing all of the best Naruto content currently cannot stream the two canon movies that are arguably the best films in the popular franchise. Although the final chapter of Masashi Kishimoto’s beloved Naruto manga was released in 2014, the story has continued exploring life in Konoha after Naruto and Sasuke’s climactic duel at the end. The two films released after the series ended feature some of the franchise’s best action and character development but are currently difficult to find on streaming platforms.

Before the sequel series Boruto: Naruto Next Generations began, two films were released that significantly helped bridge the gap between Boruto and the ending of the Naruto series. The Last: Naruto the Movie and Boruto: Naruto the Movie are canonical to the franchise’s storyline and are currently unavailable on the same streaming platforms as the animes.

One of the significant pitfalls of streaming platforms that is becoming increasingly apparent is that movies or series can easily be removed from streaming services. For now, most of the Naruto franchise is available on Netflix, Hulu, and Crunchyroll. However, the only two cannon movies are not.

The Last Movie Has Amazing Character Growth And One Of The Best Fights Available To Purchase Through: Amazon, Apple TV, and YouTube

The Last: Naruto The Movie takes place two years after Naruto’s ending. The film explores a time in Naruto’s life that many longtime fans wish to see more of: the years after the Fourth Shinobi War and before he became the Seventh Hokage. The Last delivers on that desire while effectively building Naruto and Hinata’s relationship. While the “final” Naruto movie does feature the most romance in the series, it also features beautiful animation and one of the franchise’s best fights against Otsutsuki Toneri.

The Last: Naruto the Movie is available to stream through Funimation, but that streaming service is due to shut down April 2024.

Otsutsuki clan members have continued to be frighteningly powerful enemies well into the Boruto series. The Last’s primary antagonist, Otsutsuki Toneri, proved to be one of the series’ strongest foes in their battle against Naruto in the movie’s climatic final fight. After the War, Naruto was easily the strongest shinobi in the world. Still, he’d have to use his most powerful form to take down Toneri. The movie is a must-see for fans who want to see how Naruto and Hinata’s relationship blossoms and for those who want to see Naruto’s stunning battle on the moon.

The Boruto Movie Is An Emotional And Effective Passing Of The Torch Available To Purchase Through: Amazon, Apple TV, and YouTube

The manga’s ending of Naruto’s story was poetic and incredibly satisfying, and thankfully, so was the excellently directed and written Boruto: Naruto the Movie. It effectively introduced the next generation of ninjas living in The Village Hidden in the Leaves. Now serving as the Seventh Hokage, Naruto takes a step back as the franchise’s main character so that his son Boruto can take on the mantle. While the movie does include some immense scale battles starring Naruto and Sasuke, the Boruto movie includes many incredibly emotional moments for fans and Boruto’s character.

The events of Boruto: Naruto the Movie were adapted in episodes #53 - #66 of the Boruto anime with a few major differences, especially in direction, pacing, and certain character designs.

The movie was released before the filler-filled Boruto anime. It does a better job of establishing Boruto’s character and the rift between him and his father in a way that doesn’t feel like an insult to viewers’ time. Within the movie’s 95-minute runtime, it presents viewers with an effective understanding of the franchise’s new cast, great action scenes, and emotionally satisfying moments that bridge the themes of the Naruto series into Boruto’s. Ultimately, Boruto: Naruto the Movie is the send-off Naruto deserves and is a must-watch experience for fans who grew up with the series.

Naruto’s Author Helped With The Last And Boruto Movie’s More Than The Others Naruto Was Written And Illistrated By Masashi Kishimoto

With several popular anime series like Dragon Ball Super, One Piece, and Demon Slayer successfully making millions of dollars with films in movie theaters around the world, it’s surprising that there have not been any Naruto or Boruto films released since Boruto: Naruto the Movie in 2015. While the last two films in the franchise are considered its best, it is disappointing that they are not more readily available on the streaming platforms that host the anime series. Hopefully, that changes, and the Naruto movies will become easier for new fans to enjoy.
