Literature Review

The No Return mode of The Last of Us 2 Remastered has different challenges you can complete for various rewards, including skins, features, and mods.

A run in No Return starts when you pick a character like Ellie or Abby, who both have unique skills that let them approach encounters with different strategies. The challenges within a run are divided into boss, core, gambit, mod, and hero objectives in TLOU 2 Remastered. Many of these demand multiple runs, whether you win or lose against the enemy waves fought during missions.

Every No Return Boss Challenge and Rewards

The end of every run in No Return is punctuated by a deadly boss, but you only have one unlocked at first. In this game mode, six bosses make up the possible encounters after you defeat them all at least once. Many of these foes are familiar enemies that you may have faced before as either Ellie or Abby during the main story.

Boss Challenge Reward Defeat the Arcade Bloater Unlocks the Theater Infestation boss. Defeat the Theater Infestation Unlocks the Rattler Captain boss and Faction. Defeat the Rattler Captain Unlocks the Garage Bloater boss. Defeat the Garage Bloater Unlocks the Seraphite Elite boss. Defeat the Seraphite Elite Unlocks the Rat King boss. Defeat the Rat King Unlocks Randomized Bosses, which lets your run cycle between all the bosses listed instead of specific ones.

Every No Return Core Challenge and Rewards

Core challenges unlock new features for No Return, which allow you to adjust the settings of your run or attempt new mission types. The rewards for these goals are obtained simply by attempting any string of encounters. Whether you win or lose, the number of runs you try in TLOU 2 Remastered is tied to the prerequisites for core challenge rewards.

Core Challenge Reward Description Play 1 Run Unlocks Custom Run feature. Custom Run lets you adjust the rules of your run, which includes changes to enemies, mods, and more. Play 2 Runs Unlock Gambits feature. Your run now has a random chance for a Gambit to appear. These are smaller challenges that could lead to extra rewards if you complete them. Play 3 Runs Unlock Dead Drops feature. A Dead Drop now has a chance to randomly appear during an encounter. Depositing a specific item in these places can cause a new weapon or recipe to become available when you return to the Hideout. Play 5 Runs Unlock Game Mode - Holdout. Holdout encounters now become available during a run. During this mission type, you have to survive against waves of enemies with infinite reinforcements until a timer runs out. Play 7 Runs Unlock Game Mode - Capture. Capture encounters now become available during a run. This mission type demands that you break into a safe full of valuable supplies that a group of enemies is guarding. Play 9 Runs Unlock Game Mode - Daily Run. You may now attempt a single Daily Run in No Return, and try to make it through a randomly generated series of encounters to achieve the highest rank on an online leaderboard.

Every No Return Gambit Challenge and Rewards

Challenges tied to small Gambit goals change as you progress to higher Tiers, and they usually include a number of small activities to complete throughout a run. For example, one Gambit might be to get three kills from the prone position or stun two enemies with stun bombs. Completing these tasks could reward you with more currency, extra ammo, health recovery, or other smaller buffs.

The buffs provided by completing Gambits are stronger if you are at a higher Gambit Tier. Make sure to choose the playable characters in No Return that have the best chance at meeting these goals before starting a run.

Success in No Return can be determined by which supplies you have at your disposal between encounter rounds. As a result, you should pursue these Gambits for the resources they offer in addition to the progress needed to achieve better rewards at a higher Tier.

Every No Return Mod Challenge and Rewards

Mod challenges are similar to Gambits, but each Tier you reach unlocks new mods for you to apply in a run rather than a new set of goals. Three-four mods are rewarded when you complete encounters with other mods active in TLOU 2 Remastered. To improve your Mod Tier, you’ll want to find characters on your roster who pair well with the equipment you already have available.

Every No Return Hero Challenge and Rewards

The most specific challenges that show up in TLOU 2 Remastered are the ones tied to individual heroes like Ellie or Abby. Completing encounters with certain characters could unlock new people to your roster or give you skins for the one you’re playing as. Out of the 10 playable characters in the game, each has two-five challenges tied to them.

Final Challenges and Rewards in The Last of Us 2 Remastered

The last two rewards in TLOU 2 Remastered depend on whether you are playing as Ellie-aligned heroes or ones who are associated with Abby. You need to complete every challenge for either faction of characters to unlock unique weapon skins for everyone on your roster. Finishing every goal with Ellie’s people gives you the Moth Skin, while doing the same for Abby’s will give you the Wolf Skin.

Any objectives you tackle do not have to be repeated in order to reach a new Tier or higher caliber of cosmetic. All the challenges and rewards from No Return in The Last of Us Part 2 Remastered are there to increase your enjoyment of this new roguelike survival mode.
