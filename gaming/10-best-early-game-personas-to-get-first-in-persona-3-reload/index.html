10 Best Early Game Personas To Get First in Persona 3 Reload

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1900 words 9 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Literature Review

Though there are tons of Personas in Persona 3 Reload, options are limited early on, so deciding what to use to form a powerful team can be difficult.

Since none of the early game Personas will be extremely powerful, it’s more important to think about other ways they may be useful. For example, Personas that have a variety of attacks or offer good buffing skills may be smart options to have on your team, even if they’re a little weaker than preferred.

10 Fortuna Fortune Arcana

Fortuna is an early-game Fortune Arcana Persona that, unlike the others on this list, requires a specific combination of three Personas to make it. It is the first Special Fusion Persona players will have access to since everything required as part of its recipe can be found almost immediately upon starting the game. The required Personas to fuse Fortuna include Angel, Silky, and Unicorn. Although Fortuna is not overly powerful in Persona 3 Reload, she offers a good range of coverage for Wind and Ice damage that is worth having early on in Persona 3 Reload. Fortuna is immune to Wind and Fire attacks but weak to Electric damage. One of her main downsides is that her associated Social Link is not acquired until mid-game, so she cannot gain enhanced EXP upon fusing early on.

9 Archangel Justice Arcana

The Archangel is an early-game Justice Arcana Persona that players will encounter early that can do Light damage, also known as Bless Damage in Persona 5 Royal. Though the stats of this Persona are pretty well-rounded, the early access to Light damage is the main reason for adding Archangel to your roster. However, it’s also worth noting that this Persona offers resistance to Slashing damage and immunity to Light damage. On the other hand, this Persona is weak to Dark or Curse Damage and Electric, which can make it tricky to use against certain enemies in Persona 3 Reload. The Justice Social Link, Chihiro Fushimi, is not accessible until the tail end of the early game, but if you’re still holding onto this Persona by then, it can help boost your rank when hanging out with this Social Link in Persona 3.

8 Gurulu Moon Arcana

Gurulu of the Moon Arcana is the first Persona players will likely encounter that offers Dark damage, a rarity for this early in Persona 3 Reload. It also offers immunity to Dark damage from enemies in return and resistance to Wind attacks. Though it also offers good physical attacks, the use of Dark attacks is the primary reason for grabbing this Persona as early as possible.

Note that Gurulu is weak to Fire and Light damage, so you will need to watch out for that. Additionally, the Social Link of the Moon Arcana is unlocked in late-game, so there will not be any EXP bonus when fusing this Persona early on.

7 Neko Shogun Star Arcana

Neko Shogun is from the Star Arcana and is perhaps one of the most recognizable Personas in the franchise. While its attacks are not particularly strong, Neko Shogun offers something potentially better. This Persona has access to a ton of passive buff moves, specifically to several that offer a crit ratio increase, like Sharp Student, Auto Rebellion, and Crit Rate Boost. Though these may not seem as useful as outright damage, they can make all the difference when battling enemies with a significant amount of HP. Neko Shogun offers immunity to Light and Dark damage, a rare combination, and resistance to Electricity. The only weakness this Persona has is to Wind damage, while every other type of attack is neutral.

6 Forneus Emporer Arcana

Forneus is the earliest Persona players can access, as it is one of the first to be encountered in Persona 3 Reload. While this Persona of the Emporer Arcana is not significantly strong, it is the first Persona that will have Tarakuja, an Attack Boost move. He also offers a Theurgy attack with Decarabia, called Best Friends. Decarabia is a late-game Persona, so Forneus is likely one that players will want to hold onto and level up so that they may get back to it later. Forneus is resistant to Ice attacks but weak to Electric damage, as are many early-game Personas. The associated Social Link, Hidetoshi Odagiri, is not unlocked until the tail end of the first part of Persona 3 Reload, but if you hang onto this Persona for a while, you are likely to be able to get the mutual benefit between the two for a while. Forneus may be worth keeping around if you have an extra slot available solely for the added boost with Hidetoshi until another Emperor Persona can replace it. Having Personas that match the Arcana of a Social Link can be extremely helpful in speeding things up. While it does have a reverse benefit of giving the Persona extra EXP when fused if that Social Link rank is higher, using the Persona to help boost your rank with the Social Link is more important, especially with such limited time.

5 Lilim Devil Arcana

Of the Devil Arcana, Lilim is an excellent all-around Persona in terms of stats. As with Neko Shogun, Lilim is not prioritized for its attacks but rather for what it offers in terms of buffs. Specifically, Lilim is one of the first Personas to access both Tarukaja and Rakukaja, moves that buff both attack and defense for allies. While it may seem risky to have a Persona whose main focus is boosting the party rather than attacking, this can often make or break a fight with strong enemies.

Lilim will also have access to Marin Karin, a status ailment move in Persona 3 Reload with a high chance of inflicting Charm and Life Drain, which is rare for a Persona this early. Lilim is resistant to Electric and Dark damage while weak to Light attacks. Unfortunately, the associated Social Link for this Arcana is very late in the game. So this Persona will likely have been replaced by then, so there will be no benefit for Lilim or the Social Link.​​​​​​​

4 Jack Frost Magician Arcana

Jack Frost is another Persona that many are likely to recognize. It is from the Magician Arcana and, therefore, has a decent magic stat compared to other Personas in the early portion of Persona 3 Reload. While it has good moves, the primary reason for recruiting Jack Frost is its Theurgy attack with Jack-o’-Lantern, an attack type similar to Showtime in Persona 5 Royal, but with Personas instead of Party Members. Additionally, Jack Frost is the first Persona that will have an item attached to it. The item, Jacktite, can be unlocked once this Persona has been leveled up to a certain point, and it’s worth obtaining as it can then be used for gear crafting. Its immunity to ice and weakness to fire are apparent traits that probably won’t do much for you one way or the other. However, the Magician Social Link, Kenji Tomochika, is one of the first in the game to unlock. Hence, starting this Persona off with a significant boost and using it afterward to continue leveling up your rank with Kenji is possible.

3 Rakshasa Strength Arcana

Rakshasa is one of the best early-game Physical Attack Personas players can obtain now. The Persona of the Strength Arcana has access to medium-level physical attacks like Fatal End and Assault Dive, some of the strongest you will be able to find early on in Persona 3 Reload. Additionally, Rakshasa can learn good passive moves, like Regenerate, Auto Rebellion, and Counter. The last of which is not as common for early-game Personas. This Persona is resistant to both Slashing and Piercing damage, making it a great Persona to have on hand as early as possible. Its weaknesses include both Wind and Light, nothing too uncommon for other Personas around this level. Similarly to the Magician Arcana, the Strength Arcana Social Link is unlocked very early. Therefore, players should be able to fuse this Persona with a significant boost and use it to continue increasing their rank with Yuko Nishiwaki, one of the female protagonists in Persona 3 Reload.

2 Jack-o’-Lantern Magician Arcana

Jack-o’-Lantern is one of the best early-game Personas available, as it has a very high magic stat for its level and access to a wide variety of elemental-type coverage, depending on how it’s fused. Players should make an effort to grab this Persona as early as possible since it is the one that has the Theurgy attack with Jack Frost called Jack Brothers, which can be a great addition to combat, especially when Theurgy combos are hard to come by early on. Jack-o’-Lantern is Jack Frost’s polar opposite when it comes to typing in that he is weak to Ice damage while immune to Fire attacks. Since this Persona is also from the Magician Arcana, players will get the same benefit from the associated Social Link, Kenji Tomochika, as they do with Jack Frost. However, if you use both, there won’t be any stacked boost to ranking up the Social Link since only one Persona of that Arcana is required for that boost.​​​​​​​

1 Pale Rider Death Arcana

Pale Rider is the best Persona under level 30 that players can access in Persona 3 Reload. It has high Magic and Strength stats for its level, with good moves to match it. In addition to having access to Dark attacks, Pale Rider will also be able to learn significant early-game Physical attacks like Fatal End as well as passive buffs like Auto Sukukaja and Resist Fear. This Persona is one of the few that will last players well beyond when it is initially acquired. Pale Rider is resistant to Wind with the power to reflect Dark Attacks. This Persona is weak to Light, also known as Bless Damage, but is otherwise neutral to all other attacks. Unfortunately, the Social Link associated with the Death Arcana is Pharos, who only levels up through story progression, so there will be no benefit in that area. However, even still, Pale Rider is a Persona every player should have in their inventory throughout the first part of their playthrough of Persona 3 Reload.
