SUMMARY

Natalie’s first husband Zenon Pierides was someone her mother approved of and wanted her to stay with, but Natalie left him when he filed for bankruptcy. Natalie secretly wishes to go back to Zenon and believes things would be different if she met him now. Natalie complained about her current boyfriend Josh for not being clear about their relationship and not inviting her to stay at his apartment in Los Angeles or meet his kids and mother. Her mother didn’t understand why Natalie would be with a man who didn’t want to marry her.

Literature Review

Natalie Mordovtseva spoke about her first ex-husband for the first time on 90 Day: The Single Life season 4. Natalie has been married three times.

90 Day: The Single Life star Natalie Mordovtseva is finally opening up about her first husband, Zenon Pierides. Natalie from Kyiv, Ukraine, was engaged to Mike Youngquist during her first 90 Day Fiancé season. Natalie came to the U.S. on a K-1 visa in 90 Day Fiancé season 8 and got married to Mike on the day her visa was about to expire. Natalie faced challenges in her marriage. She decided it was best for her to leave Mike and head to a different state. Natalie then met Josh Weinstein in Florida before moving to California.

Natalie and her mom, Neliia, discussed her relationship with Josh in a recent episode. Natalie’s mom compared Josh to Natalie’s first two husbands. Natalie complained that she and Josh had been dating for a year and a half, and their relationship was still unclear to her. She said Josh had an apartment in Los Angeles and didn’t even invite Natalie and her mom to stay there. Josh hadn’t even invited Natalie to meet his kids or his mother. Neliia didn’t get the point of being with someone who didn’t want to get married.

Natalie’s Married Zenon Pierides At 22

Neliia reminded Natalie that if she was in Ukraine and had dated someone for even a month, the man would have immediately asked Natalie if she wanted to live together. She gave the example of Natalie’s first husband. Natalie had been married before Mike and spoke about her failed marriage during 90 Day Fiancé season 7. However, it was her second marriage that Natalie was talking about and not about her first. Natalie’s first husband Zenon, was a man from Cyprus. Instagram blogger @90daythemelanatedway shared photos of Natalie and Zenon’s wedding ceremony.

Why Natalie Left Zenon Despite His Good Qualities

Neliia reminded Natalie of how Zenon wanted to move in with her, even giving Natalie a ring. “Isn’t that what you want? You were his bride,” her mom told Natalie. Natalie told the cameras that her mom loved her first husband a lot. “He was very educated, nice, polite,” she confessed. Natalie; however, failed to add the reason why she left him. According to the 90 Day Fiancé blogger, Natalie and Zenon lived together in Cyprus during their marriage. They were married for two years. “When he filed for bankruptcy, she left him,” the source mentioned.

Natalie Secretly Wishes To Go Back To Zenon

Natalie came back to Ukraine after leaving Zenon. Natalie described Zenon as someone very sensitive and an overall decent human. “Never fight, always smooth,” she remembered. Natalie said she was the one who was always “sparkle, sparkle,” perhaps referring to her picking fights with Zenon. “If I could meet him now things would be different,” Natalie confessed. Natalie said she was “very happy” back then, but it wasn’t the “right time” for the 90 Day: The Single Life season 4 star.
