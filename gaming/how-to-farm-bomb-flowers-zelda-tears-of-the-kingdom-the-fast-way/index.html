Literature Review

Bomb Flowers are very useful items for combat or solving puzzles in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, so players should try to farm plenty in fast ways.

One of the most common uses for Bomb Flowers is for breaking cracked rock formations, which are seen in caves or across Hyrule in different locations. During the early stages of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, this plant is the only way to get past these rocks. However, when you complete the “Yunobo of Goron City” quest, Link can use the ability of a new companion to destroy these rocks without having to use any Bomb Flowers. However, you can still use Bomb Flowers as a weapon by attaching it to an arrow and shoot it at hordes of enemies or bosses.

              How To Farm Poe In Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom (The Fast Way)   While players can farm lots of Poe in The Depths of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, there are fast ways to get this important currency while exploring.

Easiest Ways To Farm Bomb Flowers In Zelda: TOTK

Bomb Flowers can be found in specific locations across Hyrule, and farming them at these spots is the best way to acquire them in Zelda: TOTK. These plants are located in the Royal Hidden Passage, Bottomless Cave, the East and West Wells in Hateno Village, and the North Well in Popla Foothills. To effectively farm, you should visit the Royal Hidden Passage, as it has twice as many Bomb Flowers as other locations. You can find this place close to Lookout Landing in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom at coordinates (-0254, 0104, 0008).

You can also gather Bomb Flowers from any Well you come across in Hyrile. If you are in the Great Sky Island, head to the Bottomless Cave, as it also has many Bomb Flowers growing within it. By fast traveling to a Shrine or Skyview Tower near a Well, you can quickly snatch up at least a few Bomb Flowers for the journey ahead. For example, a cave next to the Popla Foothills North Well holds some Flowers in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom.

Complete any quest to automatically respawn all Bomb Flowers in any location on the map instead of waiting for a Blood Moon.

Unlike other important resources like Wild Greens in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, the Blood Moon does not reset Bomb Flowers. While these items will eventually respawn over time, you can complete any quest to reset all the Bomb Flowers at any location. Doing this creates a fast way to farm Bomb Flowers from their dedicated locations in The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom at any time where tons of the plant may be collected.
