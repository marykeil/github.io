Literature Review

By leveling Xianyun and her Talents, you can unlock her full potential as a support unit in Genshin Impact. Here are the materials you need to farm.

In essence, Xianyun allows her party members to jump higher and, with it, they can become Plunging Attack DPS. This gameplay mechanic has been present since the very beginning of the game, though only a few characters actually benefit from Plunging Attacks. The most useful character that can accomplish this on his own is Xiao, an Anemo DPS unit who can jump higher with his Elemental Burst and continuously dive onto enemies. Xianyun’s Burst allows her teammates to do the same. Before taking advantage of her abilities, however, you should Ascend Xianyun in Genshin Impact by farming the right materials.

Ascension Materials To Farm For Xianyun In Genshin Impact

According to Honey Hunter World’s data compilation for the character, Xianyun will require most of what is needed for 5-star characters. As an Anemo unit, the first materials you will need to farm are from the Vayuda Turquoise stone family. Vayuda Turquoise Sliver, Fragment, Chunk, and Gemstones can often be obtained from most bosses that have some sort of wind-based power. This includes the Anemo Hypostasis, Maguu Kenki, and the Setekh Wenut, for instance. This is not limited to world bosses in Genshin Impact, however, as Stormterror and the Shouki no Kami weekly bosses can also provide Vayuda Turquoise stones.

The data was compiled by Honey Hunter World before Xianyun’s official release, so the exact amounts for each Ascension level may be slightly different, though the required materials themselves are accurate.

Other than the elemental stones, you will also need to farm Divining Scroll and its evolved forms, Sealed Scroll and Forbidden Curse Scroll, respectively. These special materials are often dropped from Samachurls, the small shaman-like creatures that are part of the Hilichurl tribes. These opponents can be found throughout Teyvat, so it is best to use your Adventurer Handbook to mark these targets on your map and then proceed to eliminate the Samachurls. Some Divining Scrolls and Sealed Scrolls can be purchased from the Stardust Exchange in Genshin Impact, while Forbidden Curse Scrolls can be acquired from the Starglitter Exchange.

You are encouraged to use the Crafting Bench to transform lower-grade materials into higher-grade ones as your Ascension needs start to get more demanding. Three Divining Scrolls, for instance, can be transmuted into one Sealed Scroll.

The next material needed to farm for Xianyun is called Clearwater Jade. This item is a Version 4.4 addition, so it could not be pre-farmed. To obtain it, you will need to explore Chenyu Vale in the north portion of Liyue. This is a local specialty and, like many of the items in this category, Clearwater Jade is designed to stand out in the open world, ripe for the taking. A large amount is needed, however, so it may take a few days before you can obtain everything required to unlock all of Xianyun’s Ascension thresholds in Genshin Impact.

The final material you need to farm is called Cloudseam Scale. This is a special material that can only be obtained from the Solitary Suanni, a world boss added in Version 4.4’s Chenyu Vale. Not too many are needed in comparison to other materials, but you will need to challenge this boss quite a few times to obtain everything necessary to get Xianyun past her level 80 Ascension threshold. You will also need a large sum of 420,000 Mora in Genshin Impact to get through her entire Ascension process.

If you intend on leveling Xianyun solely with Character EXP Materials, you will consume an additional 1,673,400 Mora.

Materials For Xianyun’s Talents (Abilities) In Genshin Impact

In addition to her character Ascension, you will also need to farm materials for Xianyun’s Talents. Increasing the level of her abilities is vital to maximizing her potential. Divining Scroll, Sealed Scroll, and Forbidden Curse Scroll are needed for her Talents, so you should farm a surplus when eliminating Samachurls during her Ascension process. The Talent books you will need to farm for Xianyun in Genshin Impact are of the Gold family. Teachings, Guide, and Philosophies of Gold can be farmed from the Taishan Mansion Domain, in Liyue. The Gold books only drop on Wednesdays, Saturdays, and Sundays.

The table below lists all the materials needed to increase all of Xianyun’s Talents to level 10:

Xianyun Talent Materials In Genshin Impact Teachings of Gold 9x Guide to Gold 63x Philosophies of Gold 114x Divining Scroll 18x Sealed Scroll 66x Forbidden Curse Scroll 93x Lightless Eye of the Maelstrom 18x Crown of Insight 3x Mora 4,957,500x

The calculations do not consider the cap upgrade Xianyun gets to her Skill and Burst at C3 and C5, respectively. More items will be needed to fully upgrade her abilities if you unlock C3 or both of these Constellation levels.

As you progress, Xianyun’s Talents will start requiring an item called Lightless Eye of the Maelstrom. This item can be farmed from the All-Devouring Narwhal boss in Genshin Impact. Not too many are needed, but the cap on weekly boss challenges can put your farming back a few weeks. At their last levels, Xianyun’s Talents will need Crowns of Insight, a rare material that can be obtained from limited-time events and reputation systems, such as the Sacred Sakura. A huge amount of Mora will also be needed to max out Xianyun’s Talents.

The farming process to fully Ascend Xianyun and level her Talents is not overly complicated, but it does require some dedication. It can also be quite time-consuming, especially considering enemy respawn times, weekly limits on certain boss challenges, and daily Original Resin limitations. Knowing what you need to farm for Xianyun in Genshin Impact should put you on the right path, even if you have not yet unlocked her as a playable character.
