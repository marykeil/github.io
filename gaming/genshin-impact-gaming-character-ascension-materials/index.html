Genshin Impact Gaming Character Ascension (& Materials)

SUMMARY

Literature Review

It may consume some time and resources, but it is worth Ascending Gaming in Genshin Impact to unlock his potential as a main DPS Pyro unit.

The 4-star Pyro character can be built into a fine DPS character, even if subpar to other 5-star heroes who execute the same job as him. Nevertheless, Gaming can be considered a F2P option, as you can grab him as a free promotional character – you can obtain one 4-star character from Liyue for free during the main event of Genshin Impact 4.4 if you accomplish a set of menial in-game tasks. Before building him, however, it is recommended to fully Ascend and level Gaming, as this will unlock the ability to increase all of his stats.

Ascension Materials To Farm For Gaming In Genshin Impact

As a Pyro character, some of the first materials you will need to farm for Gaming are Agnidus Agate stones. This item family is divided into four rarities: Slivers, Fragments, Chunks, and Gemstones. All of them will be needed, so you may have to use the Crafting Table to transform batches of lower-quality materials into higher-quality ones. Agnidus Agate stones can be mostly farmed from Pyro bosses in Genshin Impact, such as Pyro Hypostasis, Pyro Regisvine, Primo Geovishap (when it is in its Pyro form), Azhdaha, and La Signora.

The table below lists all the materials and Mora you will need to fully Ascend Gaming, divided by level threshold, according to Honey Hunter World:

Honey Hunter World’s data compilation was made before the character’s official release and was based on leaks and other 4-star Pyro character releases, so some materials and quantities may be inaccurate.

Slime items are the next material family you will need to farm for Gaming. Slime Condensate, Slime Secretions, and Slime Concentrate are all farmed from slime opponents, which are scattered across all the regions in Teyvat. An effective way of establishing a farming route is to track slimes with your in-game Adventurer Handbook in Genshin Impact. This will mark slime locations on the map – though you can use external ones, such as the official interactive map on HoYoLAB. You can also buy Slime items from the Stardust and Starglitter Exchange features in the shop.

The Local Specialty required for Gaming’s Ascension is Starconch. This item can be found across some of the beach portions of Liyue. As stated on the official map, there are 77 Starconch nodes available in the game. As is the case with most Local Specialities in Genshin Impact, these take to two real-life days to respawn (fast-forwarding time in the game does not force the items to respawn), which means you will be unable to collect everything that is required to Ascend Gaming in a single run. In fact, two full runs should also come short of the 165 required.

If you do not have any Starconch, it may take up to a week to acquire everything that is needed.

The last item needed is the boss material: Emperor’s Resolution. This item is exclusively obtained from the Emperor of Fire and Iron, located in the region of Fontaine. Only 65 are needed, but each challenge only rewards players with a few copies of the item, and given the daily limit to Original Resin, needed for claiming the rewards, it could take some days to farm all the Emperor’s Resolution you need.

Though not a material per si, you will also need to invest a sum of Mora into Gaming’s Ascension process. To unlock all the level thresholds that allow him to get to level 90, you will be charged a total of 420,000 Mora. If you are using Character EXP Materials to level up Gaming, you will also have to use an extra 1,673,400 Mora in Genshin Impact. This, however, is not the most expensive process, as leveling Gaming’s Talents to their maximum level requires a lot more farming and Mora.

Materials For Gaming’s Talents (Abilities) In Genshin Impact

Gaming’s Talents are a lot more demanding than is Ascension process, which makes farming for it much harder as well. Many more materials are needed and some limitations can set you back a few weeks. The Slime family of items is the first thing you will want to farm. While farming these for Gaming’s Ascension, you should farm a surplus of Slimes, since many Slime Condensate, Slime Secretions, and Slime Concentrate are needed to max out his Talents in Genshin Impact.

The table below lists everything that is needed to level all three of Gaming’s Talents to their maximum level:

Gaming Talent Materials In Genshin Impact Teachings of Prosperity 9x Guide to Prosperity 63x Philosophies of Prosperity 114x Slime Condensate 18x Slime Secretions 66x Slime Concentrate 93x Lightless Mass 18x Crown of Insight 3x Mora 4,957,500x

Gaming uses the Prosperity Books to level his Talents. Teachings, Guide, and Philosophies of Prosperity can be farmed from Taishan Mansion, on Mondays, Thursdays, and Sundays. You will need a lot and there is a limit on Daily Resin consumption, which means it may take a few days or even weeks to get all that is necessary. Another item you need to have is Crown of Insight, though only three are needed. The last item you need is Lightless Mass. This is one of the three Talent materials that can be obtained from the All-Devouring Narwhal boss in Genshin Impact.

The amount of resources described considers Gaming at C0. At C3 and C5, the caps on his Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst, respectively, are increased to level 15.

Since you have a weekly limit on this boss challenge and a random chance of dropping the desired item, you may take some time to adequately farm it. In addition to the materials, you will need a large sum of Mora to fully level up Gaming’s Talents. Nearly 5 million Mora is required, so you may have to challenge Ley Line Outcrop: Blossom of Wealth a few times to stock up on the currency. Although it is still time-consuming and expensive, farming with precision should shorten the period you spend optimizing Gaming in Genshin Impact.
