How To Unlock All Social Links In Persona 3 Reload

SUMMARY

Literature Review

Social Links are one of the biggest aspects of Persona 3 Reload aside from combat and while maxing them out is hard, so is unlocking them.

There are several Social Links in Persona 3 Reload and, like the confidants in Persona 5 Royal, they each associate with a specific Arcana. Every Social Link has a maximum of 10 ranks and while it isn’t imperative to max them all out to complete the game, it can occasionally result in missed content if you don’t, especially with the 6 romance options.

How To Unlock Every Social Link

Once unlocked, every Social Link will have its own individual availability, so it’s important to stay aware of what’s going on with everyone, so you can maximize your time. If you are ever unsure of how to spend your day, you can also always reference the Network Function to see how other players spent that same day, giving you some insight on who may be available later that evening. Note that not every Social Link can increase ranks through hangouts and will, instead, progress naturally, like Morgana in Persona 5 Royal.

Many Social Links will require certain Social Stats to be at a specific rank or maxed out entirely before they can be fully engaged, so try to prioritize this as early as possible, so you’re not forced to delay Social Links later on.

SEES (Fool)

The Fool Arcana is a unique one because, instead of encompassing one person, it involves the entire S.E.E.S. group. This is one of the few Social Links that will unlock and progress naturally as it is part of the story. Therefore, there is nothing required to unlock this Social Link nor is there anything to worry about in terms of raising its rank.

Unlock Date 4/18 Requirements None Availability Progresses naturally with the story

Kenji Tomochika (Magician)

The Magician Arcana appears in the form of Kenji, a local classmate. This Social Link is the first one that players will unlock, and it does not require any special task to unlock it. The only requirement is that players must wait until the specified day on which he becomes available and then he will unlock naturally. Following that, players can typically find him in the classroom when looking to hang out.

Unlock Date 4/22 Requirements None Availability Tuesdays, Thursdays, & Fridays

Back To Top

Nozomi Suemitsu (Moon)

Suemitsu is one of the few Social Links without a specific unlock date. Instead, his availability is triggered by an occurrence in-game. However, he will require players to jump through quite a few hoops to unlock his Social Link in Persona 3 Reload, so he may not be easy to unlock once he is available. Once unlocked, however, he can be found at the Paulownia Mall.

First Available Unlock Date Any day after Kenji mentions the “Gourmet King” Requirements Charm Rank: Unpolished (2) Kenji mentions the “Gourmet King” Finish the Gourmet Quiz Find an Odd Morsel Availability Everyday

Back To Top

Kazushi Miyamoto (Chariot)

Kazushi is similar to Hidetoshi in that he also unlocks after joining his associated club. He is one of the first Social Links to become available so it’s easy to prioritize him early on and go ahead and get him out of the way so you don’t need to worry about him later. Once unlocked, he can be found in Classroom 1F of the high school whenever players would like to spend time with him.

First Available Unlock Date 4/23 Requirements Join the Track Team Availability Mondays, Tuesdays, Thursdays, & Fridays

Back To Top

Bunkichi and Mitsuko (Hierophant)

Bunkichi and Mitsuko are another unique Social Link in Persona 3 Reload because their Arcana applies to both of them. They’re available fairly early but do have a requirement that must first be met before their Social Link fully becomes available. Once unlocked, they will be found in their shop, which you cannot actually shop at, Bookworms Used Books in the Iwatodai Strip Mall.

First Available Unlock Date 4/25 Requirements Obtain a Persimmon leaf from the tree in the 1F corridor at school. Availability Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays.

Back To Top

Hidetoshi Odagiri (Emperor)

As one of the other early game Social Links, Hidetoshi luckily does not require anything from players to unlock him. Instead, he will unlock naturally when players join the Student Council. While progressing, Makoto will receive notice that the Student Council is accepting new members, so keep an eye out for that. Once unlocked, Hidetoshi can be found in the Student Council Room.

Unlock Date 4/27 Requirements Join Student Council Availability Mondays, Wednesdays, & Fridays (Before January). Every Day (During January)

Back To Top

Yuko Nishiwaki (Strength)

Yuko is associated with the Strength Arcana and is also one of the romance options for Makoto in Persona 3 Reload. While she doesn’t necessarily unlock naturally through story progression, there isn’t anything you specifically need to do as she will prompt you with her requirements as long as you are ranking up the Chariot Social Link regularly. Once unlocked, she can be found outside of classroom 2F of the High School.

First Available Date 4/28 Requirements Walk Yuko home twice (occurs automatically while in rank 2 & 3 with the Chariot Arcana) Availability Wednesdays & Saturdays

Back To Top

Mutatsu (Tower)

Mutatsu is one of the trickiest Social Links to unlock as he has several obscure requirements including a specific Social Stat rank as well as a certain rank with another Social Link in Persona 3 Reload. There is no specified date on which Mutatsu becomes available. Instead, he simply unlocks once his requirements are met. Once unlocked, he can be found in Club Escapade in Paulownia Mall.

First Available Unlock Date Any day after reaching Rank 4 with Yuko (Strength) Requirements Rank 4 with Strength Social Link Courage Rank: Tough (4) Speak with Yuko Availability Thursday Nights, Friday Nights, Saturday Nights, and Sunday Nights.

Back To Top

Maya (Hermit)

Maya is a Social Link that Makoto will unlock without ever fully meeting. While Futaba from Persona 5 Royal and her certainly have a lot in common, Maya is unique in that she serves as an online companion throughout Persona 3 Reload. She unlocks naturally through the story, and from then on, Makoto will be able to game with her from the laptop in his room to increase her Social Link. Unlike in Persona 3, she will no longer take up your whole day if you choose to hang out with her.

First Available Unlock Date 4/29 Requirements Wait until Junpei gives you a copy of Innocent Sin Online, an MMORPG game. Availability Holidays & Sundays

Back To Top

President Tanaka (Devil)

Tanaka is one of the few that doesn’t have a specified unlock date. Rather, he only becomes available after reaching a certain rank with another Social Link, Maya. Following that, he will also have a couple of other tasks he requires, including a specific Social Stat rank as well as a large chunk of Yen. Therefore, be prepared to cough up some cash as he will ask for it all over several days. Once unlocked, he can be found at Paulownia Mall.

First Available Unlock Date Any day after reaching Rank 4 with Maya (Hermit) Requirements Charm Rank: Smooth (4) 40,000 Yen Availability Tuesday Nights & Saturday Nights

Back To Top

Maiko Oohashi (Hanged Man)

Maiko is one of the few child Social Links in Persona 3 Reload. Most of your time with her will be spent comforting her about her disruptive home situation, and she is fairly easy to unlock since she just requires Makoto to purchase a couple of things for a few hundred Yen. Once unlocked, you can find Maiko hanging out around the Playground by the Naganaki Shrine. This is also where you’ll initially hear about her, as some locals will mention the sad girl hanging around the playground.

First Available Unlock Date 5/10 Requirements Purchase 1 Mad Bull from the dorm vending machine. Purchase Weird Takoyaki from the Iwatodai Strip Mall. Availability Mondays, Wednesdays, & Saturdays

Back To Top

Chihiro Fushimi (Justice)

Chihiro is another one of Makoto’s romance options in Persona 3 Reload and thus, she is a rather important Social Link to unlock as early as possible, so you have time to max her out. Her requirements to unlock are fairly simple since she would just like a chat. Once unlocked, she can be found in the Student Council Room of the High School for any hangouts.

First Available Unlock Date 5/28 Requirements Speak with her 3 times Availability Tuesdays, Thursdays, & Saturdays

Back To Top

Bebe (Temperance)

Bebe’s Social Link is one of the more interesting in the game, as he is a character with a lot of personality straight from the start. To unlock Bebe, players must meet a specific Social Stat requirement as well as have reached a specific rank with the Hierophant Social Link. Once he is unlocked, Bebe can be found in Classroom 2F for hangouts.

First Available Unlock Date 5/29 Requirements Academics Rank: Above Average (3) Rank 3 with the Hierophant Social Link Availability Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.

Back To Top

Pharos (Death)

As one of the most ominous Arcanas, Death will not require any tasks to be completed to unlock it. Pharos is a major part of Makoto’s journey throughout Persona 3 Reload and thus his Social Link will rank up naturally with story progression until it is maxed out. There is also no way to hang out with this Social Link since he will naturally appear to you whenever the timing is appropriate.

Unlock Date 6/12 Requirements None Availability Naturally, it ranks up with the story.

Keisuke Hiraga (Fortune)

Keisuke is unlocked towards the end of the first semester of school. As with some of the other Social Links, his unlock is associated with joining a specific club. His Social Link is also required to unlock Mitsuru later on, so it’s important to at least begin his Social Link early on. Once unlocked, Keisuke can be found in the Art Club Room whenever you’re ready to hang out with him. Note that unlocking Mitsuru does not require you to max out Keisuke, just begin it.

First Available Date 6/17 Requirements Join the Art Club Availability Tuesdays, Wednesdays, & Thursdays.

Back To Top

Fuuka Yamagishi (Priestess)

Unlike some of the others, to unlock Fuuka’s Social Link, players must first reach a certain stat level and have already unlocked another corresponding Social Link. As she is one of your party members in Persona 3 Reload as well as one of your romance options, it’s important to work on progressing her as quickly as possible so that you can reach max rank with her, if you desire to. Once unlocked, she can typically be found outside the bathroom on the 2nd floor of Gekkoukan High School.

First Available Unlock Date 6/19 Requirements Courage Rank: Badass (MAX) Have unlocked the Fortune Social Link. Availability Mondays, Fridays, & Saturdays

Back To Top

Yukari Takeba (Lovers)

As the Lovers Arcana, players may notice many resemblances to Ann from Persona 5 Royal in Yukari’s character. Though she will be one of the first people you interact with, her Social Link will not become available until later in the semester. Additionally, she will require a specific Social Stat rank before she can be unlocked even once she’s available. Be sure to focus on this if you have an interest in pursuing her romantically, so you have time to max out her Social Link. Once unlocked, she can be found in the back of classroom 2F.

First Available Unlock Date 7/25 Requirements Charm Rank: Charismatic (MAX) Availability Mondays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, & Saturdays.

Back To Top

Mamoru Hayase (Star)

Mamoru unlocks around the start of the second semester and is fairly easy to unlock. Not only that, but he is also one of the easiest Social Links to max out due to his wide-open availability. Once unlocked, Mamoru can be found at the Iwatodai Strip Mall for hangouts. Try to get him out of the way immediately because some more important Social Links will be unlocked not long after that and will need all the time you have.

Unlock Date 8/2 Requirements Speak with him at the Sports Meet on 8/2 Availability Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Fridays, and Sundays.

Back To Top

Akinari Kamiki (Sun)

Akinari is perhaps one of the saddest Social Links players will encounter in Persona 3 Reload as the majority of time spent with him will be talking about mortality. He will unlock around the start of the second semester of school and requires quite a few things to be completed before unlocking him. Once unlocked, he has very limited availability, so it’s important to see him whenever he’s around. He can be found at the Naganaki Shrine whenever he is available.

First Available Unlock Date 9/6 Requirements Academics Rank: Smart (4) Meet Akinari during Rank 3 of your Hanged Man Social Link A Red Fountain Pen Availability Sundays Only

Back To Top

Mitsuru Kirijo (Empress)

Mitsuru is one of the latest Social Links that can be unlocked since she won’t become available until nearly the end of the second semester. This will make her more difficult to max out, so prioritize her when you can and have as many other Social Links completed as you can before she unlocks. She does require certain Social Stat ranks, but given her late appearance, this should not be a problem. Once unlocked, you can find her hanging around the Faculty Office Entrance if you would like to hang out with her.

Mitsuru is also one of the many romance options in Persona 3 Reload, and unlike the romance options in Persona 5 Royal, there is significantly less time to pursue them all, so manage your time wisely if this is your goal.

First Available Unlock Date 11/21 Requirements Academics Rank: Genius (MAX) Availability Tuesdays, Thursdays, & Saturdays

Back To Top

Nyx Annihilation Team (Judgement)

The Judgement Social Link does not require players to do anything. This Social Link will not only naturally progress throughout the story, but it will also unlock naturally, on a specific date, without Makoto needing to do anything. As with the Fool Social Link, this is not specific to any singular character, but rather a group, so there is nobody to hang out with in order to advance this Social Link.

Unlock Date 12/31 Requirements Automatic after Fool reaches its max rank. Availability N/A

Back To Top

Aigis (Aeon)

Aigis is the most unique to Persona 3 Reload, primarily because she was not originally a Social Link in the original game, one of the major differences from Persona 3. Unfortunately, she unlocks very late in the game, so it’s important to make her a priority once she does. To do so, ensure all other Social Links you intend to max out are either at rank 10 or very close to it by this point. Once unlocked, she will be found in classroom 2F.

Unlock Date 1/8 Requirements None Availability Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays, and Saturdays.
