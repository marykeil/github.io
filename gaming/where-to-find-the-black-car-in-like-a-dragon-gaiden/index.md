## Literature Review

Finding and photographing this NPC&#39;s black car is a quick way to earn some cash in Like a Dragon Gaiden: The Man Who Erased His Name.





The &#34;Calm My Nerves and Find My Car!&#34; side mission, which requires you to photograph the black car, can be unlocked as early as chapter two in Like a Dragon Gaiden. All you have to do is run into the stressed-out business person on Sotenbori St., and he&#39;ll fill you in on the details. That is, photograph and prove the black car is safe, and you&#39;ll be rewarded with 10,000 Yen and 500x Akame Network Points upon completion.

 Black Car Location in Like a Dragon Gaiden 
          

The black car in Like a Dragon Gaiden: The Man Who Erased His Name can be found in a small parking lot on the southwest corner of Shofukucho Street in Sotenbori. The precise location is highlighted in the image above. In-game, you&#39;ll recognize the location by the obvious signs indicating a parking lot. The black car is parked in the ninth spot, leaving the tenth empty, and a white/gray sedan in the eighth.



If it says &#34;Take Photos&#34; when you pull out your camera and look at the vehicle, you&#39;ve found the right car.
