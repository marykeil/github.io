Literature Review

Archives are collectible journal entries, notes, and documents found in Granblue Fantasy: Relink found in various locations across different chapters.

There are 75 total Archives hidden throughout various sections of the game, but each can be found with any crew member. You don’t need to use your crewmate cards in Granblue Fantasy: Relink to unlock a character with a special ability suited for hunting down these collectibles. You will have to carefully explore hub areas or replay mission levels to find the items you are looking for.

Every Archive Location For Chapters 1 - 7

Every Archive shows up as a glowing purple orb on the ground for whatever character leads your party to pick up. Grabbing some of these items may trigger enemies to appear, but only after you have already collected the document. Use save points whenever you can during Chapters to navigate various maps and find every Archive with you or your friends in Granblue Fantasy: Relink.

Some Archives are needed for side quests around hub areas like Folca. Don’t forget to receive rewards from different NPCs for picking up the right Archive Entries during your travels.

Every Archive Location for Chapters 8 - Final Chapter

Once you reach later Chapters of Granblue Fantasy: Relink’s story, you should have a good grasp on where Archives tend to appear. Remember to check locations that will only be around for a limited time since some areas are inaccessible even if you replay the same quest later. Some documents will show up in hub locations you’ve already visited, now unlocked when you complete new sections of the story.

How to Unlock Every Other Archive

Some Archives are not found in physical locations but are rather given as rewards for completing challenges or specific quests tied to different characters in Granblue Fantasy: Relink. According to YouTube creator 100% Guides, a few documents are only unlocked when you have certain crew members in your party.

Obtaining every document, note, or other journal entry will give you more context on how characters interact with the story’s events. Insight into certain characters and the actions of the larger world make the setting feel like a vibrant land with many people. Finding every Archive location in Granblue Fantasy: Relink will also earn you a rare trophy that might help you 100% complete the game.
