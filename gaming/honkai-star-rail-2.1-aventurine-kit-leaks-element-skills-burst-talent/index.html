Honkai Star Rail 2.1 Aventurine Kit Leaks Element, Skills, Burst, Talent

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1700 words 8 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Aventurine is a new playable hero in Honkai: Star Rail 2.1, confirmed by developer announcements. Aventurine is a shielder character and the leaks mention he focuses on protecting teammates and debuffing enemies. His abilities allegedly include offensive moves, applying shields to allies, and a powerful Coin Attack that deals damage and debuffs opponents.

Literature Review

The complete gameplay kit for Aventurine in Honkai: Star Rail 2.1 has leaked, hinting at what may be a very offensive shielder character.

Aventurine’s gameplay kit in Honkai: Star Rail 2.1 has allegedly leaked, revealing all the details about the new and upcoming playable hero. The 5-star unit has already been confirmed as one of the new characters in the next patch thanks to drip marketing pieces shared by the developer HoYoverse. Aventurine has also already appeared in the game with the release of Penacony in Honkai: Star Rail 2.0. The enigmatic member of the IPC (Interastral Peace Corporation) is one of the first new faces that players encounter when trying to check into The Reverie Hotel on the Planet of Festivities.

The motivations behind Aventurine’s actions and overall behavior in the game are currently unknown – this becomes especially true when considering the twists and turns of the narrative presented in Version 2.0. As such, it is currently difficult to pinpoint Aventurine as an ally or an opponent, and, for now, he is simply an acquaintance of the Trailblazer. Now, accompanying some game-breaking leaks in Honkai: Star Rail 2.1, a large portion of his alleged gameplay kit has also been shared by leakers, which reveals more details about how Aventurine will behave as a playable character on the battlefield.

Honkai: Star Rail’s Aventurine - Element & Path

As has been officially confirmed by a post on the official Honkai: Star Rail account on X, Aventurine will use Imaginary DMG in battle, joining the ranks of characters like Welt, Dan Heng - Imbibitor Lunae, and, most recently, Dr. Ratio. Contrary to their DPS-focused builds, however, Aventurine follows the Path of the Preservation, which establishes him as a shielder of sorts. He will be responsible for protecting the rest of his teammates. In a sense, he will work in a similar way as the Preservation Trailblazer (Fire) in Honkai: Star Rail or even Gepard and Fu Xuan.

While most Preservation characters create shields for teammates, Fu Xuan lowers the damage income for party members by absorbing a portion of it while also boosting their Max HP.

Honkai: Star Rail’s Aventurine - Basic Attack

Some information obtained by the leaker known as HomDGCat was shared by a user on Reddit, revealing what is rumored to be Aventurine’s abilities in the game. As is the case with any leak, this information should be taken with a grain of salt as it may be inaccurate or subject to change. According to the post, Aventurine’s Basic Attack will be an offensive move that deals Imaginary DMG against the target, and its damage scales off the character’s DEF stat. As a shielder, this is expected to be the least important part of Aventurine’s kit in Honkai: Star Rail.

The leak is tagged as “ Reliable ” on Reddit.

Honkai: Star Rail’s Aventurine - Skill

Aventurine’s Skill, however, will likely be a key part of his rotation. According to the leaks, this ability will apply special Aventurine Shields on all allies, with its strength scaling off his DEF stat. When Aventurine creates these Shields, allies will draw one of four cards (labeled as A, B, C, and D) if they do not already have an A card. These cards are said to disappear after the character’s Aventurine Shield expires. Only the A card has a bonus effect, and it is rumored to influence the way Aventurine’s Ultimate works in Honkai: Star Rail via his Talent.

Honkai: Star Rail’s Aventurine - Ultimate

The Ultimate used by Aventurine, as described in the leaks posted on Reddit, sees the 5-star Imaginary shielder obtain a random number of Coins and deal Imaginary DMG to opponents based on his DEF stat. This ability allegedly has a chance to increase the target’s incoming CRIT DMG for some turns, which makes Aventurine something of a debuffer as well. The CRIT DMG punishment he applies through his Ultimate can be improved through his Talent. This seems to be the key point of his leaked gameplay kit in Honkai: Star Rail 2.1.

Honkai: Star Rail’s Aventurine - Talent & Technique

According to the leaks, Aventurine’s Talent, which operates in a passive manner, influences a lot of his abilities. The first notable aspect is that Aventurine will gain a Coin whenever an ally protected by an Aventurine Shield executes an attack. When these protected allies suffer an attack, Aventurine also gains a Coin. This is where the card mechanic comes into play. If the attacked character is holding an A card, Aventurine gains bonus Coins. Aventurine’s leaked Talent sees that when he obtains a total of six Coins in Honkai: Star Rail, he consumes them to unleash Coin Attack.

This move allegedly deals multiple instances of Imaginary DMG to random targets, and they have a chance of lowering the affected targets’ Effect Hit Rate. Additionally, all the allies that have Aventurine Shield will discard their current card and pull a new one. Based on the rumored description of his kit, Aventurine’s rotation will be shielding allies, trying to grant them A cards, and then using up Coins to deliver the special attack, which will contribute to the team’s damage output while also debuffing opponents for the rest of the team comp in Honkai: Star Rail to take advantage of.

Aventurine’s leaked Technique allows him to roll a die with three possible results and gain a DEF buff for the next battle based on the roll – leaks mention that there is a pity with a guaranteed 3 after some failed attempts. This ability also applies Aventurine Shield and lets them pull a card immediately after entering battle.

Honkai: Star Rail’s Aventurine - Passive Traces

The three passive Traces normally unlocked by Aventurine as he levels up and Ascends are also mentioned in the leaks. His first Trace will grant him a CRIT Rate bonus based on a percentage of his DEF stat. The second passive Trace will see that all allies protected by an Aventurine Shield will have their Effect RES stat increased. The third and final passive triggers after a Coin Attack. Performing the special move will allow him to stack Aventurine Shield for all allies, with an extra number of stacks dedicated to the least shielded ally in Honkai: Star Rail.

The second effect to trigger from his Coin Attack-based passive Trace will allegedly increase the duration of the Aventurine Shield on all allies and, if Aventurine himself has an Aventurine Shield, the duration is increased even more. The final effect rumored to be granted from his passive Trace will allow allies to pull a card if they do not have one. These leaked Traces seem to improve Aventurine’s presence as a shielder on the field, extending the duration of his protection and granting them even more shields when he takes the offensive in Honkai: Star Rail.

Honkai: Star Rail’s Aventurine - Eidolons

When players get an extra copy of a character they own, they unlock Eidolons. Split into six tiers, one is granted for each extra copy. Aventurine’s Eidolons are rumored to have leaked. Eidolon level 1, commonly known as E1, will provide characters protected by an Aventurine Shield and who are holding a B card with a CRIT DMG boost. Those who have an A card will get an even larger boost to the stat. Aventurine’s leaked E2 will allegedly increase the number of attack instances in the Coin Attack in Honkai: Star Rail.

Additionally, after executing the move, E2 will allegedly see that Aventurine’s DEF stat is increased for some turns. E4 is rumored to trigger Aventurine’s follow-up attack after he performs a Basic Attack, dealing Imaginary DMG based on the sum of all allies’ shield HP. E6 allegedly increases the damage Aventurine deals based on the number of allies protected by a shield. Of course, increasing Aventurine’s Eidolons in Honkai: Star Rail can be an expensive feat, and these extra abilities are considered mostly optional.

E3 and E5 will boost his Skill to 15 and Ultimate’s level by 3, respectively, while also increasing their level cap.

If the leaks are true, it is safe to assume that Aventurine’s main goal will be to shield his allies, but also contribute to their performance. This will allegedly come in the form of debuffs against enemies and through buffs to allies, as well as his own damage output that mostly stems from his DEF stat. This rumored gameplay kit should establish him as a very offensive shielder, much different from the majority of those that are currently available in Honkai: Star Rail.
