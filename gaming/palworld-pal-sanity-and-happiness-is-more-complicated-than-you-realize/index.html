Palworld Pal Sanity And Happiness Is More Complicated Than You Realize

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1300 words 6 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Pal sanity is crucial for efficient base work and can be raised in a multitude of ways. Factors like passive skills, injuries, and sickness can impact Pal sanity, affecting their productivity. The future of Pal sanity may involve changes to increase the challenge and incentivize strategic approaches to raising sanity.

Literature Review

Palworld’s sanity and happiness for Pals is more complicated than one may think, which is why it is crucial to understand it and how to raise it.

Palworld brings a complex mechanic to players with a sanity and happiness meter that Pals have, making it important to understand how it works. Knowing how to raise Pal’s sanity (SAN) can avoid complications later in the playthrough. When keeping Pals happy and working efficiently, Palworld can become a much easier game for players. It may not be the hardest survival game out there, and though its settings can be tweaked to increase the difficulty, it still poses a compelling challenge.

Pal sanity is crucial for Pals working on a player’s base. These Pals can be assigned to farm, gather resources, cook, and craft for players while they are away. These sometimes hidden features in Palworld lessen the time players have to spend at the base and accomplish things while they are away from the base. Overall, it is great at reducing the constant survival genre grind, so players can spend more time enjoying the unique features of Palworld.

What Is Pal Sanity?

Sanity is a stat that determines how happy a Pal is, and the happier they are, the more efficiently they will work at the base. Conversely, if a Pals’ sanity gets too low, it will start to disobey orders. This situation can get very problematic if multiple Pals have low sanity, as it will effectively shut down a player’s base work. Luckily, all Pals, even the best Pals to have for bases, have the same starting sanity, though there are multiple factors in deciding its changes.

Pal sanity will drop over time as they are out of the box and working. However, this drop is not rapid, though with a monitor stand set to higher work speeds, it becomes rapid. If the monitor stand is not used, it will be difficult to cause a Pals’ sanity to drop dangerously low unless players neglect them. Generally, basic methods of raising a Pals’ sanity will suffice a player’s needs through a whole campaign.

The reason sanity is so complex is to balance out the usefulness Pals bring to the game. The survival genre has always been keen on making the player work to get by, but Palworld usurps that. While some aspects still require the player, since most survival aspects can be handled by Pals, it lessens difficulty greatly. Without this balance mechanic, Pals could work endlessly and thus make the game too easy.

How To Raise Pal Sanity

The two most basic ways of keeping Pals happy while staying at working speed is keeping them fed while having places to sleep. These two things alone will suffice, but other ways will help keep them in top form for long periods. The first method is building hot springs that Pals can relax in. These pools will raise sanity at a moderate rate, and Pals will go to them independently when they need a rest. Another method is swapping a Pal back into the box. This allows for sanity to go back up while not losing a worker.

Two other ways to raise sanity are food and giving attention to Pals. The latter is very easy, as players need to use the Pal interaction menu and select the pet option. What food Pals eat can have a very large or very minimal effect depending on the food. The most popular method is using berries, as they are easy to come by and work fine. However, the better quality food fed to Pals, the more sanity they will recover. This method can work great in a pinch or if players have a surplus of good quality food.

A great simple way to keep a steady food supply for Pals with minimal player effort is a berry farm. As long as some of the base Pals can farm, water, gather, and transport, this will create an endless supply of berries. This is made even easier by the AI of the game, which will direct Pals to transport the berries straight to the feeding bowl. The only drawback to this plan is that berries will not dramatically increase sanity; they will only prevent it from worsening.

Factors That Impact Pal Sanity

Outside of working and being neglected, some other factors do impact sanity. Passive skills can play a huge part in deciding what role a Pal fits, and the same can be said for their sanity. Currently, three passives in the game can increase the rate of sanity depletion, while two can slow the rate. Pals with these traits can greatly help or hinder a base’s production, making it important to notice them.

Like with other roles, worker Pals can be bred to have both skills that slow sanity depletion.

Another factor in Pal sanity is injuries and sickness. Injuries will occur randomly or when a Pals’ sanity is too low. When this happens, players need to use the Medieval Medicine Workbench to craft the right medicine for the job. This station is easily acquired early, only needing Wood, Paldium Gragments, and Nails. These medicines won’t increase sanity greatly, but they will cure the Pal and allow them to regain sanity at their normal rate.

The Future Of Sanity In Palworld

Given how easy it is to raise sanity and the effect Pal workers have on the game, it wouldn’t be unreasonable to expect future changes to the system. The most likely change would be a faster drop rate on sanity, so a more dedicated effort is needed to keep Pals happy. The other option would be increasing the benefit of the monitor stand, as currently, it is not worth the drawback. However, increasing the benefit will incentivize players to use greater ways of raising sanity at their first and second base in Palworld.

For an early-access game, Palworld has done a great job blending a balance system with its creative and worker mechanics. However, it is still a basic system that comes with flaws to improve that will boost the feeling of it. If improvements can be made, then Palworld will have an attractively complex sanity system that players want to engage in.
