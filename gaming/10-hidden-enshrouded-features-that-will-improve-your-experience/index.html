10 Hidden Enshrouded Features That Will Improve Your Experience

SUMMARY

There are a variety of in-game hidden mechanics that enable people to save resources easily and efficiently in Enshrouded . A huge variety of new recipes can be unlocked in Enshrouded, some of which possess

game-changing benefits. Prioritizing quests based on color to maximize rewards and progression is a great strategy to use to level up fast.

Literature Review

With so much to do, it can be easy to overlook some of Enshrouded’s best-kept secrets. Understanding these key features can make all the difference.

Survival games are notorious for their hands-off approach, and yet mastering intricate mechanics quickly in titles like Enshrouded is crucial for optimizing resources, progressing efficiently, and sidestepping unnecessary frustration. Gamers who live and breathe the genre know survival tends to be hardest when resources are especially slim at the very beginning. Luckily, mastering these mechanics not only provides a solid foundation for navigating the early challenges but also sets the stage for a more rewarding and immersive gaming experience. From seemingly innocuous environmental details to subtle NPC interactions, the game abounds with valuable insights for its seasoned players. Although some of Enshrouded’s more useful features require a bit of digging to understand, a surprising number of its best-kept secrets hide in plain sight. Taking the time to learn the systems at play becomes its own reward, however: it’s how the novice transforms from a mere survivalist to a thriving adventurer.

10 NPCs Require Fewer Resources To Craft In Enshrouded Players Should Craft Through NPCs To Save Resources

Players in Enshrouded benefit from crafting through NPCs due to the reduced resource requirements per craft. Considering how frequently recipes like nails will be used over the course of a single playthrough, taking advantage of this feature could easily mean doubling or tripling a player’s resources. Since crafting through NPCs is free and doesn’t take extra time, this is a great method for getting resources (like metal scraps) to stretch — especially in the early game. While there are many valid reasons for crafting while exploring, this is also an equally valid reason for crafting at base as often as possible.

9 Craft Everything In Enshrouded At Least Once To Unlock New Recipes Discovering New Items Can Unlock New Recipes

Acquiring new items in Enshrouded has the potential to unlock new recipes, and that includes items created through crafting. For instance, the recipe for goo will unlock automatically when players craft bug dust. This is because bug dust has an association with goo since it’s one of the mats required to make it. Unfortunately, these connections aren’t always intuitive, but players can take advantage of the game’s discovery feature by crafting every recipe at least once. While it might seem like a waste of materials in the moment, the benefit of unlocking new recipes quickly is game-changing.

8 Quests Are Color-Coded In Enshrouded Understanding How Quests Are Organized Can Help Players Prioritize

Enshrouded categorizes quests by color, and learning to decipher them can help players prioritize what’s most important to them. While every quest type offers great rewards, new players may want to prioritize NPC quests for early upgrades to inventory, crafting, and gear. Here’s a breakdown of what each color means: Quest Color Quest Type Gold (Flame) The main scenario quests that progress the game’s central plot. Blue Given by NPCs that progress their storylines. Gray Miscellaneous quests which often focus on the game’s lore and encourage players to investigate the game’s rich history.

Gray quests are obtained by reading in-game materials such as books and scrolls scattered throughout the world. It’s important to pay attention to what these materials say, since they provide clues regarding how to solve the quest they’re associated with.

7 Toggle “Item Deletion Protection” In Enshrouded Better To Be Safe Than Sorry

Enshrouded’s “Item Deletion Protection” feature is pretty self-explanatory, but it’s an easy option to forget until after something unfortunate happens. Fortunately, the feature is remarkably robust within the game, allowing players to safeguard items ranging from “common” to “legendary,” depending on their current preferences. It’s all too easy for items to magically disappear, and adjusting this setting early is a great way to ensure that a stray cat or a random sneeze doesn’t lead to losing hours of work. The “Item Deletion Protection” option can be found by navigating to the “Game” submenu in Settings.

6 The Enshrouded “Effective” Tooltip Explained Damage Type Is Important To The Game’s Combat System

While not exactly hidden, the “Effective” tooltip may confuse new players, and it’s critical to understand what it means in the larger scheme of Enshrouded’s combat system. In the early game, it’s easy to prioritize getting the best skills and overlook how weapon damage type impacts combat effectiveness. Enshrouded features many weapon types, including slashing, piercing, and blunt damage.

As players progress, however, they’ll encounter stronger enemies that are resistant to one type of damage or another. The “Effective” tooltip guides players in making tactical decisions regarding their choice of weapons. If players do not see the text after landing an attack on an enemy, it means they may want to swap weapons to be more effective.

5 Terrain Effects Stamina Depletion Stamina Depletes Faster Off-Road

Roads provide comfortable travel and reduce the stamina needed for sprinting.

Stamina management is critical in many survival games, but in Enshrouded, the terrain’s difficulty secretly impacts the rate at which stamina depletes. The game dynamically adjusts stamina depletion based on the roughness of the terrain: the rougher it is, the faster stamina diminishes. To optimize travel efficiency, players may want to stick to roads over long distances. Dirt roads offer better stamina balance, while stone roads provide the most efficient routes. While explorers are sure to find plenty of reasons to leave the game’s roads behind, understanding how the terrain affects stamina is crucial for planning ahead.

4 How “Cancel Action” Improves Gliding Control Gliding Is Fun, But Landing Can Be Dangerous

Gliding is an addictive mode of transportation in Enshrouded, but using “Cancel Action” gives players more control over where they land. More specifically, when players use “Cancel Action” near their target landing site, they’ll cancel gliding so they don’t accidentally veer off into dangerous territory. This is especially useful when aiming for cliffsides, lava banks, or near a camp of enemies. With more control over exploration capabilities, players can confidently traverse landscapes without hitting unwanted snags, effectively making the “Cancel Action” function similar to riding a bike with a helmet.

3 Improving The Rested Bonus in Enshrouded: Comfort Level, Explained A Player’s Comfort Level And Rating Positively Impact The Game’s Rested Bonus

The rested bonus conveys valuable buffs to players in Enshrouded, and those buffs get even better if their comfort level is high. Rested bonuses improve a character’s stamina and stamina regen – which is very useful at all stages of the game. Thankfully, maximizing a character’s comfort level can be achieved by simply idling in a safe and comfortable location without nearby threats. Players will see checkboxes labeled “Sheltered,” “Warmth,” and “Comfortable” at the top left of their screen. Once checked, these boxes ensure that their character is prepared to receive an enhanced rested bonus.

2 Why Quickswapping To Ranged With Q Is Even Better Than You Think In Enshrouded Holding Q Enables Players To Swap Between Ammo As Well

Learning to quick-swap to a ranged weapon using the Q key is a game-changer in Enshrouded, but the feature is secretly even better because holding Q enables players to swap between ammo as well. For those not in the know, players need to equip a ranged weapon before this option becomes available. Once equipped, however, using the Q key makes swapping between melee and ranged mindless. The added benefit of being able to swap between ammunitions by holding Q is just icing on the cake.

1 Stop Searching For Stored Crafting Materials: Use Magic Chests Instead Crafting Stations Can Access A Magic Chest’s Inventory From Anywhere On Base

Crafting menus in Enshrouded can access a magic chest’s inventory from anywhere on their base, making it the go-to place for storing crafting materials. While the extra storage space is a welcome benefit in itself, the real reason to invest in magic chests is the pivotal link they share with crafting stations in the area. Gamers can unlock the magic chest recipes by progressing the Carpenter NPC’s questline, and the freedom to stop tracking every piece of wood, stone, and string is a compelling argument for making it a priority. In addition to upgrading storage crates through the Carpenter’s questline, players can craft better bags by progressing through the Hunter’s questline.
