Pokémon Day 2024 - When It Is, What s Confirmed & What To Expect

SUMMARY

Pokémon Day 2024 is expected to bring new announcements and surprises, building anticipation for the highly anticipated event. Past Pokémon Day announcements have included new game titles, promotional events, and merchandise. Speculation for Pokémon Day 2024 includes potential new game reveals, including Generation 5 remakes, as well as new titles in sub-series and existing games.

Literature Review

Pokémon Day 2024 is quickly approaching, and the highly-anticipated annual event will undoubtedly have plenty in store for all Pokémon fans.

Pokémon Day, the Pokémon franchise’s annual celebration, will soon be capturing the minds of people around the world once more. As Pokémon’s most exciting annual event, Pokémon Day 2024 is expected to bring a slew of new announcements and commemorative surprises, and, as such, is the focus of much speculation. In addition, some aspects of 2024’s lineup have already been confirmed, further increasing the excitement for this highly anticipated event.

Past Pokémon Day announcements have included everything from the reveal of new titles to special promotional events and merchandise. For example, as part of the franchise’s 25th anniversary in 2021, a virtual concert featuring Post Malone was announced. Prior to this, as part of the inaugural 2016 Pokémon Day, the Pokémon Sun and Moon were announced to the world, beginning a trend for similar announcements of Pokémon Sword and Shield and Pokémon Scarlet and Violet in subsequent years. Likewise, last year’s Pokémon Day 2023 also revealed Pokémon Scarlet and Violet’s new DLC, The Hidden Treasure of Area Zero.

When Pokémon Day 2024 Will Be Held And What Has Been Confirmed

Pokémon Day celebrates the day of the original 1996 release of the Pokémon franchise’s Pokémon Red and Green versions in Japan. As such, Pokémon Day is celebrated on February 27 every year, and Pokémon Day 2024 is no different. It should be noted that no details have been given of a Pokémon Presents or Pokémon Direct digital broadcast on the day as yet. However, considering the usual content of past Pokémon Days, one is almost certain to be part of the day’s proceedings and will most likely be announced closer to the time.

UPDATE: 2024/02/20 12:06 EST BY LARA JACKSON

Pokémon Presents confirmed for Pokemon Day

Pokémon has now confirmed a Presents event will take place on its official YouTube channel at " 6:00 a.m. PST on February 27th to view exciting Pokémon news in celebration of #PokemonDay 2024! "

After Pokémon Red and Green , the equivalent international versions, Pokémon Red and Blue, were first released in North America on September 28, 1998.

Although details concerning the full Pokémon Day 2024 lineup are still sparse, one promotional product has already been unveiled. A new Pokémon TCG card featuring Ceruledge will see a limited-time release through several retailers, including official Pokémon Center stores. Notably, this promo card will become available on November 23, the day before Pokémon Day itself, and as with any limited edition product, the cards will likely sell out. Likewise, although not yet announced, there’s a good chance that other forms of celebratory merchandise will also be available closer to the actual date.

It has also been announced that a series of exclusive Timed Research Tasks for Pokémon GO will be available to those who visit the Empire State Building on February 27, with a party hat-wearing Pikachu encounter among the rewards. Simultaneously, Unown is set to appear more commonly at PokéStops than usual when Lures are used. The Empire State Building itself will have special blue and yellow Pokémon-themed lighting in the evening, while its famous observatory will play host to the Pokémon Horizons anime’s Captain Pikachu on social media platforms.

What Can Be Expected As Part Of Pokémon Day 2024

Beyond this, there has been widespread speculation over potential new game reveals on Pokémon Day. Among these possible titles being discussed, the most prominent are undoubtedly the long-anticipated Generation 5 remakes. These games would be a major release for the franchise, making them the perfect centerpiece to this year’s Pokémon Day announcements. However, these remakes are not the only titles that could be announced; other, entirely new games could also be in store. For example, although somewhat more unlikely than the Gen 5 remakes, there may also be news relating to the core games of Gen 10.

    Pokémon Gen 10 Games Are Probably Releasing On Switch 2  Circumstances surrounding the future of the Pokémon franchise suggest that the Generation 10 games will be released for the Switch 2 console.

Of course, there are a range of sub-series in the Pokémon franchise, which many would be glad to see gain a new title as well. Pokémon Mystery Dungeon, Pokémon Ranger, and even a new Pokémon Legends game are all possibilities in this vein. Smaller, entirely new titles and applications in the vein of Pokémon Café ReMix or Pokémon Sleep may also be revealed on February 27. There is a multitude of such possibilities, which is part of what makes the approach of every Pokémon Day so exciting.

Likewise, there will almost certainly be new content for other existing titles to mark Pokémon Day. Pokémon Café ReMix has already established a trend of having special events for Pokémon Day, for instance. The debut of a new Pokémon and other content for Pokémon UNITE is another distinct possibility. Moreover, it should be noted that as Ceruledge is already the subject of this year’s Pokémon TCG promo card, and it may also play a promotional role in these games as well.

The Possibilities Of Pokémon Day 2024 Are Endless

In terms of wider speculation for Pokémon Day 2024, there are countless possibilities. The Pokémon Company could have any number of carefully guarded secrets ready to reveal on the day, and anticipating all of them would be impossible. Nevertheless, common points shared between past Pokémon Days, such as the tendency to announce major new releases, can provide some hints for people’s expectations. For instance, Pokémon Day 2020 brought Pokémon Sword and Shield a new Gigantamax Pokémon, which could provide precedent for a new Paradox Pokémon to be distributed now, although there is currently no real evidence of this being planned.

Pokémon Day 2024 will give people an important glimpse of what Pokémon has in store for the next year. Some surprise announcements will undoubtedly be a part of that, although the heightened anticipation for news about titles like the Generation 5 remakes means that not all of these surprises will necessarily be shocking developments. In any case, with all corners of the franchise coming together to celebrate the day it all began, February 27, 2024, is sure to be full of excitement for everybody who loves Pokémon.
