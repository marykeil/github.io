Literature Review

90 Day Fiancé alum Loren Brovarnik is slowly losing her fan-favorite status as she continues to create a commotion online with controversial posts.

90 Day Fiancé alum Loren Brovarnik is slowly turning into one of the most controversial franchise cast members thanks to her social media behavior. Loren, 35, originally from 90 Day Fiancé season 3, has built her follower network across multiple social media platforms over the years. She has amassed millions of Instagram fans who consume her content now that Loren is a full-time mommy influencer. Loren let go of her corporate job after having her second baby and now makes a living through social media. Several brands approach Loren so she can advertise their products on her page.

Loren has modeled plenty of outfits, selling everything from jeans to tops, has hosted giveaways, and even spread awareness about services on Instagram. Loren’s recommendations are trusted because of her down-to-earth personality and relatable parenting content. However, Loren’s new content is making her come across as a sell-out. Loren has become another influencer peddling untrustworthy brands and products that are shady to her followers.

Loren Brovarnik Got Trashed For Promoting A Controversial Cosplay Company

Loren has repeatedly promoted a cosplay brand that’s allegedly known for using sweatshops to produce low-cost goods. Loren was under fire for a post in August 2023 when she admitted she loved the website and called it her “[secret] obsession.” 90 Day Fiancé fans slammed Loren for supporting the Shanghai-based online marketplace that “utilized modern day slavery.” They asked her to stop “misleading” people and to not lose her “credibility.” Loren wore Valentine’s Day-inspired outfits in a January 2024 post where the clothes came from the same brand. A fan commented, “So confused about why you’d do this when you seem to be politically savvy.”

Loren Brovarnik’s “Stay At Home” Video Got Shade

In January 2024, Loren also posted a Reel on the “#OfCourse” trend by sharing the challenges of being a stay-at-home mom. Loren said things such as, “I’m a stay-at-home mom. Of course, I haven’t brushed my hair yet today.” While Loren wanted to keep things fun, some of her 1.4 million followers were not impressed by Loren calling herself a stay-at-home mom. Amid the thousands of likes and views she got on the video, fans slammed Loren for putting her kids in daycare while also calling herself a stay-at-home mom. “I’m a stay-at-home Mom….my kids are actually home during the day,” a follower told Loren.

Loren Brovarnik’s Post With Obscene Gesture Was Criticized

Loren had posted a mirror selfie captioned, “Blazer szn,” as she posed with a peace sign. Loren may have expected fans to admire her style, but instead, they commented on how unhappy they were with her content. “I really used to like her. Suddenly a mommy makeover and modeling her clothes,” someone wrote. Loren’s content was “truly annoying” to some followers. Loren lashed out at the critics by posting an Instagram Story captioned, “Ooooff the haters are out today. Have a blessed day.” Her selfie showed her resting her chin on one hand with Loren then placing her middle finger on her cheek to show an obscene gesture.
