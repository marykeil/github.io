Literature Review

The best Necromancer class build in Last Epoch chooses strong abilities and skills that use dark magic to summon allies and deal ridiculous damage.

Necromancers are one of the Master classes for the Acolyte in Last Epoch, but the way this archetype uses magic is vastly different from other spellcasters. Most builds revolve around creating minions and finding unique strategies to manipulate the health of an otherwise frail character. While Lich and Warlock Acolytes have their strengths, Necromancers typically focus more on buffing their summoned army.

How To Start Making The Best Necromancer Build

The best Necromancer build can be made when you dedicate all your skills to summoning minions and strengthening them in Last Epoch. This allows you to easily approach combat with tons of allies and above-average health management. The right skills will give you tons of variety in your undead army and let you act as a tank within your group under the right circumstances.

The only major weakness of this Necromancer build comes from how it deals with bosses. Low-level minions can be wiped out fast by tougher opponents if you do not invest skills in making your undead creatures stronger.

Your Skill Tree progression will focus on Summon Skeleton and Summon Volatile Skeleton from the Acolyte class. Eventually, you also need to get Summon Bone Golem to add a creature to your roster with more survivability. When you can take on the role of a Necromancer, you should put points into Summon Skeletal Mage and Summon Wraith to complete your undead horde in Last Epoch.

Many aspects of this build were discovered by Lilydayo on Lastepochtools and include how powerful Area of Effect (AoE) attacks could be deadly against your Necromancer. You’ll want to increase your Vitality, Health, and Minion Health stats for your character to overcome this vulnerability. Just like the best Action RPG games, Last Epoch gives you plenty of tools to balance your build’s weaknesses.

Every Skill For The Best Necromancer Build

Minions can be modified when you invest skill points in different aspects of the ability used to summon them. For example, spending points in the Flame Wraith portion of the Summon Wraith tree allows that type of minion to cast fireballs on your enemies for extra damage. Boosts to damage and reduced Mana costs in Last Epoch are just some of the advantages you could receive by picking the right upgrades.

Best Passive Skills For A Necromancer Build

Assuming your character has reached the max level in Last Epoch, you need to pick Passive Skills from both the Acolyte and Necromancer Trees. Increased Minion Damage, Minion Armor, and any bonuses to Minion Health Regeneration are all the best ways to enforce your build’s strategy. Keep in mind that you need to survive fights too, so invest some points in your own Vitality or Health Regen.

Similar to an endgame build for Necromancers in Diablo 4, another Action RPG title, you should focus your skills on a specific damage type. The active skills from this build convert many minion attacks into fire damage, which can be boosted even further by some Passive Skills. However, as seen by YouTube creator Boardman21 - Last Epoch Gaming, you can easily pivot into another element like Cold for your undead.

A great example of a Fire-related Passive Skill is Effigies from the Necromancer Tree. This feature lets you consume a fallen minion for a health boost along with an increase to all your minion’s fire and bow damage.

Strongest Gear For A Necromancer Build

Gear in Last Epoch is divided into Equipment, Idols, and Blessings for your character to wear at any given time. Equipment includes your standard Armor and Weapons, while Idols and Blessings are smaller, unseen additions that only provide passive traits. Any item that bolsters your Intelligence stat will help you in the long run by giving you more of your Mana resource to summon allies in battle.

Continue to focus on gear tied to Minion buffs, especially ones like the Reach of the Grave Wand weapon. This tool increases your minion’s attack and bow damage, along with increased attack speed for any undead using ranged attacks. Your army of the dead also leeches 10% of the damage dealt by their spell and bow attacks as recovered health in Last Epoch with this weapon, allowing them to last longer in fights.

Try to have two Grand Bone Idols in your possession for this build, since it gives your minions increased fire damage. You should pair these gear with two Ornate Bone Idols to improve your Volatile Zombies’ explosion radius and damage as well in Last Epoch. A buff to Necrotic Resistance from this Idol will also give your character better defenses against enemies using the same magic as your class.

Even with every skill and gear piece at your disposal, you may still struggle to combat endgame challenges. By the time you reach the maximum level, you should have a good idea about which strategies will work against your enemies. This Necromancer build in Last Epoch utilizes an approach to combat that can be effective at nearly all levels of play when you have a good plan to use its best features.
