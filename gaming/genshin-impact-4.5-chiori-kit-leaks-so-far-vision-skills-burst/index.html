Genshin Impact 4.5 Chiori Kit Leaks (So Far) - Vision, Skills, & Burst

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1300 words 7 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Chiori is expected to be a playable 5-star character in Genshin Impact 4.5 and may have tailoring abilities reflected in her gameplay kit. Chiori’s Elemental Skill summons a yellow scissor shape that gathers enemies and deals damage, while her Burst creates scissor symbols that grant buffs to Normal Attacks. Chiori’s leaked Passive Talents suggest she will be a Geo support unit, empowering Geo DPS heroes and potentially operating as a sub-DPS with coordinated attacks.

Literature Review

The leaks about Chiori’s gameplay kit in Genshin Impact 4.5 indicate she may be both a support and sub-DPS unit in team comps centered around Geo.

Chiori’s alleged gameplay kit has been mentioned in new Genshin Impact 4.5 leaks. While it is still early to determine the veracity of the claims, the reports come from multiple sources and seem to converge on several points, which may indicate exactly how the Geo unit will operate in team comps. Chiori is expected to be a playable 5-star character in Genshin Impact 4.5 and the only new one during the upcoming update. She was officially introduced as an NPC during the Roses and Muskets event in Version 4.3, which confirmed her previously leaked design and her Geo Vision.

The owner of a serious demeanor, Chiori is originally from Inazuma and is Ayaka’s childhood acquaintance. After fleeing the Nation of Eternity, she now spends most of her time tending to her store, Chioriya Boutique. She is a fashion designer and a tailor and is known to have created Kirara’s outfit for free, as a way of spreading the good word about her commercial endeavors. Chiori has also designed the standard outfits for Navia, Lyney, and Lynette. According to the leaks, it seems like these tailoring abilities will also be somewhat reflected in Chiori’s gameplay kit in Genshin Impact 4.5.

              Genshin Impact Leaks: Weapons Update May Significantly Change The Game   A new leak suggests that an update will offer players better options when trying to acquire 5-star weapons for their characters in Genshin Impact.

Chiori’s Weapon Type & Vision In Genshin Impact 4.5 Leaks

Chiori has been confirmed to own a Geo Vision. The item can be seen dangling from the right side of her kimono’s obi belt. Chiori’s Geo Vision was obtained in Inazuma, so it does not have Fontaine’s casing. As such, it is unlikely that Chiori will have an Arkhe system alignment. When it comes to her weapon, the information right now is mostly based on speculation. The most common leak, reported by Sportskeeda, is that Chiori will be a sword user in Genshin Impact. This information has yet to be backed up by other leaks before Version 4.5’s release.

Chiori’s Vision is often hidden since she crosses her arms and covers it with her clothing.

Chiori’s Elemental Skill & Burst In Genshin Impact 4.5 Leaks

The heavy bulk of leaks mention Chiori’s alleged Elemental Skill and Burst. As seen in a leak by Uncle PP, shared by a user on Reddit, Chiori’s Elemental Skill summons a yellow scissor shape, which gathers enemies in a straight line and causes damage. The leak mentions that it can also be held, making the scissors move forward and, upon release, it closes and deals damage. This is somewhat backed up by some information by the leaker known as White, which was shared in another Reddit post. White’s leak also mentions that Chiori’s Skill in Genshin Impact gathers opponents.

              New Genshin Impact Leak May Be Bad News For One Long-Awaited Character   Amidst the exciting new Genshin Impact leaks, one bit of information may actually be upsetting for players expecting a long-awaited character.

Uncle PP’s leak also details Chiori’s alleged Elemental Burst. According to them, Chiori has a short animation in which she wields a scissor. After that, three scissors spawn around her, rotating around her before opening. The nearby enemies will then explode. After this, three scissor symbols appear behind the character. White’s leak mentions that Chiori’s Burst deals damage to nearby enemies, thus sustaining the previous leak, while also mentioning that it provides a buff to the active character in Genshin Impact. The secret to understanding what buffs Chiori may provide may be hidden in yet another leak by Uncle PP.

According to the leaker, in a Reddit post, the scissor symbol created behind the active character will support Normal Attacks. This buff may be what her Burst provides. One of Chiori’s alleged Passive Talents will boost the entire team’s Geo DMG whenever the scissor is active. Another leaked Passive Talent highlights a coordinated attack in Genshin Impact. When there is one active scissor, it will open, and when there are two or three active scissors, the coordination attack will see the scissor close. These coordination attack scissors are said to last six seconds, up to a total of three.

All the leaks should be taken with a grain of salt at this point. The information can be wrong or simply subject to change before the official reveal.

What Chiori’s Early Leaks Say About Her Role In Genshin Impact

Analyzing Chiori’s alleged gameplay kit leaks, it does seem like Sportskeeda’s report on Version 4.5 leaks about her being a support and sub-DPS unit seems accurate. With her Elemental Skill’s ability to gather opponents and deal damage – probably Geo DMG – this could be a way of gathering opponents together, in a similar way that Kazuha regularly does. It does, however, seem like Chiori’s Elemental Burst is the star of her kit. The leaks imply that Chiori will start dealing damage, but then activate a series of effects that can be beneficial to the team comp in Genshin Impact.

              Genshin Impact: Navia Character Builds (Weapons, Artifacts, Team Comps)   You can increase Navia’s value as a DPS unit in Genshin Impact with the best build and ideal team comp to accompany her on-field Geo presence.

The Burst allegedly creates small scissor symbols, which can grant the active character a buff to Normal Attacks. This should be a way of supporting the main DPS character in a party. In addition to that, Chiori’s leaked Passive Talent further indicates her role as a support, as having the scissor symbols active can grant the team a Geo DMG buff. With these two pieces of information, it can be interpreted that Chiori will be a Geo support unit, empowering Geo DPS heroes that rely on Normal Attacks for damage output, such as Arataki Itto or Navia in Genshin Impact.

What may indicate that Chiori can also operate as a sub-DPS is the fact that the leaks mention coordinated attacks. According to Uncle PP, Chiori’s scissor symbols, which are created with her Elemental Burst, can also provide scissor-like coordinated attacks – usually, coordinated attacks occur even if the character that triggered it is off the field, and they are best fit for support and sub-DPS units. Raiden Shogun, Xingqiu, and Yelan are some of the characters that provide the best and most consistent coordinated attacks in Genshin Impact, dealing damage and spreading their elements while off-field, for instance.

              Genshin Impact Leaks: There's Bad News For The First Natlan Character   According to leaks, there may be bad news for players looking forward to playing with Iansan, the first character from Genshin Impact’s Natlan region.

With Version 4.4 yet to arrive, it is too early to tell exactly how Chiori will work. The leaks so far detail what could be a promising Geo unit that can further expand one of the most underused elements in the game. However, even if the current leaks are accurate, her entire gameplay kit may be changed and tweaked before her release, ultimately transforming Chiori into an entirely different character in terms of gameplay. Nevertheless, more consistent details should be either revealed or leaked as Genshin Impact 4.5 approaches.
