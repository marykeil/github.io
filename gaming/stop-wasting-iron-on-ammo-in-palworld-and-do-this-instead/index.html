Stop Wasting Iron On Ammo In Palworld And Do This Instead

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1300 words 6 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Crafting ammo in Palworld is resource-intensive, and if players can acquire enough gold, purchasing ammo en masse may be the better option. Killing a certain Black Marketeer and buying ammo from a specific Wandering Merchant lets players get ammo easily. This will save the lengthy production time required to make bullets, and lets players use their resources on other crafting items.

Literature Review

Ammo in Palworld can become a very resource-heavy investment, which is why this method can save players mass amounts of their hard-earned metal.

Despite how easy it can be to craft ammo in Palworld, there is an easier way to acquire ammo that prevents players from wasting hordes of metal. Guns became the ultimate fantasy for players in the creature-collecting genre, with Palworld being one of the first to do it correctly. Along with the other mechanics blended in, the simple but enjoyable gunplay has helped propel Palworld into one of the most successful game launches in history. This successful launch has already created a roadmap of future content planned for the next year of early access.

Palworld’s weapons are simple but remain helpful as players progress through the game. All players start with clubs, bows, and spears that advance to modern-day firearms through the technology tree. However, there is the caveat that, as higher-level Pals are found, most weapons won’t match the damage a player’s own Pals can put out. This is not to say the weapons will be useless, but they will feel more like long-range support than the main source of damage.

How To Acquire Ammo In Palworld Without Metal

Thanks to Reddit user Otherwise_Food875, a way to acquire ammo en masse without using metal to craft it has been found. The first step is traveling to an island on the eastern edge of the map, which contains the boss battles for Mossanda and Mossanda Lux. On the far side of this island, near the fast travel point, will be one of Palworld’s Black Marketeers. The next part will be the toughest part, which includes killing the Black Market trader, as they have a higher health pool and are equipped with a chaingun. The reward for killing him will be around 24 thousand gold coins every time.

Once killed, look for a giant rock that shoots into the sky on the island, and using whatever mount is easiest, do a circle around it back to the Marketeer. After that, he will have respawned and can be killed again for the same rewards. Repeat this process for as long as desired and then move on. Once players have satisfied their need for gold, travel to Fisherman’s Point and speak to the Wandering Merchant wearing green. He sellsevery type of ammo for a relatively cheap price, making it the best source of bulk ammo.

Players can also catch this Wandering Merchant and move them back to their bases for ease of access.

This process may seem lengthy, but in comparison to the amount of resources that would be used and gathered anyway, it is easier. Also, if players have mass amounts of gold already, then the Black Marketeer can be skipped entirely. Even though these resources can be gathered mostly by Pals, this option opens up the ability to use these resources in other areas, such as base building or crafting other items. Perhaps the best use would be to craft more Pal Spheres, for capturing large numbers of Pals for perfect breeding.

How To Defeat The Black Marketeer In Palworld

The Black Marketeer is no easy fight when done legitimately, but luckily, there is an easy way to trick him. Players simply need to build a Palbox to establish a base, and then build a hanging trap so the rope lands at the marketeer’s feet. This will propel him into the air and make it impossible for him to move or fight back. After that, players just whittle down their massive health pool until death. This will be fastest with a Pal that can deal large amounts of damage in a short period.

There are two important things to know about this trick before trying to do it. First, this is the only Black Marketeer that the gold farm works on, as the rest will not respawn as quickly. Secondly, it is necessary to break the Palbox after defeating him, or he will not respawn. Besides that, this is a great method to use to farm massive amounts of gold, whether that is to buy ammo or anything else that can be sold in Palworld.

Everything To Know About Ammo In Palworld

Currently, five types of ammo exist in Palworld, with the only differences being crafting effort and what gun they are used for. These five are Coarse, Handgun, Rifle, Shotgun, and Assault Rifle, used for the Musket, Handgun, Single Shot Rifle, Shotgun, and Assault Rifle respectively. The ammo used does not affect damage and carries no special effects with it. However, it would be an interesting feature for the future if more powerful types of ammo could be crafted/acquired.

Coarse and Handgun ammo are not that expensive to make, only requiring normal ingots and gunpowder, but this price will add up if crafted in significant quantities. However, the other three ammo types need refined ingots, which is a lengthier and more costly process. This is the great part of buying ammo rather than crafting it. Though it will take some time to acquire gold, whether done legitimately or through tricks, it is less time-consuming and more resource-saving than crafting.

If players do want to acquire bullets without spending money or through tricks, the best method, outside minimal enemy drops, would be Pal workers. The biggest challenge of this will be finding a base or bases that can produce massive amounts of charcoal, sulfur, coal, and ore. The latter two will be used to make both types of ingots, while charcoal and sulfur will be added together to make the gunpowder. Overall, making ammo is not the hardest process, but could be done more cost-effectively if it matters to the player.

There’s a chance the mechanics around guns and ammo will change as Palworld evolves into the game Pocketpair desires it to be. The possibility of the Black Marketeer trick being patched out or nerfed in some fashion does exist, but since the game does seem to pride itself on funky and fun gameplay, there is a chance it will never be touched. Regardless of what the future holds, this method should be used by Palworld players to acquire ammo and avoid wasting mass amounts of their hard-earned metal.
