Literature Review

To get Black Swan to the maximum level in Honkai: Star Rail and unlock all her abilities, you will need to farm. Here are the required materials.

Penacony is a new destination in the turn-based RPG, also known as the Land of Dreams. This location is considered peaceful, which may reflect its alignment with the Path of the Harmony. In fact, the Astral Express crew and even the Stellaron Hunters are invited by the leaders of Penacony to attend the Charmony Festival. Penacony is set to feel like a vacation destination, though there seems to be more hidden beneath its harmonious visage. Black Swan is not a character from Penacony in Honkai: Star Rail but she does have a connection to the Planet of Festivities.

Ascension Materials To Farm For Black Swan In Honkai: Star Rail

Most of the materials for Black Swan are fairly easy to obtain. Extinguished Core, Glimmering Core, and Squirming Core are some of the first materials you will need to gather when farming for the 5-star character. These items drop from enemies like Windspawns, Thunderspawns, Frostspawns, Flamespawns, Ice Out of Space, Blaze Out of Space, and Mask of No Thought. They can also be gathered from Assignments and purchased from world shops and the Embers Exchange store. As of Honkai: Star Rail 2.0, you can farm these materials at Calyx (Golden), as they are listed as some of the potential rewards.

You will need Extinguished Core, Glimmering Core, and Squirming Core for Black Swan’s Traces as well, so be sure to farm a surplus when gathering.

For Black Swan’s Ascension, you will also need to obtain Ascendant Debris. This is a Wind-exclusive material that can only be farmed from Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Celestial. This material is locked behind the use of Trailblaze Power so, even if you only need 65 copies of Ascendant Debris, the daily limitation on Trailblaze Power may set your farming process back a few days. Alternatively, you can use the Synthesizer to transform two spare Ascension materials into one Ascendant Debris. In addition to the materials themselves, you will also need to provide Credits. A total of 308,000 Credits are used to unlock all of Black Swan’s level thresholds in the game. This does not, however, consider the Credits used when leveling Black Swan with Character EXP Materials. If you are doing so, you are expected to spend an extra 580,100 Credits. If you need more Credits in Honkai: Star Rail, it is recommended that you farm Calyx (Golden): Bud of Treasure. Even more are consumed when leveling her Traces, so it is wise to have a large sum available.

Materials For Black Swan’s Traces (Abilities) In Honkai: Star Rail

Extinguished Core, Glimmering Core, and Squirming Core are some of the first materials you will need when leveling Black Swan’s Traces in the game. They can be obtained from the previously mentioned sources, though her Traces require a lot more copies of the items when compared to how much is consumed by her Ascension process. The next item family you will need to farm is composed of Fiery Spirit, Starfire Essence, and Heaven Incinerator. These three items were added with Honkai: Star Rail 2.0 and, thus, could not be pre-farmed in any way before Black Swan’s release.

Fiery Spirit, Starfire Essence, and Heaven Incinerator are materials exclusively used by Nihility characters and can be farmed by running Calyx (Crimson): Bud of Nihility on the Alchemy Commission map. Other Nihility items, such as Obsidian of Dread, do not serve Black Swan, even if they are farmed from the same Calyx. As an item family, you should use the Synthesizer to transform lower-grade materials into higher-quality ones as the Trace-leveling demands increase. The table below lists all the materials and amounts of each item you will need to completely level all of Black Swan’s Traces in Honkai: Star Rail:

Black Swan Trace Materials In Honkai: Star Rail Extinguished Core 41x Glimmering Core 56x Squirming Core 58x Fiery Spirit 18x Starfire Essence 69x Heaven Incinerator 139x Past Evils of the Borehole Planet Disaster 12x Tracks of Destiny 8x Credit 3,000,000x

As Black Swan’s Trace levels get higher, she will start requiring the usage of Past Evils of the Borehole Planet Disaster. This is a weekly boss material and can only be obtained from facing the Echo of War: Borehold Planet’s Old Crater activity, found in Herta Space Station’s Seclusion Zone map. You are limited to three boss challenges per week, however, so farming the 12 required copies may take a while. For reference, this is the same material used in Dr. Ratio’s Ascension in Honkai: Star Rail. You can use the Interastral Peace Guide’s Survival Index to locate the activity.

This Echo of War boss fight unlocks after you have completed the “When the Stars of Ingenuity Shine” quest.

To top off Black Swan’s Traces, you will need Tracks of Destiny. This is an advanced Trace material that can be obtained from limited-time events, as well as the Simulated Universe – one copy of Tracks of Destiny can be claimed per week via the Simulated Universe. You can also get up to two Tracks of Destiny from the Embers Exchange store’s monthly refresh. Fully Ascending Black Swan and leveling her Traces is not too hard. Nevertheless, it is necessary to run some activities in Honkai: Star Rail to get Black Swan to her maximum level.
