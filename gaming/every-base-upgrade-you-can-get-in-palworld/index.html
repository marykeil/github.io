SUMMARY

Literature Review

Getting every base upgrade in Palworld will allow players to maximize the potential of their Pals and resource generation in the game.

When you are first starting, the base is more like a campfire with a few beds. But by the end of your journey, the bases will become automated fortresses with lots of materials being farmed by your Pals at every moment. These upgrades will significantly affect how much you can explore and do in the game.

              What Makes Legendary Pals In Palworld So Special?   Capturing Legendary Pals seems like an obvious goal in Palworld, but knowing exactly what sets them apart from other creatures can be less clear.

All Base Upgrades & What They Do In Palworld

There are currently twenty levels players can upgrade their base through, although that may change during the pre-release period. This is separate from the player’s level, and all bases you build in Palworld will be of the same level. You can currently build up to three bases, which can hold fifteen Pals each. Additionally, all required upgrades to increase your Base level must be built within the boundaries of one of your bases.

The number of Pals per base can be changed in the settings to be a maximum of 20.

Once the player reaches the final base level, they will have maximized their Pal and Base numbers. Of note, the number of Pals will stop increasing after level 15 if players do not change the default setting. This could all change as more updates to the game are rolled out, but for the time being, that is all that can be done to upgrade your Base in Palworld.
