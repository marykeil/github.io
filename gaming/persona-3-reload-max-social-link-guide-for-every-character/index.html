SUMMARY

Literature Review

Those hoping to max out every Social Link in Persona 3 Reload will have to do meticulous planning and select the correct dialogue choices every time.

There are over 20 Social Links in Persona 3 Reload, and while a couple of them will rank up naturally through story progression, the majority will require the protagonist to put in some effort with them. Be warned, however, that the wrong dialogue options can cause you to lose progress with a Reverse Social Link interaction.

    Persona 3 Reload Interview - "The Story That We Want To Tell"  The producer and game director behind the upcoming Persona 3 Reload discuss its key changes, most impactful improvements, and biggest challenges.

Every Social Link Answer Increase Ranks With The Correct Dialogue Choice

During hangouts with each Social Link, there will be different points where you must respond. Based on your response to this Social Link, they will either increase in rank, remain stagnant, and occasionally even reverse. The way to know if a response has had a positive impact is if musical notes appear above the character after you select your choice. If none appear, the answer is either neutral, which will have no effect, or negative, which may result in a reverse Persona.

Note that not every positive dialogue option is necessarily the right one. Often, there will be two answers that are both okay, but one will be better than the other. The goal is to get the most correct answer for the highest number of music notes and the best chance at increasing your rank. However, with that in mind, there will also occasionally be instances where any available options will have the same effect, so you can select the one you prefer.

The last day to rank up school-related Social Links in Persona 3 Reload is 1/30 , and the last day for all other Social Links is 1/31 . Therefore, ensure you spend your time wisely, as some Social Links will not even unlock until January and require your full attention.

Kenji Tomochika Magician Arcana

Kenji Tomochika is one of the first Social Links to be available to the protagonist at the beginning of Persona 3 Reload. He is of the Magician Arcana and will become available on 4/22 without special requirements. His availability for hangouts includes Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays for the game’s duration. Once he reaches rank ten and maxes out, you will unlock the ability to create the Persona Futsunushi.

Utilize Personas of the same Arcana for each Social Link to maximize the possible musical notes for each correct answer.

Fuuka Yamagishi Priestess Arcana

Fuuka is another early Social Link unlock and one of your party members. Fuuka will serve as the navigator for S.E.E.S. once she is unlocked, and, as with all party members, she should be a priority to max out. She is available on Mondays, Fridays, and Saturdays. Not only is Fuuka also one of the romantic Social Links, but maxing her out will result in unlocking the Scathach Persona in the Velvet Room.
