10 Best Athra Surges In Prince Of Persia The Lost Crown

2020-10-16 About 2100 words 10 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents

    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Literature Review

Sargon can unleash Athra Surges in Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown with different properties against his enemies, but the best have strong effects.

Sargon can have only two Athra Surges equipped at a time, but you can easily switch them out by resting at a Wak-Wak Tree. Similar to how you would change out your best Amulets in Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown, unlocked Athra abilities can be mixed and matched in a custom loadout of sorts for Sargon. Feel free to experiment with Surge combinations to see which two work best together.

10 Verethragna’s Smite How To Unlock: Play Through the Main Quest

Athra Surge Level Description Verethragna’s Smite 1 Rush toward an enemy with a piercing strike that shatters all defenses.
The first Athra Surge Sargon acquires is Verethragna’s Smite during the first level of Prince of Persia: TLC. Obtained right before you have to beat the Sword Master boss, this Surge acts as your introduction to Athra as a concept. You build Athra by landing combos against enemies and not taking hits in return, as they fight back with a variety of their own deadly strikes. Make sure to use defensive techniques such as dodging, sliding, and parrying against enemy attacks to keep your Athra meter growing and eventually unleash a Surge.

This Athra Surge may be limited to the ground and only deal a slight amount of damage to enemies, but it has the capability to destroy shields and other defenses. Several foes you come across in the Lower City and Hyrcanian Forest can be hard to deal with. Since Verethragna’s Smite is a Level 1 Surge, it can be easily used to clear rooms full of tougher basic enemies.

9 Arash’s Ray How To Unlock: Beat Alternate Sargon in The Depths

Athra Surge Level Description Arash’s Ray 2 Fire a powerful beam that goes through enemies. Hold the attack button to aim accurately and release to fire.
Menolias’ Bow can be an incredibly useful weapon that Sargon obtains after you defeat Erlick in Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown within the Hyrcanian Forest. However, the Arash’s Ray Athra Surge obtained later in the “Tiger and Rat” main story quest gives more utility to ranged attacks in the game. This Surge is special among other Athra abilities for allowing you to aim and control where its attack goes in combat. The beam produced by this Surge pierces through multiple foes, which makes it a great Athra skill to use against larger crowds of enemies. Unfortunately, the damage from this ability is not too strong like many of the projectile attacks in Prince of Persia: TLC. The Level 2 cost also makes it difficult to use consistently unless you are very careful not to get hit by enemy attacks for a longer amount of time.

8 Vayu’s Wave How To Unlock: Complete Artaban’s Combat Challenges

Athra Surge Level Description Vayu’s Wave 1 Release a powerful, unfurling wave of Athra that pushes back the enemy. The end of the move can be interrupted.
Finishing all the combat tutorials with Artaban in The Haven hub of the Lower City unlocks Vayu’s Wave, a Level 1 Surge that is designed to help Sargon against overwhelming odds. Unleashing this wave of Athra not only pushes back your foes but also can deal tons of damage if enemies are caught in the attack early. This skill is best used in Prince of Persia: TLC when you are experiencing low health and need space fast.

8 Beginner Tips You Need To Know For Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown The combat and exploration of Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown can be confusing for beginners, but a few tips can mold Sargon into a great warrior.

The only lackluster aspect of this Surge comes from its vulnerability toward the end of the move. Enemies that the attack misses can easily punish Sargon as he finishes channeling Athra, and they typically deal twice as much damage in the same way as missing a parry. Thankfully, this Level 1 Surge in Prince of Persia: TLC can be used often both on the ground and up in the air.

7 Hadhayans’ Might How To Unlock: Beat Both Alternate Sargons at the Soma Tree

Athra Surge Level Description Hadhayans’ Might 2 Teleport over enemies to crush them to the ground from the heavens.
The Level 2 Athra Surge Hadhayans’ Might is one of the most straightforward skills Sargon can equip in Prince of Persia: TLC. The brief moment of invulnerability you gain as Sargon teleports over an enemy can be a great way to deal guaranteed damage without the threat of being punished. Unlike other Surges though, this attack is only effective against a single target.

6 Shahbaz’ Spirit How To Unlock: Beat Alternate Sargon in the Sacred Archives

Athra Surge Level Description Shahbaz’ Spirit 1 Launch a powerful cross slash attack above Sargon. Very useful against flying enemies.
Shahbaz’ Spirit is an Athra Surge that overcomes one of Sargon’s greatest weaknesses – dealing with enemies that fly high above him in the sky. The cross slash that you perform with this Surge covers a ton of space in front of Sargon in addition to the upward strike. This attack also deals a lot of damage in Prince of Persia: TLC, despite only being a Level 1 Surge that can be used fairly often. Since this Athra Surge specializes in hitting foes above Sargon, those who want to conserve arrows for Menolias’ Bow can build up this attack to hit flying enemies instead of using their limited projectiles. This Surge can be used in the air or ground to pursue this strategy even more.

5 Rashnu’s Judgment How To Unlock: Defeat the Immortal Menolias

Athra Surge Level Description Rashnu’s Judgment 3 Ready a stance which allows for a remarkable counter-attack when timed just right.
The last Athra Surge you can unlock in Prince of Persia: TLC is Rashnu’s Judgment, which you receive after defeating the Immortal Menolias in battle. This Level 3 Surge is incredibly expensive to use, but no one can deny how powerful it can be when it activates. This ability depends on a player’s mastery of the game’s parry system that they have been using since the beginning of their adventure. The tight timing of Rashnu’s Judgment can make it one of the most difficult Athra Surges to use since many others are simple attacks that are easy to understand. This high skill ceiling rewards players who have invested a lot of time into learning the combat of Prince of Persia: TLC through Artaban’s training. As a result, this Surge is not for everybody, especially since it takes so much time to charge. Those who want to use this Athra Surge may want to employ the Free Training mode with Artaban at The Haven to refine their understanding of Sargon’s parry and his additional time powers in Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown.

This Athra Surge functions differently from Vengeful Counters, which can only be performed against an enemy attack where the foe’s eyes turn yellow. Any enemy attack can trigger Rashnu’s Judgment unless it is a red unstoppable attack indicated by red eyes. This move works best against powerful bosses seen in the later missions within Prince of Persia: TLC.

4 Wind of Sistan How To Unlock: Interact With the Skeleton in The Depths

Athra Surge Level Description Wind of Sistan 1 Create a swirling wave of Athra that cripples enemies. Very effective when Sargon is surrounded. The end of the move can be interrupted.
The strongest Level 1 Athra Surge in Prince of Persia: TLC is undoubtedly Wind of Sistan, which deals incredible damage over a large area. Even though the end of the move can be interrupted, the large radius it covers prevents any enemies from being near Sargon as the attack continues. This move is effective against bosses, elite enemies, and basic foes alike anywhere in the Citadel. Similar to Vayu’s Wave, this Surge can act as a move you unleash when many enemies are about to overtake Sargon in an area. Certain combat challenge rooms that guard treasure are particularly tough to beat without using your Athra at least once. The ease at which you can charge this attack means that activating Wind of Sistan often isn’t uncommon as you meet different obstacles in Prince of Persia: TLC.

3 Bahamut’s Rage How To Unlock: Defeat the Immortal Orod

Athra Surge Level Description Bahamut’s Rage 3 Unleash a large and devastating whirlwind attack that throws light foes in the air and then smashes them violently to the ground.
As seen in a video by YouTube creator Rubhen925, Bahamut’s Rage is an Athra Surge of pure power that makes it worth the Level 3 charge it takes to use. The whirlwind that Sargon conjures pulls tons of different enemies into their attack, which can decimate smaller foes instantly. Even elite enemies and bosses in Prince of Persia: TLC are susceptible to this move’s raw damage output when it’s used correctly. The only downside to this Athra Surge is that you get it around 60% of the way into Prince of Persia: TLC’s main story. This means you won’t have as much time to actually use it in comparison to other Surges unlocked earlier in the journey. There are still some new areas you could travel to with this ability equipped, but most of your map may already be revealed by the time you get the skill.

2 Bahman’s Breath How To Unlock: Heal at the First Wak-Wak Tree in the Hyrcanian Forest

Athra Surge Level Description Bahman’s Breath 2 Create an explosion and a healing zone that gradually restores health. Nearby enemies are pushed back.
The only Athra Surge with an ability on top of damage is Bahman’s Breath due to the skill’s healing properties. Standing in the space created by this Surge for long enough will recover all of Sargon’s health extremely quickly to prolong certain fights. This ability works masterfully against bosses and lets you keep fighting against them for longer to achieve victory. The explosion from Bahman’s Breath also pushes back enemies, similar to Vayu’s Wave or Wind of Sistan. This Level 2 Surge gives you the space to heal not only through the zone it conjures but also by knocking back foes that would prevent Sargon from standing in the healing area. This Surge can be a consistent and reliable tool to use in any battle you come across in Prince of Persia: TLC.

1 Soul of Gilgamesh How To Unlock: Beat the Alternate Sargon in the Pit of Eternal Sands

Athra Surge Level Description Soul of Gilgamesh 3 Channel Athra to become temporarily stronger and faster. Improve all moves to allow for new combos. Increase defense against all melee damage.
The best Athra Surge in Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown is Soul of Gilgamesh due to its ability to heighten every one of Sargon’s skills in combat. Movement becomes faster, attacks become stronger, and defenses become more resilient to the myriad of enemy strikes you come up against throughout your adventure. Longer combos are only possible through Soul of Gilgamesh, which makes its Level 3 charge worth the effort. This Surge can be difficult to find since you need to defeat a hidden Alternate Sargon boss, but a video by YouTube creator 100% Guides showcases exactly where you can pick up this ability. You may need to practice using Soul of Gilgamesh in Artaban’s Free Training mode to truly master it. Impossible attack strings and move combinations turn into possibilities solely from activating Soul of Gilgamesh.
