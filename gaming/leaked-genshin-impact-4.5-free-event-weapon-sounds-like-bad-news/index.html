Leaked Genshin Impact 4.5 Free Event Weapon Sounds Like Bad News

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1300 words 6 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Version 4.5 of Genshin Impact may introduce a F2P 4-star weapon through an event, but it may disappoint players due to its similarities to an existing weapon. The leaked event weapon may be mostly beneficial for specific, unpopular characters, making it underwhelming compared to previous event weapons. While the rumored event weapon is given out for free, its potential use is limited and may not be worth the investment compared to other options.

Literature Review

Leaks indicate that Version 4.5 of Genshin Impact will offer a free event weapon, but its details may sound like bad news for some players.

There has been a leak about the free event weapon in Genshin Impact 4.5, and the details so far indicate bad news for players. Version 4.5 is expected to arrive in mid-March if the regular six-week calendar for updates is kept up-to-date without any unexpected delays. The upcoming version is expected to finally introduce the playable version of Chiori in Genshin Impact, following her debut as an NPC in Version 4.3’s Roses and Muskets event. As it is still early in Version 4.4, there have not been too many reliable leaks about the planned content for the next patch.

However, there has been a mention of a F2P (free-to-play) 4-star weapon handed out with Version 4.5’s flagship event. Weapons of this category are given out frequently, and they can only be acquired during the event itself, with no means of gaining them afterward. Some of the best event weapons that are no longer available include Festering Desire, Oathsworn Eye, Cinnabar Spindle, and Ibis Piercer. These weapons are often the best substitutes for certain 5-star character builds, which is why they are usually sought after. Genshin Impact 4.5 leaks, however, indicate an addition that may be bad news for some.

              Genshin Impact 4.5 Chiori Kit Leaks (So Far) - Vision, Skills, & Burst   The leaks about Chiori’s gameplay kit in Genshin Impact 4.5 indicate she may be both a support and sub-DPS unit in team comps centered around Geo.

Genshin Impact 4.5 Event May Give F2P HP-Scaling Polearm

According to some information acquired by the leaker known as Foul, later shared by a user on Reddit, Version 4.5 will feature an event that hands out a F2P 4-star polearm. This weapon will have a base ATK of 510 at level 90, with a 41.35% HP sub-stat bonus, also at level 90. The weapon’s skill will see that when healing is triggered, the user will restore 8 Energy. The Genshin Impact leak also mentions that this effect can be triggered every 10 seconds and can be activated even if the user is not present on the field.

The Version 4.5 event and F2P polearm’s names have not yet been shared by leakers.

Why The Leaked Event Weapon May Be Bad News In Genshin Impact

As noted in some of the replies in the Reddit post, the addition of this rumored F2P weapon with Version 4.5’s flagship event is not too exciting. The fact is that this HP-scaling polearm allegedly presents a similar bonus to the one provided by Rightful Reward, another F2P weapon that is already available in the game. Rightful Reward is a craftable polearm presented with Version 4.0’s Fontaine region. It offers more base ATK (565 at level 90) but less HP (27.6% at level 90) than the rumored event weapon in Genshin Impact 4.5.

              Genshin Impact Leaks: There's Bad News For The First Natlan Character   According to leaks, there may be bad news for players looking forward to playing with Iansan, the first character from Genshin Impact’s Natlan region.

Rightful Reward’s weapon skill sees that, when the wielder is healed, they restore 8 Energy, an effect that can be triggered every 10 seconds and can occur even if the user is off-field. There is a slight difference in how the Energy restoration skill is activated, but it is still very similar. With weapon billets, players can craft multiple copies of Rightful Reward and take it to Weapon Refinement level 5, which then boosts the Energy restored to 16 points. The main complaint is that players have already invested time and resources into R5 Rightful Reward in Genshin Impact.

Getting the correct weapon billets can be a daunting task, so increasing the F2P’s Refinement level to R5 is tough. With Version 4.5 allegedly now offering a weapon with a similar effect and somewhat similar stats, the hard work put into refining Rightful Reward seems like a waste. This is especially true when considering that weapons granted by events can also easily be refined to R5, as the event itself offers exclusive refinement material to upgrade the weapon. Toukabou Shigure in Genshin Impact 3.3 was provided through an event, as well as its Parasol Talcum refinement material, for instance.

Genshin Impact 4.5’s Leaked Weapon May Mostly Serve Unpopular Characters

Another fact that may be disappointing to some players is that the allegedly leaked event weapon from Version 4.5 will only serve some very specific and quite unpopular characters, in terms of gameplay. As a weapon that provides an HP bonus but only activates its skill when the user themselves triggers healing, the rumored 4-star polearm should only serve healing polearm characters. This includes heroes like Mika, Yaoyao, and Chevreuse in Genshin Impact. It is good news for these often-underused characters, but it ultimately does not match the potential of other event weapons offered by the game so far.

Toukabou Shigure, for instance, is a fantastic weapon for sword characters that rely on Elemental Mastery. The 4-star parasol sword is a great match for 5-star units like Kaedahara Kazuha and Ayato Kamisato, for example. Similarly, the Version 2.3-exclusive Cinnabar Spindle is still considered the best weapon for Albedo, surpassing even many 5-star recommendations that could suit it. Oathsworn Eye is also a great choice for 5-star catalyst units that rely on Energy Recharge, such as Yae Miko and Klee. If Genshin Impact 4.5’s leaked F2P polearm is true, it will be significantly underwhelming when compared to previous event weapons.

Users in the Reddit post’s comments mention that the weapon’s skill leaked by Foul may actually be a simple placeholder based on Rightful Reward’s skill, which means it may be subject to change.

The HP bonus provided by its sub-stat would most likely be used by shield-focused Zhongli builds, but its rumored weapon skill only really fits one 5-star character, and somewhat poorly. Hu Tao is capable of triggering healing through her Burst, which could activate the weapon’s Energy Recharge effect, but there are better options for her. As a DPS, 4-star polearms like Battle of the Fjords and Dragon’s Bane are more appropriate. The best heroes for the rumored weapon would be polearm healers and, currently, there are only 4-star units that do not usually fit meta team comps in Genshin Impact.

              Genshin Impact Leaks: Weapons Update May Significantly Change The Game   A new leak suggests that an update will offer players better options when trying to acquire 5-star weapons for their characters in Genshin Impact.

Of course, as this is a leak, it should be taken with a grain of salt. The alleged information may be incorrect or subject to change ahead of the update’s official release in March. Although recent leaks have mostly been very reliable, there is still a chance for the shared information to be completely inaccurate. Regardless, the rumored event weapon in Version 4.5 is good news because it is allegedly given out for free, but may be bad news due to its similarity to Rightful Reward and the lack of better Genshin Impact characters to equip it.
