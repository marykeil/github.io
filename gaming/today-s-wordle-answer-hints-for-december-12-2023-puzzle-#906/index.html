The daily Wordle is ready to be solved, if you need a little help we’ve got some hints, and the fully spoiled answer, for the December 12th Wordle.

Wordle has been popular for quite some time and its rise to fame happened quite quickly. Despite its simple premise, there are several Wordle tips and tricks that players can utilize to become better at the game. One of the most important ones is to have a consistent starting word. Doing this allows you to train yourself on what direction to take after receiving the clues for the day. This is a great way to lower the amount of guesses it takes to solve a daily Wordle.

Today’s Wordle Hints December 12th #906

Before we fully spoil today’s Wordle answer we wanted to provide a few hints to help out those who may just need a little push in the right direction.

Hint 1 There is one repeating letter. Hint 2 The answer is used as a pronoun or determiner. Hint 3 The vowels are in the 3rd & 5th position. Hint 4 This word alludes to someone or something.

Today’s Wordle Answer December 12th #906

The December 12th Wordle answer is THESE.

For our starting Wordle word today we used POUND which provided absolutely nothing except some incorrect letters. Generally, when this happens we have our backup starting word IRATE which we use second. This locked in an ‘E’ and a misplaced ‘T’.

Moving forward we tried to adjust the ‘T’ and did THEME as our third guess, this all but gave it away by locking in four of the five letters. Our final guess scored us a Wordle win-in-four with the correct answer THESE.
