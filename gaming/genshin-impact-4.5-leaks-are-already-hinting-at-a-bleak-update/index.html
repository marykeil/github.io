Genshin Impact 4.5 Leaks Are Already Hinting At A Bleak Update

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1300 words 7 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Genshin Impact 4.5 may only add one playable character, disappointing players who were expecting more. The leaked information also suggests that the new character may be a bad 5-star unit due to her double-scaling abilities. The rumored free-to-play weapon in Version 4.5 is also predicted to be unsatisfactory, adding to the overall disappointment of the update.

Literature Review

The leaks about Version 4.5 for Genshin Impact are starting to sound worrying, with a notable lack of content at the forefront of the rumors.

The several Genshin Impact 4.5 leaks are pointing toward what is likely going be a very bleak update in terms of playable content, despite multiple rumors about what may be added. At the moment, the action RPG is in the middle of Version 4.4, a big and important patch for the game. The update brought forth the annual Lantern Rite Festival, telling a unique storyline set in Liyue. This is usually the most important event in the title every year. Lantern Rite, however, was not the only significant thing that came with the release of Genshin Impact 4.4.

The update also added the region of Chenyu Vale to the northern portion of Liyue, a completely new explorable area with its own World Quests, exclusive gameplay mechanics, materials, and even a world boss. Version 4.4 also added Xianyun, the human and playable form of Cloud Retainer, as well as the 4-star Pyro DPS unit, Gaming. Version 4.4 is a massive update and one that rivals x.0 patches that tend to introduce full new Nations. In contrast, however, the leaks are hinting at Genshin Impact 4.5 being the direct opposite, with very little enticing content to play through.

Only One Character Is Being Added In Genshin Impact 4.5

One of the most notable aspects of the upcoming patch is that Version 4.5 is expected to only add one playable character. As the game progresses further into its year after a major Nation release, the number of new characters per patch tends to lower proportionally. This has happened in previous years. Nevertheless, it is still a worrisome thing, especially considering there are several Fontaine characters that have yet to be added as playable. For now, only Chiori’s release in Genshin Impact 4.5 has been confirmed, and no other unit, be they 4-star or 5-star, is expected to be introduced.

Chiori May Be A Bad 5-Star Unit In Genshin Impact

In addition to the fact that only one character is being added, there are rumors based on her leaked gameplay kit that Chiori may be bad in Genshin Impact. The leaks mention that the 5-star Geo unit’s abilities scale off two stats, which is never a good sign. Both her Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst allegedly rely on her ATK and DEF stats being increased and, given the game’s history with double-scaling characters, this is a formula for disaster. Heroes that need two stats to effectively operate on the field tend to be extremely hard to build.

This is mainly related to Artifact farming. When obtaining these items, their stats and sub-stats are randomized, which makes it all the more difficult to obtain one with the ideal stat distribution needed for a certain character. For double-scalers, players have to try to simultaneously level up two stats, as their kit only works with both. Ignore one of the stats and their performance is suboptimal. This happened in the past and one great example is Dehya, who relies on Max HP and ATK stats in Genshin Impact. Increasing both is tough and concessions can lead to underwhelming damage output.

If Chiori is a double-scaler, she may turn out to be a bad character to have. This would also mean that the only new addition in Version 4.5 is not worth pulling for. Of course, all of this is based on leaks, so the information may be incorrect or subject to change. There is also a chance that Chiori’s kit will be good, even if she is a double-scaler. Nevertheless, the leaked information hints at a risky pull that may not be as good as initially expected. Even if Chiori is good in Genshin Impact, Version 4.5 still seems plagued.

Genshin Impact 4.5’s Rumored F2P May Also Be Bad

The rumors about the F2P (free-to-play) weapon offered in the update’s flagship event are also worrying. According to the leaks, the event’s 4-star polearm will have a similar skill to that of Rightful Reward, another polearm that players can forge. The event’s weapon is said to offer more Max HP via its sub-stat, but the rumors are that the differences between the new weapon and the currently available polearm are too small to be satisfactory. If the rumored free event weapon in Genshin Impact 4.5 is truly bad, that will stack up with the previously leaked problems in the patch.

Albedo’s Cancelled Rerun & Smaller Updates In Genshin Impact 4.5

Another big concern is the lineup of rerun banners in Version 4.5. While previous leaks mentioned that Albedo would finally rerun, nearly two years after his last appearance, more recent rumors indicate he will not show up in either Phase 1 or Phase 2 banners. This can be very disappointing, as Albedo is regarded as one of the best Geo support and sub-DPS units in the game. Having Albedo not rerun in Genshin Impact 4.5 may be a way of avoiding conflict, as he and Chiori occupy the same role in team comps.

With only Chiori available, players looking for a Geo support and sub-DPS unit may pull for her to supply their needs.

The lack of an Albedo rerun may be addressed by a new mechanic in the game. The leaks point toward a new pulling system in Genshin Impact 4.5, which would work thematically. This would introduce an entirely new banner that allows players to pull randomly for characters and weapons from a specific region, and it would have a mechanism similar to the weapons banner’s Epitomized Path, ensuring a desired 5-star reward after a specific number of pulls. The information has been shared and confirmed by multiple credible leakers. This is somewhat positive news among a series of bad rumors.

There are also some quality-of-life (QoL) updates expected to arrive in Version 4.5, according to the leaks. There is a rumored new graphic quality setting being added in the patch, though it is currently uncertain if this may affect the specifications to run the game. If so, that could be bad news for players with a less potent gaming system. Another rumored QoL update hints at a recommendation system for Artifacts that would allow players to check the most used gear for specific characters and, thus optimize their builds in Genshin Impact 4.5 accordingly.

    Genshin Impact 4.5 Update May Have The Biggest Improvement To The Game Ever  A new leak about a quality-of-life update arriving with Genshin Impact 4.5 may hint at one of the game’s biggest improvements since its launch.

Other than the flagship event, which still lacks more details, it seems like events in Version 4.5 will be minor. One of the most notable ones is the rumored cat event in Genshin Impact 4.5, which will see players increase their reputation with feline friends. If that is one of the largest events in the patch, then there may not be much to look forward to. Not every update can be as large and significant as Version 4.4, but Genshin Impact 4.5 leaks point to a skippable update altogether, mostly due to the rumors about its dull and bleak content.
