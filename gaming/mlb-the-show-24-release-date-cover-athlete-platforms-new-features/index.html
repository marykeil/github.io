MLB The Show 24 Release Date, Cover Athlete, Platforms, & New Features

SUMMARY

MLB The Show 24 features the first father and son duo to be featured on the game’s covers. The game will feature various modes, including Storyline, Diamond Dynasty, March to October, Road to the Show, and Franchise, allowing players to immerse themselves in different aspects of baseball. MLB The Show 24 will release for multiple platforms and will include cross-progression and cross-platform play for players to compete against each other.

Literature Review

MLB The Show is back for 2024, with a new athlete gracing its cover and a ton of new features to be revealed over the coming weeks.

MLB The Show 24 has announced its cover athlete for the year, along with a host of new features that fans of the virtual baseball game can look forward to. Not simply a baseball simulator, MLB The Show has made a name for itself in the world of baseball video games by including real-life examples through history, a career storyline, and much more. Each year the game releases new versions that allow players to enjoy the game throughout the real-world baseball season, and every year builds upon the foundation of the past.

The gameplay of MLB The Show lets players take their pick of managing and building a team to lead to the World Series in Franchise Mode, becoming a ballplayer to grow a career through the Minor and Major Leagues and make tough choices, or build a fantasy team out of stars and legends of the sport to play against other players. The variety of game styles available means that everyone can have their own experience with the game, tailored to their desires, and even learn about the history of baseball legends and their career-defining moments.

MLB The Show 24 Release Date, Platforms, And Editions Pre-Orders For The Standard Edition Are Available Now

MLB The Show 24 officially releases on March 19, 2024, for Nintendo Switch, PS4/5, Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S, with pre-orders for the game currently available. The standard edition of the game for current generation consoles will include five The Show packs and 5,000 Stubs. The Nintendo Switch, PS4, and Xbox One Standard Edition will cost $59.99, with the PS5 and Xbox X/S versions costing $69.99. MLB The Show will also be available on Xbox Game Pass the day it releases. MLB The Show 24 will also have a Collectors Edition, with more details being released on February 6.

Those who purchase the digital edition of MLB The Show 24 on older-generation consoles can upgrade to PS5 or Xbox Series X/S version for $10.

MLB The Show 24 will be cross-progression and will also support cross-platform play, so people across consoles can compete against each other. In addition, all custom stadiums and logos from MLB The Show 23 will be available in the vaults when the game launches. Unfortunately, there will be no pre-launch test for people to try the game before it releases. Important to note that Stadium Creator will only be available on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S.

This Year’s Cover Athlete Is Vladimir Guerrero Jr. First Father And Son To Be Featured On MLB The Show Covers

This year’s MLB The Show 24 cover athlete is Vladimir Guerrero Jr. from the Toronto Blue Jays. Guerrero Jr. made his debut in the MLB in 2019 and is the son of Hall of Famer Vladimir Guerrero, who was the cover athlete for MLB 2006. The two will be the first father and son to be featured on the game’s covers. As the youngest player to be voted MVP in All-Star history and leading the major leagues in home runs in 2021, Guerrero Jr. looks to have a bright future ahead of him.

Everything New Coming To MLB The Show 24 MLB The Show 2024 Release Schedule

MLB The Show 2024 will include Storyline mode, Diamond Dynasty, Multiplayer, March to October, Road to the Show, and Franchise modes. Much like previous years, Storylines will delve into iconic players, reliving their most iconic moments, while Diamond Dynasty enables people to build their favorite fantasy teams with player cards. Road to the Show is more about an individual player’s career, while Franchise mode takes teams to the World Series.
