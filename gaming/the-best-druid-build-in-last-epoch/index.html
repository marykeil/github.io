The Best Druid Build In Last Epoch

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1300 words 7 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Literature Review

The Wearbear is an incredible Druid build in Last Epoch. It enhances several Primalist attacks, while still having its own unique features.

Druids in Last Epoch have some similarities to certain Druid builds in Diablo 4. While you will focus the majority of your Passive and Skill Points on the Druid progression, many valuable are in the Primalist base class. You will be utilizing preliminary moves like Swipe, Fury Leap, and Warcry. Additionally, you may even dive into the other two subclasses of Shaman and Beastmaster for bonus traits.

Tank Primalist Passives

Druid in Wearbear form has incredible survivability from hitpoints, life leech, and armor. By focusing on survival and healing, you can create one of the best tanks in Last Epoch. Still, it is advised to ally up with a Necromancer buddy for even more protection.

Primalist Passives Passive Allocation Benefit Primal Strength 8 Gain additional strength and health regeneration. Hunter’s Restoration 5 Increased health and every three seconds your direct hit heals you. Survival of the Pack 5 Increased melee damage and health leech for you and any minions. Tempest Bond 2 Increased physical, cold, and lightning damage. This effect doubles when with a minion. Berserker 1 Increases damage output and lowers damage taken when at critical health.

Druid Passives Passive Allocation Benefit Chitinous Plating 3 Gain bonus endurance and armor. Primordial Resonance 5 Increases all damage. Druid Prowess 5 Gain more attunement and strength. Thicket of Thorns 5 Possibly cast Thorn Shield on yourself. Also gain armor and damage reflection. Focused Wrath 5 While transformed gain extra damage and lose less Rage. Rancor 1 Do not lose Rage when out of combat. Aspects of Might 5 Your total strength improves armor while in human or Spriggan form, and melee damage when in Wearbear or Swarmblade form. Primal Shifter 6 Grants extra damage and armor when first transforming. Overgrowth 8 Improves health and mana regen. Tiger Spirit 2 Increases critical chance and critical damage while Primal Shifter is active. Hideskin 10 Gain extra health and endurance to a cap of 60%. Impervious 5 Melee hits have a 20% chance to grant a stack of Impervious, which reduces damage by 4% each. You can have five stacks maximum.

Beastmaster Passives Passive Allocation Benefit Ursine Strength 4 You gain additional strength and have damage reduction per nearby enemy. Savagery 1 Increases melee damage for you and your minions. Ambush 1 Every five seconds, your next melee attack grants Aspect of the Shark for three seconds. This improves damage and attack speed. Lamprey Teeth 6 You and your minions leech more health on melee hits. Aspect of the Boar 5 Gain Aspect of the Boar when hit by an enemy melee attack. This reduces damage taken for five seconds. Porcine Constitution 5 Aspect of the Boar gains even more damage reduction and increases health regen. Boar Heart 5 Aspect of the Boar can proc faster and improves strength.

Shaman Passives aren’t entirely needed for this build. However, Shamanic Infusion can bypass enemy resistance. Unlike in

Palworld where rebuilding uses consumable items , it is fast and easy to reallocate points right in the Passive menu in Last Epoch .

Near Permanent Wearbear Skills

Wearbear Druids will have access to two exclusive new skills: Rampage which charges forward, and Maul which slams. These don’t have specializations on their own and are only improved via the Wearbear tree. While in this form, you will always have access to Swipe and Warcry will change into Roar.

Finish up the build by taking Primalist’s Fury Leap and Druid’s Entangling Roots. Combining all of these can make a tough character similar to those that use the Juggernaut Aspect in Diablo 4.

While Druids lack the damage output of Rogues, it still remains one of the best class options in Last Epoch. Keep your Rage high and most of your Wearbear skills will keep you alive. This will allow anyone else in the party to clean up foes without drawing attention.
