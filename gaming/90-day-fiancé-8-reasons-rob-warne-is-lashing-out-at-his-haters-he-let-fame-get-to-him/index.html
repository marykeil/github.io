90 Day Fiancé 8 Reasons Rob Warne Is Lashing Out At His Haters (He Let Fame Get To Him)

SUMMARY

Rob Warne is becoming increasingly arrogant and entitled, letting his newfound fame get to his head. Rob has been dismissive and gaslighting towards his girlfriend Sophie, causing online criticism from viewers. Rob is actively feuding with other cast members, exhibiting a superiority complex and accusing them of being obsessed with him.

Literature Review

90 Day Fiancé star Rob Warne is coming across as a self-absorbed, narcissistic, and entitled person on social media while he tackles his “haters.”

90 Day Fiancé season 10 star Rob Warne is letting his 15 minutes of fame get to his head. Rob is a 32-year-old starring in the reality TV show with Sophie Sierra, a 23-year-old from England. Rob and Sophie started dating sometime in 2019 after she approached him on Instagram. Rob and Sophie connected over their shared experiences of being mixed race, but that’s where their similarities seemingly ended. Rob claimed he came from a modest background and was working hard to make it to the top, while he accused Sophie of being a spoiled princess. Rob has been dismissive of Sophie’s requests in their relationship and has been caught gaslighting her to make himself look like the victim. Rob’s on-screen behavior gets criticism online, but when viewers talk about him generally, Rob accuses them of making unwarranted attacks. Rob has been coming across as entitled and superior to others when it’s common for fans to discuss a cast member, whether they are good or bad. Rob has been finding it difficult to keep his feet on the ground, and it shows when he tries to call these unbothered fans out online.

8 Rob Claims He’s Gone From Rags To Riches Rob Is Living In A Fantasy Land

In December 2023, Rob showed fans his “Rags to Riches” storyline by creating several AI-generated pictures of himself. Rob was seemingly poking fun at his financial situation, but in reality, he wanted to brag about being a self-made man. While Sophie said Rob was doing odd jobs “here and there” while expressing concern about his financial situation, Rob claimed he was working three jobs when he was filming 90 Day Fiancé. Rob posted the Christmas-themed album in which the first picture showed him freezing shirtless in the snow as he progressively got wealthier with each swipe. The final photos showed rich Rob playing with dollar bills.

7 Rob Is Feuding With Cast Members Rob Thinks Statler Is Obsessed With Him

Rob’s ego is not letting him stay calm, even on social media. Since arguing with Sophie on camera is not enough for him, Rob keeps himself busy by lashing out at Statler Riley, a 90 Day Fiancé franchise alum. It was Statler who started the drama by calling out Rob in the comment section of an Instagram post of Rob and Sophie’s mom, Claire Sierra. Rob, in turn, called Statler disgusting and accused her of being the leader of the trolls and an annoying “know-it-all all.” Rob claimed Statler jumps from one comment section to another, passing judgments on other cast members and trying to seek attention by creating controversy. Statler, in turn, tried to expose Rob for lying about his humble beginnings. She figured out Rob had a wealthy upbringing and that he grew up in Overland Park, Kansas, which is one of the top three richest cities in the U.S. Statler also discovered a photo of Rob’s mom, who looked like the kind of woman who would never let her son live on the streets. Statler also found out two of Rob’s uncles had respectable jobs at San Diego State University and Johns Hopkins. Rob, meanwhile, accused Statler of being obsessed with him and claimed he wasn’t close to his family members.

6 Rob Is Sending Warning Messages To His “Haters” Rob Threatens To Block His Critics

Rob thinks he has become so famous that he has “haters” who are actively harassing him on social media. Rob called out these “haters,” who were following him just to hate on his photos. However, there don’t seem to be any negative comments on Rob’s public posts on Instagram from fans. Rob warned these “haters” that he was going to block them if they continued to hate on him. Rob asked them people why they hated someone whom they never met before. “I will sleep like a baby after you’re gone,” he wrote in his Instagram caption.

5 Rob Believes People Make “Speculations” About Him Rob Gives Himself Too Much Credit

While Rob is under the illusion that people are out to get him, he also can’t stop thinking about the ones spreading rumors about him. Rob seemed to have sparked rumors of him working at Cap’s Service and Repair in Austin, Texas, in 2023 via his social media. He recently asked fans to stop speculating about where he works. In the past, Rob has worked for a moving company, a grow facility, and hung signs around L.A. overnight. On 90 Day Fiancé ﻿season 10, Rob said had three jobs while living in an apartment with no bathroom.

4 Rob Is Peddling Cameos At Hefty Price Rob Lowered The Price After Realizing He Didn’t Have Customers

Several cast members, such as Big Ed Brown, David and Annie Toborowsky, and Larissa Lima, have managed to earn a sizable income from making Cameo videos for their fans. Rob did the same after his season 10 introduction. However, Rob, thinking he’s a 90 Day Fiancé star in high demand, decided to sell each personal video at the hefty price tag of $75. According to Reddit user bbykenz666, Rob’sCameo didn’t receive many requests, so his price kept dropping. “It was 75, then 45, now 40,” they wrote, alongside a screenshot. In 2024, Rob is charging $35 for a Cameo video request.

3 Rob Is Flirting With Fans When He’s Not Single Rob Posts Thirst Traps

90 Day Fiancé fans are under the impression that Rob and Sophie are no longer together because they are not featured in each other’s social media updates. Rob doesn’t post anything about Sophie and doesn’t seem to talk about her, either. However, they have accidentally revealed clues about them living together in the same house in Austin, two years after they filmed 90 Day Fiancé season 10. Considering Rob isn’t single, he openly posts thirst traps on Instagram and even replies to flirty comments while liking them.

2 Rob Is Charging Fans To Follow Them On IG Rob Wants Money In Exchange Of An Instagram Follow

Rob appears to be making these fans believe they have a chance with him while possibly being married to Sophie for over two years. His relationship status isn’t confirmed, and Sophie seems to be entertaining his schemes. Rob is also trying to fool fans into paying him for giving them attention on social media. Rob has an account on Fanbasis where he gives fans an “IG Follow back” for $50. Rob claims it will be an “Exclusive follow back” and unlock access to his daily posts, stories, and life behind the scenes.

1 Rob Wants People To Pay Him For “Airport Dance Rob’s Cringey Airport Proposal Wasn’t Meant Just For Sophie

What’s more disturbing is Rob charges $50 for him to “slide in your DMs” so he can chat with fans. Rob wants them to drop their Instagram handles so he can shoot them a personal message. It remains to be seen how Sophie would react; however, she likely won’t be pleased. Meanwhile, Rob is also available to perform his “one and only” airport dance for his special fans. The dance was supposed to be exclusive for welcoming Sophie to Los Angeles, but Rob is trying to earn $60 through his not-so-well-choreographed and cringy moves at the airport. 90 Day Fiancé airs Sundays at 8 p.m. EST on TLC.
