How To Find (& Defeat) A Bile Titan In Helldivers 2

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1300 words 6 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Literature Review

One of the strongest Terminid enemies in Helldivers 2 is a Bile Titan or a massive insect you can find and defeat with the right gear and strategies.

Like Hulk Automatons in Helldivers 2, Bile Titans resemble boss enemies you can encounter during Terminid missions or at other points of interest. Many of the insect types you could be up against typically appear randomly as you are trying to meet different objectives. Sometimes you may not even notice a Bile Titan has shown up until it starts to attack your character.

Specific missions called " Exterminate " may revolve around a Bile Titan that spawns as your direct target for that task. This is, unfortunately, perhaps the only way to guarantee an encounter with this Terminid boss in the game.

Best Weapons To Use Against A Bile Titan

Starting a mission with the right equipment will make all the difference against a Bile Titan, especially when it comes to what weapons you use. The best weapons to bring into battle against these Terminid bosses are the Recoilless Rifle, Railgun, and Expendable Anti-Tank (EAT) Launcher. All enemies in Helldivers 2 have unique defenses that can all be broken with relentless firepower.

The Railgun and EAT Launcher are straightforward, explosive guns that pack quite a punch against any enemy. Since both weapons deal some of the biggest damage in the game, you or your friends in co-op multiplayer for Helldivers 2 should stock up on these tools of mass destruction. Teaching a Bile Titan about the liberty of Super Earth should start with the loudest ordinance possible.

Many Bile Titans appear along with several low-level Terminid enemies. Remember to bring along other weapons that can quickly clear out waves of smaller bugs before you have to focus on fighting a Titan.

The Recoilless Rifle counters one of the biggest threats the Bile Titan poses to you or your squad. As the insect’s name would suggest, the Titan sprays poisonous acid similar to the smaller Bile Spewers you could also fight during Terminid missions. As a result, you want to stay as far away from a Bile Titan as you can to avoid the large Area of Effect (AoE) of the poison it creates in Helldivers 2.

Strongest Stratagems For Bile Titans

Stratagems range from large collections of carpeting bombs to lasers shot directly from space onto a targeted area. The large size of Bile Titans makes them much easier to hit with Stratagems that cause smaller but far more damaging AoE effects in Helldivers 2. The strongest Stratagems you could use are the 50kg Bomb, the Orbital Laser, the Orbital Railgun, or the Eagle Cluster Bomb.

In the same way that you would take down Charger Terminids in Helldivers 2, breaking the defenses of a Bile Titan with powerful DPS should be your main focus. However, the only way these Stratagems can work is if you manage to keep a Titan in a location long enough for it to work. Unless your Stratagem has “Eagle” in its name, it only has a limited number of uses during a mission.

The Orbital Laser can only be used three times, and cannot be recharged like other Stratagems. While the Railgun has unlimited uses, it doesn’t deal nearly as much damage to a Bile Titan.

As you may see in a video by YouTube creator Elbethium, you might want to use a Stratagem as your opening gambit against a Bile Titan before it becomes hostile. Be prepared to stall the massive bug for some time as your other attacks like the Railgun recharge after this initial activation. Not all the Stratagems at your disposal in Helldivers 2 can kill a Titan in one hit unless every hit somehow lands on their target.

Bile Titan Attacks and Weaknesses

You might challenge a Bile Titan during certain missions that spawn individual targets on Challenging difficulty, but these creatures can appear randomly on any expedition set to Hard or above. No matter where you find a Bile Titan in Helldivers 2, the boss Terminid always has the same attacks and weaknesses. A Titan will always use one of these three attacks:

Attack Description How to Counter Fast Stab The Bile Titan raises one of its sharp legs into the air before quickly stabbing down into the ground where your character is. Dodge out of the way or move to a place either under the Titan’s belly or away from its legs. Stomping Limbs After a short roar, the Bile Titan stabs all of its legs into the ground one after the other, making every place near its feet a dangerous spot to be. Move far away from the Titan and use long-range weapons to attack it while this attack animation continues. Acid Spray The Bile Titan rears its head back before spewing a massive amount of poisonous acid around it in a large area. Get behind the mouth of the Bile Titan since this attack only covers the ground in front of the creature.

Keep in mind that your character’s Stamina should be managed carefully so you can run, dodge, or sprint out of the way of the Bile Titan’s attacks.

Every insect or robot fought in Helldivers 2 has a weak point somewhere on its body. For a Bile Titan, its mouth and small green spots on its body are vulnerable spots you should target with every shot you make on the Terminid. Getting out of range of the Acid Spray attack lets you open fire into the Titan’s wide open mouth with your strongest weapons on your character.

Other Tricks For Beating A Bile Titan

While you could easily approach a Bile Titan with a plan to hit its weak spots and dodge every attack, there are some exploits you could try to use instead. If you or any of your teammates die to the Terminid, you can respawn in a world via a hellpod. Hellpods can be aimed at enemies to deal huge damage from the impact of their landing and act in the same way as a Stratagem’s projectile.

Leaving the boundaries of a mission’s map in Helldivers 2 will cause the forces of Super Earth in space to fire at you as they label you a traitor. You can take advantage of this to lure a Bile Titan to the shots being fired at you, which have a chance to greatly damage the insect. Both of these strategies are a great risk to your safety, so you should only use them when you are incredibly desperate to eliminate a Titan.

It takes time for these plans to work unless you have great timing and coordination with other members of a multiplayer team. It is recommended to only try either option when you have other players to support these unorthodox antics. Anyone who finds a Bile Titan in Helldivers 2 may need to use every trick in the book to defeat these bugs in the name of democracy.
