10 Best Items To Sell For Gold In Palworld

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1000 words 5 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Literature Review

Selling excess items in Palworld is a great way to make extra Gold. Here are the ten best items that can be sold to make a large profit.

Every item in the game has a set value. This can change depending on the rarity or other properties, such as cooking food. Players can also sell Pals for Gold in Palworld to a Black Marketeer. Those who want to avoid this method will instead need to secure a healthy supply of items to trade to a Wandering Merchant. While you would think expensive goods would make the most profit, a user on Reddit with the username Borax_the_Mighty found otherwise. They found that cheaper, more abundant items are better to sell.

Where To Find Every Legendary Schematic In Palworld
Some of the strongest Legendary items in Palworld can only be crafted when you find their schematics dropped from powerful Pal bosses in the world.

10 Mega Shield Over 3000 Gold

The Mega Shield is the only armor on the list of the best items to sell in Palworld. This sells for 3120 Gold and can be easily crafted. You will need to collect 30 Palladium Fragments and 5 Ancient Civilization Parts. This Technology is unlocked at level 16 and will be one of the best sources of Gold in the early-game. Certain drops from Alpha Pals, such as Precious Pelts, sell for a high price. This makes gathering Ancient Civilization Parts for Mega Shields even more profitable.

9 Jam-Filled Bun Easily Repeatable 60 Gold

The Jam-Filled Bun is one of the earliest food items that requires more than one ingredient. This sells for 60 Gold a piece and can be easily farmed, literally. The recipe requires two Red Berries and one unit of Flour. For the former, you can either grow or gather the berries. The latter can be bought from a Wandering Merchant or created using the Mill to grind Wheat in Palworld. The recipe for a Jam-Filled Bun can be unlocked at level 17.

8 Cooked Meat Cheap & Fast Profit

While exploring the world and defeating Pals, you will acquire a large supply of various raw meats. These can all be cooked and can be sold for a large profit. Depending on the Pal defeated, the meat will have multiple recipes. Chikipi Poultry, for example, can be turned into Grilled Chikipi, Chikipi Sauté, or Fried Chikipi. If you have a Pal with Kindling level 2 or 3, cooking any quantity of meat will not be an issue.

7 Repair Kit Easy Resources Make For Quick Gold

One Repair Kit sells for 20 Gold. While this may not seem like a great way to make a profit, you will only need to collect five Fiber and five Stone. These resources are abundant and easy to obtain. The recipe can be unlocked at level 3, making this one of the best ways to make money early on in Palworld.

6 Tomatoes Start A Tomato Farm

Tomatoes are a food item that can be grown in a Tomato Plantation. The recipe can be unlocked at level 32 and requires Pals with the Planting, Watering, and Gathering suitabilities. Each Tomato only sells for 15 Gold a piece, but this can add up quickly with multiple farms.

5 Wheat Fast Growing Crop

Much like Tomatoes, Wheat is an easily renewable resource in Palworld. The recipe for a Wheat Plantation can be unlocked at level 15. This will also require Pals with the Planting, Watering, and Gathering suitabilites. One unit of Wheat sells for only 10 Gold, but this crop grows quickly. Players will soon find that they have an abundance of Wheat stockpiled.

4 Wool Easy Early-Game Gold & XP

Wool in Palworld is one of the first materials you may acquire when starting your adventure. This is because many of the early-game Pals, such as Lamball, will drop this resource Additionally, certain Pals assigned to the Ranch will passively drop Wool over time. This can lead to an overabundance of this material. Wool sells for 10 Gold, but don’t let this price fool you. Just 1,000 will net a profit of 10,000 with practically zero effort.

3 Baked Berries Grows The Fastest

Baked Berries sell for 10 Gold a piece, but are the fastest-growing crop in the game. The Berry Plantation can be unlocked at level 5. You will need Pals with the Planting, Watering, and Gathering suitabilites. While Red Berries grow quickly, players will still need to cook them. Fortunately, this becomes almost instant with a Kindling level 2 Pal and an upgraded Work Speed.

2 Pizza Make More With Cooking

One Pizza sells for 290 Gold to a Wandering Merchant. This is a more advanced recipe and requires an Electric Kitchen to make the item. This Technology can be unlocked at level 41. You will need four ingredients: mill, Red Berries, Tomatoes, and Flour. At this point in the game, you should have plenty of supplies and Pizza is a great way to use up any excess materials.

1 Nail Use Your Technologies To Make More Gold

One of the earliest Technologies you can unlock in Palworld is to make a Nail. This is most importantly used to craft a High-Quality Workbench. However, one Nail sells for 160 Gold to a Wandering Merchant, while only requiring one Ingot. This material is made in a Smelter using a Pal with the Kindling suitability. Players can make a large amount of Nails by simply mining Ore. Most players would assume that high-cost items would be the most profitable. However, that appears to not be the case in Palworld. Borak_the_Mighty on Reddit said they have played for 120 hours, crafted 140 items, and made 6.5 million Gold to settle on these 10 items.
