The Traitors US Season 3 Latest News

Although there isn’t much news surrounding the production of The Traitors US season 3, the series was recently announced to be picked up for a third season by Peacock’s parent company, NBCUniversal. In their press release, the company explained that after the massive success of The Traitors US season 2, they’d be choosing to renew the reality TV competition series for a third season. While typically renewals don’t happen until a season has been completed, The Traitors US was an exception after the series was so highly rated and viewed. The Traitors US season 3 has yet to begin casting, but will be doing so in the coming months.

The Traitors US Season 3 Potential Release Window

Although there isn’t any confirmation about when The Traitors US season 3 will be released, it will likely follow a similar schedule to the last two seasons of the series. The Traitors US and The Traitors UK are produced by the same company, and run concurrently in their own markets, meaning their releases are typically similarly timed. The Traitors US season 2 aired in January 2024, meaning it’s likely that The Traitors US season 3 will air sometime in January 2025. While the show could be on an earlier schedule, January appears to be a good month for the series to start.

The Traitors US Season 3 Potential Cast

Without much about The Traitors US season 3 publicized, there’s no current information about the upcoming cast. However, if The Traitors US season 2 is anything to go off of, the next season of the series will feature some incredibly heavy-hitting reality TV stars. During their press tour for the current season, the cast of The Traitors US season 2 shared who they’d like to see compete on an upcoming season. The front-runners were Big Brother’s Britney Haynes, The Challenge legend Wes Bergmann, and Survivor’s Ethan Zohn, so they could be featured on The Traitors US in the future.

Has The Traitors US Season 3 Started Filming?

Although The Traitors US season 3 has been renewed, it hasn’t started filming as of February 2024. The series typically films in the Scottish highlands later in the year, going into production around August or September of the year so the competition can take place. After the filming period has ended, The Traitors US is edited and gets itself ready to be released early the following year.
