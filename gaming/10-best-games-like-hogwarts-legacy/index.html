10 Best Games Like Hogwarts Legacy

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 2000 words 10 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Hogwarts Legacy has captured a wide audience, and there are plenty of games that can provide similar experiences. MMOs like RuneScape and The Elder Scrolls Online let players wield magic with multiplayer opportunities, which Hogwarts Legacy lacks. Open-world RPGs such as The Witcher 3 , Elden Ring , and more can provide the same sense of exploration while giving players plenty of sorcery to use in combat.

Literature Review

The Harry Potter prequel is a unique wizarding experience, but there are other games like Hogwarts Legacy that can give players even more to enjoy.

Between killing dark wizards and exploring the secrets of the castle, Hogwarts Legacy has created a truly magical experience, and so it’s no wonder that some players may be looking to pick up a similar game after finishing the Harry Potter prequel. Luckily, there are quite a few open-world RPGs that feature some form of magic or spell-casting for diehard witches and wizards. Among these many options, a select few stand out as the best of the bunch. Anyone wanting another adventure set in the universe of the Harry Potter wizarding world will find there are many spin-off games created to go along with many of the movies or just as an off-shoot of the novels. A Harry Potter: Quidditch Champions game has also been announced to be in development, although no release date has been given yet. Those who love the adventure they got in Hogwarts Legacy and are sad to think they have seen all it has to offer may be surprised to learn that more content updates are coming to Hogwarts Legacy in the summer of 2024. Until these new experiences are available for everyone, here are some games that just may satisfy the craving for the time being.

Hogwarts Legacy Review: A Living, Breathing, Wizarding World Hogwarts Legacy beautifully recreates the world of Harry Potter and fills it with quirky characters, tense combat, and a huge amount of collectibles.

10 RuneScape (2001) Available For PC

Publisher Jagex Genre Fantasy Mode MMORPG Platform PC

Regardless of whether they opt to play the newer RuneScape 3 or Old School RuneScape, players will find that magic is one of the most versatile and satisfying elements of the game, which can be useful both in and outside of combat settings. Players can choose to either focus on the use of runes or a magic weapon and can have fun with many different spells covering elemental magic or even blood or chaos spells. The freedom of choice in RuneScape should fulfill the fantasies of players looking for a game that leans further into the RPG elements in Hogwarts Legacy, and the social aspect provides another major point of attraction.

9 Elder Scrolls Online (2014) Available For PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, & Xbox Series X/S

Platforms Windows, macOS, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S Publishers Bethesda Softworks Mode Single player

Like RuneScape, The Elder Scrolls Online is an MMORPG that includes all types of magical abilities for mages to experiment with. The Sorcerer class in particular is extremely powerful, and when equipped the right way, can give players the feeling that they have chosen a dark wizard path in Hogwarts Legacy. The Elder Scrolls universe as a whole is ripe with in-game history and lore, and so those who enjoy the depth of Harry Potter’s world may find themselves slipping down a similar rabbit hole in this franchise. Elder Scrolls Online allows sorcerers to play alongside their friends and against other players - a feature that Hogwarts Legacy is missing.

8 Dragon Age: Inquisition (2014) Available On PC, PS4, & Xbox One

Franchise Dragon Age Platform(s) PlayStation 3 , PlayStation 4 , Xbox 360 , Xbox One , Microsoft Windows Released November 18, 2014 Developer(s) BioWare Publisher(s) Electronic Arts Genre(s) Action RPG ESRB M Sequel Dragon Age: Dreadwolf Prequel Dragon Age 2

With the Dungeons & Dragons origins of Dragon Age: Inquisition, it’s no surprise that this fantasy RPG boasts a detailed magic system. Although Hogwarts Legacy features the four Houses, Dragon Age: Inquisition provides a slightly more complex experience in that it allows players to choose between three distinct Mage classes with unique abilities and plenty of spells for witches and wizards to experiment with. Whether playing as a Knight-Enchanter, Rift Mage, or Necromancer, Dragon Age: Inquisition’s best Mage builds each harness the power of the arcane arts in a satisfying way.

7 Fable 3 (2010) Available For PC & Xbox 360

An older game, but one that is surprisingly similar to Hogwarts Legacy is Fable 3. Here, players trade their wands in for Will Gauntlets - magical bracers that allow the player to cast spells using nothing but their limbs. While the game doesn’t feature nearly as many spells as the Harry Potter universe does, it finds its footing in the various spell combinations that players can use to defeat their opposition on the fantastical continent of Albion. Fable 3’s ability to dual-wield elements like fire and shock may win some points in the hearts of wizarding world enthusiasts. Those who enjoy it will also have the upcoming Fable 4 to look forward to.

6 The Elder Scrolls 4: Oblivion (2006) Available For PC, Xbox 360, & PS3

The Mages Guild in The Elder Scrolls 4: Oblivion is quite similar to Hogwarts Legacy’s own eponymous school, sending players on a slow-burning, overarching journey to master the arcane arts. Morrowind may offer the best Elder Scrolls spell system, but the story-driven nature of Oblivion’s Mages Guild ultimately parallels Hogwarts Legacy’s main quest line. Sorcerers will need to climb the ranks as an Associate of the Mages Guild to become an Arch-Mage in Oblivion, and along the way, they’ll have the pleasure of learning a wide range of magics, from the restorative powers of Restoration to the chaotic Destruction spells.

5 The Legend Of Zelda: Breath Of The Wild (2017) Available For Nintendo Switch & Wii U

Franchise The Legend of Zelda Platform(s) Nintendo Switch Released March 3, 2017 Developer(s) Nintendo EPD Publisher(s) Nintendo Genre(s) Action-Adventure , Open-World , RPG ESRB E10+ Sequel The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Although The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild doesn’t allow players to cast spells per se, the experience in and of itself is a magical fantasy adventure. The kingdom of Hyrule is a gorgeous, open-world map full of secrets that’s just as exciting to explore as the Scottish Highlands in the Harry Potter prequel. Nintendo Switch enthusiasts of Hogwarts Legacy may find that, despite the lack of wands, BOTW and its sequel, Tears of the Kingdom, are fun adventures that hold a similar feeling.

Hogwarts Legacy On Switch Is 100% A Game For Muggles After multiple delays, Hogwarts Legacy has finally been released on the Switch. Though highly anticipated, it fails to deliver in a few key ways.

4 Elden Ring (2022) Available For PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, & Xbox Series X/S

Platform(s) PlayStation 4 , Xbox One , PlayStation 5 , Xbox Series X/S , PC Released February 25, 2022 Developer(s) FromSoftware Publisher(s) Bandai Namco Entertainment Genre(s) Action , RPG ESRB M

Elden Ring certainly doesn’t come without a challenge, as through and through, it’s a Soulsborne-style game with difficult bosses. But even though a magic-wielding Tarnished might not feel as powerful as Hogwarts Legacy’s protagonist initially, it’s well worth the trials and tribulations to master the arcane arts in the Lands Between. Not only are sorcerers among the most powerful classes in the game, they’re also woven deeply into Elden Ring’s complex and fascinating lore. Players will truly feel like a part of something larger, and in this way, Elden Ring can be quite like Hogwarts Legacy itself.

3 The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt (2015) Available For PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, & Nintendo Switch

Franchise The Witcher Platform(s) PlayStation 4 , PlayStation 5 , Xbox One , Xbox Series X/S , Nintendo Switch , Microsoft Windows , Steam , Epic Games Store Released May 19, 2015 Developer(s) CD Projekt Red Publisher(s) CD Projekt Genre(s) Action RPG , Open-World ESRB M Prequel The Witcher 2 Mode Single player

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt expands and improves upon the prior Witcher games, earning its place as one of the most critically acclaimed RPGs of all time. Just based on its immense, detailed open-world map and the impact of player choices on the story, anyone who enjoys Hogwarts Legacy will also likely find themselves entranced by the adventures of the Witcher Geralt and his sorceress friends. Combat in The Witcher 3 also incorporates just the right amount of magic in between swordsmanship, as players can cast a handful of powerful Signs and brew potions and alchemic concoctions.

2 Baldur’s Gate 3 (2023) Available For PC, PS5, & Xbox Series X/S

Franchise Baldur’s Gate Platform(s) macOS , Microsoft Windows , PlayStation 5 Released August 31, 2023 Developer(s) Larian Studios Publisher(s) Larian Studios Genre(s) RPG ESRB M Prequel Baldur’s Gate 2

Baldur’s Gate 3 is an RPG set entirely within the world of DnD, featuring rich narratives and lots of magical spells. Though not precisely the same as Hogwarts Legacy’s witches and wizards, BG3’s spellcasters are extremely fun classes to make use of the arcane powers of this mystical world. With many of the numerous DnD spells programmed into the game, players can easily craft a character to portray Harry Potter-themed mechanics if they so choose, and then imagine they are a graduated Hogwarts Legacy student.

10 Best Spells To Unlock In Baldur’s Gate 3 Players can wield all kinds of arcane magic in Baldur’s Gate 3. Here are some of the best spells for use during combat and exploration.

1 The Elder Scrolls 5: Skyrim (2011) Available For PC, PS3, PS4, PS5, Xbox 360, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, & Nintendo Switch

Franchise The Elder Scrolls Platform(s) Nintendo Switch , PlayStation 4 , PlayStation 5 , Xbox One , Microsoft Windows Released November 11, 2011 Developer(s) Bethesda Publisher(s) Bethesda Genre(s) Action Engine Creation Engine ESRB Blood and Gore, Intense Violence, Sexual Themes, Use of Alcohol

The Elder Scrolls 5: Skyrim has withstood the test of time, and any witch or wizard who has yet to play the game should not hesitate to give it a go (or replay it all over again). Wielding the best magic spells in Skyrim will require that the Dragonborn join the College of Winterhold, but after completing that quest line, players will be free to wreak havoc in any way they see fit among the beautiful mountains and expansive plains of the Nords’ homeland. Even after completing the game, Skyrim’s modding community has created no shortage of mods that bring even the most outlandish of arcane possibilities to fruition. From overpowered versions of previous Elder Scrolls spells, to magical abilities from completely separate franchises (including Harry Potter), Skyrim is the best game to play when looking for something like Hogwarts Legacy.
