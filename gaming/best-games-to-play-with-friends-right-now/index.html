Best Games To Play With Friends Right Now

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1600 words 8 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Games like Helldivers 2 and Palworld create funny scenarios with different playstyles and personalities in a group. The best games to play with friends can be hard to narrow down, so having a solid selection is key. Titles like Helldivers 2 , Super Smash Bros. Ultimate , and Mario Kart 8 Deluxe are among the best multiplayer games to play with friends right now.

Literature Review

Gaming is always best done with a friend or, better yet, a group of friends. Here are a few couch co-op and multiplayer games you can start playing.

Games like Helldivers 2 and Palworld show that even though a game can be played solo, adding in a group of different personalities and playstyles creates a ton of opportunities for funny scenarios. Whether it’s couch-coop games, online multiplayer shooters, or party games to play at gatherings, gaming doesn’t have to be an independent endeavor all the time. Gaming is fun, but playing with a group of friends is even more of a blast. With a lot of options out there in just about every genre, narrowing down the best games to play with friends can actually be a fairly daunting task. Sifting through game reviews or Reddit posts is no one’s form of fun, so having a solid selection is key, especially when one is trying to find a new game ASAP. These are the best video games to play with friends right now.

20 Best Online Games To Play With Friends Right Now
If you’ve been seeking that perfect title to play with your friends, you’ll need to investigate these solid multiplayer titles.

10 Helldivers 2 (2024) Platforms: PC, PS5

Franchise Helldivers Platform(s) PlayStation 5 , Microsoft Windows Released February 8, 2024 Developer(s) Arrowhead Game Studios AB Publisher(s) PlayStation Studios Genre(s) Action , Sci-Fi , Third-Person Shooter ESRB M

Helldivers 2 was a surprise smash success when it launched on February 8, 2024, and quickly amassed over one million sales in less than a month. The sequel to the top-down isometric original, Helldivers 2, takes the same story but places it in a third-person view in new vivid and terrifying interplanetary environments. With up to four-player local and online co-op, the party must take on different missions to dispatch ravenous aliens and cybernetic threats in this intense and frenetic action shooter.

Helldivers 2 Review: “A Refreshingly Fun Power Fantasy”
Helldivers 2 is just about the best-case scenario for a live-service game, and its show-stopping gameplay delivers incredibly visceral moments.

9 Super Smash Bros. Ultimate (2018) Platforms: Nintendo Switch

Franchise Super Smash Bros. Platform(s) Nintendo Switch Released December 7, 2018 Developer(s) Bandai Namco Studios , Sora Ltd. Publisher(s) Nintendo Genre(s) Action , Fighting ESRB E10+

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is the newest game in a franchise that popularized the arena fighter genre and is arguably one of the best games in the franchise beyond the highly acclaimed Super Smash Bros. Melee released for the GameCube. Maintaining a 93 critical review score on Metacritic, the title is undoubtedly one of the most fun games to play with a group of friends. With up to 8-person multiplayer, people can take control of iconic Nintendo characters like Mario, Link, Samus, and others.

8 Palworld (2024) Platforms: PC, Xbox One & X/S

Platform(s) PC , Xbox One , Xbox Series X/S Released January 19, 2024 Developer(s) Pocket Pair Inc. Publisher(s) Pocket Pair Inc. Genre(s) Crafting , Open-World , RPG , Survival ESRB T

When it comes to meteoric rises in terms of popularity, there are few examples that can match the acceleration of Palworld. Released from a small Japanese gaming studio named Pocketpair, the creature-collecting survival title has gained over 19 million players since its launch on January 19, 2024. Although the game is still in an Early Access state, it currently maintains a “Very Positive” rating on Steam. People can team up in four-player online co-op, though dedicated servers can host 32 players in the same world. The Palworld roadmap outlines online PvP, which may indicate even bigger servers in the future of Palworld.

Palworld Preview: “For All Its Faults, It Teeters on the Edge of Early Greatness”
Palworld wears its many inspirations on both sleeves, but this monster-catching open-world survival sim could have some serious legs.

7 Deep Rock Galactic (2020) Platforms: PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One & X/S

Though Deep Rock Galactic has been out since May of 2020, continuous updates from the game’s developers, Ghost Ship Games, have kept the dwarven co-op FPS title feel fresh. In DRG, a group of one to four mining dwarves takes on the game’s vicious creatures in procedurally-generated caves while overcoming a series of challenges. The game requires coordination and teamwork, making it the perfect game to play with friends right now. With new seasonal events and additional missions added for free, it boasts one of the best values for any game.

6 Jackbox Party Pack 9 (2022) Platforms: PC, Mac, PS4, PS5, Nintendo Switch, Xbox One, Xbox One & X/S

Though Jackbox Party Pack 9 isn’t the latest iteration of the game — that would be Jackbox Party Pack 10 released in the latter half of 2023, number nine in the franchise stands out as one of the better entries for this party game. Jackbox Party Pack 9 is a compilation of fun party mini-games and supports up to 10 players, depending on the game. With classics like Fibbage 4, Quixort, and Roomerang, there’s a variety included in the game for just about any friend group.

5 Mario Kart 8 Deluxe (2017) Platforms: Nintendo Switch

Platform(s) Wii U , Nintendo Switch Released May 30, 2014 Developer(s) Nintendo Publisher(s) Nintendo Genre(s) Racing ESRB E

The Mario Kart franchise has been an iconic mainstay among multiple generations, having originally been introduced by Super Mario Kart in 1992 for the SNES. Mario Kart 8 Deluxe is the latest iteration of the franchise, improving and learning from decades of experience and creating a modern title that’s a blast to play with up to 4 players on a single Switch or up to 8 players using Wireless Play. With brand-new mechanics, seasonal updates, and the addition of new tracks, racers, and karts, Mario Kart 8 is one of the best multiplayer games to get for the Nintendo Switch, even though it was originally released in 2017.

4 Lethal Company (2023) Platforms: PC

Platform(s) PC Released October 23, 2023 Developer(s) Zeekerss Publisher(s) Zeekerss Genre(s) Comedy , Survival Horror , Sci-Fi ESRB Rating Pending

is an indie title that was released into Early Access in 2023 and quickly gained a cult following. Currently sitting with an “Overwhelmingly Positive” rating on Steam, the game was created by a single developer by the name of Zeekerss. Lethal Company is a first-person co-op horror game where players must enter dangerous locales to collect items for an evil overlord Company. Upon exploring these locations, rabid aliens and hazardous encounters are around every corner. Though the aesthetic is overall pretty cartoonish, the thrills and scares packed within the game are still fun for friends of any age.

3 Valorant (2020) Platforms: PC

Platform(s) Windows Released June 2, 2020 Developer(s) Riot Games Publisher(s) Riot Games Genre(s) FPS ESRB T

For those looking to get a bit more competitive with their friends, Valorant is a solid 5v5 FPS akin to iconic predecessors like Counter-Strike. With a roster of 23 distinct Agents with a wide variety of playstyles and abilities, as well as an active esports community, the title has a pretty steep learning curve but certainly requires a tight-knit group of friends to truly master.

2 Mario Party Superstars (2021) Platforms: Nintendo Switch

Platform(s) Nintendo Switch Released October 29, 2021 Developer(s) NDcube Publisher(s) Nintendo Genre(s) Action , Party Game ESRB E

Arguably, one of the best interpretations of a board game made into a video game is Mario Party. Mario Party Superstars is the latest iteration in the series of games that began with the first game that was released in 1998 for the Nintendo 64. Much like the previous entries, people roll a die to make their way around a virtual game board, with a ton of fun mini-games and random occurrences that make the title a lot of fun at parties. With up to four players on a single Switch, the game can elicit laughs or full-blown arguments depending on how each match turns out.

1 Baldur’s Gate 3 (2023) Platforms: PC, Mac, PS5, Xbox X/S

Platform(s) macOS , Microsoft Windows , PlayStation 5 Released August 31, 2023 Developer(s) Larian Studios Publisher(s) Larian Studios Genre(s) RPG ESRB M

Based on Dungeons and Dragons 5e, Baldur’s Gate 3 is the perfect game for a group of friends looking to scratch that RPG itch. Acclaimed by just about every gaming publication, including Screen Rant’s BG3 review, the game has won a number of awards and has taken the world by storm since it was originally released. With constant updates, including the most recent Patch 6, which overhauled and fixed a number of the game’s bugs, the title continues to get better over time. In BG3, players can team up via couch-co-op on the PlayStation 5 or via online multiplayer for the Xbox and PC into groups of four to take on the darkest monsters of Faerûn. Whether it’s the high-octane action of Helldivers 2, the interesting and compelling story of Baldur’s Gate 3, or the random fun of Palworld, there’s a ton to choose from.
