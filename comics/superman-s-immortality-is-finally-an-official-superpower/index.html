Superman s Immortality Is Finally an Official Superpower

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 600 words 3 minutes 
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Superman’s Chinese counterpart, Super-Man, is brought back to life thanks to Clark Kent’s secret death-defying life force. Superman’s secret identity has caused psychic attacks for anyone who learns it, almost killing Perry White. Superman’s ability to defy death and his extended lifespan suggest that he is virtually unkillable and potentially immortal.

Literature Review

If it were ever in doubt how powerful Superman is, his ally Super-Man confirms it by using Clark Kent’s power to escape an untimely death.

Warning! Spoilers ahead for Action Comics #1059!DC Comics has once and for all confirmed that Superman is an immortal being. The Man of Steel’s Chinese counterpart Super-Man nearly buys the farm after a psychic attack puts him in grave danger. But Kenan Kong manages to return to life all thanks to Clark Kent’s secret death-defying life force.

In Action Comics #1059’s story “Secret Identity Part Two” by Gene Luen Yang and Viktor Bogdanovic, Super-Man is in a state between life and death thanks to the mental attack brought on by learning Superman’s secret identity. In this state, however, Kenan meets his old mentor, I-Ching.

After fooling Super-Man into believing he was actually dead, I-Ching reminds Kenan that his American counterpart has conquered death numerous times before and Kenan’s life force is intrinsically tied with Superman’s. Kenan guides his qi through the Eight Trigrams of Bagua as he did when he first became Super-Man and comes back to life, much to the relief of the Super-Family.

Super-Man Just Secretly Confirmed Superman’s Immortality

In a moment that stunned the world, Superman revealed his identity as Clark Kent. Heroes and villains alike were shocked, but none more than Lex Luthor. After stewing for a bit, Lex decided to put the genie back in the bottle by using Manchester Black’s psychic abilities to make the world forget Superman’s real name. However, now anytime someone is confronted with that information, they experience a strong psychic attack, something that nearly killed Superman’s old boss Perry White. Unfortunately, Kenan found that out the hard way when he accidentally discovered Superman’s identity, triggering a psychic attack in his mind.

When Kenan gained his powers, he was infused with Superman’s qi, or life force. This gave him Clark’s powers (though Kenan had to master his abilities by channeling his own qi through the Eight Trigrams). During Kenan’s brief time with his former mentor, I-Ching raised a good point: Superman has defied death plenty of times, including his infamous revival after fighting Doomsday. While it’s never been officially listed as one of Clark’s powers, the event presented here is one of the strongest arguments that Clark is immortal. After all, if Super-Man’s power is strong enough to prevent him from crossing into the afterlife, then so is Superman’s.

DC Confirms That Superman is Virtually Unkillable

Superman’s longevity has never really been in doubt as alternate Earths and potential futures have shown Clark easily living centuries if not thousands of years. But Kenan’s near-death experience highlights an important aspect of Superman’s abilities. It’s not just that Clark has an extended lifespan, it’s that Superman has the power to will himself back to life. A shocking discovery, sure, but a welcome one. This means no matter what an enemy throws at Clark, no matter how gravely he’s injured, Superman is just too powerful to cross into the afterlife.

Action Comics #1059 is on sale now from DC Comics.
