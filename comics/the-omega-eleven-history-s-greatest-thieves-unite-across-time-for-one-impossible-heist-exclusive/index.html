SUMMARY

“The Omega Eleven” Kickstarter campaign is now live! Don’t miss out on this thrilling sci-fi comic series featuring a time-heist with classic characters. Award-winning creative team James Aquilone and Zac Atkinson bring readers this five-issue epic, with stunning covers by various artists. Monstrous Books, known for their horror works, is publishing “The Omega Eleven,” showcasing their focus on science fiction and adventure. Support the campaign now!

Literature Review

Screen Rant is excited to exclusively announce the start of the Kickstarter campaign for Monstrous Book’s The Omega Eleven, featuring a time-heist.

Screen Rant is excited to announce the Kickstarter campaign for The Omega Eleven has officially started. This comic series features a time-heist involving classic characters readers shouldn’t pass up. It features an award-winning creative team and a thrilling sci-fi adventure.

The Omega Eleven has the creative team of James Aquilone and Zac Atkinson. It will be a five-issue sci-fi epic. There will be covers from various artists, including Zac Atkinson, J.K. Woodward, and Dave Acosta. The series is slated for publication by Monstrous Books, which is owned by Aquilone and was founded in 2022.

The publisher has a focus on horror works, releasing novels, anthologies, and comics centered on the genre. However, it also has science fiction and adventure offerings among its publications. The Omega Eleven will be the latest comic project from Monstrous Books.

A Classics-Inspired Time Heist Comes To Comics In The Omega Eleven

In addition to the debut issue of The Omega Eleven, this Kickstarter campaign also includes the third issue of Monstrous, a horror comic magazine from Monstrous Books. Doctor Omega, the protagonist of the series, will be gathering together the greatest thieves in history to pull off an impossible heist. The heist, should it succeed, will save the Omniverse. It will require travel to famous literary locales and notable thieves, like the Artful Dodger, who is still a teenager in this upcoming series. The synopsis is as follows:

The Omega Eleven #1 - The Merlin Job follows Doctor Omega, the character who inspired Doctor Who, and his companion, 14-year-old Jack Dawkins, aka The Artful Dodger, as they travel to Camelot to steal the Philosopher’s Stone from Merlin.

James Aquilone is an award-winning comic creator, who has won numerous awards. These include a Bram Stoker Award, a Rondo Hatton Award, a Scribe Award, and an Imadjinn Award for the graphic novel, Kolchak: The Night Stalker - 50th Anniversary. This graphic novel is an anthology featuring numerous creators telling stories centered around the titular hero, who is a newspaper reporter and was the protagonist of a television series from 1974 of the same name. Zac Atkinson has previously worked on comic titles, including Young Justice, House of Whispers, Teen Titans, Billy Batson and the Magic of Shazam!, and Transformers.

The Omega Eleven Creators Are Ready To Deliver A Pulpy Adventure

Series artist Zac Atkinson says, “THE OMEGA ELEVEN is a project I’ve been dreaming about forever! James and I are manifesting the craziest adaptations of history’s most classic pulp heroes and thieves. It’s Doctor Who turned up to Ocean’s 11.”

Series writer James Aquilone adds:

THE OMEGA ELEVEN is packed with amazing characters from just about every corner of pulpdom and beyond. Zac’s artwork for the project has blown me away. You can see the love and care in every stroke of his pen. It’s been a blast creating this world. Readers of THE OMEGA ELEVEN are in for one wild ride through space and time.
