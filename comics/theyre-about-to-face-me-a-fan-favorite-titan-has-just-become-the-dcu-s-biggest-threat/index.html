Theyre About to Face Me” A Fan-Favorite Titan Has Just Become the DCU s Biggest Threat

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 700 words 3 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Raven’s dark side, Doctor Hate, is now running free and has taken over her life, leaving the true Raven trapped in a gem. The Titans are in danger as Doctor Hate, working for Amanda Waller, plans to destroy them one by one. The team must discover the truth behind the swapped Raven or they could be in serious danger.

Literature Review

The Titans might have survived “Beast World” but the team has an unexpected threat in their midst as an ally is replaced by an evil doppelganger.

Warning! Spoilers ahead for Titans: Beast World #6!“Beast World” comes to its game-changing conclusion, leading one Titan to become the next biggest threat to the DC Universe. The latest crisis saw things go from bad to worse as people across the world turned into animal-human hybrids. However, one unexpected development proves that things are about to get a whole lot worse.

In Titans: Beast World #6 by Tom Taylor, Ivan Reis, Lucas Meyer, and Eduardo Panisca, Raven is still in shock after discovering that her dark half has manifested as the evil Doctor Hate. Raven spirits Nightwing away so she can properly fight her doppelganger.

Raven returns from her battle, having successfully imprisoned her demonic side in her gem once more. However, the story ends on a shocking cliffhanger when it’s revealed that the evil Raven is still free and has assumed her counterpart’s life, leaving the true Raven trapped in the gem with no one the wiser.

Raven Has Been Trapped and Her Dark Side is Running Free

After a confrontation with the demon lord Neron, Raven was warned that a part of her was missing. This ominous message came back to haunt her in “Beast World” in the form of Amanda Waller’s newest asset, Doctor Hate. Hate was a mysterious individual Amanda Waller recruited to advance her anti-metahuman agenda armed with the Helmet of Hate and the Nightmare Stone. Hate was also responsible for mind-wiping Gar Logan, leaving him stuck as the rampaging Garro. It wasn’t until Raven personally confronted Waller and Hate that she discovered that Doctor Hate was secretly a manifestation of her evil side.

While it was revealed that the Titans would make it out of “Beast World” alive, one curious thing mentioned in the solicitations for Titans #9 hinted at a threat within the team. Now it’s known the threat the team is about to face off with is the evil Raven. Waller has been wanting to exterminate the Titans for some time now, and now she’s got an operative who’s replaced one of the team’s most trusted members. If the team doesn’t discover the truth behind the swapped Raven, the Titans could be in serious danger.

Doctor Hate Could Destroy the Titans For Good

Ordinarily, the Titans would probably be able to suss out something being wrong with Raven. But they’re going to have their hands full with the world turning against them post-“Beast World”. While Nighting, Starfire and the rest of the team deal with the fallout, Doctor Hate can make her moves and do what she set out to do. One by one, she can take out each of the Titans, or subtly manipulate them against each other, giving Waller all the time she needs to take the team down. Unless the Titans realize what’s going on, the evil Raven is going to be their undoing.

Titans: Beast World #6 is available now from DC Comics.
