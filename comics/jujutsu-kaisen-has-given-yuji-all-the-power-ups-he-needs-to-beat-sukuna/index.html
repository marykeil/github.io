Jujutsu Kaisen Has Given Yuji All The Power-Ups He Needs To Beat Sukuna

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 700 words 4 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Yuji Itadori has learned how to effectively utilize reverse cursed technique, giving him the ability to heal his own wounds and potentially overcome Sukuna. The Executioner’s Sword, a valuable weapon given to Yuji, had no effect on Sukuna, possibly due to its creator’s death. However, Yuji’s proficiency in reverse cursed technique compensates for this setback. There is a mysterious dormant technique within Yuji’s body that even Sukuna perceives as a significant threat. The full extent of Yuji’s power and cursed technique will be revealed in future battles.

Literature Review

Yuji has obtained two incredible new advantages in Jujutsu Kaisen that may help him defeat Ryomen Sukuna for good.

Warning: SPOILERS for chapter #248 of Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuji Itadori has grown greatly in strength and abilities throughout the course of Jujutsu Kaisen, and the latest chapters have given him more power-ups, including the ability to use reverse cursed technique. Even when Ryomen Sukuna hit him with a powerful Cleave attack in chapter #247 of the manga, he remained alive. A very similar attack was able to effectively kill Gojo Satoru in chapter #236 of Jujutsu Kaisen.

So many other Jujutsu sorcerers have failed against Sukuna, but Itadori seems to have a chance at beating the King of Curses. A few factors could contribute to Yuji’s newfound success against the terrifying King of Curses in Jujutsu Kaisen.

Chapter #248 of Jujutsu Kaisen makes it clear that Itadori has learned how to effectively utilize reverse cursed technique and incorporate it into his style. Together with the (still unconfirmed) acquisition of the Blood Manipulation cursed technique, it’s clear that Yuji now has all the power-ups he needed to fight against his nemesis.

Yuji Now Can Use Reverse Cursed Technique Reverse Cursed technique allows jujutsu sorcerers to heal their own wounds

Another possible boost to Yuji’s abilities seemed to be the use of an incredible weapon previously owned by the lawyer/sorcerer Hiromi Higuruma, the Executioner’s Sword. Even if he was killed by Sukuna in chapter #247, Higuruma still threw the Executioner’s Sword to Yuji before he died, offering him a valuable tool to use. Unfortunately, chapter #248 revealed that the sword had no effect on Sukuna, possibly because it dissipated after its creator’s death.

Fortunately, the chapter also confirmed that Yuji is now able to use reverse cursed technique very effectively to heal his wounds. It is very likely that he was taught to utilize reverse cursed energy right before this particular battle with the King of Curses. Choso and Kamo, both talented Blood Manipulation users, mentioned training with Itadori in chapter #244 of the manga, and Yuji may have learned more than Blood Manipulation.

Yuji’s Growth Surprises Even Sukuna

There are also hints in Jujutsu Kaisen that there is a mysterious technique laying dormant in Yuji’s body, waiting to be unleashed. A lot is unknown surrounding Itadori’s full capabilities, and Sukuna’s comments in this chapter confirm that his former vessel is a bigger threat to him than he admitted earlier. The extent of Yuji’s Cursed Technique will likely become clear in future battles and more of his power will be disclosed. Although Sukuna, the King of Curses, seems still unbeatable, Yuji’s recent power-ups, along with Yuta Okkotsu’s arrival on the battlefield, are the best hopes for the sorcerers’ eventual victory over Sukuna in Jujutsu Kaisen.
