Top 10 Most Powerful Birds of Prey Members in DC History (Ranked Weakest to Strongest)

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1800 words 9 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

The Birds of Prey, led by genius hacker Oracle and secret agent Black Canary, tackle street-level crime and often align with useful villains. The team calls on ultra-skilled martial artists and stealth operatives, including master assassin Lady Shiva and alien warrior Zealot. However, the most powerful members are the New God Big Barda and the mystic power thief Black Alice.

Literature Review

The Birds of Prey are at home bringing justice to the streets, but they can call on immortal aliens, warrior gods, and DC’s most powerful magic user.

Answering to no-one, the Birds of Prey are a force of nature when it comes to the DC Universe’s street-level crime. Made up of some of the most capable ass-kickers in the publisher’s roster, the Birds of Prey do what other teams won’t - often finding themselves aligned with useful villains in temporary partnerships. Characterized by the leadership of genius hacker Oracle and secret agent Black Canary, the Birds of Prey have fought everyone from super-genius the Calculator to the world’s deadliest assassin Lady Shiva. To tackle their diverse rogues gallery, the Birds of Prey often call on ultra-skilled martial artists and stealth operatives to get the job done. However, every so often, they also need a dose of raw power. Here are the ten heaviest hitters to serve on the Birds of Prey roster.

10 Oracle Is DC’s Master Hacker and Peerless Information Broker Birds of Prey: Black Canary/Oracle #1 (1996) by Chuck Dixon, Gary Frank, John Dell, Sherilyn Van Valkenburgh, Jamison, Matt Hollingsworth, and Albert DeGuzman

Barbara Gordon is a founder of the Birds of Prey and has played an invaluable role in defining the team. As Oracle, she directed and provided communications and intel for the Birds of Prey. Her information, leadership, and huge list of contacts helped the Birds get off the ground, giving Barbara renewed purpose after she was forced to retire as Batgirl. However, Barbara wasn’t just the voice in the real heroes’ ears - Oracle has used her skills to battle Brainiac and defeat the Calculator, while also holding her own in combat. There is no better hacker in the world - which counts for even more in a universe characterized by advanced technology and sci-fi villains. In adventures that are often about uncovering secrets or keeping vital information out of the wrong hands, Oracle is often the MVP.

9 Lady Shiva Is the Deadliest Woman in the World Birds of Prey #92 (2006) by Gail Simone, Paulo Siqueira, Robin Riggs, Hi-Fi Design, and Pat Brosseau

Lady Shiva is the single deadliest fighter on the planet, to the point that even Batman can’t beat Shiva in hand-to-hand combat. She is known as the most deadly woman in the world, and it’s an apt description. She joined the Birds of Prey as part of a deal with Black Canary to try and see the world from her point of view. Black Canary hoped that this would soften Lady Shiva and maybe give her a purpose in life other than seeking out death. While Shiva returned to her villain life eventually, she will always have a strong bond to the team as a frequent ally and enemy.

8 Cassandra Cain’s Batgirl Is DC’s Most Skilled Fighter of All Time Birds of Prey #1 (2023) by Kelly Thompson, Leonardo Romero, Jordie Bellaire and Clayton Cowles

There are tons of amazing fighters in the DC Universe, but Cassandra Cain’s Batgirl is the greatest of them all. While she has vowed never to take a life after her past as a child assassin, Batman has admitted he couldn’t beat her in a fight and she has even defeated Lady Shiva (her biological mother.) It doesn’t matter how many dangerous enemies go up against Cassandra, she always comes out on top. Trained to read body language as her primarily form of communication, Batgirl can predict what other fighters will do, allowing her to replicate near-superhuman feats, like dodging incoming bullets before they’re even fired.

Even Nightwing Admits Batgirl/Cassie Cain is Gotham’s Best Fighter Nightwing is a versatile and effective fighter along with the other members of the Bat-Family, but Cassie Cain aka Batgirl is better than all of them!

7 Black Canary Is an Expert Fighter Whose Scream Can Kill Birds of Prey: Black Canary/Oracle #1 by Chuck Dixon, Gary Frank, John Dell, Sherilyn Van Valkenburgh, Jamison, Matt Hollingsworth, and Albert DeGuzman

While Oracle is in the clock tower running the Birds of Prey, they need a combat leader out in the field. This job often falls to Black Canary, who uses her covert ops expertise to get things done. Black Canary is one of DC’s best hand-to-hand fighters and her Canary Cry can even take down Superman. Dinah’s power gives her an advantage over the other human fighters on this list, although Lady Shiva has confirmed she’s one of the world’s greatest combatants even without it.

6 Zealot Is an Immortal Alien Super-Soldier Birds of Prey #1 (2023) by Kelly Thompson, Leonardo Romero, Jordie Bellaire, and Clayton Cowles

Zannah of Khera is a Kherubim alien and has increased strength, agility and healing because of her species, as well as outright immortality. While in the Birds of Prey, Zealot has also demonstrated magical abilities, such as enchanting her blade to harm Amazons but not kill them, no matter how lethal her strikes are. Zannah may not be the friendliest or most talkative member of the team, but there’s no denying how dangerous she is in a fight.

5 Poison Ivy May As Well Be a God Birds of Prey #1 (2011) by Duane Swierczynski, Jesus Saiz, Nei Ruffino, and Carlos M. Mangual

The Birds of Prey have had a ton of different members over the years and not all of them have been strictly heroes. Way back in the New 52, Poison Ivy ended up joining the Birds of Prey as well. This was in part due to Amanda Waller manipulating Black Canary to unknowingly act as her agent. Nevertheless, Ivy was an immensely powerful member of the team. Poison Ivy’s control over nature has made her an avatar of the Green on par with Swamp Thing in the past and has made her a literal Goddess at times. At her weakest, Ivy is a genius scientist who can develop deadly poisons and mind-bending hallucinogenics on the fly - at her strongest, she’s a god of the natural world.

Poison Ivy Just Set Up Her Ultimate Ending as a DC God Poison Ivy’s new powers and a near-death experience hint that when she dies, she’ll become an avatar of DC’s Green, aka the future Swamp Thing.

4 Vixen Can Summon the Powers of Any Animal - Including Other Heroes Birds of Prey #69 (2004) by Gail Simone, Ron Adrian, Rob Lea, Hi-Fi Design, and Jared K. Fletcher

While Vixen wasn’t on the team long, the Birds of Prey have just invited her back onto the roster, showing they see her as a dependable ally. Vixen can use her powers to tap into the abilities of any animal. This allows her to be as strong as an elephant or as fast as a cheetah. She’s even been able to use her powers to steal the abilities of other metahumans. This versatility makes her a surprisingly powerful member of the team, to the point that the first time she fought Superman, she not only managed to knock him down with her strength, but even slashed his invulnerable skin with her claws.

3 Powergirl Was a Superman-Level Recruit Birds of Prey #100 (2007) by Gail Simone, Nicola Scott, Doug Hazlewood, Hi-Fi Design, and Travis Lanham

There are very few members of the Birds of Prey who are stronger than Powergirl. As an alternate universe version of Supergirl, Powergirl has all the powers of a Kryptonian. This makes her extremely dangerous, but her teamwork skills weren’t anywhere near as impressive. Powergirl was the original partner to Oracle, but their partnership fell through due to interpersonal tension. This resulted in Powergirl leaving the team. While she was invited back in #100, she stated she wouldn’t return until hell froze over, and she hasn’t been on the roster since.

2 Big Barda Is a Warrior New God Respected by Darkseid Birds of Prey #100 (2007) by Gail Simone, Nicola Scott, Doug Hazlewood, Hi-Fi Design, and Travis Lanham

The absolute strongest member of the Birds of Prey is without a doubt Big Barda. Big Barda is a New God from Apokolips and was the leader of Darkseid’s elite Female Furies. She has gone through the most vicious training imaginable and decided to use that training for good. She joined the Birds of Prey after helping Batman and Superman save Supergirl from Darkseid. Recently, she was invited to rejoin the team where Big Barda fought Wonder Woman blow for blow during a mission. Durable, incredibly strong, and immortal, Big Barda combines her godly physique with tactical genius and unsurpassable combat acumen.

1 Black Alice Is DC’s Most Powerful Magic User Birds of Prey #76 (2005) by Gail Simone, Joe Prado, Ed Benes, Hi-Fi Design, and Phil Balsman

Easily DC’s most powerful magic user, Black Alice has the power to steal the mystical abilities of DC’s most prominent magic beings, including Zatanna, Doctor Fate, and even the Spectre. Not only does Alice get access to all their powers (eventually at any distance), but they’re even depowered in the meantime, meaning she can take out major heroes like Wonder Woman and Raven with little more than a split-second decision. Alice’s biggest weakness was that she was young and inexperienced, but even at her greenest she was a god-tier operator who could down normal enemies with a snap of her fingers. Having served with the Birds and the Secret Six, Black Alice will soon return as a member of Amanda Waller’s ‘Dream Team’ Suicide Squad. While the Birds of Prey may not be a household name on the same level as the Justice League, they’re consistent fan favorites with a fascinating roster of surprising members - many of them the acknowledged superiors of DC’s most iconic heroes. With DC’s most powerful magic user, the deadliest woman in the world, and several members who have taken Superman down to the wire, the Birds of Prey are powerful heroes who should never be underestimated.
