Hulk vs Ghost Rider Is Too Close to Call, As a Fiery New Weapon Evens the Odds

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 800 words 4 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Ghost Rider’s hellfire bazooka closes the power gap between him and the Hulk, making their fights more evenly matched. The Hulk, possessed by War Devil, turns on his sidekick Charlie, but is knocked out by Ghost Rider’s hellfire bazooka. Previous fights between the Hulk and Ghost Rider have shown that they are evenly matched, and now Ghost Rider finally has an attack that can match the Hulk’s raw strength.

Literature Review

The Hulk is currently facing off against Ghost Rider once again, only this time, Ghost Rider has an all-new weapon that has greatly evened the odds.

Warning! This article contains spoilers for The Incredible Hulk #8 It would be difficult to find two Marvel Comics heroes more powerful than Ghost Rider and Hulk, with one wielding supernatural abilities fueled by hellfire and the other being strong enough to crack continents with a single stomp. Therefore, they’re already pretty evenly matched (with their previous bout in World War Hulk cementing that fact), and Ghost Rider’s fiery new weapon just closed the power gap between them even more.

In a preview for The Incredible Hulk #8 by Phillip Kennedy Johnson and Nic Klein, the Hulk and his new sidekick, Charlie - a runaway teen from an abusive home - have temporarily taken refuge with a community of migrant workers, resting after their multiple encounters with the monsters hellbent on killing them. Hulk has been protecting both of them by tearing every monster who challenges them to shreds - but now, he’s become the monster (even more so than usual).

After the Hulk is possessed by the elder beast known only as War Devil, he takes his most monstrous form, and even tries to kill Charlie herself. Until, the Ghost Rider that protects this community shoots the Hulk with a hellfire bazooka, instantly knocking the Green Goliath out of the fight. While the Hulk is shown slowly recuperating moments after he’s shot, this weapon proves strong enough to knock him on his back, which is a level of firepower Ghost Rider did not have during the last fight.

Ghost Rider Eliminates the One Advantage Hulk Had in Their ‘VS’ Fights

The last time Ghost Rider and the Hulk fought was during the aforementioned World War Hulk event, where the Hulk returns to Earth after his planetary exile to exact vengeance upon those who sent him away to begin with. Ghost Rider eventually realized the Hulk was justified in his thirst for vengeance, and stepped away from the fight entirely. However, before Ghost Rider made that call, he and Hulk battled it out in a brutal manner. And even with all of Ghost Rider’s otherworldly power, the Hulk had one obvious advantage: his strength.

Ghost Rider may have had the power of hellfire at his disposal, with near-invulnerability to boot, but the Hulk’s raw strength was simply unparalleled. Hulk was throwing Ghost Rider around like a rag doll, and no level of attack that Ghost Rider could muster even came close to matching the Hulk. But now, with this hellfire bazooka, that has completely changed, and has effectively evened the odds between them.

Even Now, Hulk & Ghost Rider Fights Can Never Have a Clear Winner

Hulk vs Ghost Rider matchups are fairly rare in Marvel Comics, and that’s assuredly due to the fact that their fights are classic cases of an unstoppable force meeting an immovable object. Ghost Rider can unleash all manner of hellfire attacks on the Hulk - including and especially this new one - and while they may slow him down, they can’t kill him for good. Similarly, the Hulk can beat Ghost Rider into a crater in the Earth - just as he did in World War Hulk - yet the demonic antihero will still get up and ride away like nothing happened.

The truth of the matter is that their fights will never have a true winner. However, this new fiery weapon wielded by Ghost Rider at least makes the battle itself feel more even, as Ghost Rider finally has an attack that can match the Hulk’s raw strength - something he did not have during their last matchup.

The Incredible Hulk #8 by Marvel Comics is available January 31, 2024.
