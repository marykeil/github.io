The Bachelor 10 Women From Joey Graziadei s Season We Want To See On Bachelor In Paradise, Ranked

SUMMARY

Sydney Gordon’s dramatic behavior on The Bachelor makes her a potential fit for Bachelor In Paradise. Madina Alam’s age insecurity could be resolved on Bachelor In Paradise with women closer to her age. Allison Hollinger’s genuine connection with Joey and her transparency would make her a great candidate for Bachelor In Paradise.

Literature Review

The Bachelor season 28’s cast may shrink each week as Joey Graziadei chooses who to bring forward, but who could appear on Bachelor In Paradise?

The Bachelor season 28 has introduced both Joey Graziadei and Bachelor Nation to an incredible cast of women, many of whom may be great on Bachelor In Paradise. With Joey having to narrow his group down each week, Bachelor Nation knows that only one will wind up with him in the end. Despite only one lucky lady winning Joey’s heart, the rest of the cast may still be in luck, as Bachelor Nation contestants typically find their way to other series in the franchise, like Bachelor In Paradise. With some of the most successful Bachelor Nation relationships coming from the beachside series, it’s a possibility for the women he eliminates. After a disappointing outing with Bachelor In Paradise season 9 where none of the couples wound up staying together after the show, the series is likely looking for some more compatible candidates. While Joey’s group of eliminated contestants may not have been a fit for the tennis instructor, it’s possible they could work with some of the men who have come to be part of Bachelor Nation, especially those who were recently on The Bachelorette season 20 with Charity Lawson. Many would love the chance to look for love on Bachelor In Paradise, but some may fair better than others.

12 Sydney Gordon The Unexpectedly Dramatic Contestant

Although Sydney Gordon didn’t get a lot of screen time in the premiere episode of The Bachelor season 28, she was a major part of the drama during the second episode of the series. Despite knowing that Joey was looking for someone who was immature or creating drama, Sydney made the decision to talk about her fellow Bachelor contestant Maria Georgas behind her back rather than simply talking to her one-on-one. Creating drama in the house may not be great for The Bachelor, but it can be interesting when it comes to Bachelor In Paradise, so it’s possible Sydney would be a good fit for the series.

11 Madina Alam The Center Of The Drama

Madina Alam, who was part of the drama with Sydney and Maria during The Bachelor season 28 episode 2, would be another person who could potentially fit into a future season of Bachelor In Paradise. Though Madina has been somewhat quiet during the recent episodes of The Bachelor, she was the center of the drama when Maria chose to comment about her insecurities surrounding her age. Madina, who’s 31, felt insecure being the oldest woman on the cast. It’s likely that on Bachelor In Paradise, Madina would be around women closer to her age and have the time to get over her insecurities.

10 Allison Hollinger The Last Sister Left Standing

Allison Hollinger was part of one of the twists of The Bachelor season 28, joining the series along with her sister Lauren Hollinger. Both of the sisters were hoping to date Joey, sharing that they’d dated the same guy before, though not at the same time. While some were hoping that Lauren and Allison’s appearance would be more of a joke, both women seemed to take it seriously, and as Allison’s connection with Joey developed further, it was clear that she was more of an option for the tennis instructor. Allison’s genuine affection and her full transparency with him would make her a great candidate for Bachelor In Paradise.

9 Lexi Young Quiet, But Hopeful For Love

Although Lexi Young has been relatively quiet on The Bachelor season 28 so far, she seems like she would be a great choice for Bachelor In Paradise season 10. While Lexi seems like a sweet, wholesome woman originally hailing from Ohio, her opening night introduction shared that she’s facing a medical condition behind the scenes that could keep her from becoming a mother in the future. As the first woman out of the limos, Lexi has been one to watch on The Bachelor, but would also make a great candidate to find lasting love on Bachelor In Paradise if things with Joey don’t work out.

8 Edwina Dorbor A Fun, Flirty Choice

Originally from Liberia, Edwina Dorbor currently lives in Atlanta, Georgia, and is hoping that she can win Joey’s heart, but if that doesn’t work in the long term, she’d be a great choice for Bachelor In Paradise. Edwina has been one of the more fun contestants to watch on The Bachelor season 28 with a sweet disposition and somewhat goofy sense of humor. As an entrepreneur who has been looking to find love in her life, she’s ready for something real. Though she has her heart set on Joey, if Edwina doesn’t find love with The Bachelor, she’d be a great cast member on Bachelor In Paradise.

7 Taylor Wiens The Surprisingly Early Elimination

While Taylor seemed like she was cast as the villain of the season, her short time on The Bachelor season 28 made it clear that she should be on the upcoming season of Bachelor In Paradise. Taylor, who had a sweet first meeting with Joey on opening night, was one of the earliest eliminated contestants, going home after the first group date. While Taylor may not have been the bully some believed her to be, her personality was made for reality TV as she’s not afraid to say what she thinks. Taylor would be a great addition to Bachelor In Paradise season 10 as she’s outspoken and ready for drama.

6 Rachel Nance Hopeful For A True Connection

As a native Hawaiian, Rachel Nance already has the beachside portion of Bachelor In Paradise down. Though she’s looking for love with Joey on The Bachelor, Rachel’s aware that there’s some fierce competition for Joey’s heart. Though she’s hopeful that she’ll be the one to end up with Joey, Rachel would be a great pick for Bachelor In Paradise if she’s available. With her interesting sense of humor, which she showed off comparing herself and Joey to Friends characters on Instagram, and her gorgeous looks, she’d be a great catch for some new guys to get to know on Paradise.

5 Jess Edwards The True Villain Of The Season

While she’s currently being played out as the true villain of The Bachelor season 28, Jess Edwards would be gold for Bachelor In Paradise season 10. Her attitude surrounding her relationship with Joey has been off-putting for the rest of the women vying for his heart, but her loyalty and passion for drama would be great on the spin-off series. While Jess appears to be someone who’s wholeheartedly in the experience for Joey, if she gets her heart broken on The Bachelor, there’s no doubt that she’ll be on her way to Paradise to keep looking for love with other Bachelor Nation alumni.

4 Evalin Clark The Shocking Group Date Member

While Evalin Clark is still hoping for Joey’s heart on The Bachelor season 28, if things don’t work out for the San Antonio, Texas native, she’d be great for the Bachelor Nation spin-off. Evalin’s already caught Bachelor Nation’s attention with her breakdown in the premiere episode and her follow-up performance on The Bachelor season 28 episode 2. During a group date, Evalin did her best to win a game of musical chairs by launching herself over a table to get the chair from someone else. Knowing she’s competitive, driven, and hopes to find the love of a lifetime, Evalin would be a great cast member on Bachelor In Paradise.

3 Lauren Hollinger The Misunderstood Sister

After her time on The Bachelor season 28 was cut short of her own volition, Lauren would be a great pick for Bachelor In Paradise. Her time on Joey’s season was colored by her relationship with her sister Allison, but Lauren would’ve been a great presence on The Bachelor without her sister around. With an independent attitude and a fun spirit, Lauren seemed put off by the fact that Allison was getting more attention than she was, but on Bachelor In Paradise, it’s more likely that the sisters would be after different guys. Lauren would be great for Paradise because she advocates for herself and wouldn’t be afraid of drama.

2 Lea Cayanan The Sweetheart Of The Season

Meeting Joey during The Bachelorette season 20 finale put a target on Lea Cayanan’s back for the women in the house, but her relationship with Joey has been flourishing in the first episodes of the season. Though Lea chose not to use her advantage and steal a date from another contestant, her time on The Bachelor season 28 has still been more dramatic than tame. Lea herself doesn’t seem to be interested in the drama but does hope that she gets the chance to fall for Joey as the season continues. With her sweet disposition, Lea’s a shoo-in for Bachelor In Paradise if she doesn’t win Joey’s heart.

1 Maria Georgas A Natural Born Villain-Turned-Hero

After making an impression early on The Bachelor season 28, Maria Georgas is someone Bachelor Nation hopes makes it to the end of the season, but if she doesn’t win Joey’s heart, she’d make a great cast member for Bachelor In Paradise season 10. Maria may appear like a villain to some, but her unique attitude and outlook seem to be qualities that Joey’s interested in. Happy to speak her mind and share her opinions about the other relationships forming on the series, Maria would be great casting for Bachelor In Paradise, as she’s not afraid to confront drama or go for what she wants on The Bachelor.
