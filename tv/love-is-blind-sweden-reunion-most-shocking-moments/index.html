SUMMARY

Fortune teller predicts a short future for Krisse-Ly and Rasmus, but their love has grown stronger. Meira clarifies that she genuinely loves Oskar despite the challenges in their relationship. Catja leaves Christofer but finds love with a former pod member named Adde.

Literature Review

Emotions ran high during Love Is Blind: Sweden’s reunion, revealing some of the most shocking details this season. The host left no stone unturned.

Love Is Blind: Sweden reunion gave some of the most shocking moments on the show. The whole cast looked great and arrived in the dark, an important aspect that preceded some of the different revelations later on in the show. By the third episode of Love is Blind: Sweden, five couples left the pods engaged. That included Krisse-ly Kuldkepp and Rasmus Hedenstedt, Emilia Holmqvist and Lucas Gustavsson, Amanda Jonegard and Sergio Rincon, Meira Omar and Oskar Nordstrand and Catja Lövstrand and Christofer Pocock. After their honeymoon in Cyprus, they returned to Sweden to try and live together for the first time.

Of the five Love Is Blind: Sweden season 1 couples, four made it to the altar, but only three exchanged vows. Christofer and Catja experienced challenges immediately after meeting in person. Catja’s likeness towards Christofer was superficial, and she struggled with her lack of attraction towards him. Eventually, after an argument, Catja left Christofer without saying goodbye. Lucas and Emilia experienced similar attraction problems after leaving the pods. However, Emilia said yes to Lucas, but he later rejected the marriage. Now, Lucas is currently dating, while Emilia remains single.

Krsisse-Ly & Rasmus Shocking Revelation Fortune Teller Predicts Their Future

Rasmus was one of Love Is Blind: Sweden’s shallowest cast members, based on what he wanted in a partner. The 32-year-old indicated looks mattered to him, and luckily, he hit the jackpot by matching up with Krisse-ly and later getting married. During the reunion, the couple revealed they visited a fortune-teller who indicated their relationship was built on attraction, not love.

The psychic stated she didn’t see what their marriage would be beyond two years. They also talked about children and mentioned something a a “bad fetus” becoming a baby boy. The psychic asked Rasmus if he was getting tired lately, to which he responded yes. She advised the Love is Blind: Sweden star to increase his vitamin B12 intake to curb that issue. Although Rasmus and Krisse-ly seemed pretty shaken by the revelation, they stated that their union had become stronger, proving love could be blind as they shared their behind-the-scenes reunion pictures online (via Krissy Kuldkepp).

Meira & Oskar’s Relationship Has Improved Meira Genuinely Loves Oskar

Meira and Oskar didn’t have an instant connection after the pods. It was hard for Oskar to get close to Meira, who had stereotyped him as a Swedish man from an affluent family who didn’t understand anything about other cultures. Although Meira grew up in Sweden, she has Arabian roots, which made her feel he came from a different world than hers, which affected their relationship.

At one point, she questioned her decision to pick Oskar over Johannes, another cast member she connected with at the pods. However, after the reunion, Meira posted a lengthy post on her Instagram account, blaming the production for twisting their storyline and making her look like she didn’t love Oskar. In the same post, Meira declared her love for Oskar and added that their love story would continue to blossom.

Catja’s New Boyfriend He Is From The Pods

Catja left the pods engaged to Christofer, whom she had zero physical attraction to, and disliked his choice of clothes and purple hair. There was a lot of overcompensation on Christofer’s part, which turned Catja off. He was trying to smother Catja since the Love Is Blind: Sweden star feared she would leave, which she eventually did.

“I didn’t try too much but, when it got intense, I was terrified to lose Catja. I was really afraid of that. I was so blinded that I just chose to coddle her. I get how annoying that must have been.”

Catja admitted to feeling unsafe and slept at Oskar and Meira’s apartment one night after an argument erupted between her and Christofer; she had asked him to stand up for himself. This led to the end of their relationship, leaving Christofer heartbroken (via Netflix). Unlike Christofer, who is still single, Catja isn’t and revealed during the Love Is Blind: Sweden reunion episode that she is dating Adde Tzelidis, whom she dated in the pods.​​​​​​​

Throughout Love is Blind: Sweden, Christofer referred to Catja as the “joy of my heart and eternal longing.”

Amanda & Sergio’s Bombshell Sergio Presented Amanda With Half-Truths

Perhaps one of the biggest bombshells from the Love Is Blind: Sweden reunion is Amanda and Sergio. From the start, their union was shaky due to Sergio’s narcissistic and egocentric mannerisms. He seemed like a red flag to Amanda, a church-going girl who valued Christian principles and needed that from a partner. However, Sergio, who stated religion wasn’t important to him, showed Amanda how untruthful he could be. Before the nuptials, rumors started spreading that Sergio was expecting a child in Barcelona, allegations Sergio denied.

During the reunion, Sergio and Amanda revealed he had a child in Sweden, not Barcelona, stating they had learned about his child two months after their wedding. The Love Is Blind: Sweden cast members were shaken, with Rasmus calling Sergio out for not presenting the truth to Amanda. Sergio stated he had no idea a child was involved before courting and marrying Amanda.

When asked if things between them would have changed had she known Sergio was expecting a child, Amanda said no and that she still would’ve married Sergio. That wasn’t all from the Love is Blind: Sweden cast members, as Amanda and Sergio revealed they are expecting their first child, with the due date one day before their anniversary.
