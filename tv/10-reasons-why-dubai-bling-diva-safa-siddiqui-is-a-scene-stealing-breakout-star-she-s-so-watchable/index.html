10 Reasons Why Dubai Bling Diva Safa Siddiqui Is A Scene-Stealing Breakout Star (She s So Watchable)

SUMMARY

Safa is a materialistic and entitled reality TV star who is willing to go to great lengths to get what she wants, including demanding a bigger house and a push present. Safa and Fahad have a luxurious lifestyle, with Safa being spoiled by Fahad and enjoying the finer things in life like designer clothes, jewelry, and lavish parties. Safa is known for her drama and explosive moments on the show, often feuding with friends and holding grudges, but her authenticity and charisma make her an engaging and watchable star.

Literature Review

Although many Dubai Bling viewers consider Safa Siddiqui as the villain in season 2, she is still the most entertaining cast of them all.

Safa Siddiqui is the breakout star of Dubai Bling season 2, a show that presents a high-flying circle from Dubai. Safa is an Iraqi-British celebrity married to billionaire Fahad Siddiqui from India. The two have served the audience with juicy drama, lavish parties, and expensive plane and yacht rides since their debut on the show. The billionaire couple stole most of the screen time, especially in the new season. Safa always brought the drama. During her first on-screen debut, Safa threw her husband’s clothes on the floor after an argument about a property deal. It is perhaps one of the reasons why Safa’s cast mate Zeina Khoury described her during episode 1 of the Dubai Bling season 1 introduction as “crazy.” Season 2 showed how crazy, materialistic, and interesting Safa could get. Some viewers consider Safa the real villain due to her explosive moments on the show, such as feuding with her friends, holding grudges, and her level of entitlement to how Fahad should treat her. However, Safa is the one cast member that is hard to dismiss. She has an ego and is intelligent and likable.

10 Safa Is Bossy, Sassy & Delusional Safa Is Who Safa Thinks She Is

Safa has gained recognition due to her expensive taste, ranging from her luxurious gray Lamborghini to lavish dinner parties, to her Anna Shay and Christine Chiu’s jewelry collection. She doesn’t shy away from declaring her love for her luxurious items. “Material things do make me happy, so yeah, I’m materialistic and proud,” Safa said in season 1, a statement she has owned throughout the show. Additionally, Safa believes the world revolves around her. For instance, she demanded Fahad get them a bigger house before giving birth to their second child. Safa never backs down when needing something, and her expensive taste keeps growing. After their second child’s birth, Safa wanted a push present and took many approaches to let Fahad know about it. She dressed their daughter in socks with a “push present” message and wore a hair clip written the same. As delusional and sassy as this seemed, Safa got an indulgent bracelet that allowed her to choose from three luxurious cars wrapped in a red bow.

9 Fahad Goes All In When Spoiling Safa Safa Comes Off As A Brat

Fahad has ensured Safa is comfortable. She is a housewife who has everything provided for her. She owns designer clothes and jewelry, drives luxurious cars, and has different chefs to make her favorite meals anytime. Safa is a high-maintenance fashion addict who loves the good things in life and swears by everything extravagant. She lives in a plush apartment with stunning interior decor, massive chandeliers, and subtle color palettes. The house was a gift from her husband, Fahad, and opens to the beach, giving a fantastic view.

8 Safa’s Audacity In Testing Fahad’s Patience Is Unmatched Safa Is Impatient

Safa gets upset when Fahad leaves for India and is adamant that she doesn’t want to disrupt their lives in Dubai. She gets harsh with Fahad about the farmhouse he was meant to fix in India and is mad when he says he hasn’t fixed it yet. Before Fahad left, she checked his bag without an explanation of what she was looking for, an indication she might have been losing patience and didn’t believe Fahad was just going on a business trip. Fahad remained calm and collected during that incident as he had done on many different occasions.

7 Safa’s Clapback Remarks Are Top-Tier Dubai Bling’s Cast Members Know Not To Cross Safa

Safa has been open about how she felt neglected by her friend and cast member Lojain Omran, who didn’t visit her after she gave birth. Lojain brought her gifts two months later, and Safa opened her present in Lojain’s presence, only to find a snake-like bracelet to which she said, “The gifts are cute. To protect her from the snakes, because we have to be careful of all the snakes.” She was digging at Lojain for not visiting her earlier after she claimed to be busy. In another incident, she clapped back at Farhana Bodi, who claimed to be close friends with Loujain Adada, AKA LJ. “Öh she didn’t know? I thought they were great friends,” referring to Farhana not knowing that LJ had become a waitress.

6 Safa and Fahad Can Make A TV Show On Their Own Dubai Bling’s Star Is Watchable

Safa and Fahad have a rare blend of authenticity and charisma that would make their reality show an instant hit. Safa’s eloquence, blended with Fahad’s intelligence and charisma, would create a seamless chemistry onscreen, thus engaging the Dubai Bling audience with unique perspectives on their luxurious lifestyle. Safa seems to have a list of famous friends, including Darcey Silva, who has appeared in different 90 Day Fiancé franchises. Therefore, their show would be fun to watch.

5 Safa Is A Queen Of Instagram Captions It Is Never A Dull Day On Safa’s Socials

Safa doesn’t shy away from posting her glamorous life. Most of her captions are followed by a captivating message which keeps lots of Dubai Bling’s audience up with her life. From calling herself a"helicopter wife" to telling women to get a man who treats her like Fahad does, she has lots of funny captions. Safa has 1 million Instagram followers that she keeps entertained with her posts and comments.

4 Safa Is Obsessed Over Fahad Safa Tracks Fahad

Safa has a serious grip on Fahad and endeavors to know his whereabouts all the time. For a long time, she tracked Fahad’s phone while he was in India working. At one point, the apps showed Fahad had gone to a different location than his usual itinerary, prompting Safa and Zeina to fly to India and investigate. Safa wore a camouflaged outfit and found Fahad in the middle of a business deal which showed how possessive she was.

3 Safa Is A Fashion Killer Dubai Bling’s Star Safa Loves To Look Good

Safa is a fashion designer and has admitted to loving fashion since she was a child. The audience knows her as a trendsetter who is bold in her clothes choices, thus inspiring others to develop a unique sense of style. This passion led Safa to launch her clothing collection with Shein. Safa has also shown her modeling skills by being part of India’s Lamke Fashion Week in 2022.

2 Safa And Fahad’s Relationship Is Goals Will Fahad Put Up With Safa’s Behavior Forever?

Many Dubai Bling audience have noticed how Safa is unappreciative of how Fahad treats her, such that she always has something to complain about in their marriage. No amount of designer bags and luxurious items can keep Safa happy because she always wants more. Luckily, Fahad admires her and is always looking forward to giving her the world. Their union is filled with extravagant gifts, love, bling, and fun.

1 Safa Loves Drama She Looks Like She Is Acting Out Her Favorite Script

Safa lives on her planet and knows she is the star of the show. She has some of the most savage and best comebacks that are better than any other cast members. For instance, Safa told Danya Mohammed she is an Alibaba version of her. The revenge red snake dress made her look like Dubai’s Princess Diana. Although Netflix hasn’t announced a season 3 of Dubai Bling, the audience will keep getting drama on the show as long as Safa is in it. Dubai Bling season 2 is available for streaming on Netflix.

Dubai Bling
Dubai Bling is a Netflix reality tv series that focuses on the Dubai elite’s expensive, high-speed, and posh lifestyle. Following eight people with different backgrounds, such as a DJ and an influencer, Dubai Bling will explore the daily lives of the cast as they shop for luxury cars and new fashion while travelling to some of Dubai’s most exotic restaurants and hotspots. Competition between friends and their social status will elevate tensions and enflame old rivalries as their histories come to light.
