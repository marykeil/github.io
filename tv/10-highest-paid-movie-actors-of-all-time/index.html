10 Highest Paid Movie Actors Of All Time

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1900 words 9 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

A-list actors in Hollywood earn enormous paydays for their biggest roles, making them the highest-paid actors of all time. Some actors negotiate pay structures that tie their salary to the performance of the movie, allowing them to earn a percentage of the film’s total earnings. Actors can make incredible amounts of money by reprising roles in hit franchises or taking risky bets on the success of their movies, while still delivering iconic performances.

Literature Review

While most actors struggle achieve a livable wage, these actors have collectively made hundreds of millions of dollars for just one performance.

The biggest names in Hollywood earn some enormous paydays for their biggest roles which creates a lot of curiosity about who is the highest-paid actor of all time. 2023 saw the historic SAG strike shutting down Hollywood over disagreements about fair compensation, a reminder that the majority of professional actors don’t make millions of dollars. The same can’t be said for the other end of the scale though, as the highest paid movie actors in Hollywood earn some incredible sums. Unsurprisingly, the collection of the highest-paid actors of all time is a roster of the biggest names in the business, with A-list superstars and Oscar winners. However, the roles for which these actors have received their biggest paydays vary from award-winning dramas to ambitious sci-fi thrillers to superhero adventures. In some cases, the highest paid actors in Hollywood earned their largest take-homes for reprising roles in hit franchises, while others took risky bets on their movie earning more than expected. The amount of money these actors earn is hard to believe but their roles remain as iconic as ever.

10 Tom Hanks - Forrest Gump (1994) $60 million

Forrest Gump

Release Date July 6, 1994 Director Robert Zemeckis Cast Mykelti Williamson , Gary Sinise , Tom Hanks , Robin Wright , Sally Field Runtime 142 minutes

Tom Hanks earned $60 million for his star performance in the Robert Zemeckis movie Forrest Gump. According to Entertainment Weekly, Hanks had a contract that determined his final salary based on the performance of the movie. Since Forrest Gump generated roughly $680 million at the global box office, Hanks was able to walk away with a major payday that equaled just under 10% of the film’s total earnings. Hanks negotiated for a similar pay structure on Saving Private Ryan, for which he reportedly earned somewhere between $30-40 million for that movie alone. Watch On Prime Video

9 Adam Sandler - Murder Mystery (2019) $62.5 million

Murder Mystery

Release Date June 14, 2019 Director Kyle Newacheck Cast Adam Sandler , Jennifer Aniston , Gemma Arterton Runtime 97minutes

Adam Sandler and Netflix made a deal for the actor to make four movies with the streaming service which ended up scoring him a huge payday, according to Forbes. The deal guaranteed Sandler a whopping total of $250 million, which means that he will have earned $62.5 million for each of the movies that are part of the contract. Sandler’s partnership with Netflix has produced 2020’s Hubie Halloween, 2022’s Hustle, and 2023’s Murder Mystery 2. Sandler renewed the original 2014 Netflix deal in 2017, indicating that he has made a $62.5 million salary from eight separate movies. Watch On Netflix

8 Harrison Ford - Indiana Jones And The Kingdom Of The Crystal Skull (2008) $65 million

Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull

Release Date May 22, 2008 Director Steven Spielberg Cast Harrison Ford , Shia LaBeouf , Cate Blanchett , Karen Allen , Ray Winstone , John Hurt , Jim Broadbent Runtime 122 Minutes

Before Harrison Ford’s time as Indiana Jones ended in Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny, he earned a staggering amount for his return to the role in Indiana Jones And The Kingdom Of The Crystal Skull. Ford reportedly took a major pay cut for his appearance in Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny but raked in a sizable salary for Indiana Jones 4. According to Men’s Health, Harrison Ford received a major payday of $65 million from Paramount to return for the highly-anticipated fourth installment of the Indiana Jones franchise. The deal encouraged Ford to reemerge out of semi-retirement and may have ignited the spark for Ford to continue his acting career into his eighties. Watch On Disney+

7 Johnny Depp - Alice In Wonderland (2010) $68 million

Even though he was not in the main role, Johnny Depp earned $68 million for his role as Mad Hatter in the 2010 live-action Alice in Wonderland. The movie brought together Depp and Tim Burton again in a collaboration that previously generated classics such as Edward Scissorhands, Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street, and Charlie and the Chocolate Factory. According to The Telegraph, Depp was paid £50 million British pounds, which was the equivalent of $68 million USD, making him the highest-paid actor in Hollywood in 2010 thanks to his other role as Captain Jack Sparrow in the Pirates of the Caribbean franchise. 6 Sandra Bullock - Gravity (2013) $70 million

Gravity

Release Date October 3, 2013 Director Alfonso Cuarón Cast Sandra Bullock , George Clooney , Ed Harris , Orto Ignatiussen , Paul Sharma , Amy Warren Runtime 1h 31m

Sandra Bullock was paid $20 million upfront for her role in the epic space film Gravity but was able to earn much more thanks to her contract which secured her 15% of the total box office revenue. Bullock was able to rake in over $70 million for this single performance, according to The Hollywood Reporter. Because of the film’s success at the global box office, generating over $700 million in 2013, Bullock was able to bring home a total paycheck that was more than three times her original salary. Her achievement represents another example of actors betting on themselves and their movies at the box office. Watch On Netflix

5 Robert Downey Jr. - Avengers: Infinity War (2018) $75 million

Avengers: Infinity War

Release Date April 27, 2018 Director Joe Russo , Anthony Russo Cast Chris Hemsworth , Anthony Mackie , Elizabeth Olsen , Robert Downey Jr. , Scarlett Johansson , Mark Ruffalo , Sebastian Stan , Paul Rudd , Chadwick Boseman , Don Cheadle , Samuel L. Jackson , Josh Brolin , Tom Holland , Evangeline Lilly , Paul Bettany , Chris Evans , Jeremy Renner Runtime 149 minutes

Robert Downey Jr. earned $75 million for his role as Iron Man/Tony Stark in Avengers: Infinity War. It makes sense that the lead actor in one of the highest-grossing franchises of all time would individually make one of the biggest salaries for a single film. Downey Jr. reportedly has made roughly $435 million from his involvement in the Marvel Cinematic Universe which started with 2008’s Iron Man and ended with Downey Jr’s final Iron Man appearance in Avengers: Endgame (via The Daily Mail). Watch On Disney+

4 Tom Cruise - Mission: Impossible – Ghost Protocol (2011) $75 million

Mission: Impossible - Ghost Protocol

Release Date December 15, 2011 Director Brad Bird Cast Tom Cruise , Paula Patton , Simon Pegg , Jeremy Renner Runtime 2h 13m

Tom Cruise earned $75 million for his role as Ethan Hunt in Mission: Impossible – Ghost Protocol, according to Forbes. This figure was given to Cruise in order for him to successfully revitalize the Mission: Impossible franchise after the lackluster responses to its second and third installments, an objective which Cruise achieved with the massive success of 2015’s Mission: Impossible – Rogue Nation and 2018’s Mission: Impossible – Fallout. While Mission: Impossible – Dead Reckoning Part One disappointed at the box office, both Cruise and the franchise remain a huge financial draw which will hopefully rebound with the upcoming eighth movie. 3 Keanu Reeves - The Matrix (1999) $83.3 million

Keanu Reeves earned $250 million for all of The Matrix movies, which equates to $83.3 million for each. Reeves once made in the tens of millions upfront from his movies before taking a share of the total film’s profits, which is how he was able to achieve such enormous totals for his Matrix trilogy. Reeves’ more recent salaries for his John Wick films are determined in part by an ownership stake in his successful franchise. Watch On Max

2 Will Smith - Men In Black 3 (2012) $100 million

Men in Black 3

Release Date May 25, 2012 Director Barry Sonnenfeld Cast Will Smith , Josh Brolin , Tommy Lee Jones Runtime 1h 46m

Will Smith earned $100 million as part of a deal that combined upfront salary with a cut of the movie’s gross for Men In Black 3. Smith has become one of the highest-paid consistent actors in Hollywood, earning $35 million just for his most recent performance in Apple’s Emancipation. At the peak of his career, Smith was reportedly earning anywhere from $20-30 million per movie, which isn’t much compared to the $100 million he was able to secure for Men In Black 3. The actor got paid just $5 million for the original Men In Black movie. His $100 million Men In Black 3 contract is the highest-paid single movie payout of all time. Watch On AMC+

1 Tom Cruise - Top Gun: Maverick (2022) 100+ million

Top Gun: Maverick

Release Date May 27, 2022 Director Joseph Kosinski Cast Jennifer Connelly , Jake Picking , Raymond Lee , Tom Cruise , Lewis Pullman , Monica Barbaro , Miles Teller , Danny Ramirez , Val Kilmer , Manny Jacinto , Ed Harris , Glen Powell , Jon Hamm Runtime 130 minutes

Tom Cruise has remarkably made more than $100 million from his one performance in Top Gun: Maverick alone . In the best case of an actor betting on himself, Cruise accepted only $13 million upfront to reprise his role as Maverick from the original 1986 movie Top Gun. Cruise orchestrated a prolific backend deal that has allowed him to earn more than $100 million at the box office thanks to the movie’s noteworthy success. Cruise remains the highest-paid movie actor of all time and has likely set a new precedent that even he will find difficult to outperform.
