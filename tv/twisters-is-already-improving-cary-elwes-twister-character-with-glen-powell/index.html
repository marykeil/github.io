Twisters Is Already Improving Cary Elwes Twister Character With Glen Powell

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 800 words 4 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

The new Twisters trailer reveals improvements in a character, making him more likable than the original film’s counterpart. Glen Powell, known for Top Gun: Maverick, is currently enjoying success with a romantic comedy and is set to star in Twisters. The decision to create a more likable and unique character for Powell’s role is a smart move, avoiding repetition from Cary Elwes’ character in the original Twister.

Literature Review

The new Super Bowl trailer for Twisters features a first look at Glen Powell’s protagonist, which looks to be a great improvement from the original.

A new trailer for the highly-anticipated disaster movie Twisters, which premiered during Super Bowl LVIII, already shows clear improvements from one of the original film’s main characters. The Twisters Super Bowl trailer sets up Glen Powell’s Tyler Owens to be the same as Cary Elwes’ Jonas Miller in Twister, a stormhunter with a reputation for being cocky and a piece of work. The first look at Powell’s character hints that while Owens is certainly a showboat, he looks like he will be a more likable character.

This major character enhancement looks to already be a great improvement on the original Academy Award-nominated movie. Powell, who is best known for his breakout role in Top Gun: Maverick, is coming off the surprising box office success of Anyone But You, a romantic comedy he stars in with Euphoria star Sydney Sweeney. It’s a smart creative decision, considering it would be uninspired just to repeat the same story that Cary Elwes’ character had in Twister in the upcoming 2024 blockbuster. Twisters is currently expected to release in theaters on July 19, 2024.

Glen Powell’s Tyler Owens Is Cary Elwes’ Jonas Miller Replacement In Twisters

As a leading man in another major potential franchise, Powell must be a likable and captivating main character, even if he does come off as reckless and even somewhat dangerous in the upcoming 2024 movie. Powell’s Tyler Owens looks to possess some of the best qualities of Cary Elwes’ Jonas Miller, such as his ambitious sense of adventure, without taking on all of the negative baggage and the full-blown antagonistic role of Jonas Miller in the original film.

As the self-proclaimed “Tornado Wrangler”, Powell’s Tyler Owens appears to be a larger-than-life symbol of the American West, which is emphasized by his cowboy hat and Top Gun-esque sunglasses. There’s no doubt the Owens will be a major draw for audiences to check out Twisters in theaters this July, hinting that while he may lead his supporting characters into some tense situations, he will likely be playing more of the hero than his predecessor Jonas Miller.

    Is Twisters A Sequel Or A Reboot? How It Connects To The 1990s Movie  The trailer released for Twisters during the Super Bowl has viewers wondering if its a sequel to the 1990s movie or a complete franchise reboot.

Why It’s Smart That Twisters Isn’t Repeating Elwes’ Storyline With Glen Powell

From the trailer alone, there seem to be many references and odes to the original movie, meaning that there could even be plot and character connections between Twister and Twisters. It’s a major relief that Twisters is enhancing the original stormchaser character from the 1996 movie and revamping it with Powell’s natural charisma and action hero attributes. In this way, Twisters is already set up to feel much more like a highly-anticipated sequel rather than a senseless cash-grab remake of the second highest-grossing movie of 1996.
