Casting Athena & 10 Other New Characters For Percy Jackson & The Olympians Season 2

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1900 words 9 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Alexandra Shipp could be a great choice to portray the complex character of Athena in Percy Jackson season 2. Gaten Matarazzo’s adorkable charm makes him an ideal fit for the role of Percy’s half-brother Tyson. Jeffery Dean Morgan’s versatility as an actor could make him a great choice to play the humorous yet terrifying villain Polyphemus.

Literature Review

Disney will need to cast fan-favorite characters including Athena, Blackjack, Tyson, and Thalia Grace for Percy Jackson and the Olympians season 2.

Should Disney move forward with a second season of Percy Jackson and the Olympians, the producers will need to cast many new characters from Rick Riordan’s novels. Percy Jackson and the Olympians season 1 featured new talent as the demigods, the half-human descendants of the Greek gods. The younger, relatively unknown actors shared the screen with some industry veterans, including Lin-Manuel Miranda as Hermes and the late Lance Reddick as Zeus. While season 1 follows the events of Riordan’s novel The Lightning Thief, Percy Jackson and the Olympians season 2 will presumably be based on the next book, The Sea of Monsters. In season 2, Percy (Walker Scobell) and Annabeth Chase (Leah Sava Jeffries) will find themselves on a mission to rescue their friend Grover Underwood (Aryan Simhadri) from a mythological monster and retrieve an ancient artifact needed to protect Camp Half-Blood. Along the way, there will be plenty more gods and monsters – likely played by some of the film and TV industry’s finest actors.

11 Alexandra Shipp As Athena Best known for playing Kim Woodruff in Straight Outta Compton (2015) and Author Barbie in Barbie (2023).

Percy does not meet Athena until the third novel in Riordan’s series, The Titan’s Curse. However, she will probably appear next season. Athena, the goddess of wisdom and Annabeth’s mother, is an immeasurably complicated character who is always considering several different strategies in her head. Fans have proposed stunt-casting Athena with Alexandra Daddario (Annabeth’s movie actress) or type-casting her with Gal Gadot. However, the show would probably benefit from a lesser-known, unexpected casting choice who can convey Athena’s complexities without being weighed down by audience expectations. Alexandra Shipp is an underrated actress who starred in Tick, Tick…Boom!, Straight Outta Compton, Barbie, and Fox’s X-Men movies. While Shipp’s character Storm in X-Men is similar to Athena, her heartrending delivery in Tick, Tick…Boom! proves that she is capable of much more. Shipp’s acting history demonstrates how she could embody Athena’s shifting and calculated persona.

8 Biggest Unanswered Questions & Mysteries After Percy Jackson Season 1 Percy Jackson and the Olympians’ season 1 finale resolves the quest for Zeus’ Master Bolt, though still leaves several questions unanswered.

10 Gaten Matarazzo As Tyson Best known for playing Dustin Henderson in Stranger Things (2016-2025).

Tyson is Percy’s extremely friendly Cyclops half-brother, who grows on Percy and Annabeth despite annoying them at first. Gaten Matarazzo is the ideal actor to cast as Tyson because he has perfectly played Dustin, the most adorkable member of the Stranger Things cast, for years. Matarazzo is older than Tyson is in Sea of Monsters (in the same pre-teen age range as Percy and his friends), but Matarazzo would still work for the role. Tyson is described as being much taller than humans the same age as him and probably appears older.

9 Luke Evans As Argus Best known for playing Owen Shaw in Fast & Furious 6 (2013) and Vlad in Dracula Untold (2014).

Argus is the head of security at Camp Half-Blood who is notably absent from season 1 – but it is not too late to introduce him! In Sea of Monsters, Chiron (Glynn Turman) and Argus are blamed for poisoning Thalia’s tree and are fired from their positions. Including Argus in season 2 could help convey the urgency of the situation. The campers would be even more let down if two characters beloved to them were not present. Argus looks like a regular person, but he is a giant and has a hundred eyes on his body. Luke Evans would be great as Argus and has shown interest in different genres and characters. Evans plays a more serious character in the Fast & Furious movies, which would probably be Argus’ personality on the show. Additionally, Evans’ history of fantasy movies, including The Hobbit and Dracula Untold, suggests that he might be interested in appearing in Percy Jackson.

8 Bill Skarsgård As Tantalus Best known for playing Pennywise in It (2017) and the Marquis in John Wick: Chapter 4 (2023).

Tantalus replaces Chiron as activities director at Camp Half-Blood when Chiron is suspected of poisoning Thalia’s tree. Tantalus has a somewhat generic description in the books but is a sadistic instructor, and Bill Skarsgård has proven his ability to play a monster several times. He plays Pennywise in the recent It movies and a more suave villain in John Wick: Chapter 4. If he is open to it, Skarsgård would excel as Tantalus. Additionally, an actor from John Wick was cast as Zeus for season 1, and the producers might watch the franchise again for inspiration.

7 Chris Rock As Blackjack Best known for playing Marty in Madagascar (2005), Andre Allen in Top Five (2014), and his stand-up comedy.

Blackjack is a Pegasus whom Percy frees as he and Annabeth voyage through the Sea of Monsters. Blackjack then promises to always help Percy and assists him in many future quests. All of Blackjack’s interactions with Percy are nothing short of hilarious – which is why Disney should look for a comedian to voice him. Chris Rock is, of course, one of the most celebrated comedians and has voice acting experience from the Madagascar movies. However, Rock also recently gave an excellent performance in the drama Rustin and his acting abilities in different genres should not be underestimated.

6 Sonoya Mizuno As Circe Best known for playing Caitlin in La La Land (2016) and Mysaria in House of the Dragon (2022-Present).

Sonoya Mizuno appeared as supporting characters in several movies before landing the role of Mysaria in House of the Dragon season 1. Mysaria is a rising mistress of spies; she is mysterious, cunning, and at least partially motivated by a desire for justice. Mysaria is not unlike Circe from The Odyssey, whom Percy and Annabeth also meet in the second book when they come across her spa in the Sea of Monsters. What will define Mizuno’s (or another actor’s) performance as Circe will be if the writers find time to explore her backstory as they did for Medusa (Jessica Parker Kennedy) in season 1. In House of the Dragon, Mysaria demands that Otto Hightower outlaw pit fights between children in King’s Landing. Mizuno could deliver a compelling monologue as Circe defends her practice of turning men into pigs as a form of self-defense (she has moved on to guinea pigs in Percy Jackson because they are easier to take care of).

5 Jensen Ackles As Blackbeard Best known for playing Dean Winchester in Supernatural (2005-2020).

When Percy and Annabeth are trapped at Circe’s spa, they discover that Circe has also imprisoned the historical pirate Blackbeard and his crew. Annabeth inadvertently frees the pirates while she and Percy are escaping. Whoever plays Blackbeard in Percy Jackson season 2 will have a brief window of time to make an impression. If Jensen Ackles brings the same energy he did for Dean Winchester’s most reckless, abrasive moments, he could pull it off. It’s easy to imagine him leading some kind of absurd charge to reclaim the Queen Anne’s Revenge.

4 Jeffrey Dean Morgan As Polyphemus Best known for playing Negan Smith in The Walking Dead (2010-2022) and Harvey Russell in Rampage (2018).

Jeffrey Dean Morgan gave a very dramatic and sinister performance as Negan in The Walking Dead, which is why the Percy Jackson producers might consider him to play another villain. Polyphemus, the Cyclops who fights Odysseus in Greek mythology, is a very different character from Negan. He is supposed to be terrifying yet ridiculous because he falls for the famous “Nobody” trick. However, Morgan is a skilled actor who has taken on a variety of other roles and could play a more humorous villain.

3 Isabela Merced As Thalia Grace Best known for playing Dora in Dora and the Lost City of Gold (2019) and Rachel Cooper in Sweet Girl (2021).

As explained in season 1, Thalia Grace is the daughter of Zeus who lived on the run with Luke (Charlie Bushnell) and Annabeth before she was mortally wounded by monsters outside of Camp Half-Blood and her father turned her into a tree to save her life. Thalia is a stubborn and tough fighter and has a punk sense of style. In the books, her dark hair is always cut short. With a pixie cut, Isabela Merced looks exactly like Thalia. Merced has been slowly building her repertoire as an action star in recent years. She could draw upon her experience playing energetic, adventure-savvy young women in Sweet Girl and Dora and the Lost City of Gold, as well as her upcoming role in Madame Web. Thalia is definitely edgier than Dora, but Merced could adjust her approach. Merced is older than the target age range, but her round face makes her look younger. Thalia is also older than Percy and Annabeth (15-16 years old).

2 Chosen Jacobs As Charles Beckendorf Best known for playing Mike Hanlon in It (2017) and El in Sneakerella (2022).

Charles Beckendorf is a son of Hephaestus, who is a few years older than Percy and is friendly to everyone. Chosen Jacobs would be a great choice to play Charles because of how the character recalls his past roles. Jacobs’ character Mike Hanlon is arguably the “Losers’ Club’s” most dependable member in IT and dependability is one of Charles’ characteristics. Jacobs does an amazing job portraying Mike as terrified, but Charles’ friendly and sometimes shy personality is closer to Jacobs’ character El in Sneakerella. El’s passion for designing sneakers also oddly resonates with Charles’ craftmanship.

1 Freya Allan As Silena Beauregard Best known for playing Ciri in The Witcher (2019-Present) and Mae in Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes (2024).

Silena Beauregard is a daughter of Aphrodite and Charles’ girlfriend. She is characterized as one of the kindest people at camp and is an adept fighter. The actor cast as her will need to be able to portray her complicated feelings about her shifting loyalties. Silena has dark hair in the books, but Freya Allan would be able to embody the character with any hair color. Allan most notably plays Ciri throughout The Witcher TV show. Ciri is somewhat naïve in the first season but becomes more headstrong later while retaining a kind of inquisitive intelligence. This demonstrates how Allan could portray all aspects of Silena’s character. Therefore, she is the perfect person to complete the cast of Percy Jackson and the Olympians season 2.
