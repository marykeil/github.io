90 Day Fiancé diva Angela Deem never holds back, and that’s why some of her revelations about her sex life are cringe-inducing. Her honesty can be brutal, but at least she’s not playing a part. For years, she’s been just the same - erratic, passionate, loudmouthed and funny. Angela will never change. She seems to enjoy their intimate life, and that means that everyone has to hear about it.

Angela believed that Michael would use his IG account to find women. She was probably not wrong. To add insult to injury, he blocked the famous “meemaw” on Instagram. Apparently, after Angela and Michael get into no-holds-barred fights, they make up in an equally dramatic fashion. Angela enjoys bragging about their sex life, which may be the reason why she’s still in this ludicrous relationship. These two aren’t on the same page. However, if Angela’s “satisfied” with Michael in other ways, she may be willing to overlook even the most blatant red flags. The best thing she can do is throw in the towel, but she seems to love Michael, although she’s a bit of a flirt herself. Maybe Angela doesn’t want to admit defeat even though she should. She’s invested so much time, energy and money. This relationship has dragged on for years, and her dream was Michael coming to America to live with her. Now, that dream has come true, but is it really such a fairytale? What she has is a man who’s proven again and again that he’s conning her. It’s such a hollow win, and it’s bound to turn into a major loss someday. She should cut her losses immediately.
