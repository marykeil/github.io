SUMMARY

Loren and Alexei Brovarnik have one of the most successful marriages on 90 Day Fiancé, with three children and a strong relationship. Larissa Lima, Big Ed Brown, Molly Hopkins, Kimberly Rochelle, Jasmine Pineda, Natalie Mordovtseva, Jamal Menzies, and Paul Staehle all struggled to handle their relationships on and off the show. These cast members made mistakes, had toxic behavior, or lacked communication skills, leading to failed relationships and unhappiness.

Literature Review

Some cast members are so emotionally immature that they haven’t been able to form stable relationships since their debuts on 90 Day Fiancé.

90 Day Fiancé has featured many cast members, a few of whom are incompetent at handling their relationships. Since the series’ inception in 2014, many cast members have showcased their unique love journeys in front of reality TV cameras. One of them is Loren Brovarnik, who has had one of the most successful marriages on the show. Loren married her Israeli husband, Alexei Brovarnik, in 2015. Loren and Alexei welcomed three adorable kids in the last four years, showing their relationship is the healthiest it has ever been. Paola and Russ Mayfield from 90 Day Fiancé season 1 have also built a great relationship. The couple was once on the verge of breaking up but have remained together over the last few years. Robert Springs and Anny Francisco’s family is another successful couple from the franchise. They tied the knot several years ago but have remained inseparable since. The two sacrificed for each other and bought a new home in Florida some time ago. They also faced a tragedy when they lost their newborn son, but it made them even stronger as a couple. Robert and Anny haven’t overcome the pain but still have the love of their other son and daughter.

8 Larissa Lima Larissa Was An Unsupportive Wife

Many 90 Day Fiancé stars have failed to handle their relationships on and off the screen. One example is Larissa Lima, who was on the show with her ex-husband, Colt Johnson, in 90 Day Fiancé season 6. Larissa had a bad attitude about her relationship with Colt from the get-go. She behaved terribly and demanded things, which made fans label her a gold digger. Larissa made every mistake a person could make in a relationship, from flushing her ring down the toilet to creating drama with her mother-in-law. Eventually, Colt filed for a divorce after Larissa got arrested for domestic battery.

7 Big Ed Brown Big Ed Can’t Even Be Friends With His Co-Stars

Big Ed Brown has also shown that he can’t handle any relationship, be it romantic or personal. The 58-year-old Arkansas resident has made many mistakes, which have often caused him to be single and alone. Ed hasn’t had a good relationship with his daughter for the longest time because of his inclination toward dating younger women. He also never had a stable relationship with his ex, Rose Vega, and his fiancée, Liz Woods, because he treated them terribly. While men often become buddies easily, Ed even failed to befriend co-stars like Jovi Dufren and Asuleu Pulaa because he leaked their strip club plans on 90 Day: The Last Resort.

6 Molly Hopkins Molly Is A Bad Friend, Mother & Lover

Molly Hopkins has ruined too many relationships with her terrible behavior over the years. While the single mother is a brilliant businesswoman, she’s the opposite when handling personal relationships. Molly’s daughter, Olivia, has accused her of being physically abusive in the past. Similarly, Molly’s ex, Kelly Brown, accused her of being mean and treating him like a worker. Besides her relationships with her daughter and ex-boyfriend, Molly had one of her worst friendship failures in 2022. Molly broke up with her friend, Cynthia Decker, whom she first met in the early 2000s. The two ladies were entrepreneurs and handled their brand well. However, Molly’s personality allegedly caused them to drift apart. Cynthia hinted that Molly’s personality caused the demise of their friendship.

5 Kimberly Rochelle Kimberly Fought With Her Brother-In-Law

When Kimberly Rochelle debuted in the series, she was eager to marry her India-born partner, Tejaswi “TJ” Goswami. However, her behavior changed once she realized she had to follow Indian traditions and be part of her husband’s culture. Kimberly failed to maintain a good relationship with TJ and his family. She had many arguments with her brother-in-law, Yash, about the smallest things. Similarly, she disappointed her mother-in-law by wearing unacceptable outfits and not being considerate. The Alabama resident complained so much throughout her journey on the show that TJ asked her to return to the United States.

4 Jasmine Pineda Jasmine Keeps Ruining Her Romantic Relationships

Jasmine Pineda is another cast member who hasn’t been able to handle any of her relationships. TJasmine’s first marriage ended in divorce, and she dropped the ball with her ex-boyfriend, who truly cared for her. When Jasmine debuted on the 90 Day Fiancé spin-off, she pursued her American boyfriend, Gino Palazzolo. While he truly loved her, she ruined her relationship by being toxic. Over the months, viewers have seen Jasmine lie about cheating on Gino. She used her ex to get butt implants and Gino to pay for her apartment. She even complained when Gino invited her to the U.S. on a K-1 visa, stating that Michigan was too cold.

3 Natalie Mordovtseva Natalie Doesn’t Have Many Strong Relationships

Initially, Natalie Mordovtseva seemed like a mature woman who knew what she wanted from her relationship with her husband, Mike Youngquist. Unfortunately, she disappointed everyone by proving herself to be one of the most immature cast members in the franchise. Natalie ran away from Mike for a minor reason and proceeded to embarrass herself on social media with her posts. She later pursued meaningless relationships with other men on 90 Day: The Single Life and continued dating Josh Weinstein. Natalie hasn’t formed a good relationship since moving to the United States. Mike no longer wants her, Mike’s mother hates her, and Josh only wants to use her.

2 Jamal Menzies Jamal Has Poor Communication Skills

Jamal Menzies won every fan over with his quirky personality. He debuted as a supporting cast member in his mother Kimberly Menzies’ journey with Nigerian rapper Usman “Sojaboy” Umar. Unfortunately, Jamal hurt his reputation when he began dating his co-star, Veronica Rodriguez, knowing she was looking for a husband. He can’t give her what she wants but continues to be with her casually. Jamal isn’t too serious about his relationship with Veronica and doesn’t give her enough time. He doesn’t pay attention to her like a true gentleman does. Instead, he doesn’t reply to her and only visits her on occasion.

1 Paul Staehle Paul Is A Terrible Father, Son, & Husband

The franchise had many terrible cast members, but Paul Staehle probably takes the cake for being the worst. The father of two married his Brazilian wife, Karine Martins, a few years ago. He acted like he cared for her but soon revealed his true colors. Paul has been a part of many criminal cases since his debut on 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days season 1. He was once accused of kidnapping his son, using Karine for money, and causing the entire internet to believe Paul was dead. Paul hasn’t been able to handle any relationship with his wife, sons, parents, or even fans. 90 Day Fiancé airs Sundays at 8 p.m. EST on TLC.

90 Day Fiance
90 Day Fiance is a reality TV series that follows the trials and tribulations of Non-U.S. citizens who travel from abroad each season to meet their potential spouses utilizing a K-1 visa. This three-month visa gives the pair 90 days to determine whether or not their romantic and life goals are aligned before they’re forced to return home unmarried. Drama and tension unfold as the couples navigate the tricky dynamics of international marriage.
