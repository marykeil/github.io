Literature Review

Vanderpump Rules star Tom Sandoval appeared on Nick Viall’s podcast, Viall Files, and proved that he hasn’t changed after his affair went public.

Vanderpump Rules star Tom Sandoval’s most recent podcast appearance on Viall Files proved that he hasn’t changed at all after sharing some of his most genuine cringey thoughts surrounding his affair with Raquel Leviss. As Vanderpump Rules season 11 gets started, many have been wondering how Tom would handle the criticism he’s faced throughout the months the show has been off the air. Tom, whose long-term partner Ariana Madix found out he was cheating on her with Raquel during a performance of his band when his phone fell out of his pants pocket, has claimed to take ownership of his actions, though he has yet to prove it.

During an interview with former Bachelor Nick Viall on Viall Files, Tom made some comments about his affair with Raquel, proving that though he’s claimed to have changed post-scandal, he truly hasn’t. Tom shared that while he “[regrets] it, hardcore, I really do,” he cheated on Ariana with Raquel because he was looking for validation. “[I] acted out because I have such low self-worth,” Tom shared. “I’m just trying to be a better person, I swear to God, like I really am…What have I learned?! To not ever do that again…To not ever, like, cheat that way.”

Tom Sandoval Claims He’s “Ready To Protect Somebody’s Heart”

While Tom explained that he’s changed throughout the process of dealing with such a public scandal, he believes he’s not the person who the media has been portraying him to be. Nick, who’s had some very public relationship drama within Bachelor Nation, asked Tom point blank if he’d be ready to “protect somebody’s heart” in a relationship moving forward. “Am I ready to protect somebody’s heart? Yes, of course I am," Tom exclaimed. “I don’t know what to say except I fed up, and I know that I did that. It doesn’t matter the scenario…I fed up, and I do know that. I do love hard, I really do.

Although Tom seems to be struggling to come to terms with the public persona he’s been made to wear after his major scandal, the Vanderpump Rules star shared that he’s happy to see his ex-girlfriend thriving. Ariana, who has been vocal about the fact that she’s gone no-contact with Tom, has been doing incredible things since her split with Tom. From her stint on Dancing With The Stars season 32 to her current role as Roxie Hart in Chicago on Broadway, Ariana has been fulfilling her dreams while welcoming new love into her life. Even though Tom broke her heart, Ariana has been doing better than ever.

With Tom working hard to deflect on Viall Files, it’s clear that he’s having difficulty shedding the image he’s had for the better part of the last year. Despite knowing that he’s been playing the villain in his own story for quite some time, Tom hasn’t done the work to prove that he’s not still convinced he was in the right to cheat on his girlfriend of nearly a decade. Though Tom’s trying to speak up about his behavior and work to change his image, if he still believes a lot of what he says about his actions being justifiable, the Vanderpump Rules star is in for the long haul.
