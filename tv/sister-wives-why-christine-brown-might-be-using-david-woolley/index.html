Sister Wives Why Christine Brown Might Be Using David Woolley

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1300 words 6 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Christine Brown may have married David for revenge, seeking to make her ex-husband Kody feel as miserable as she did. Christine constantly posts cuddly pictures with David on social media, possibly using their relationship to gain more views and promote her weight loss brand. Christine’s wedding with David lacked traditional vows and focused more on herself, suggesting that she prioritized her own goals over making him feel special.

Literature Review

Christine Brown could be genuinely in love with David Woolley, but her actions suggest she’s also taking advantage of his gentle and loving nature.

Sister Wives star Christine Brown may have married David Woolley for personal gains and ulterior motives. The 51-year-old reality TV star was once in a stable polygamous relationship with the show’s lead star, Kody Brown. She had six children with him and seemed content with her life. Unfortunately, the happy days ended for Christine when Kody married Robyn Brown and began spending all his time with her. He stopped tending to his other wives as a husband and caring about their emotional needs. He also started purposely offending Christine and his other partners, Meri and Janella Brown, making them look worse than Robyn.

Christine was initially saddened by how her life changed. However, she slowly recovered from the heartache and rediscovered herself as a person. She gave Kody multiple chances to prove himself but eventually decided to dump him because he didn’t reciprocate. In 2021, Christine left her polygamist husband and his big family. She returned to the dating game and found her soulmate, David, whom she introduced on social media in early 2023. Christine achieved countless milestones with her new beau in the last few months. She not only married him in the most perfect ceremony but bought a house with him.

Christine Brown Appears To Be Using David For Revenge

From a distance, it looks like Christine loves David and wants to live her fantasies with him. However, some of her actions suggest she may have married him to exact revenge. The Sister Wives star was previously in a one-sided relationship with Kody. She felt worthless as his wife and never got the love she deserved. When Christine left Kody in 2021, she took a big risk and betrayed her polygamous commitment. She got criticism from everyone, including Kody, who continually reminded her she wasn’t doing the right thing by turning her back on her religion and following her heart.

Christine felt hurt over the last thirteen years with Kody. Therefore, she’s doing everything possible to make him feel as miserable as she did. When Christine found David a few months ago, she immediately showed him off on social media, knowing Kody wouldn’t feel good. Soon after, she achieved various milestones with him, knowing that her former husband wouldn’t like hearing about it. In 2023, Christine even got TLC to give her two special episodes of her wedding with David, making him a reality star. If Christine truly loved David, she would’ve waited to bring him to the show or televised her new romantic journey.

Christine Brown Constantly Posts Cuddly Pictures With David

Christine and David seem like two love birds who can’t get enough of each other. They have shared dozens of posts over the last twelve months and flaunted that they’ve got a perfect monogamous relationship. From family celebration videos to traveling photos, the two have built a memoir of their adventures on social media that is visible to everyone. While the new couple’s content is cute and cuddly, it’s strange how much Christine has shared in a year. She only posted a few photos or promotional posts in the past. However, she has suddenly become aggressively active on social media and utilized her relationship with David, probably to get more views.

Christine’s social media posts hint that she’s using her marriage and new relationship to get more eyes on her other content. She has been promoting her weight loss brand too much and rebranding herself as a confident and positive female influencer. Christine even televised her marriage on TLC, ensuring that viewers checked her new life out and she stayed relevant. Christine and Meri have also left Kody Brown. However, they never try to seek affirmation or discuss much about their relationships on social media. However, Christine has truly changed herself, almost making it look like she’s using David to stay in the limelight.

Christine Brown Had A Wedding Without Vows

Christine has dropped a few more hints over the months that show she probably cares more about herself than David. In early 2024, she talked to InTouch and shared that her wedding was unconventional. The Sister Wives cast member explained that she didn’t pick bridesmaids or groomsmen, like a traditional wedding, and opted for family members instead. She claimed she wanted to keep the event short and sweet and didn’t want anyone with her up there. Christine also didn’t make any vows to David like most wives do. Instead, she simply had the wedding that looked great in pictures and videos, with her getting all the attention.

If Christine loved David as much as she claims, she wouldn’t have made her wedding all about herself. Instead, she would’ve tried to make him feel more special and ensured the day was memorable. The lack of vows is one of the biggest red flags in Christine and David’s relationship. Most people want to say special words or make sweet promises to make their significant others happy. However, Christine just wanted to fulfill her own goals. If she had truly felt for David, she would’ve found a way to say the vows. After all, no wedding is complete without this sweet and romantic tradition.

Christine Brown Married David Soon After Meeting Him

Most relationships develop and end over time. When Christine met Kody in the 90s, she didn’t immediately fall in love with him. Instead, she married into his family, became part of his life, and had kids with him. She lived with him for nearly thirty years before realizing she didn’t love him, and he didn’t either. In comparison, Christine found David and tied the knot with him within a year. She spent no major time with him before deciding to marry him. Instead, Christine acted on a whim and started claiming he was her soulmate. She didn’t just marry him but bought a home with him.

Christine’s journey with David seems shady and impractical. It looks like she quickly tied the knot with the man to become the stand-out star of Sister Wives. If Christine hadn’t married David, most viewers would be talking about Janelle and Meri, thinking about their next moves and new lives. Christine got all the attention by having her dream wedding and letting TLC televise it. It seems she tried to take the limelight from Janelle and Meri, who both divorced Kody last year. If she truly loved David, she would’ve dated him for a while before deciding to tie the knot in the Sister Wives special episodes.
