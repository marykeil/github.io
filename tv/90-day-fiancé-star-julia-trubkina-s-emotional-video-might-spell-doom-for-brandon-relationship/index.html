Literature Review

90 Day Fiancé star Julia Trubkina is crying on her Instagram Stories again. The Russian cast member, however, is not telling her fans what happened.

Former 90 Day Fiancé star Julia Trubkina crying in her recent cryptic social media post suggests she and Brandon Gibbs could be headed towards a divorce. Julia is a Russian 90 Day Fiancé cast member from Krasnodar. She worked as a go-go dancer when Brandon, who’s from Virginia, was introduced to her. Julia moved to the U.S. in 90 Day Fiancé season 8. She began living on Brandon’s farm, where the couple got zero privacy thanks to Brandon’s parents. Julia and Brandon got married and moved out but have since faced several challenges in their life off-camera. Julia faced a deportation threat, while she and Brandon argued about her job and baby plans.

Although Julia has not been a part of 90 Day Fiancé for more than a year, she keeps her fans updated via Instagram. She shares the highs and lows of her life on her account, but most of Julia’s recent Stories have suggested she’s unhappy in her life. Julia recently shared a crying selfie captioned, “Do you want to hear a funny story? When I calm down I’ll tell you,” (as shared by Miz_momo82 on Reddit).

Julia seemingly received a lot of messages from her followers, which made her post another video that showed her crying. She said, “I have a thousand messages now. So…I’m not okay, I’m not okay. I’m just down.” Julia wept as she explained she was feeling “down” and asked her fans to let her calm down.

She revealed she would talk to them the next day after a break. Julia wiped her tears and told her fans she would be “back tomorrow” with the answers. In July 2022, Julia claimed she was “cut” from the franchise. However, she did appear on 90 Day Diaries with Brandon the same year.
