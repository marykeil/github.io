SUMMARY

Amazon Prime Video offers a vast library of content, including thousands of on-demand movies and series from every era. The streaming service continues to expand its collection with new shows and movies added almost daily. Prime Video has the resources and backing of Amazon to produce high-quality original content, with Emmy-winning shows and big-name directors contributing to its success.

Literature Review

Prime Video has one of the largest streaming catalogs, and this is every movie and TV show coming to the Amazon service this month.

There are plenty of new movies & TV shows arriving on Amazon Prime Video in February 2024. First launched in 2006, Amazon Prime Video quickly proved itself to be so much more than a repository of content, and it even began producing its own shows and movies as well. Backed by thousands of on-demand movies and series from every era, Amazon Prime Video is one of the largest streaming services available. Despite its already massive collection of content to stream, Amazon Prime Video continues to grow each month as new shows and movies are added almost daily.

With backing from a massive entity like Amazon, Prime Video has almost unlimited resources at its disposal when it comes to producing original content. Some of the best Amazon Prime Video original films are on the level with theatrical Hollywood productions, and big-name directors have worked with the studio. Emmy Award-winning shows like The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel have also proven Amazon Prime Video’s success in the realm of original content, and new Prime-exclusive shows and movies drop on the platform all the time. Exciting new projects and old fan favorites on Prime Video this month offer something for everyone.

              The 30 Best Movies On Amazon Prime Right Now (January 2024)   In a vast landscape of streaming, Prime Video may very well have the best selection of movies around, and there are some great movies on the service.

The first of the month usually sees the highest number of new entries being added to Amazon Prime Video and that is once again the case in February. There will be over 50 new movie titles added to the streaming service on February 1, expanding the extensive library of acquired titles. Interestingly, while there are some recent big-name titles coming later in the month, February 1 features

a great collection of older movies that could be introduced to some users for the first time.Some of the standout titles include David Lynch’s heartbreaking and riveting biographical movie

The Elephant Man about a man born with a deformity that makes him regarded as a sideshow “freak” as he searches for acceptance from humanity. There is also the hugely entertaining period crime movie

The Great Train Robbery , the influential rom-com

Annie Hall, and the legal drama

12 Angry Men, which is often regarded as one of

the greatest movies ever made .For more modern hits, there is a wide variety of titles for Prime users of all tastes. Horror fans will have Jordan Peele’s masterpiece

Get Out. Those looking for laughs can enjoy Will Ferrell’s NASCAR comedy

Talladega Nights . There are even some cult favorites such as Edgar Wright’s

Scott Pilgrim vs. the World .

February 2 A New Action-Comedy Series Starring Donald Glover And Maya Erskine

Mr. & Mrs. Smith (2024)

One of the most anticipated titles coming to Amazon Prime Video in February is the

television reboot of the action movie Mr. & Mrs. Smith. Following the end of

Atlanta , Donald Glover took on an unexpected new project in this reimagining with Glover and Maya Erskine playing assassins paired together on a mission where they must pose as a married couple. It will be interesting to see what this new approach will be like, but with the two talented leads and

supporting cast members that include Paul Dano, Sarah Paulson, and John Turturro , it is an enticing new series.

February 6 An R-Rated Talking-Animals Movie

Strays (2023) Surrounded (2023)

Two movies from 2023 arrive on Amazon Prime Video on February 6. The R-rated comedy

Strays takes a wild and vulgar approach to the talking animals genre with this hilarious story of a dog who is abandoned by his worthless owner and strikes up a friendship with a gang of fellow strays. The

Strays voice cast includes

Will Ferrell, Jamie Foxx, and Isla Fisher who add a lot of humor to the wacky story. Also arriving is the Western drama

Surrounded starring Letitia Wright as a woman who must fend off a gang or outlaws.

February 8 Reese Witherspoon Stars In A Comedy Drama

The Silent Service (2024) Home Again (2017)

Two titles will be coming to Prime Video on February 8. Reese Witherspoon stars in

Home Again , a comedy-drama in which she plays a single mother whose chaotic life is thrown into even more chaos as three young men move into her house. This is also joined by the new Japanese thriller

The Silent Service about Japan’s first nuclear submarine.

February 9 A New Rom-Com And A Bill Murray Vehicle

St. Vincent (2014) Upgraded (2024)

A

new Prime Video Original movie arrives on February 9.

Upgraded is a romantic comedy starring Camila Mendes as a young professional who goes on a work trip to London where she is upgraded to a first-class ticket. After meeting a man during the flight, she fabricates a life for herself to impress him with her lies gradually spinning out of control. This new rom-com will also be joined by the comedy

St. Vincent which stars Bill Murray as a cynical war veteran who becomes the unlikely mentor to his young neighbor.

February 13 An R-Rated Comedy And A Mission: Impossible Entry

Bottoms (2023) Five Blind Dates (2024) Mission: Impossible - Rogue Nation (2015)

One of the most acclaimed comedy movies of last year arrives on Amazon Prime Video on February 13.

Bottoms is an outrageous comedy

starring Rachel Sennott and Ayo Edebiri as two outcast high school girls who attempt to get closer to the popular girls they have crushes on by starting a female fight club at school. It is a wild premise that allows the movie to have fun with a somewhat elevated sense of reality.Bottoms will be joined by the fifth entry in the

Mission: Impossible movie franchise which is notable for being the first directed by Christopher McQuarrie who has taken over the series since. Finally, the Prime Original rom-com

Five Blind Dates also debuts on February 13.

February 16 Jennifer Lopez’s Ambitious Project And A Supernatural Horror Movie

Dark Harvest (2023) This Is Me…Now: A Love Story (2024)

February 16 will see the debut of what is sure to be one of the most interesting projects on Prime Video this month.

This Is Me…Now: A Love Story is a

passion project from Jennifer Lopez which she co-wrote as an exploration of her career and life. The movie takes a loose and ambitious narrative approach to telling this epic story while also featuring the likes of Keke Palmer, Trevor Noah, and Ben Affleck. It will be joined by the horror movie

Dark Harvest in which a ritual unleashes a deadly evil force on a small town.

February 19 A Sports Documentary

Giannis: The Marvelous Journey (2024)

A new sports documentary arrives on Prime Video on February 19.

Giannis: The Marvelous Journey is the story of Giannis Antetokounmpo, the

NBA champion and league MVP who rose from obscurity in the streets of Athens to reach the heights of basketball superstardom.

February 21 The Latest TMNT Movie

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Mutant Mayhem (2023)

February 21 will be the debut of the latest Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles movie,

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Mutant Mayhem . The animated movie is a funny, action-packed, and charming take on the heroes in a half shell which explores their struggles as young teenagers who cannot experience the real world as they are considered monsters. With its talented voice cast and fun-loving approach, many fans have called this the

best Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles movie to date with there already being high anticipation for the sequel.

February 23 An Animated Series, A Korean Series, And A Comedy Special

Apartment404 (2024) Jenny Slate: Seasoned Professional (2024) Soltos em Salvador S4 (2024) The Second Best Hospital in The Galaxy (2024)

Four titles will be added to Prime Video on February 23 including another exciting entry into Amazon’s animated release schedule.

The Second Best Hospital in the Galaxy is an animated comedy series featuring the voices of Maya Rudolph and Natasha Lyonne as alien surgeons investigating the mysteries of the universe through their medical cases. It also features the voice talents of Kieran Culkin and Keke Palmer.February 23 will also include a new stand-up comedy special from Jenny Slate with

Seasoned Professional . There will also be two new international titles with the South Korean series

Apartment404 and the Brazilian reality series

Soltos em Salvador. February 29 A Zombie Horror-Comedy

Red Queen (2024) Scouts Guide To The Zombie Apocalypse (2015)

The final two titles for the month are coming to Prime Video on February 29. The horror-comedy

Scouts Guide To The Zombie Apocalypse follows a group of young boy scouts on their last campout who deal with friendship and growing older as they attempt to save their town from a zombie outbreak. Finally, the Spanish series Red Queen arrives, based on the trilogy series by Juan Gómez-Jurado about the world’s smartest woman who is sucked into a vast conspiracy.
