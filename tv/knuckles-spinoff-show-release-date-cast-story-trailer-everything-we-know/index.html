The Sonic the Hedgehog franchise continues on with the upcoming Knuckles TV show. After being teased at the end of the first movie, Knuckles made his big screen debut in Sonic the Hedgehog 2 with Idris Elba voicing the fan-favorite character. The red echidna creature began as an enemy to Sonic only to team up with him in the end, setting the stage for Knuckles’ story to continue on the small screen with the Knuckles TV show ahead of Sonic the Hedgehog 3. While the Sonic franchise has spawned several popular supporting characters, including Tails, Shadow, and Amy Rose, arguably the biggest star of the series next to Sonic himself has been Knuckles. It took a bit for Knuckles to make his Sonic debut though, as the echidna didn’t arrive on the scene until 1994’s Sonic the Hedgehog 3 video game, although he quickly rose to co-protagonist status in the follow-up Sonic & Knuckles. It’s not surprising that Knuckles quickly joined Sonic’s big screen adventures and now there is hope that he can headline his own story in the Knuckles TV show. Knuckles Show Latest News A Trailer is Revealed

Helping to drum up even more interest in the Sonic franchise’s small-screen debut, the trailer for the Knuckles TV show has been revealed. Besides showing off the character, the teaser also reveals a bit of the plot of the show as Knuckles attempts to put the events of Sonic 2 behind him, but finds himself face-to-face with one of Dr. Robotnik’s former acolytes who hope to seize power. The trailer also hints at the emotional struggle that Knuckles faces as he has a hard time balancing his life as a warrior and his new life on earth.

Knuckles Show Release Date The Echidna Warrior Arrives In April

Releasing ahead of the highly anticipated Sonic the Hedgehog 3 , the Knuckles show will premiere on April 26th, 2024 on Paramount+. The series will drop all six episodes at the same time and will be available to binge-watch only on Paramount’s signature streamer.

Knuckles Show Cast New & Returning Faces

The Knuckles TV show cast will be led by Idris Elba with his performance in Sonic the Hedgehog 2 being a fan-favorite aspect of the sequel. It was confirmed that there would be other returning Sonic the Hedgehog characters joining him with Adam Pally as Wade playing a big role. Tika Sumpter will also reprise her role as Maddie in the series. Ben Schwartz will return as the voice of Sonic the Hedgehog alongside Coleen O’Shaughnessey as Miles “Tails” Prower. Knuckles Show Story Details What Will Happen?

The Knuckles TV show story will center on Knuckles as he “agrees to train Wade as his protégé and teach him the ways of the Echidna warrior.” It also features the return of a former Robotnik follower, The Buyer, as he attempts to harness Knuckles’ power for himself. Most importantly, the series will bridge the gap between the second and third movie, and serve as important character development as Knuckles attempts to fit in on earth after being trained for so long as a merciless warrior.

Knuckles Show Trailer The First Look At Knuckles

Just a few months before the show’s debut, Paramount+ revealed a trailer for the Knuckles TV show that shines a light on what fans can expect to see. Not only does the trailer introduce Knuckles and Wade’s burgeoning relationship as mentor and protégé, but it also establishes a new challenge for the echidna as he faces off against a former Robotnik henchman who wants to steal his power. Furthermore, the trailer gives insight into Knuckles’ emotional struggles as he tries to unlearn his lifetime of warrior training.
