Kung Fu Panda 4 Voice Cast & Character Guide What The Actors Look Like In Real Life

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1400 words 7 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Kung Fu Panda 4 features a star-studded ensemble cast of Hollywood actors who lend their voices to bring the eccentric characters to life. The film follows the ongoing adventures of Po, a panda who is the Dragon Warrior and must protect his town from evil spirits. The new installment introduces new characters like Zhen, Po’s sidekick, and the villainous shapeshifter, the Chameleon, voiced by Awkwafina and Viola Davis respectively.

Literature Review

The fourth Kung Fu Panda movie should see plenty of the existing cast reprising their roles for another exciting adventure with the Dragon Warrior.

Kung Fu Panda 4 is the fourth installment in DreamWorks Animation’s beloved family franchise, but given the nature of the characters, it’s not always obvious what the film’s voice actors look like in real life. The ensemble cast is made up of Hollywood stars who lend their voices to these eccentric characters, bringing them to life and giving them distinct personalities that allow the film’s story to work so effectively. From what’s already known about Kung Fu Panda 4, it’s clear that several existing members of the cast will be returning alongside plenty of new faces.

The Kung Fu Panda franchise tells the ongoing story of a panda named Po, whose interest in martial arts finds him selected to be the Dragon Warrior, a legendary prodigy said to have unlimited power. The series chronicles his adventures throughout this world, battling old enemies and making new friends along the way. The first trailer for Kung Fu Panda 4 is out now, showing off Po’s new sidekick Zhen, and the adventure that they have to go on to stop the villainous shapeshifter, the Chameleon.

Jack Black As Po

Actor: Jack Black, 54, was born in Santa Monica, California in 1969. The actor is best known for his work within the comedy genre. The actor’s breakout role was in 2000’s High Fidelity, with some of his other most famous roles including the enthusiastic teacher Dewey Finn in School of Rock, the titular wrestler in Nacho Libre, the video game character Professor Sheldon “Shelly” Oberon in the Jumanji movies, and photographer Carl Denham in Peter Jackson’s King Kong remake. He also voiced the villain Bowser in The Super Mario Bros. Movie.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: Mars Attacks! (1996), Tenacious D (1997-2000), High Fidelity (2000), School of Rock (2003), Shark Tale (2004), King Kong (2005), Nacho Libre (2006), Tenacious D in The Pick of Destiny (2006), Kung Fu Panda (2008), Tropic Thunder (2008), Goosebumps (2015), Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle (2017), The Super Mario Bros. Movie (2023), The Mandalorian (2023), Kung Fu Panda 4 (2024).

Character: Po is the protagonist of the Kung Fu Panda franchise, and it’s typically from his perspective that the stories are told. He’s the Dragon Warrior of the Valley of Peace, meaning it’s his responsibility to protect the town and keep away any evil spirits — this frequently sends him on wide-eyed adventures throughout ancient China. The third movie installment saw Po training a whole new generation of pandas in martial arts, adopting a more mentor-focused role that he carries forward into Kung Fu Panda 4.

Awkwafina As Zhen

Actor: Awkwafina, 35, was born in Stony Brooks, New York in 1988. Her role as Zhen adds to her long list of voice performances for the actress, who previously played Skuttle in 2023’s The Little Mermaid, Sisu in Raya and the Last Dragon, and Otto in The Spongebob Movie: Sponge on the Run. Awkwafina has also provided critically acclaimed performances in live-action dramas, such as The Farewell, where she played protagonist Billi. On top of that, she famously starred as the title character’s best friend Katy in the MCU’s Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: Tawk (2015-2017), Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising (2016), Ocean’s 8 (2018), Crazy Rich Asians (2018), The Farewell (2019), The Angry Birds Movie 2 (2019), Jumanji: The Next Level (2019), Awkwafina Is Nora from Queens (2020-present), Raya and the Last Dragon (2021), Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings (2021), The Bad Guys (2022), Renfield (2023), The Little Mermaid (2023), Quiz Lady (2023), Migration (2023), Kung Fu Panda 4 (2024).

Character: Awkwafina will be playing a corsac fox named Zhen in Kung Fu Panda 4, with the character enlisting Po’s help on an all-new adventure. Zhen is a master thief with wanted posters plastered all over, causing her to have an interesting dynamic with the Dragon Warrior, one that will be expanded upon in the final film.

Viola Davis As The Chameleon

Actor: Viola Davis, 58, was born in St. Matthews, South Carolina in 1965. Viola Davis started as a stage actor, with her finding much success in various plays throughout the start of her career. Davis’ breakout role was in 2008’s Doubt, and although it was small, it left a big enough impression to get her nominated for Best Supporting Actress at the Academy Awards. Davis has continued to have major roles in art films like Fences as well as major franchises like the DCU and now Kung Fu Panda.

Notable Movies & TV Shows:Doubt (2008), The Help (2011), Prisoners (2013), How to Get Away With Murder (2014-2020), Suicide Squad (2016), Fences (2016), Widows (2018), Ma Rainey’s Black Bottom (2020), The Suicide Squad (2021), Peacemaker (2022), The Woman King (2022), Black Adam (2022), Air (2023), The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes (2023), Kung Fu Panda 4 (2024).

Character: Viola Davis will be voicing Kung Fu Panda 4’s new villain, The Chameleon. The Chameleon is a powerful shapeshifting sorceress who has the ability to absorb the abilities of others, making her one of Po’s deadliest enemies yet. The Chameleon hopes to absorb the spirits of past Kung Fu Panda villains, giving her the power to take down the Dragon Warrior and accomplish her evil desires.

Dustin Hoffman As Master Shifu

Actor: Dustin Hoffman, 86, was born in Los Angeles, California in 1937. Hoffman had his big break as the star of 1967’s The Graduate, with him quickly going on to star in other popular art films of the era like Midnight Cowboy and All the President’s Men. Hoffman’s appearances in films have become more sparse in the past few decades, but the first Kung Fu Panda movie featured a rare voice role for the actor, with him reprising his character in all four films so far.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: The Graduate (1967), Midnight Cowboy (1969), All The President’s Men (1976), Kramer vs. Kramer (1979), Tootsie (1982), Rain Man (1988), Meet the Fockers (2004), Kung Fu Panda (2008), Chef (2014), Kung Fu Panda 4 (2024).

Character: Master Shifu is one of the main characters in the Kung Fu Panda franchise, with him being a red panda who is the senior master of the Jade Palace. Although Shifu initially doubted Po’s potential as the Dragon Warrior, he eventually accepted it and has become a faithful ally of the titular panda since. Shifu will once again be a main character in Kung Fu Panda 4, helping Po continue on his spiritual journey.

Kung Fu Panda 4’s Supporting Characters

Kung Fu Panda 4 also has all kinds of supporting characters, with many reprising their roles from the previous films. James Hong will return as Mr. Ping, Bryan Cranston as Li Shan, and Ian McShane as Tai Lung. The film will also feature Ke Huy Quan as Han, Lori Tan Chinn as Granny Boar, and Ronny Chieng as an unnamed fish character. There is also speculation surrounding if characters like the Furious Five will return, with the franchise’s other beloved icons hopefully returning in Kung Fu Panda 4 as well.
