Oscars 2024 Best Picture Predictions Nominees & Winner

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 2700 words 13 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

The 2024 Oscars race for Best Picture is competitive, with Christopher Nolan’s Oppenheimer and Martin Scorsese’s Killers of the Flower Moon leading the pack. Past Lives, despite being a critically acclaimed movie, received the lowest number of nominations among the Best Picture nominees. Anatomy of a Fall, Maestro, American Fiction, and The Zone of Interest are also strong contenders, but face challenges in key categories like Best Director and Best Actress.

Literature Review

The official Oscars 2024 nominations have been announced, including the 10 Best Picture nominees, and here’s a prediction of who will win.

The Oscars 2024 race is nearing its conclusion, and the official list of Best Picture nominations gives a few movies the hope of winning the Academy Awards’ top prize. Hollywood’s biggest awards ceremony has undergone significant changes in recent years that have brought in an expanded and more diverse voting body, adding more variety in genre and style to Best Picture than before. With 10 guaranteed nominees every year now, early predictions for the 2024 Oscars nominees put Christopher Nolan’s Oppenheimer and Martin Scorsese’s Killers of the Flower Moon as frontrunners. Many other 2023 movies went through the awards season process of jockeying and campaigning for the hopes of earning a Best Picture nomination in January 2024. All the precursor awards and pundit predictions outlined a strong group of favorites ahead of the official 2024 Oscar nomination announcements. It came as no surprise that Oppenheimer and Killers of the Flower Moon received nominations. Now that the full list of 2024 Best Picture Oscar nominees are known, only one will emerge as the winner. 10 Past Lives

Past Lives

Release Date January 21, 2023 Director Celine Song Cast Greta Lee , Teo Yoo , John Magaro

Past Lives has been pinpointed as a likely 2024 Oscars contender since before the 2023 ceremony even took place. The emotional romantic drama had its debut at the Sundance Film Festival in January 2023, which is where the first wave of rave reviews came from. The movie had a slow rollout as it was taken to many other film festivals ahead of its June theatrical release. Past Lives is among the best-reviewed movies of 2023, and the 98% Rotten Tomatoes score started bringing in recognition, including a Best Motion Picture - Drama nomination from the Golden Globes. Its chances at the 2024 Oscars and winning Best Picture appear to be rather slim, though. Past Lives only received two nominations total, which is the lowest amount for any of the 2024 Oscars Best Picture nominees. Its Best Original Screenplay nomination is a big spot to get, but it would take winning that award to give Past Lives any real shot at taking home the top prize. Without a Celine Song nomination for Best Director or Greta Lee contending in Best Actress, Past Lives would have to do extremely well on the preferential ballot to win.

9 American Fiction

American Fiction

Release Date September 8, 2023 Director Cord Jefferson Cast Jeffrey Wright , Tracee Ellis Ross , John Ortiz , erika alexander , Leslie Uggams , Adam Brody​ , Keith David , Issa Rae , Sterling K. Brown Distributor(s) Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer

Amazon Studios and MGM have seen American Fiction earn a Best Picture Oscar nomination in 2024. The directorial debut of Cord Jefferson looks to be among the biggest crowd-pleasers of the year, one that is only bolstered by the strong performances of Jeffrey Wright and Sterling K. Brown. Its most notable awards season victory so far is winning the People’s Choice Award at TIFF following its premiere. The praise and adoration for American Fiction has only grown since then, including earning a Golden Globe nomination for Best Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy. American Fiction’s Best Picture winning chances are on the lower end of the spectrum. The movie performed quite well overall, earning five nominations in total. The Best Adapted Screenplay nomination would strengthen American Fiction’s case if it manages to pull off an upset in the category, while Jeffrey Wright and Sterling K. Brown’s acting nominations are thought to be near the back of the pack. Without Cord Jefferson sneaking into the Best Director race, American Fiction will need an incredibly strong campaign to change its fortune from here.

8 The Zone of Interest

The Zone of Interest

Release Date December 15, 2023 Director Jonathan Glazer Cast Christian Friedel , Sandra Hüller , Ralph Herforth , Luis Noah Witte , Johann Karthaus Distributor(s) A24

The return of Jonathan Glazer over a decade after his last movie helped position The Zone of Interest firmly in the Best Picture race. The historical drama focused on an Auschwitz commandant starting a life near a concentration camp has been heralded by critics for tackling difficult subjects and re-examining horrific events. The Zone of Interest had its world premiere at Cannes and continued to earn high praise at subsequent showings at Telluride and the Toronto International Film Festival. It then got a Golden Globe nomination for Best Motion Picture - Drama. The Zone of Interest performed quite well at the Oscar nominations overall, as Glazer’s movie earned five nominations. This included nominations for Best Adapted Screenplay, Best International Feature Film, and Best Director for Glazer. At this point, The Zone of Interest is more likely to win Best International Feature than Best Picture, but that could change if it can change the narrative in Adapted Screenplay or Director.

7 Anatomy Of A Fall

Anatomy of a Fall

Release Date August 23, 2023 Director Justine Triet Cast Sandra Hüller , Swann Arlaud , Milo Machado-Graner , Antoine Reinartz , Samuel Theis , Jehnny Beth , Saadia Bentaieb , Camille Rutherford , Anne Rotger , Sophie Fillières Distributor(s) Neon

Anatomy of a Fall is a strong contender in the 2024 Oscars Best Picture race. The hype for the French courtroom drama began in May 2023 after its debut at the 76th Cannes Film Festival. The reception was outstanding, propelling the movie to receive the festival’s top prize, the Palme d’Or. Winning the Palme d’Or has been a precursor to a Best Picture nomination, with two out of the last three winners - Triangle of Sadness and Parasite - doing so. With a 96% critics score on Rotten Tomatoes, Anatomy of a Fall became firmly entrenched in the running. The case for Anatomy of a Fall winning Best Picture was certainly strengthened during the Oscar nominations. The movie snagged crucial nominations in Best Original Screenplay and Film Editing, while Justine Triet surprised for Best Director and Sandra Hüller secured a Best Actress nomination. Anatomy of a Fall’s five nominations overall gives it a strong path to rise through the ranks and emerge as a legitimate contender to win if it can build on notable wins elsewhere, such as its Golden Globe win for Best Motion Picture, Non-English Language.

6 Maestro

Maestro

Release Date December 20, 2023 Director Bradley Cooper Cast Bradley Cooper , Carey Mulligan , Maya Hawke , Sarah Silverman , Matt Bomer Distributor(s) Netflix

Five years after A Star Is Born received a Best Picture nomination, Bradley Cooper’s biopic about the legendary composer Leonard Bernstein has repeated the same success. While it was always thought to be an awards contender, Maestro’s likelihood of receiving a Best Picture nomination at the 2024 Oscars increased with its debut at the Venice International Film Festival, where it was nominated for the top prize, the Golden Lion. Although the movie proved to be a bit more controversial upon its release, Maestro’s Golden Globe nomination for Best Motion Picture - Drama gave it additional support. Maestro managed to be on the higher side of total nominations among the 204 Best Picture nominees, as it earned seven total. This included key nominations in Best Original Screenplay and Cinematography, while Bradley Cooper and Carey Mulligan both secured acting nominations. Cooper’s miss in Best Director could be costly for the film’s chances of actually winning Best Picture, though. Since it is not viewed as a clear favorite in any major categories, Maestro will have an uphill battle to deliver a surprising win.

5 The Holdovers

The Holdovers

Release Date November 10, 2023 Director Alexander Payne Cast Paul Giamatti , Da’Vine Joy Randolph , Dominic Sessa , Carrie Preston Distributor(s) Focus Features

The Holdovers received its Best Picture Oscars nomination in 2024. Alexander Payne is no stranger to this success, as he delivered his first Best Picture nominee in 2012 with The Descendants. He has also won two Oscars for his screenplays, and The Holdovers is a strong contender for that award in 2024 too. From the initial reviews following the movie’s debut at Telluride to its Golden Globes nomination for Best Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy, The Holdovers was on the rise even before getting the coveted Best Picture nomination. The Holdovers managed to fall middle-of-the-pack in terms of total Oscar nominations among the Best Picture nominees with five nominations overall. Alexander Payne not being recognized in Best Director might be difficult to overcome, but The Holdovers has emerged as a frontrunner elsewhere. Da’Vine Joy Randolph is the predicted Best Supporting Actress winner, while Paul Giamatti is certainly in the Best Actor race. If both performances secure wins and The Holdovers can surprise in Film Editing, its Best Picture chances will become much more real.

4 Barbie

Barbie

Release Date July 21, 2023 Director Greta Gerwig Cast Margot Robbie , Ryan Gosling , Simu Liu , Ariana Greenblatt , Helen Mirren , Nicola Coughlan , John Cena , Will Ferrell , Ritu Arya , Michael Cera , America Ferrera , Alexandra Shipp , Kate McKinnon Distributor(s) Warner Bros. Pictures

The overall success of Barbie translated to a 2024 Oscars nomination for Best Picture. The movie surpassed all box office expectations on its way to becoming the biggest movie of the year, and it now is on track to continue Greta Gerwig’s Oscars streak. Her previous two movies, Little Women and Lady Bird, both received Best Picture nominations under the older format. The support for Barbie’s 2024 Oscars campaign can be seen with its various love from critics circles to dominating the 2024 Golden Globes nominations, where it tied for the second most nominations by a single film in history. Still, Barbie’s performance at the 2024 Oscars nominations was both a success and cause for concern when it comes to winning Best Picture. It received eight nominations overall but Greta Gerwig and Margot Robbie were crucially snubbed for Best Director and Best Actress. The movie’s biggest nominations came from Best Adapted Screenplay and Ryan Gosling and America Ferrera being recognized for their performances. While not typical, perhaps Barbie’s multiple snubs will rally more support for it in Best Picture.

3 Killers of the Flower Moon

Killers of the Flower Moon

Director Martin Scorsese Cast Leonardo DiCaprio , Louis Cancelmi , Jillian Dion , Scott Shepherd , Jesse Plemons , JaNae Collins , Robert De Niro , Jason Isbell , Cara Jade Myers , Lily Gladstone Studio(s) Paramount Pictures Distributor(s) Apple , Paramount Pictures

Martin Scorsese has one of the strongest Best Picture contenders for the 2024 Oscars with Killers of the Flower Moon. Its premiere at Cannes led to a rapturous response heralding the true story as the latest Scorsese masterpiece, bringing all three of its leads to be viewed as locks for their own Oscar nominations. Martin Scorsese’s Oscars nomination history is significant, as he’s had nine movies nominated in the biggest category, but only The Departed won. The career legacy angle gives Killers of the Flower Moon a strong narrative to win. Killers of the Flower Moon was one of the biggest winners from the Oscar nominations, as it earned 10 nominations overall. The most important for its Best Picture chances are Film Editing and Martin Scorsese’s Best Director nomination. Lily Gladstone and Robert De Niro’s nominations will bolster its case, but Leonardo DiCaprio missing out will hurt, as will its snub from Best Adapted Screenplay. Killers of the Flower Moon is certainly still near the front of the pack when it comes to contending, even if it is not the predicted winner right now.

2 Poor Things

Poor Things

Release Date September 8, 2023 Director Yorgos Lanthimos Cast Emma Stone , Willem Dafoe , Mark Ruffalo , Ramy Youssef , Jerrod Carmichael , Christopher Abbott Distributor(s) Searchlight Pictures

Yorgos Lanthimos and Emma Stone have joined forces again to create one of the biggest contenders to win the 2024 Oscars’ Best Picture. Following their shared success with The Favourite in 2018, Poor Things has been lauded for its creativity and uniqueness ever since it premiered at Venice, where it won the prestigious Golden Lion award. The result greatly increased the chance of winning Best Picture. Recent Golden Lion winners that became Best Picture winners include Nomadland and The Shape of Water. Winning a Golden Globe for Best Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy should help too. All the precursors pointed to Poor Things’ dominance at the 2024 Oscars nominations. The movie earned 11 nominations overall, the second most for any film this year. This includes key nominees for Yorgos Lanthimos in Best Director, Emma Stone in Best Actress, Mark Ruffalo in Best Supporting Actor, Best Adapted Screenplay, Film Editing, and Cinematography. Its chances of winning any of these categories are still quite strong, especially as Poor Things’ campaign can grow and help the movie gain more momentum.

1 Oppenheimer (Winner)

Oppenheimer

Release Date July 21, 2023 Director Christopher Nolan Cast Cillian Murphy , Emily Blunt , Matt Damon , Robert Downey Jr. , Rami Malek , Florence Pugh Distributor(s) Universal Pictures

Oppenheimer winning Best Picture at the 2024 Oscars has been the most likely outcome for some time, and the Oscar nominations only reinforce that. Christopher Nolan’s biopic about J. Robert Oppenheimer is arguably his best movie to date. A Nolan movie has never won Best Picture, but his movies have only received nominations twice before. Oppenheimer’s case to win the award comes from the overall acclaim and success of the movie, which now includes a Best Motion Picture - Drama win from the Golden Globes. The 2024 Oscar nominations secured Oppenheimer’s place as the frontrunner for Best Picture as Nolan’s movie led the way with 13 nominations overall. This included Christopher Nolan in Best Director, Cillian Murphy in Best Actor, Emily Blunt in Best Supporting Actress, and Robert Downey Jr. in Best Supporting Actor, as well as nominations in Film Editing, Cinematography, Adapted Screenplay, and several other below-the-line categories. With Oppenheimer viewed as the favorite in many of these categories, winning Best Picture at the 2024 Oscars is lined up.
