90 Day Fiancé Signs Miona Bell Never Loved Jibri (He Missed All Of Them)

    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Miona Bell never tried to win over her in-laws, behaving like a stranger and showing minimal effort. Miona didn’t defend Jibri against trolls or show much appreciation for his love, focusing more on herself. By mocking Jibri’s financial condition and belittling him, Miona appeared selfish and unappreciative of his efforts.

Literature Review

90 Day Fiancé’s Miona Bell is smitten in love with her new boyfriend. Her latest social media actions suggest she never truly admired Jibri Bell.

Miona Bell from 90 Day Fiancé dropped various hints that she never cared for her husband, Jibri Bell. The 25-year-old Serbian woman debuted on the TLC franchise in early 2022. She joined 90 Day Fiancé season 9 to showcase her journey from Serbia to the United States. Initially, Miona seemed like a great fit for Jibri. She complimented his vibrant fashion, wore matching outfits, and seemed to care for him. She also looked impressed by Jibri’s personality and appeared on board with the idea of marrying him at such a young age.

During her 90 Day Fiancé season 9, Miona boldly told Jibri about her plans and goals. She revealed she wanted to move to Palm Springs and build a new life there. Jibri also seemed on board with the plans. He fought with his family and ultimately tied the knot with Miona in a barren wedding ceremony that his parents didn’t attend. In 2022, the couple moved to California and began working on their careers. Miona launched her hair extension business while Jibri supported her. The two seemed to be on a good note until mid-2023 when they sparked breakup rumors.

Miona Bell Didn’t Even Try To Win Her In-Laws Over

Miona has been flaunting a new partner on social media while Jibri is on his world tour. She has dropped hints that she has separated from her husband, which isn’t surprising to most fans. The first time Miona showed she didn’t care for Jibri was when she came to South Dakota and made zero efforts to win her in-laws over. Miona did nothing an ideal daughter-in-law would do for her soon-to-be family. Instead, she behaved like a stranger with them, which was a big red flag that Jibri overlooked. She also didn’t try to befriend her husband’s friends.

One of the scenes in 90 Day Fiancé season 9 showed Miona almost purposely cooking a bad meal for Jibri’s family. She said that she knew they wouldn’t like the frozen Alfredo, but she still served it to bring the point home that it was the best they could expect from her. If Miona had truly wanted to bond with Jibri’s family, she would’ve shown some effort and behaved nicely. However, she remained sarcastic and rude, proving she wasn’t a good daughter-in-law. When Jibri’s parents didn’t attend the wedding, Miona seemed unbothered. She only seemed to care about herself.

Miona Bell Never Defended Jibri Bell Against Trolls

People who love their significant other show it by supporting them in their journey and correcting them when they are wrong. However, Miona never tried to show that she cared about Jibri on a deeper level. In 2022, Jibri was criticized for his over-the-top style and demeanor. However, Miona never attempted to shut trolls down. In comparison, Jibri supported Miona till the end. He promoted her business, told fans to buy from her, and even helped her raise her business in its initial phase. Nowadays, Miona is sharing good words for her new man, something she never did for Jibri.

Miona Bell Never Seemed Appreciative Of Jibri’s Love

Mutual appreciation is very important in a relationship. However, Miona rarely ever showed her love for Jibri at all. People nowadays use social media to prove how much they love their partners. Jibri did this by cherishing Miona’s career and discussing his love for her. He put a lot of effort into her, invited her to the United States, and followed her dream of moving to California. He even gave up on his family and had a wedding without his mom to appease Miona. He left no stone unturned in appreciating her despite her flaws.

Unfortunately, Miona never did the same for Jibri. Over the months, the Serbian woman only talked about herself, her business, and her achievements. She rarely gave the time of her day to appreciate Jibri. She made more posts about her dog than she did about her husband. Miona has recently been talking about her new man on social media. On Valentine’s Day, the young entrepreneur posted a photo of herself surrounded by heart-shaped balloons, chocolates, a bouquet, a cake, and a teddy bear. She wrote, “woke up like this on Valentine’s Day I’m feeling so loved.”

Miona Bell Pit Jibri Against His Friends

When Miona debuted on 90 Day Fiancé, she pitted Jibri against his long-time friends, ruining his connections. In one of the episodes, Jibri’s friend David had a big argument with him about Miona and shared his concerns. He said, “I feel like she’s trying to have him for herself,” stating that Miona is stopping Jibri from being his real self. He added, “she’s, like, slowing him down so much. We can’t record! We can’t do nothing,” hinting that Miona used Jibri for herself, effectively ruining his career as a musician. Jibri no longer mentions his music career in his bio.

Miona Bell Seemingly Mocked Jibri’s Financial Condition

Miona has also mocked Jibri, showing that she’s all about herself. A few months ago, the beauty business owner shared an Instagram story where she talked about herself. According to 90dayfianceupdate, she titled the post “get to know me,” and shared details like her age, height, birthday, and more. In one of the points, Miona talked about her phobia and what irks her. She wrote “broke men,” insinuating that she likely didn’t love Jibri. Miona doesn’t like men who aren’t making bank. As viewers know, Jibri struggled with financial issues, which may have been the reason Miona left him.

Miona has since taken even more shots at Jibri, hinting at the reason behind their breakup. In a recent Instagram story, she said she wouldn’t have to demand anything from her man. Instead, he’d do anything without waiting for her to ask for something. Miona may have used the post to praise her new man and let fans know he had got her. However, she may have also shared it to take a shot at Jibri, hinting that he never understood her feelings. It’ll be interesting if Miona returns to the 90 Day Fiancé franchise to tell her story.
