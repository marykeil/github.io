Star Trek s Biggest Virtual Con & Awards Show Huge Guest Stars & Events Lined Up

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 800 words 4 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Virtual Trek Con 5 is an online convention that brings the Star Trek convention experience to fans on YouTube for free in February. The event features several days of events, games, live panels, and the 3rd annual LLAPy Awards, the only Star Trek awards show voted on by fans. The convention includes appearances from major Star Trek names such as Jonathan Frakes, Walter Koenig, Nana Visitor, and Denise Crosby, among others.

Literature Review

Many of the biggest names in Star Trek, as well as the LLAPys, Star Trek’s only awards show voted by fans, are coming to Virtual Trek Con 5.

Virtual Trek Con 5, Star Trek’s biggest online convention from the minds behind The 7th Rule podcast, is back in February with huge events and Star Trek guest stars. Star Trek conventions take place all over the world each year, with the biggest being August’s annual Creation convention in Las Vegas, dubbed STLV: Trek 2 Vegas this year. However, The 7th Rule’s producers and co-hosts Cirroc Lofton of Star Trek: Deep Space Nine and Ryan T. Husk, with Malissa Longo and their team, are aware many Star Trek fans can’t travel to conventions. Thus, they created Virtual Trek Con, which brings the Star Trek convention experience to fans on YouTube for free.

Taking place from February 15-19 on Virtual Trek Con’s YouTube, Virtual Trek Con 5 features several days of events, games, and live panels, culminating with the 3rd annual LLAPy Awards on Sunday, February 19th. The LLAPys are the first and only Star Trek awards show that is voted on by Star Trek fans.

Virtual Trek Con 5’s website will have the full lineup, and the major events include:

The premiere of Walter Koenig on The 7th Rule. The legendary actor joins Cirroc Lofton and Ryan T. Husk on The 7th Rule podcast to review episodes of Star Trek: The Original Series. Virtual Trek Con 5 will premiere 2 episodes of Walter Koenig on The 7th Rule reviewing Star Trek’s original unaired pilot, “The Cage,” and Star Trek: The Original Series’ season 2 premiere, “Amok Time,” which is the first appearance of Walter Koenig as Ensign Pavel Chekov. The 3rd Annual LLAPy Awards. Star Trek’s only awards show voted on by the fans, with celebrity guests, and 3 special awards: The Lifetime Achievement Award, The Aron Eisenberg Community Award, and The Kenneth Mitchell Hope Award. “Yesterday’s Enterprise” - Live Episode of The 7th Rule. Denise Crosby returns to The 7th Rule to review the classic Star Trek: The Next Generation season 3 episode “Yesterday’s Enterprise” in a special live podcast.

In addition, many of the biggest names in Star Trek will appear at Virtual Trek Con 5, including:

Jonathan Frakes, Walter Koenig, Nana Visitor, Armin Shimerman, Denise Crosby, John de Lancie, Cirroc Lofton, Bonnie Gordon, Robin Curtis, Kitty Swink, Lolita Fatjo, John Eaves, Dr. Mohamed Noor, David Gerrold, Juan Carlos Coto, and Dr. Abigail Fraeman.

Virtual Trek Con 5’s theme of “The Cage” celebrates 60 years of Star Trek in 2024, since “The Cage” began filming in 1964 soon after NBC gave Gene Roddenberry’s Star Trek the green light.

The Sisko Day Returns In May 2024 The 7th Rule celebrates Star Trek: Deep Space Nine’s Avery Brooks

In May 2023, The 7th Rule created The Sisko Day, a celebration of Star Trek: Deep Space Nine’s Captain Benjamin Sisko and the actor who portrayed him, Avery Brooks. The Sisko Day honors Captain Sisko in the same vein as Captain Picard Day, the annual June tribute to Star Trek: The Next Generation’s Captain Jean-Luc Picard (Patrick Stewart). The Sisko Day trended for 2 days on Twitter, as fans wrote tributes to Captain Sisko that his TV son, Cirroc Lofton, who played Jake Sisko, delivered to Avery Brooks.

Captain Sisko’s absence from Star Trek since the series finale of Star Trek: Deep Space Nine in 1999 has been glaring, especially since Patrick Stewart returned in Star Trek: Picard and Kate Mulgrew reprised Star Trek: Voyager’s Captain Kathryn Janeway on Star Trek: Prodigy. While it’s quite likely that Avery Brooks has retired from Star Trek, the impact of Captain Sisko on Star Trek: DS9 fans has never abated and continues to resonate. The 7th Rule created a way for Star Trek fans to give tribute to Captain Sisko each year, and it remains to be seen how The Sisko Day 2024 will be bigger than the inaugural event.

Virtual Trek Con 5 happens February 15-19 on YouTube.
