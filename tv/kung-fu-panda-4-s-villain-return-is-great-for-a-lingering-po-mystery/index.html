Kung Fu Panda 4 s Villain Return Is Great For A Lingering Po Mystery

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 700 words 4 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Kung Fu Panda 4 will feature multiple returning villains and may solve the mystery of Po’s background. Director Mike Mitchell confirmed that all major characters from the previous films will return, including potential villain Lord Shen. Lord Shen’s appearance could help to clear up the ambiguity surrounding Po’s mother’s death.

Literature Review

Multiple villains will likely appear in the upcoming Kung Fu Panda 4 movie, and one character may be the key to understanding a massive Po mystery.

Confirmation of multiple villains returning for the upcoming Kung Fu Panda 4 movie indicates that the new sequel might set out to clarify a lingering mystery about Po. The substantial gap in time since Po’s last adventure, depicted in 2016’s Kung Fu Panda 3, has raised waves of fan anticipation for the giant panda’s next great caper to hit big screens. Tantalizing details about the sequel, set for release in March 2024, have cropped up here and there to suggest that the Kung Fu Panda 4 movie may be the most exciting installment yet.

Not long ago, Kung Fu Panda 4 director Mike Mitchell teased that the newest sequel would reprise droves of fan-favorite franchise characters. In an interview with Collider, Mitchell asserted, “Every single character that you remember comes back, and a few villains that I don’t want to spoil.” Mitchell’s statement excitingly puts many major characters in play for Kung Fu Panda 4’s untold story, including one likely villain whose presence could resolve a big franchise mystery about Po’s background.

Kung Fu Panda 4’s Story Could Mean Lord Shen Returns Too

Mitchell’s revealing interview effectively places one of Kung Fu Panda’s franchise villains, Lord Shen, on the table. Lord Shen debuted in Kung Fu Panda 2 as the power-hungry peacock heir to the Gongmen City throne, who infamously led a genocide against pandas. Although his appearance isn’t confirmed yet, a combination of Mitchell’s commentary and teases in the franchise’s first released trailer hint that there’s a considerable chance that Lord Shen will be a part of Kung Fu Panda 4’s story.

Kung Fu Panda 4’s trailer reveals a new franchise villain called Chameleon, who is a shape-shifting sorceress intending to possess the kung fu of all master villains as a means to becoming the world’s most powerful being. In a massive trailer reveal, Chameleon (Viola Davis) appears to have the unique ability to access the Spirit Realm, which she uses to resurrect and possess the powers of former Kung Fu Panda villain Tai Lung. If her menacing agenda is any indication, she’ll also aim to do the same to other master villains like Lord Shen.

              Kung Fu Panda 4's Trailer Sets Up The Franchise's 3 Biggest Villains Returning   Kung Fu Panda 4's trailer teases the main plot of the sequel, and it sets up the franchise's biggest villains, Tai Lung, Shen, and Kai, all returning.

Shen’s Return In Kung Fu Panda 4 Can Bring Answers About Po’s Mom

Lord Shen would be a compelling individual to include in Kung Fu Panda 4, and the reason why is not just because he is a formidable master villain. Having Lord Shen in Kung Fu Panda 4’s cast of characters could be the gateway to setting the record of Po’s mother’s fate straight. According to Kung Fu Panda 2, long ago, Lord Shen led his army of wolves in a raid on a farming town of pandas, where a young Po and his family resided.

Po survived the invasion only because his mother sacrificed her life to lead the wolves away from his hiding spot. While the movie suggested that Po’s mother perished in the attack, Kung Fu Panda 2 did not depict the incident on screen, and the Kung Fu Panda franchise never confirmed her death. If Lord Shen appears in Kung Fu Panda 4, then the master villain himself could clear up the ambiguity surrounding Po’s mother’s death.
