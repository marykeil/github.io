SUMMARY

The Bachelor season 28’s Rachel Nance is a 26-year-old ICU nurse from Honolulu, Hawaii. Rachel is hoping to build a connection with leading man Joey Graziadei. Rachel has a strong example of true love from her parents.

Literature Review

The Bachelor season 28 features 32 women competing for Joey Graziadei’s heart. One of them is Rachel Nance. Here’s what to know about her.

The Bachelor season 28 stars Joey Graziadei on a journey to find his wife from a group of 32 women, and Rachel Nance is one of those contestants. Joey is a 28-year-old tennis pro originally from Collegeville, Pennsylvania, who’s also lived in Koloa, Hawaii. During his premiere episode, he revealed that he recently moved back to the Philadelphia area. Joey got his start as the runner-up in Charity Lawson’s Bachelorette season. Despite Joey and Charity’s strong connection, Charity chose to get engaged to Dotun Olubeko. Although Joey was shocked and heartbroken, he was happy to eventually accept the role of the Bachelor.

The Bachelor season 28 has an interesting mix of women from all over the United States and Canada. Joey had already met one of the women, Lea Cayanan, when he was announced as the Bachelor during Charity’s “After the Final Rose” episode. Lea was given a mystery envelope, which the premiere revealed contained a card that allowed her to steal a one-on-one date from another woman. However, Lea burned the card. In addition, sisters Allison and Lauren Hollinger both appear on the show. As Joey begins the search for his wife, here’s what to know about Rachel, one of the women who could steal his heart.

Rachel Nance’s Age

Rachel is 26 years old. She was born on January 28, 1997. She’ll turn 27 while The Bachelor season 28 airs. Rachel’s zodiac sign is Aquarius. Joey is a 28-year-old Gemini born on May 24, 1995. Joey and Rachel not only match because they’re close in age, but the Aquarius and Gemini zodiac signs are a magical combination.

Rachel Nance’s Hometown & Family

Rachel is from Honolulu, Hawaii. According to her ABC bio, Rachel is immensely proud of her Hawaiian roots. She has a big, tight-knit family. She can’t wait to start a family of her own. Rachel believes that there’s nothing better than a Hawaiian Christmas. She hopes that she and Joey will bond over her love of Hawaiian food because he has lived in the Aloha state.

Rachel Nance’s Job & Education

Rachel is an ICU nurse at a hospital. Her ABC bio states that she’s worked hard to achieve her professional goals. However, now Rachel is ready to find the man with whom she can “enjoy the journey of life.” According to Women’s Health, for college, The Bachelor season 28 contestant attended California State University Fullerton before she transferred to the University of Main at Fort Kent, where she played volleyball. Rachel graduated in December 2020.

Rachel Nance’s Hobbies & Interests

Rachel works hard as a nurse, so she has some hobbies and interests in order to unwind. According to her ABC bio, she loves watching Friends and reading Jane Austen books. Rachel also enjoys spending time with her loved ones. In addition, she has always had an interesting sense of style. Rachel’s signature look growing up included Bermuda shorts and feather earrings. Although Rachel has varied interests, there’s one thing that she stays away from because it’s her phobia - she’s terrified of frogs.

What Rachel Nance Is Looking For

According to her ABC bio, Rachel hopes to find a man who’s honest, gentle, and supportive. She wants someone with whom she can build a life. Rachel considers herself to be “totally wifey material.” Throughout Charity’s season, Joey proved that he has all of the qualities that Rachel is looking for. He was never involved with the drama among the other men, and he always put Charity first. Joey is a gentle soul, who is very supportive of the people he loves. Joey really could be Rachel’s perfect match.

It’s also important that Rachel is ready to be a wife. Joey wants to get engaged and eventually married, and he needs someone who has that goal for the near future as well. Rachel’s statement that she’s wife material shows that she could be the right person for Joey.

The Most Romantic Thing Rachel Has Ever Done

Rachel is a very romantic person. In a handwritten note submitted to Bachelor Nation in response to the prompt, “The most romantic thing I’ve ever done for someone is…,” she revealed that she planned a get-away weekend for herself and her boyfriend at the time. She cooked a homemade meal. However, it wasn’t just any meal - it was his mom’s recipe, and she taught Rachel how to make it. Rachel shared that her boyfriend was very surprised. This is exactly the kind of thoughtful gesture that would appeal to Joey and win his heart.

Rachel Nance’s Instagram

Rachel can be found on Instagram at @rachelmariean. As of this writing, her page has over 2,500 followers. Rachel’s first post is from 2013. She has posted pictures from various trips she’s taken to places such as France, Germany, New York City, Los Angeles, and Nashville, among others. Rachel’s page also includes many photographs of her friends and family, including her parents, whom she loves and admires greatly.

In one post, Rachel paid tribute to her parents, stating that if she could find a love half as pure as her theirs, she would consider herself blessed. She went on to say that her dad is the true definition of a man, and her mom is the perfect partner for him. She said that they are the best example of what all humans deserve. They have “a raw and selfless form of love.” Rachel has had a beautiful example of true love from her parents that she can bring to her experience on The Bachelor. Hopefully, Rachel and Joey can build a relationship like her parents’ marriage.

One of Rachel’s most recent posts announced that she’s a contestant on The Bachelor season 28. It included her promotional photograph. It was captioned “Nurse Rach taking her shot at love.” Rachel also has a link to an Amazon page, through which she recommends products. The page states, “Aloha babes! Please enjoy my favorite Amazon finds and faves… they eat up my paycheck! I hope you love these items as much as I do!” Rachel’s item categories include Curly Hair Needs, Nursing Essentials, My Library, Gold Gals (Jewelry), Bedroom, and Sleeping Stapes.

Rachel is a well-rounded woman who knows exactly what she wants in a relationship. Rachel and Joey are a great match as far as their ages, personalities, and future goals are concerned. The fact that Joey has lived in Hawaii is a big plus as well, since long-distance relationships are difficult. Rachel is adventurous, kind, caring, and loving. Hopefully, she and Joey will build a strong connection throughout The Bachelor season 28. It’s highly possible that Joey and Rachel got engaged at the end of the season because they seem very compatible.

The Bachelor season 28 airs Mondays at 8 p.m. ET on ABC.
