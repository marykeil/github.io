Latest Superman & Lois Season 4 Update Hides Neat DC Comics Doomsday Easter Egg

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 800 words 4 minutes 
Contents

    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

“Superman & Lois” season 4 begins with the title “The End & The Beginning,” teasing the culmination of the show and hinting at Doomsday’s appearance. The title of episode 1 of season 4 connects to Doomsday’s first appearance in DC Comics, creating anticipation for his role in the upcoming season. The premiere of season 4 needs to resolve the cliffhanger battle with Doomsday from season 3, making the Doomsday reference in the title important for fans’ anticipation.

Literature Review

An update surrounding the upcoming Superman & Lois season 4 includes a neat DC Comics Easter egg hinting at Doomsday’s future in the show.

Superman & Lois season 4’s most recent update includes a neat Easter egg to Doomsday from DC Comics. As the final remaining Arrowverse TV show in production, Superman & Lois’ popularity cannot be understated. This popularity has led to much disappointment that Superman & Lois is ending with season 4, though the show almost being canceled after season 3 would have caused much more controversy. Nevertheless, despite the CW almost not picking up the show for a final season, Superman & Lois season 4’s story will give the beloved CW entry the send-off it deserves.

With the production of Superman & Lois season 4 now underway, updates about the show’s final season will likely come thick and fast throughout 2024. These updates will be highly anticipated by those familiar with the show after Superman & Lois season 3’s ending featured a massive cliffhanger. Superman & Lois season 3 saw Lex Luthor enact his master plan to destroy Superman by unleashing Doomsday, the iconic villain of the Man of Steel from DC Comics. Therefore, the first update from Superman & Lois season 4 contains a neat Easter egg to the character from DC Comics and further teases the continuation of season 3’s major cliffhanger.

Superman & Lois Season 4 Premiere Title References Doomsday’s First Comics Appearance

The update on Superman & Lois’ season 4 production start came from Lois Lane actress Elizabeth Tulloch who posted the script for the show’s next episode. As per the update, the title of Superman & Lois season 4, episode 1 was revealed as “The End & The Beginning.” Without any context, this title still makes logical sense given that Superman & Lois season 4 will conclude the show, with the title aptly teasing its culmination.

However, the title also marks a subtle connection to DC Comics, specifically the character of Doomsday who will undoubtedly play a major role in Superman & Lois season 4 after season 3’s cliffhanger. In DC Comics, the character of Doomsday first appeared in “Superman: The Man of Steel #18” in 1992. On the cover of this issue, the words “The beginning of the end! Doomsday!” can be spotted in the lower-right corner. Herein lies the connection between Doomsday’s first issue and Superman & Lois season 4 with the title of episode 1 of the latter echoing the former.

The Superman & Lois Season 4 Premiere Needs To Resolve Season 3’s Doomsday Cliffhanger

This Doomsday reference found in Superman & Lois season 4 only increases the need for the premiere of the show to resolve season 3’s cliffhanger. Interestingly, it was unclear for a while that Superman & Lois would continue after season 3 which left the cliffhanger battle with Doomsday in complete limbo. Even if Superman & Lois season 4 had been confirmed before season 3’s finale, the Doomsday cliffhanger would have been incredibly tantalizing regarding anticipation for a continuation.

This meant that the unclear status of Superman & Lois season 4 made the wait for a resolution to the Doomsday cliffhanger even worse. Now that Superman & Lois season 4 is thankfully confirmed, the premiere has to resolve what happens between Clark and Doomsday. With that in mind, it seems as though audiences of Superman & Lois will get their wish if the Doomsday reference in episode 1’s title is anything to go by.
