3 Yoo Hee-yeon Is Ji-hyuk’s Sister Marry My Husband episode 6

2 Su-min Once Copied Ji-won’s Handwriting Marry My Husband episode 5

A small but important detail from episode 5 is that Su-min can copy Ji-won’s handwriting.
