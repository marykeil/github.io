The Mandalorian Star Reveals The Hilarious Origin Of Bo-Katan s Name

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 700 words 3 minutes 
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Bo-Katan’s name is actually a combination of Dave Filoni’s wife, Anne Convery, and her cat, Boogie. Some people mistakenly think that Bo-Katan’s name was inspired by Katee Sackhoff, but it is actually a nod to Dave’s wife. Katee Sackhoff jokingly mentioned that her resemblance to Dave’s wife might have contributed to the character’s design.

Literature Review

The Mandalorian season 3 star Katee Sackhoff has just revealed the origin of Bo-Katan Kryze’s name in Star Wars, and it is absolutely hilarious.

Katee Sackhoff, star of The Mandalorian season 3, has just revealed the hilarious origins of Bo-Katan Kryze’s name. When Bo-Katan was first introduced in Star Wars: The Clone Wars, she was just another member of Death Watch. When it was revealed that she was the sister of Duchess Satine Kryze, it came as a shock. However, throughout The Clone Wars, audiences were treated to a softer side of her character, as she and her sister shared a brief moment before Satine was murdered. Since then, The Mandalorian has continued to flesh out her character, transforming her from a two-dimensional cartoon villain to a complex and interesting person.

Bo-Katan actress Katee Sackhoff revealed on her podcast Blah Blah Blah that Bo-Katan is named after Dave Filoni’s wife, Anne Convery, and her cat, Boogie. This is the first time the origin of Bo-Katan’s name has been discussed, other than her obvious ties to Satine with the last name “Kryze.” In an interview with Sam Witwer, Sackhoff said this:

“[Bo-Katan’s] named after Anne [Filoni] and a cat. Anne’s cat Boogie. […] Which is weird because people think that it was drafted after me, and I’m like… no, no, that’s Dave’s wife, Anne! We just look enough alike that they could actually work.”

As Sackhoff stated in her podcast interview with Witwer, Bo-Katan’s character design was also modeled after Anne. As such, it is unsurprising that Dave Filoni would be thinking of his wife when coming up with the character’s name. Hilariously, he was also thinking of his cat. From there, Boogie and Anne morphed into “Bo-Katan” in his mind. This is an adorable tribute to Filoni’s family.

Bo-Katan Is Now One Of Star Wars’ Most Recognizable Names

When Bo-Katan’s relationship with Duchess Satine was first revealed in the season 5 episode “The Lawless,” it put her character on the radar. Suddenly, she was tied to a more significant background character. Her status as a member of the villainous terrorist group Death Watch was immediately made more tragic and compelling. This was a brilliant move on Dave Filoni’s part, as it piqued interest in who had up until that point been a fairly flat background character. From that point on, the name “Bo-Katan Kryze” was recognizable to die-hard Star Wars viewers everywhere.

Since Bo-Katan’s live-action debut in The Mandalorian season 2, her character has become even more recognizable. Katee Sackhoff’s brilliant performance brought this character to life. Her character development from a disillusioned ex-member of the defunct Death Watch to the Mand’alor and believer in the Way is a compelling one. Hopefully, The Mandalorian will continue with this arc in the future.

Since Bo-Katan has played such an important role in The Mandalorian, she will likely appear in the upcoming The Mandalorian & Grogu movie. While plot details for The Mandalorian & Grogu haven’t been given out yet, Bo-Katan played a huge role in The Mandalorian season 3. As the new leader of the Mandalorians, an appearance in The Mandalorian & Grogu seems inevitable, no matter what the future of The Mandalorian itself may be.
