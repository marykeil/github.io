Every Character Who Dies In The Brothers Sun Season 1 (& Which Episode)

SUMMARY

Deaths play a major role: The Brothers Sun season 1 is full of deaths, from major to supporting characters, highlighting the dangerous world of Triad gangsters and crime. Impact on the story: Each person’s death in the show has a significant impact on the overall storyline, showcasing how every character’s demise brings about twists and turns. Mystery and action: With a large cast of characters operating within the gangster world, the show is filled with both mystery and intense action, making for a thrilling viewing experience.

Literature Review

The Brothers Sun has seen a fair number of death in season 1 and every character has had an impact on the plot and the characters’ journeys.

Warning: This article contains major spoilers for The Brothers Sun season 1. The Brothers Sun saw a lot of deaths in season 1, ranging from major characters to supporting characters. From the rivals of the Sun family to the people who are there to protect them, no one is safe from the ever-present threat of death in this show. Since it is set in the world of Triad gangsters and crime, there is plenty of room for deaths and action, showing that every person’s death has an impact on the story in some way. The Brothers Sun, which has earned great reviews, follows a Taipei gangster named Charles Sun (Justin Chien) who comes home to Los Angeles to protect his mother, Eileen (Michelle Yeoh), and brother, Bruce (Sam Song Li), after his father Big Sun (Johnny Kou) is nearly assassinated. Having no memory of his past life in Taiwan and no idea his family is a legendary Triad family, Bruce needs to adapt to life as a gangster and learns that not everything is as it seems as he learns about his family’s secret life. With so many of The Brothers Sun’s large cast of characters operating inside the gangster world, there is plenty of death and mystery in The Brothers Sun season 1.

12 The Giant Season 1, Episode 1 “Pilot”

The first real kill audiences see in The Brothers Sun is that of the Giant in season 1, episode 1, “Pilot”. After Charles arrives at his mother’s house in Los Angeles, a henchman is waiting to assassinate him. After a brief struggle, Charles manages to defeat the would-be assassin and pins his hand to the table with a knife. Charles nonchalantly offers the Giant a cigarette and asks who the assassin’s boss is, but the henchman refuses to give up any information. As a result, Charles breaks his neck and later, with the help of his mother, chops the body up. This gets the ball rolling in The Brothers Sun storyline.

The Horrifying True Story That Inspired The Brothers Sun In a recent interview, The Brothers Sun’s co-creator Byron Wu explained where the inspiration for the show’s tone and premise came from.

11 May Song Season 1, Episode 1 “Pilot”

The second casualty on the show is that of May Song, one of the local crime bosses with whom Charles hopes to become allies. After blowing his college money on improv classes, Bruce ends up attempting to sell drugs for May alongside his friend TK at a nightclub. When Charles tracks down Bruce at the club and tries to bring him back home, the two brothers end up cornered by May and her henchmen, since May believes that Charles is after her business. May is quickly defeated by Charles and ends up killed after a group of gunmen make their way inside the club and shoot her multiple times in the chest.

10 Zhi Zhu “Spider” Season 1, Episode 2, “Favor for a Favor”

In season 1, episode 2, “Favor for a Favor”, Charles reveals to Bruce the real reason behind why Big Sun sent them away and the family’s gangster roots. The two brothers head out to Arcadia to find out more about the assassin who attacked Charles. While Spider is only seen briefly, his death leaves a trail of mysteries behind him. In one of the funniest scenes in The Brothers Sun, Spider ends up stabbed by gangsters dressed as inflatable dinosaurs and Charles has to use his martial arts to fight them off.

9 Tony Tang Season 1, Episode 3, “Whatever You Want”

When Bruce gets kidnapped in season 1, episode 3, “Whatever You Want”, Charles and Mama Sun immediately set out for the mahjong club, since Mama Sun believes that the club has all the answers they need to find out where Bruce is. Those answers lead to Tony Tang, who supplies drugs for another gang in the Triad universe, the White Crane Gang, and runs the best restaurant in town. Tang ends up killed by June with his legs and hands cut off to draw out Sleepy Chan, the leader of the rival gang possibly behind the shooting of Big Sun and the man that Tang was working with.

8 Sleepy Chan Season 1, Episode 4, “Square”

As the leader of a rival gang, Sleepy Chan is a powerful member of the Triad and a force to be reckoned with. Despite the rivalries between the two gangs, it soon becomes clear that he is not behind Big Sun’s assassination attempt. When a square is called following Tony Tang’s death in season 1, episode 4, “Square”, Charles learns that there may be more behind the recent attacks against his family. Chan also tells Charles that they are being targeted by the same people, Sleepy Chan ends up getting killed by the zealot vigilante group the Boxers before revealing any more information on the attackers.

7 Drowsy Lee Season 1, Episode 4, “Square”

As the son of Sleepy Chan, Drowsy Lee is the muscle behind the rival gang and has some history with Charles. After threatening Charles during the meeting with Sleepy Chan, the two end up fighting alongside each other when Boxers gang members attack the meeting. Charles and Drowsy fight alongside each other against the Boxers’ henchmen, but it eventually comes to an end when Drowsy is killed when one of the Boxers self-detonates a bomb he’d been holding and Charles barely escapes.

6 Blood Boots Season 1, Episode 5 “The Rolodex”

As one of the longtime enforcers for the Sun family, Blood Boots was a pleasant and cheerful person who was always willing to put himself in danger to protect the family. In season 1, episode 5, “The Rolodex”, he goes with Mama Sun to find out more about the Boxers, not knowing that the gang wants Mama Sun as a hostage. The friendly enforcer is killed in cold blood by the Boxers after Mama Sun refuses to give up the identities of the members of the heads of the Triad clans, a.k.a. the ghosts. His ending is arguably the most shocking and tragic in the show as he was such an audience favorite and had stepped into an uncle-like role with Bruce.

5 Grace Season 1, Episode 7, “Gymkata”

As Bruce’s college classmate and crush, Grace has one of the darkest stories in The Brothers Sun that audiences later discover in season 1, episode 6, “Country Boy”. In one of the most shocking twists in The Brothers Sun, Bruce learns that Grace is the leader of the Boxers crew who wants to kill the Triad leaders. Her goal is to avenge the death of her father, who had been trafficked as an indentured servant, and to bring down the Triad organization as a whole. During a meeting of the Triad ghosts to elect a new Dragon Head in episode 7, “Gymkata,” Grace ends up killed by June when both SWAT team members and the Boxers disrupt the supposedly secret meeting.

4 Triad Leaders Season 1, Episode 7. “Gymkata”

In the same meeting that eventually kills Grace, the other Triad leaders come together at a high-end restaurant to elect their new Dragon Head. Mama Sun convinces the Triad leaders to make her the new Dragon Head, but before it becomes official, things get crazy. The Boxers barge into the meeting before a decision can be made and most of the Triad leaders are killed in the ensuing melee. The members of the Triad who aren’t killed end up arrested, and it soon becomes clear who set up the assassination attempts on the Sun family.

3 Lance Wang Season 1, Episode 7, “Gymkata”

One of the three Wang brothers, Lance Wang, has known Charles for many years and is one of his closest friends. They are considered hired muscle for the Jade Dragons and can be a bit eccentric when it comes to gearing up for fights. In season 1, episode 7, “Gymkata”, Lance Wang’s arrival is quickly overshadowed by the revelation that Big Sun is alive. Lance seemingly meets his end when he is stabbed after finally pulling off the pommel horse stunt from Gymkata, an action movie revered by the brothers and Charles, during the brawl at the restaurant.

2 JC and Justin Wang Season 1, Episode 8, “Protect the Family”

The other two Wang brothers, JC and Justin, are also lifelong friends of Charles and have helped him out of many scrapes in the past. Despite acting aggressively toward Bruce with macho posturing, and with plenty of backflipping off tables, Charles trusts them implicitly. In season 1, episode 8, “Protect the Family”, JC and Justin end up meeting their end when the two try to stop Charles from getting back to Bruce and Mama Sun. Not hesitating for a moment, Charles shoots both brothers point-blank before walking out the door, showing that he will stop at nothing to protect his family, even if it means killing his closest allies.

1 Xing Season 1, Episode 8, “Protect the Family”

Another one of the enforcers for the Sun family, alongside Blood Boots, Xing, is loyal to the family that she serves and will kill anyone that the family asks her to. She is very serious and has a no-nonsense approach to killing. After Charles fails to kill his brother on his father’s orders, Big Sun asks Xing to do it instead. When Xing finds the motel where Mama Sun and Bruce are, the two women get into a fight, with Mama Sun barely making it out alive in the brutal The Brothers Sun scene. The Sun family matriarch ends the fight by strangling Xing to death.

The Brothers Sun

The TV show follows a Taipei gangster named Charles Sun who travels to LA to protect his family after his father is killed by an assassin. The Brothers Sun was created by Brad Falchuk and Byron Wu.
