10 Best Crime Shows Like Griselda

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1400 words 7 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Griselda is an exciting and dark series about a drug lord’s rise and downfall, enticing audiences into the world of crime and violence. Viewers left wanting more after Griselda can binge on other series like Power, Nurse Jackie, and Snowfall that explore drugs, power, and strong characters. Shows like Mayans M.C., Dopesick, and Narcos offer similar themes of drug-related stories, corruption, and crime, making them perfect follow-ups to Griselda.

Literature Review

After binging Netflix’s newest release Griselda starring Sofia Vergara, there are countless other popular drug cartel television shows to dive into.

One of Netflix’s first successful releases of 2024 was Sofia Vergara’s newest series, Griselda, and after finishing its six episodes, audiences should consider watching these other highly bingeable, drug-related crime shows. Griselda follows the true story of Columbian drug lord, Griselda Blanco. The series sees her rise to power on the beaches of Miami, followed by her betrayal-filled downfall which results in several tragic deaths. Griselda’s cast includes Modern Family star, Sofia Vergara, Alberto Guerra, Vanessa Ferlito, and Christian Tappan. With just six episodes and a dark premise, it is no surprise that Griselda is an exciting and easy watch from Netflix. Its bloody story easily sucks audiences in and establishes them in Griselda’s world of drug cartels and crime. The only problem is that viewers are left without anything to watch once the story is over. Luckily though, there are countless amazing television series with very similar premises to Griselda. These series include drugs, violence, strong characters, and stories that are sure to enchant audiences.

Griselda Ending Explained Netflix’s Griselda sees the crime lord’s tumultuous rise to power, but the limited series’ final episode explores how this all came tumbling down.

10 Power 6 seasons

For those hoping for a television series that includes the double life of a drug dealer, as is portrayed in Griselda, a perfect option would be Power. The series, which began in 2014, follows James “Ghost” St. Patrick, a man who seemingly has it all in terms of love and career, but in reality, is hiding a massive secret. After Ghost finishes tending to his successful business, he dedicates his time to a significant drug empire that caters specifically to the wealthy. Overall, Power includes drug cartels like Griselda, but also has themes of secrets and power corrupting individuals.

9 Nurse Jackie 7 seasons

While Nurse Jackie may not be the first show one thinks of when seeking a show similar to Griselda, however the series have more in common than it seems. Nurse Jackie follows Edie Falco’s Jackie, a nurse that tries her best to do good in a healthcare system that often fails its patients. However, Jackie is also struggling as she battles an addiction to painkillers and her tendency to break the rules. Nurse Jackie explores the more personal effects of drug addiction, but like Griselda, has themes of going against authority and strong female characters following their ambitions.

8 Snowfall 6 seasons

A lesser-known television series that deserves more hype, especially after Griselda, is 2017’s Snowfall. The series takes place in 1983 and follows a young Mexican woman who convinces her drug lord father to start dealing in the popular drug crack cocaine, which gets her and her family involved with dangerous people, including young drug dealer Franklin. Snowfall shares the same historical beat with Griselda and also delves into the selling of drugs and the many parts that make it possible. It also has similar hints of violence like Griselda.

7 Mayans M.C. 5 seasons

Despite being a spinoff of another popular crime show, Mayans M.C. is the one to watch if viewers are looking for emphasis on drugs and power. Mayans M.C. follows EZ, a young man from a proud Mexican family that is seeking vengeance against the drug cartel that ruined his life. However, his journey for justice leaves him in darker and darker spaces. Set on the U.S./Mexico border, this series has a similar ambiance to Griselda, and dives into the line between getting revenge and becoming evil yourself.

6 Dopesick 1 season

For those seeking a series as short as Griselda, the miniseries Dopesick is the ideal option. The 2021 series, which has a star-studded cast including Michael Keaton, Kaitlyn Dever, and Will Poulter, follows the effects of the opiod crisis on the United States. While this series does not focus on drug cartels, it does explore the serious problems that drugs can create for individuals and the injustice that comes as a result of drug prescriptions and trade going unchecked. Like Griselda, Dopesick’s characters are torn apart by their sense of power and their relationship to drugs and crime.

5 Gangs Of London 2 seasons

Another fairly short series to dive right into is Gangs of London. After powerful crime boss Finn Wallace is mysteriously killed, his son Sean must take his place and figure out who ordered his father dead. However, the turn-over of power makes Sean vulnerable, and he must fight off his enemies to maintain his position. The series includes all levels of crime, from drug cartels to all-out mafias, and it is firmly rooted in its London location. Though this show does not have the same ambiance as Griselda, it certainly will satisfy audiences’ desire for crime and violence.

4 Breaking Bad 5 seasons

If Griselda piqued your interest, it may be time to jump into one of the most popular television series of all time, Breaking Bad. The show follows Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher who, after discovering he has cancer, decides to spend his remaining time earning money for his family via a meth lab. Along with a young drug dealer named Jesse, Walt sinks deeper and deeper into the world of drugs and crime. Breaking Bad may not have the fiery female protagonist that Griselda has, but the series has become iconic for its complicated characters and high-stakes world.

3 Ozark 4 seasons

A second wildly popular television series that has close connections to Griselda is Ozark. Starring Jason Bateman and Laura Linney, the show follows a financial planner who moves his family from Chicago to the Ozarks to escape a money laundering scheme gone wrong and pay back at Mexican drug lord. An interesting connection between Ozark and Griselda is themes of family. As Jason Bateman’s crimes bring his family closer and further apart, Griselda’s schemes also affect her family in negative ways. Though the series have vastly different settings, they share pivotal themes of corruption and power.

2 Queen Of The South 5 seasons

One series that comes close to Griselda concerning plot and atmosphere is Queen of the South. The 2016 drama follows Teresa, a Mexican woman who falls in love with a member of a drug cartel and is left adrift after his sudden murder. Teresa immigrates to America and decides to take down the drug lord targeting her and start her own cartel. Queen of the South is similar to Griselda in countless ways, but most of all, because of its head-strong female protagonist and her involvement with the drug cartel, which leads to both tragedy and success in her life.

1 Narcos 3 seasons

The most obvious series to watch after finishing Griselda is Narcos. Like Griselda, Narcos is a television series that documents the real-life rise and fall of drug kingpin, Pablo Escobar. Over the course of three seasons, the show follows Escobar from his beginnings in the Columbian drug trade to his ultimate demise. The series also explores the ways in which the authorities attempted to combat Escobar and the drug problem in Columbia. Considering that they share locations and true stories, Narcos is a perfect follow-up to Griselda. There is a continuation of Narcos from 2018 entitled Narcos: Mexico starring Diego Luna. It has three seasons.
