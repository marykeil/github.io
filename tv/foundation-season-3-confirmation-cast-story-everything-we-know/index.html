Foundation, Apple TV+’s adaptation of the Isaac Asimov novel series of the same name, first aired in 2021, with two season currently complete and a third on the way. The first season of Foundation received mixed-to-positive reviews, with praise going to the set design and acting of both Jared Harris, as protagonist Hari Seldon, and Lee Pace as the villain Brother Day, one of a series of genetic clones that rules the Galactic Empire. That first season picked up two Primetime Emmy nominations and won two Visual Effects Society Awards and that led to a second season, which premiered in 2023. The books that the series is based on took the form of a trilogy, published from 1951 to 1953, which was followed by two more books in 1982 and 1986 and prequel novels written in 1988 and 1993. The original trilogy on which the Apple TV+ show is based was originally collected as short stories and novellas and is based on The History of the Decline and Fall of the Roman Empire, but based in a sci-fi world of warring planets and a Galactic Empire. With such an intriguing premise it’s no surprise that Foundation has become so popular. Stream On Apple TV+

Foundation Season 3 Recent News Filming Is Delayed

After months-long delays were resolved after the end of the 2023 Hollywood strikes, the latest news sees Foundation season 3 delayed again. Unlike the strike delays of 2023, the latest stoppage comes from budgetary issues that the Apple TV+ original is suffering while trying to film in Europe. According to a report, the cast and crew were told to leave the set indefinitely while the issue was worked out. Reportedly, only one-third of the new season has been filmed thus far. No word has been given on how long this delay could halt production.

Foundation Has Been Renewed For Season 3 Season 3 Is On The Way

Apple TV+ officially announced the renewal of Foundation for a third season in December 2023, although there has been no mention of when it might release. At the beginning of 2024, Foundation season 3 began physical production after delays caused by the Hollywood strikes, only to once again be delayed by budgetary issues.

Foundation Season 3 Cast Who Is Returning?

Most of The Foundation season 3 cast remains unknown, but it could include many of the characters from the second season. However, there was a 138-year time jump between the first and second seasons, so there is a chance that the cast could also change. However, thanks to the sci-fi nature of the series, some characters carried over despite the time jump. Star Trek: Deep Space Nine star Alexander Siddig is returning to the cast of Foundation season 3, but will be playing an entirely different character. Siddig appeared in a single episode in season 1 as Advocate Xylas, but is being promoted to a recurring character when he debuts as Dr. Ebling Mis, a psycho-historian who studies the work of Hari Seldon. However, there do appear to be some people who won’t return to the Foundation season 3 cast, including Harris’s Hari Seldon, who died in the sixth episode of season 2, “Why the Gods Made Wine.” It also appears that Leah Harvey’s Salvor Hardin might be doomed, as Gaal Dornick saw him die 150 years in the future. Of course, this is a sci-fi series, so they could still return in some form or another. Foundation Season 3 Story What Will Happen?

The plot of Foundation season 3 is still unknown, although season 2 ended with another huge time jump (which also happened between seasons 1 and 2). The season 2 finale ended 152 years in the future, which is going to connect to the story of the Mule, a mentalic originally destined to kill Salvor Hardin. There is also the shocking fact that the main protagonist, Hari Seldon, died in the second season. Whatever the details are, it is clear that the third season of the show is going to be setting up major conflicts for all the characters. When he created the idea for the Apple TV+ story, David Goyer said this was “a 1,000-year chess game between Hari Seldon and the Empire, and all the characters in between are the pawns” (via THR). Based on that statement, the Foundation season 3 story might see Hari returning after all.

Foundation
Isaac Asimov’s novel series is brought to life in Foundation, a television adaptation of the epic sci-fi saga created for Apple TV+ but deviates somewhat from the original source material. The show follows a group of exiled humans that find themselves as the last chance to save the Galactic Empire - from itself.
