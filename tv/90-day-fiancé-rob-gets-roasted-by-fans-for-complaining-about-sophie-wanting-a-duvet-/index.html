90 Day Fiancé Rob Gets Roasted By Fans For Complaining About Sophie Wanting A Duvet

SUMMARY

Rob Warne faces backlash for belittling Sophie’s request for a duvet, showing financial disparities between the couple. #90DayFiance Fans criticize Rob’s immature behavior, comparing him to a high school boyfriend. Sophie asks for basic comforts, but Rob refuses. #RealityTV Rob’s resentment stems from his perception of Sophie as “bougie” and expectation for her to rely on family money. Literature Review

90 Day Fiancé season 10 cast member Rob Warne is accusing Sophie Sierra of being “bougie” and bickering when all she wanted from him was a duvet.

90 Day Fiancé star Rob Warne is facing the heat for making a big deal out of Sophie Sierra asking him to buy a duvet for their home. Sophie is a 23-year-old British woman who works as a social media influencer and fashion content creator, while Rob, 32, is an aspiring actor and model. Sophie grew up in a wealthy household but became independent at a young age to start earning her own income. Meanwhile, Rob seemed to be facing financial difficulties and could only afford an apartment with no bathroom. Rob didn’t appreciate Sophie asking him for an indoor bathroom and now has ridiculed her duvet request.

During 90 Day Fiancé, episode 11, Rob lashed out at Sophie for being “negative” first thing in the market and accused her of always “needing something” as pointed out by Reddit user u/RealityGays.

Fans who watched the episode on Sunday night didn’t miss the chance to sound off about Rob’s difficult behavior in the post comments. Redditor rach_rdh wrote, “This man is like that super sh**** experience of the boyfriend you had in high school lol like the one you look back on now that your older and go YIKES!”

Sophie Wanted A Duvet & Rob Called Her “Bougie” Meanwhile, u/kyliving67 questioned why Rob can’t have a place with a bathroom, a kitchen separate from a bedroom, and a TV room instead of just a corner in his house when he’s a decade older than Sophie and should have the “basic decency” to make his fiancé feel comfortable in a new country. “He’s so whiny and immature and just subpar in every way imaginable smh,” posted StrangeMood315. Rob was in a “horrible mood” and woke up with an attitude just because Sophie asked him for a duvet. She did not sleep well.

Sophie was willing to live with Rob in the apartment but was asking him to do the bare minimum which Rob wasn’t agreeing to do. Rob wasn’t okay with changing the way he lived to accommodate Sophie’s preferences. She asked him how her asking about a duvet was her being negative. “I live here too,” Sophie reminded Rob, and he told her that she could buy one herself. Sophie said that most people had a duvet at home. Rob claimed he was bothered by Sophie asking for a duvet because, according to him, what she wanted was a new house.

In reality, 90 Day Fiancé star Rob was upset because the first thing he heard in the morning was “Buy me something.” He claimed Sophie did not know what a duvet was because he already had three duvets at home. Sophie is tired of living in a small room with two people and a Pitbull. Moreover, Sophie also has to hear taunts from Rob about how she’s too “bougie” for someone who doesn’t have any money of her own. It’s easy to see that Rob expects Sophie to get money from her family to fund her lifestyle, since he doesn’t feel the need to buy anything for his future wife.
