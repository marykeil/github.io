9 Below Deck Franchise Love Triangles Ranked

SUMMARY

Below Deck is known for its entertaining and dramatic love triangles among the crew members. Love triangles are a common occurrence on Below Deck, leading to conflicts when multiple crew members are interested in someone. Some of the most memorable love triangles include Luka, Natalya and Jessika, Brooke, Kasey and João, and Daisy, Colin and Gary.

Literature Review

Since the inception of the Below Deck franchise, there have been several love triangles between cast members. Here are the best ones ranked.

Since the beginning of the Below Deck franchise, it has given viewers several love triangles that were both entertaining and shocking. It’s not surprising when cast members in the Bravo franchise develop feelings for each other. They are stuck on a boat for several weeks, barely having contact with people other than their crew members and guests. It’s only natural for the crew members to become attached to each other and develop something more than friendship. However, creating romantic relationships on board can cause issues, especially when more than one person is interested in someone. Below Deck cast members refer to these romantic dalliances onboard as “boatmances.” They don’t usually last longer than a season and sometimes end halfway through. Boatmances are quite popular on Below Deck, and there is usually at least one per season. One of the most memorable boatmances was between Dani Soares and Jean-Luc Cerza Lanaux from Below Deck Sailing Yacht season 2. What made them so memorable was what happened in the off-season, which was when Dani became pregnant and welcomed Jean-Luc’s daughter. While their boatmance was a whirlwind, at least they weren’t caught in a love triangle.

9 Camille, Ben, And Leigh-Ann Below Deck Season 10

At the start of Below Deck season 10, Deckhand Ben Willoughby fell for Deckhand/Steward Camille Lamb, and the two began hooking up right away. Ben was always the one Camille would go to whenever she had an issue with the interior team and enjoyed his company overall. Ben felt a closeness to Camille as well and was devastated when she was fired from the boat. However, after Camille left, Leigh-Ann Smith arrived, whom Ben had met via a dating app a year ago. He was left confused about his relationship with Camile after Leigh-Ann arrived, as he still had feelings for Leigh-Ann. Nonetheless, Ben and Camille reconnected after filming but ended things shortly after.

8 Alissa, Ross, And Katie Below Deck Season 10

Bosun Ross McHarg developed a boatmance with his fellow deckhand Katie Glaser early in Below Deck season 10. While the pair often fought about their complex relationship, they always seemed to gravitate towards one another. However, Ross and Katie’s relationship was on thin ice when Steward Alissa Humber began flirting with Ross. This obviously made Katie angry, but it didn’t stop her from continuing to hook up with Ross until the end of the season.

7 Ashley, Gary, And Gabriela Below Deck Sailing Yacht Season 3

During Below Deck Sailing Yacht season 3, Gary King did what he does best and started hooking up with one of the stews, Ashley Marti. The two started a boatmance early on in the season, and it continued until the end. However, midway through Gary and Ashley’s relationship, fellow stew Gabriela Barragan decided to make a move on Gary. Gabriela and Gary did hook up in his cabin for a bit, which infuriated Ashley. Nonetheless, Gabriela wasn’t that into Gary, so she decided to let Ashley have him.

6 Brooke, João And Kasey Below Deck Mediterranean Season 3

When Below Deck Mediterranean season 3 began, Bosun João Franco had a connection with stew Kasey Cohen right away. It seemed like there was mutual interest between them, especially from João. However, he had a change of heart suddenly when fellow stew Brooke Laughton also showed interest in the bosun. João and Brooke became closer and closer, but he would still flirt with Kasey sometimes. Brooke finally had enough and asked João to choose between her and Kasey. He chose Brooke in the end but did admit during the reunion that he still had feelings for Kasey.

5 Jaimee, Culver, And Luka Below Deck Down Under Season 2

During Below Deck Down Under season 2, Culver Bradbury immediately fell for new stew Jaimee Neale after she arrived on board. The two had an instant connection and started hooking up following the crew’s first night out. All seemed well for Jaimee and Culver until Luka Brunton arrived as a deckhand. Jaimee was quickly taken by Luka as not only did she find him attractive, but they both have similar backgrounds (she’s from Australia, and he’s from New Zealand). Jaimee’s attraction for Luka almost ruined her relationship with Culver as they kissed during their final night together. While she and Culver left the boat together, they ended their relationship soon after.

4 Luka, Natalya, And Jessika
Below Deck Mediterranean Season 8

The most recent Below Deck love triangle comes from Below Deck Med season 8 between Luka, Jessika Asai, and Natalya Scudder. Early in the season, Luka and Natalya had something going on between them, but her complicated open relationship made it difficult for Luka to be fully interested. So he moved on to Jessika, and it made it easier for him to do so since Natalya left the boat. Luka and Jessika were the it couple on board; however, he was still texting Natalya on the side, and the two even kissed again during the final crew night out.

3 Alli, Gary, And Sydney Below Deck Sailing Yacht Season 2

Another tumultuous Below Deck love triangle came from Below Deck Sailing Yacht season 2. During night one, the crew had a little too much to drink, and Gray hooked up with his Deckhand Sydney Zaruba. Despite hooking up with Sydney, Gary had his eye on stew Alli Dore. The two had a very flirtatious relationship, which drove Sydney mad. In the end, Gary ended up with Alli, but the relationship didn’t last after the season.

2 Wes, Malia, And Adam Below Deck Mediterranean Season 2

Before the start of Below Deck Med season 2, deckhand, at the time, Malia White and Chef Adam Glick had somewhat of a relationship. The two wanted to hide it from the cameras at first, but the chemistry they had together was noticeable. However, Malia developed feelings for Bosun Wes Walton, so Malia ended things with Adam to be with Wes fully. Adam didn’t take it so well at first, but he eventually let it go. Malia and Wes dated for a few years after the season ended.

1 Daisy, Colin, And Gary Below Deck Sailing Yacht Season 4

The most notable Below Deck love triangle comes from Below Deck Sailing Yacht season 4 between Gary (again), Daisy Kelliher, and Colin Macrae. It began when Daisy and Colin started hooking up, only for Gary to get jealous. That’s when he revealed they had hooked up in the past off-season. Since Daisy had a past with Gary, Colin found it difficult to not only be with Daisy but to trust either of them. The Gary, Colin, and Daisy love triangle kind of hurt their years-long friendship.
