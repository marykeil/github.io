Georgie & Mandys Wedding Gets Exciting Update From Young Sheldon Producer

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 600 words 3 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Georgie and Mandy’s wedding will take place in Young Sheldon season 7, despite assumptions it would be saved for the spinoff. The wedding will provide a cause for celebration amid the impending tragedy of George’s death. It is important for the wedding to happen in Young Sheldon to maintain continuity and allow the spinoff to focus on the newlywed couple’s beginning.

Literature Review

Young Sheldon co-executive producer Steve Holland offers an exciting update about Georgie and Mandy’s wedding after getting engaged in season 6.

Georgie and Mandy’s wedding in Young Sheldon gets an exciting update from the show’s co-executive producer, Steve Holland. After getting engaged in the Young Sheldon season 6 finale, planning their special event is next on the couple’s agenda. It may be hampered by the fallout from the recent Medford tornado that forces the remaining Cooper family to all live together under one roof in Young Sheldon season 7, but eventually, Georgie and Mandy will get used to their new normal and be able to focus on their wedding.

Ahead of the Young Sheldon season 7 premiere, Holland confirmed that Georgie and Mandy will tie the knot before the show ends. With the pair continuing their story on-screen via their own spinoff, there were previous assumptions that CBS would save the occasion for the offshoot. However, Holland revealed that it will go down in Young Sheldon. Read his full quote from TVLine below:

“There’s a wedding coming up. In fact, we’re actually shooting that today. Yesterday and today. That’s been really fun, and I think it’s the most people we’ve ever had in a scene together, so that’s exciting.”

Georgie and Mandy’s Young Sheldon spinoff is expected to premiere in the 2024-2025 TV season.

Why Mandy & Georgie’s Wedding Needs To Happen In Young Sheldon Season 7

Now that it’s confirmed that George will die in Young Sheldon season 7, The Big Bang Theory prequel needs something to balance out the impending tragedy. Georgie and Mandy tying the knot after everything they have been through is the perfect way to give the Coopers a cause for celebration. The question is whether the wedding will happen before or after the family unexpectedly loses its patriarch. This is vital in terms of setting up a satisfying sendoff for Sheldon and his family.

It’s also worth noting that no other actors are confirmed to return in the spinoff aside from Montana Jordan and Emily Osment. If CBS delays their wedding, they may have to do it without the majority of the Coopers from Young Sheldon, which is disappointing, especially considering how much Meemaw has helped them throughout their unexpected pregnancy journey. Additionally, the offshoot will reportedly have a multi-cam setup, which will give it a very different vibe from Young Sheldon. For continuity purposes, it’s best to pay off their arc with their wedding in the family comedy.

    Young Sheldon Season 7 Sets Up How It'll Avoid The Fallout Of George's Death  George's death is already all but confirmed, and Young Sheldon season 7 may have already revealed how it's going to soften its impact on the show.

Ultimately, it’s best for season 7 to stage Mandy and Georgie’s wedding. This way, their new show can focus on their beginning as a newlywed couple, which is integral if CBS plans to relocate them away from Medford and the rest of the Coopers. From there, they can also bring familiar Young Sheldon characters once in a while.

Young Sheldon

season 7 premieres Thursday, February 15, at 8 pm ET on CBS.
