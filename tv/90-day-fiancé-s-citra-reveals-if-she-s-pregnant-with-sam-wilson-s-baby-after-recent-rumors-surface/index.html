90 Day Fiancé s Citra Reveals If She s Pregnant With Sam Wilson s Baby After Recent Rumors Surface

SUMMARY

Citra denies pregnancy rumors in response to comments on a Facebook post, clarifying that a photo where she appeared pregnant was taken after their engagement celebration and her stomach was bloated from eating. Citra humorously questions what kind of baby she would be having if she had been pregnant since June 2021, stating that viewers do not know her life as well as they claim to. However, she does not confirm or deny if she is currently pregnant, leaving the possibility open for future revelations at the 90 Day Fiancé’s season 10 Tell All.

Literature Review

90 Day Fiancé’s star Citra is done with all the rumors that she’s expecting. She’s finally revealing if she’s pregnant with Sam Wilson’s baby.

90 Day Fiancé star Citra addressed the baby rumors that have recently been making the rounds on the Internet. Citra, who’s from West Bogor, Java, had seen Missouri’s Sam Wilson on a dating app when she was bored and looking for friends. She was interested in how Sam looked and thought the then-26-year-old had a baby face. Sam and Citra weren’t romantically interested in each other, but a year later, they became boyfriend and girlfriend. Sam proposed to her a year later, and Citra came to the U.S. to get married to Sam in 90 Day Fiancé season 10.

As Sam and Citra’s 90 Day Fiancé storyline is unfolding, there has been speculation that the couple is hiding some very big news. Citra, however, has denied that she’s pregnant via a statement she shared on Instagram.

Citra uploaded a screenshot from a post on a 90 Day Fiancé Facebook fan group called “Shaeeda and Bilal 90 Day Fiance Fans” to her Instagram Story. The picture showed Sam and Citra posing with her dad and sisters. Citra included comments like “She sure looks pregnant” and “think she’s pregnant” in the screenshot. However, Citra proved that the photo was taken back in June 2021 after her engagement.

Citra wrote, “People are so funny not every women out there has [a] flat stomach.” She explained that the photo was taken when they’d just finished celebrating their engagement with her family. Citra said they’d just had lunch and her “stomach bloated” after she ate. “People think I’m pregnant and that picture [is] from June 2021,” Citra added. “If I was pregnant at that time until now what kind of baby I’m having [if I’m pregnant for] 3.5 years, alien baby?” Citra laughed. She admitted that she sometimes loves it when viewers argue among themselves trying to figure out her life “like they know very well about your life lol.”

Sam and Citra’s marriage was in trouble because of his past arrest and failure to complete a diversion program, which could result in him going to jail. Sam worried that Citra’s police officer father, Herman, would take her back to her home country. Sam also had to convert to Islam before the wedding, and it meant that he’d have to live a much different lifestyle by removing things like alcohol and pork from his life. It is not known whether Sam really converted, but Citra’s sister Nafa spoiled their storyline by posting photos from a wedding ceremony on Instagram. The same sister is now engaged and getting married to Sam’s brother Timmy.

Sam and Citra tied the knot in September 2023 near his hometown of Cameron. They are still together, according to their Instagram updates. When Citra addressed the pregnancy rumors, she spoke about a throwback photo but didn’t really answer if she is currently pregnant. Sam, meanwhile, has mentioned that he is looking forward to having babies with Citra. Like Sam, Citra, too, is good at keeping secrets and could be saving the baby question for later, which she will confirm or deny at the 90 Day Fiancé season 10 Tell All.

90 Day Fiancé airs Sundays at 8 p.m. EST on TLC.
