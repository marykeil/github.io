Summary

In a preview clip, The Bachelor season 28’s Joey Graziadei chose Daisy Kent for his first one-on-one date. Daisy hasn’t told Joey about her hearing loss yet. Daisy and Joey have a strong connection, and Joey choosing her for the first one-on-one date shows his interest in their relationship.

The Bachelor season 28, episode 2, will feature Joey Graziadei’s first dates of the season, including his first one-on-one date with Daisy Kent. On premiere night, 28-year-old tennis pro Joey, who’s from Collegeville, Pennsylvania, was impressed by Daisy Kent, a 25-year-old account executive from Becker, Minnesota. Daisy grew up on a Christmas tree farm, so she pulled up in a bright red pickup truck with a decorated Christmas tree in the back. Daisy revealed in her confessional that she began losing her hearing when she was 17 due to Lyme disease, and got a cochlear implant last year. However, she hasn’t told Joey yet. Daisy and Joey shared a kiss later in the evening in front of her Christmas tree.

In a Bachelor season 28 preview shared by EW, it was revealed that Joey chose Daisy as his first one-on-one date recipient. They went to the BeachLife Festival in Los Angeles and had all-access passes. In the clip, Daisy said in her confessional, “Today I’m going on the first one-on-one with Joey. I’m honestly so stoked to do whatever we’re doing.” In addition, Joey gushed to the cameras, “I’m feeling great. I’m just really excited to be around Daisy right now.”

Daisy Doesn’t Want To Tell Joey About Hearing Loss Yet

Joey then led Daisy to the VIP area, where he asked her about what she does for a living. She replied that she’s transitioning out of doing software sales to run her nonprofit full-time. Joey replied, “That’s awesome.” Daisy explained that her nonprofit helps kids see that what makes them different can be empowering. Daisy then revealed in her confessional, “I definitely had to do some dancing around my cochlear implant. And I don’t want to tell him right now because it’s just not the right moment.” Daisy concluded by saying, “But right now I have a really good feeling about me and Joey.”

Joey seemed genuinely interested in and impressed by Daisy’s goal to focus solely on her nonprofit, which is called Hear Your Heart. According to Hear Your Heart’s website, the organization raises money to fund research for hearing loss and offer financial assistance to those who are equipped with hearing devices. Their number one priority is helping others hear and be heard. Joey is sure to be even more touched when he learns Daisy’s very personal connection to Hear Your Heart’s mission. Daisy wants to help others who’ve experienced hearing loss because she knows firsthand what it’s like.

Daisy and Joey seem to have a very strong connection. Out of the 32 Bachelor season 28 women, there was something about Daisy that stood out on premiere night. It’s a fantastic sign that Joey chose Daisy for the first one-on-one date because it means that he wanted to spend more time with her right away. This should give Daisy the confidence that Joey is interested in building their relationship even more. When Daisy finally tells Joey about her cochlear implant, it’s certain to be a beautiful moment that will build even more understanding and trust between them.

The Bachelor season 28 airs Mondays at 8 p.m. EST on ABC.
