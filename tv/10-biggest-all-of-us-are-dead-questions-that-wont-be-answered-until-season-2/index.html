10 Biggest All Of Us Are Dead Questions That Wont Be Answered Until Season 2

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1600 words 8 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

The release date for All of Us Are Dead season 2 has not been revealed, leaving fans with unanswered questions. The fate of Cheong-san in season 2 is still unknown, although there are hints that he may be alive. The storyline of season 2 may feature new antagonists and explore the development of Nam-ra’s powers and the existence of other halfbies.

Literature Review

From whether Cheong-san is really dead to what exactly happened to Nam-ra, All of Us Are Dead season 2 needs to answer quite a few burning questions.

All of Us Are Dead season 2’s release date still hasn’t been revealed, and there are a lot of questions from season 1 that the show will only answer once it returns. The Netflix 2024 K-drama slate all but confirmed that All of Us Are Dead 2 will not premiere in 2024, as the show was not listed as one of the upcoming Korean shows releasing this year on the platform. Therefore, it may take a while before viewers learn what happened to Cheong-san or what the show will be about now that it covered most of the webtoon. Although there are many All of Us Are Dead season 2 theories that try to predict what will happen next, some mysteries will only be solved once the hit Netflix K-drama returns. All of Us Are Dead was a major success for the platform, prompting a season 2 renewal just a few months after the show’s debut. Season 1 ended on a cliffhanger and left the door open for multiple characters to return, which is why there is so much anticipation for All of Us Are Dead2​​​​​.

10 Did Cheong-san Die In All Of Us Are Dead Season 2’s Finale? Cheong-san’s fate was left ambiguous

The biggest question going into All of Us Are Dead season 2 is whether Cheong-san is really dead. While falling from a building located in an area that would be bombarded by the government a few seconds later should mean certain death, it would not be surprising if Cheong-san returned in season 2. Firstly, Yoon Chan-young, the actor who plays Cheong-san, was featured in the season 2 announcement video. Additionally, his body was never shown, and Cheong-san is too big of a character to be written out of the show so swiftly. All of Us Are Dead Main Cast Actor Character Park Ji-hoo On-jo Yoon Chan-young Lee Cheong-san Cho Yi-hyun Choi Nam-ra Lomon Lee Su-hyeok

9 Will Gwi-nam Be In All Of Us Are Dead Season 2? If Cheong-san survived, Gwi-nam may have survived too

All of Us Are Dead season 2 can establish that Cheong-san survived the fall and the bombing, most likely due to his new halfbie powers. However, that same logic can be applied to Gwi-nam, who went through a lot in season 1 but always returned. Essentially, if Cheong-san is alive, Gwi-nam may be too. That said, Gwi-nam was already the main villain during most of All of Us Are Dead season 1, meaning the new season could benefit from new antagonists. In addition, Gwi-nam surviving yet again would be too big of a plot armor.

8 What Happened To Nam-ra After She Left The Group? Nam-ra somehow learned how to control her powers

Nam-ra left the group toward the end of All of Us Are Dead season 1 after she lost control of her powers and almost hurt On-jo. She never made it to the quarantine zone, but her friends never stopped looking for her. Nam-ra eventually contacted the rest of the group with the help of a fire, after which she explained she could now control her halfbie abilities. All of Us Are Dead season 2 could use flashbacks to show how exactly Nam-ra learned how to suppress her zombie instinct, and what she encountered during this time away from her friends,

7 How Many Halfbies Are There In All Of Us Are Dead? Nam-ra is now helping other hafbies

Nam-ra is helping protect other halfbies now that the military is looking for them. However, it remains unclear how many halfbies, or hambies, are there in All of Us Are Dead. There were a couple of examples of this different type of zombie in season 1, they were all limited to the school. The end of season 1, however, suggests that there are many of them currently living in what was left of Hyosan. Nam-ra’s All of Us Are Dead season 2 story can give a better look at just how many special zombies are left.

6 Will Nam-ra Rejoin The Group In All Of Us Are Dead Season 2? Nam-ra is currently living out in the open

Nam-ra was in a better place at the end of All of Us Are Dead than she was for most of the season. While away from her friends, she finally learned how to suppress the transformation and was no longer a risk to others. This could suggest she will rejoin the group in season 2, but things might not be that easy. Nam-ra is still a zombie, and she would never be allowed to enter the quarantine zone. The group can only reunite if they all leave the quarantine zone, which will likely happen eventually.

All Of Us Are Dead Season 2’s Nam-ra Story Means Her Season 1 Nickname Will Finally Make Sense
Nam-ra changed a lot during All of Us Are Dead season 1, and she will finally earn her previously ironic “class president” title in season 2.

5 Has The All Of Us Are Dead Zombie Virus Spread To Other Regions? This only happens at the end of the story in the webtoon

The Now In Our School webtoon’s ending sees the zombie virus infecting a Japanese fisherman, teasing that the apocalypse would soon move from one country to another. This twist was missing from All of Us Are Dead season 1, which does not mean the outbreak will not soon spread to other countries in the Netflix series. The Netflix K-drama could expand its scale and deal with a worldwide apocalypse, although this would the show’s tone and premise quite a lot.

4 Will Nam-ra & Su-hyeok Get Together In All Of Us Are Dead Season 2? They couldn’t be together before Nam-ra mastered her powers

Nam-ra and Su-hyeok fell in love with each other during season 1, but they could not be together because Nam-ra could not control her zombie transformation. Nam-ra could attack Su-hyeok or her friends at any time, so she eventually left them. However, now that Nam-ra can control her transformation, one of All of Us Are Dead best couples can finally have somewhat of a happily ever after, although new challenges will surely come up.

3 Will Cheong-san & On-jo Reunite In All Of Us Are Season 2? Cheong-san has yet to reveal himself

Even though it can be assumed that Cheong-san is alive and will return in season 2, it is unclear why he has not revealed himself to On-jo and the others yet. There was a small time skip at the end of All of Us Are Dead episode 12, which means that Cheong-san had the opportunity to let his friends know he was alive. One possibility is that Cheong-san is afraid he will hurt On-jo and the others, assuming he is now a halfbie like Gwi-nam or Nam-ra. Regardless, Cheong-san and On-jo’s eventual reunion is set to be a great moment.

2 Will Hee-su’s Child Be Important In All Of Us Are Dead Season 2? The baby was born of someone infected with the zombie virus

Hee-su’s child made for one of the most interesting storylines in All of Us Are Dead. The girl was turning into a zombie by the time the baby was born, which could indicate that this child would have powers or could even be the key to finding a cure for the virus. The detectives spent most of season 1 trying to get the baby to safety, and the child is currently living in the quarantine zone with the rest of the All of Us Are Dead characters. Whether they will be revisited in season 2 remains to be seen.

1 What Will All Of Us Are Dead Season 2 Be About? Most of the webtoon has already been adapted

One major question All of Us Are Dead still has not answered is what will season 2 be about considering most of the source material has already been covered. The Netflix show will now have to feature an original story building up from season 1’s finale, which itself changed a lot of things from the Now In Our School webtoon. All of Us Are Dead’s school setting can no longer be replicated, and the series may now take place in several locations rather than just one.
