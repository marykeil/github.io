10 Best Shows Like Netflix s One Day

SUMMARY

If you loved ‘One Day,’ these 10 shows, like ‘Normal People’ and ‘This Is Us,’ will fill that void perfectly. From ‘Daisy Jones & the Six’ to ‘Emily in Paris,’ these series offer complex romances and emotional drama like ‘One Day.’ Whether you’re in the mood for love-centered storylines or deep emotional connections, these shows will captivate you.

Literature Review

Netflix’s One Day has become a huge hit, and luckily for fans, there are 10 other TV shows that are perfect to watch after finishing the miniseries.

Netflix’s One Day has become a massive hit for the streaming giant, and luckily for fans, there are 10 perfect shows to watch after finishing the miniseries. One Day is based on the 2009 novel of the same name by David Nicholls, following the lives of main characters Dex and Emma, whose feelings for each other grow over a decade despite only seeing each other once a year. With One Day’s brilliant cast and captivating script, it’s no surprise that the show has garnered so much attention. One Day’s ending has left plenty of fans wanting more, and though the miniseries is unlikely to be renewed in any way, there are some good options for fans seeking a similar story. Not every romance series can live up to the hype surrounding One Day, but luckily, there is no shortage of shows to choose from. Ultimately, there are 10 TV shows that deserve the most consideration when looking for the right series to fill the One Day-shaped hole in fans’ hearts.

10 Normal People (2020) 1 season

Normal People

Release Date April 26, 2020 Cast Paul Mescal Streaming Service(s) Hulu Showrunner Alice Birch

Normal People is a fantastic miniseries based on the novel of the same name by Sally Rooney, and its love-centered storyline is perfect for fans of One Day. The show follows Marianne Sheridan and Connell Waldron as they navigate their entry into adulthood while trying to sort out the complicated feelings they have for each other. By the end of Normal People, it can be difficult to let go of the characters because of the deep emotional connections the show creates with the audience. This powerful story’s rich characterization is the main reason it’s so strongly reminiscent of One Day.

9 Daisy Jones & The Six (2023) 1 season

Daisy Jones & The Six

Release Date March 3, 2023 Cast Riley Keough , Sam Claflin , Camila Morrone , Suki Waterhouse , Nabiyah Be , Will Harrison , Josh Whitehouse , Sebastian Chacon , Tom Wright Streaming Service(s) Amazon Prime Video Directors Scott Neustadter

Daisy Jones & the Six is a fictional account of the titular band’s rise and fall in the 1970s Los Angeles music scene. The show is shot in a mockumentary style, using a variety of fictional interviews, concert footage, and studio sessions to make it feel like a genuine documentary rather than a fictional story. Daisy Jones & the Six is similar to Netflix’s One Day in that it follows young adults coming of age while coping with complicated romances. This show’s incredible cast and innovative approach to storytelling make it the perfect show to watch after One Day.

8 This Is Us (2016) 6 seasons

This Is Us

Release Date September 20, 2016 Cast Susan Kelechi Watson , Lonnie Chavis , Justin Hartley , Melanie Liburd , Hannah Zeile , Mandy Moore , Eris Baker , Sterling K. Brown , Milo Ventimiglia , Logan Shroyer , Faithe Herman , Niles Fitch , Parker Bates , Chris Sullivan , Chrissy Metz , Alexandra Breckenridge , Jon Huertas , Lyric Ross Asante Blackk Griffin Dunne Caitlin Thompson Chris Geere , Ron Cephas Jones , Mackenzie Hancsicsak Streaming Service(s) Hulu Showrunner Dan Fogelman

This Is Us is an NBC drama series that follows the Pearson family through decades of trials and tribulations. The show uses a combination of flashbacks, flash forwards, and the present time to explore the history of the complex Pearson family tree. With romances, family drama, and personal conflicts in abundance, This Is Us creates an emotionally gripping experience that grabs hold and doesn’t let go. Fans of One Day in search of their next series to binge should seriously consider this classic NBC drama.

7 A Million Little Things (2018) 5 seasons

A Million Little Things

Release Date September 26, 2018 Cast David Giuntoli , Allison Miller Streaming Service(s) Hulu Directors James Griffiths

A Million Little Things is an ABC original drama that follows a group of friends trying to live life to the fullest after one of their number tragically dies at a young age. This wildly popular series takes an emotional toll on the audience, which is indicative of its powerful script and talented cast. Its focus on growing up and finding love at a young age is strongly reminiscent of One Day, making it another strong option for fans of the ascendant Netflix miniseries.

6 Modern Love (2019) 2 seasons

Modern Love

Release Date October 18, 2019 Cast Anna Paquin , Dominique Fishback , Catherine Keener , Tom Burke , Lulu Wilson , Zane Pais , Tina Fey , Tobias Menzies , john gallagher , Telci Huynh , Maria Dizzia , Milan Ray , Kit Harington , Cristin Milioti , Julia Garner , Susan Blackwell , John Slattery , Larry Owens , Lucy Boynton , Marquis Rodriguez , Jack Reynor , Garrett Hedlund , Jane Alexander , Kathryn Gallagher , Dev Patel , Ben Rappaport , Gbenga Akinnagbe , Sophie Okonedo , Andrew Scott , Jeena Yi , Laurentiu Possa , jr. , Aparna Nancherla , James Saito , Don Wycherley , Shea Whigham , James Scully , Brandon Kyle Goodman , Grace Edwards , Andy Garcia , Isaac Powell , Zoe Chao , Anne Hathaway , Minnie Driver , Olivia Cooke , Sofia Boutella , Caitlin McGhee , Nikki M. James , Miranda Richardson , Zuzanna Szadkowski , Gary Carr Streaming Service(s) Netflix , Prime Video Showrunner John Carney

Modern Love is an anthology-style romantic comedy series that explores the various forms of love experienced by all human beings. It is based on the weekly New York Times column of the same name, which was started in 2004. This thought-provoking show examines more than just romantic or sexual love, but the focus it does put on these types of love makes it an attractive series for fans of One Day. Modern Love is a truly profound series, and its use of comedy nicely offsets the serious nature of the premise, making it both digestible and impactful.

5 Tell Me Lies (2022) 1 season

Tell Me Lies

Release Date September 7, 2022 Cast Grace Van Patten , Jackson White , Catherine Missal , Spencer House , Sonia Mena , Branden Cook , Alicia Crowder Showrunner Meaghan Oppenheimer Where To Watch Hulu

See at Hulu

Tell Me Lies is a Hulu original series that follows the relationship between Lucy Albright and Stephen DeMarco over an eight-year period after they meet each other in college. Of all the shows like One Day, Tell Me Lies probably has the most similar premise and storyline. Both shows follow a will-they won’t-they romance, and each of them is spread out over years. However, Tell Me Lies is tonally darker than One Day, and unlike the Netflix miniseries, its story is set to be extended into a second season, making now the right time to start this wonderfully written series.

4 First Love (2022) 1 season

First Love is a Japanese Netflix original series that follows the relationship between a man and a woman who try to rekindle their love 15 years after they grew up together. Like One Day, First Love follows a budding romance between two people trying to overcome the time they’ve spent apart. First Love benefits from strong lead performances and a thoughtful, if a bit idealistic, script. Its characterization is also thorough and believable, making it a perfect choice for fans of One Day.

3 Love (2016) 3 seasons

Love is a romantic comedy Netflix original series that explores the male and female perspectives on dating in the modern world. From the brilliant mind of Judd Apatow, this touching and hilarious show provides a fresh view on the world of dating and the process of building a relationship. Love is anchored by its nuanced protagonists, Mickey and Gus, whose untrustworthy natures and complicated pasts make it difficult for them to build a healthy relationship. This show’s talented cast and original script make it a genuine pleasure to watch, so One Day fans should strongly consider it.

2 Virgin River (2019) 5 seasons

Virgin River

Release Date December 6, 2019 Cast Jenny Cooper , Chase Petriw , Grayson Gurnsey , Martin Henderson , Colin Lawrence , Annette O’Toole , Benjamin Hollingsworth , Alexandra Breckenridge , Tim Matheson , Lauren Hammersley , Sarah Dugdale , marco grazzini Streaming Service(s) Netflix Showrunner Patrick Sean Smith

Virgin River, another Netflix original series, is a romantic drama about Mel Monroe, a nurse practitioner who moves to the titular town in northern California in an attempt to leave her past behind and start her life over. Virgin River quickly became a hit for Netflix, releasing five seasons between 2019 and 2023. The combination of romance and emotional conflict make this the perfect series to watch after One Day, and the complex characters and challenging themes make it a great show for any fan of nuanced storytelling.

1 Emily In Paris (2020) 3 seasons

Emily in Paris

Release Date October 2, 2020 Cast Lily Collins , Ashley Park , Bruno Gouery , Phillippine Leroy-Beaulieu , Samuel Arnold , Lucas Bravo , Camille Razat Streaming Service(s) Netflix Showrunner Darren Star

Emily in Paris is a romantic comedy series that follows Emily Cooper, a young American who moves to Paris when she is hired by a respected marketing firm in the city. Emily must adjust to her life in a new country as she explores new friendships and romantic endeavors. This creative series strikes a similar tune to One Day as it follows a young adult looking to put her life in order while figuring out her complicated romantic desires. With plenty of laughs and a prodigious emotional weight, Emily in Paris is an ideal series for fans of One Day.
