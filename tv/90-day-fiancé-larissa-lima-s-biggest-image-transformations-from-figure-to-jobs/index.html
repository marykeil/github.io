90 Day Fiancé’s Larissa Lima is perhaps the only cast member who’s managed to completely transform herself in many ways after leaving the franchise.

Larissa Lima from 90 Day Fiancé has had the biggest transformation in the franchise, and it’s not just her body that’s changed. Larissa is 37 years old and is best remembered for her debut in 90 Day Fiancé season 6 with her then-husband Colt Johnson. Larissa had come to Las Vegas from her native Brazil, where she left her two kids behind. Larissa’s fiery personality and penchant for drama made her the center of attention. She gave a ton of quotes to fans, which are still memorable years later, plus her shocking storyline with Colt and his mom, Debbie Johnson.

Larissa has taken a break from reality TV after she was reportedly fired in September 2020. Larissa keeps herself busy by doing social media promos and is known as a popular adult model. Larissa may have left the franchise, but how unrecognizable she is in 2024 is astounding. Larissa has managed to stay relevant by staying out of controversy, and 90 Day Fiancé fans wouldn’t mind seeing her back on their TV screens where she speaks her Larissean English that fans remember fondly.

Larissa Got Out Of Toxic Marriage With Colt Larissa Is Living The Single Life

Larissa and Colt were divorced within a year of getting married. They had one of the rockiest relationships in the 90 Day Fiancé franchise, as Colt called the police multiple times after heated arguments with Larissa. She was arrested for domestic violence after each fight. Colt was putting Larissa’s immigration in danger by calling the cops on her, clearly trying to get her deported. Larissa was charged the third time when she was accused of leaving Colt’s face “bloodied,” according to Us Weekly. The divorce turned out to be a blessing for Larissa as she began a new life as a single woman and transformed herself physically, professionally, and beyond.

Larissa Became A Successful Adult Content Creator Larissa Has Found Her True Calling

Larissa unveiled her plastic surgery makeover in September 2020 via news articles and social media posts, and they were shocking. Larissa’s Kylie Jenner-inspired look had supposedly cost her over $75,000. However, Larissa decided to do a web show on a platform called CamSoda, where she did a one-hour performance to show off her new body. According to TMZ, Larissa made over $100,000 from that show alone. The money came from people who paid to watch the show and from tips she made. Larissa also began making a living by sharing adult content on OnlyFans.

Larissa Bounced Back After Botched Surgery Larissa Lost Her Belly Button

In July 2022, Larissa decided to open up about a surgery gone wrong in an Instagram post that has since been deleted. Larissa revealed she was scared and embarrassed to speak up but admitted it’s been a year since she had botched surgery. Larissa underwent an abdominoplasty, or a tummy tuck, where her belly button was allegedly removed and disposed of without her consent. Larissa had several revisions to create a belly button, but it was never completely fixed. Still, Larissa, who found the courage to speak about the failed surgery, didn’t let it hold her back. She has continued to model and isn’t hiding her body.

Larissa Managed To Live In The U.S. Despite Deportation Scare Larissa Was Reportedly Arrested By ICE

In September 2020, TMZ reported that Larissa was “placed in removal proceedings” per the Immigration and Nationality Act, according to documents obtained by the publication. Larissa had been arrested and released by Immigration and Customs Enforcement. She was set for a hearing that was going to determine whether she was going to have to surrender for removal from the U.S. The arrest took place when Larissa was leaving Las Vegas. In January 2021, Larissa revealed that she wasn’t going to get deported to her native Brazil, mentioning that someone had planned to provoke her and have her deported.

Larissa Is Now Friends With Debbie Johnson Debbie Lives In Larissa’s House Now

Any longtime 90 Day Fiancé viewer will be familiar with Larissa and Debbie’s on-screen and off-screen arguments. Colt and Debbie used to come as a package deal as Colt lived with Debbie and was very co-dependent on her. Debbie was almost always present and involved in the drama when Larissa and Colt were fighting. Debbie was always the third wheel in Larissa and Colt’s relationship. Even after Colt and Larissa were divorced, Debbie couldn’t stop herself from interfering in their lives.

However, in a plot twist, the bitter enemies ended their feud in 2021. Time did heal all wounds, and Larissa became unlikely friends with Debbie. Perhaps no longer being involved in each other’s lives made them realize how alike they were. Larissa had time to reflect on her relationship with Debbie, so she penned an emotional apology to her former mother-in-law. Larissa now respected Debbie and thought of her as a dear friend. In 2023, Larissa was spotted out with Debbie and her boyfriend, Tony Starcevich. In all likeliness, it may have been Colt who was causing issues between the two women with similar, explosive personalities.
