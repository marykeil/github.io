10 Most Exciting Things To Expect From All American Season 6

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1600 words 8 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Season 6 of All American promises more drama on and off the field, challenging the characters to make tough decisions about their futures. Relationships like Asher & Jaymee, Spencer & Jordan, and Jordan & Layla continue to evolve, providing solid foundations for growth. Exciting storylines involving characters like Olivia, Layla, and Spencer will explore new opportunities and challenges, keeping viewers engaged.

Literature Review

As season 6 of All American draws closer, plenty of plot twists and character development are in store based on hints from the trailer and actors.

All American has kept the audience guessing from the beginning, and with season 6 around the corner, viewers can expect exciting things from the upcoming story. Loosely based on the real-life of the NFL player Spencer Paysinger, the series deals with class inequality and the pressure to perform that young athletes face. Based on the All American season 6 trailer, plenty of drama on and off the field awaits the characters, as well as tough decisions about their futures. Since All American started airing on The CW in 2018, it’s followed the lives and football careers of Spencer James (Daniel Ezra) and Jordan Baker (Michael Evans Behling). Starting with their high school team, then moving to college and delving into the drama of the young men’s personal lives, the series has grown a lot since season 1. Now that the characters are older and wiser, All American’s tone is shifting, and season 6 will cement what the next phase of the series will be. 10 Asher & Jaymee Becoming Parents Adjusting to life with a new addition to their family.

Asher (Cody Christian) and his girlfriend Jaymee (Miya Horcher) discovered they were expecting a baby in season 5 and had to tell the team, their friends, and their parents. Though their loved ones were ultimately supportive, it’s still a long road ahead to learn how to balance their responsibilities with a newborn. However, Asher and Jaymee have shown they can get through anything, even with Asher’s career on the rise as a scout for his team. This means they’re ready to welcome the baby and that the rest of the team will step up to help them.

9 Spencer & Jordan Clashing On The Field Since the beginning of the series, these two stars have had a tenuous relationship.

While Spencer is the protagonist of the series, he wouldn’t be half as interesting without the people who challenge him. Jordan has always pushed Spencer to be better, both on and off the field. At first, this was through competition, but later, they started to bond over the pressure to make it to the NFL. However, this doesn’t mean that their relationship always runs smoothly, even if they’ve matured out of the anger they felt toward each other as teenagers. The stakes to make it to the NFL are higher than ever as they get deeper into their college careers, and that means that tempers are sure to flare when they’re supposed to be playing as a team. If scouts are coming to watch them play, then Spencer and Jordan need to put aside any differences and work together so they can show off their skills as players. Though their conflicts make for good drama, it’s always hard to see beloved characters at odds.

8 Jordan & Layla Staying Together One of the best romances in the show is finally in a solid place.

Layla (Greta Onieogou) and Jordan might be the best couple on All American. They love and support each other unconditionally, and after all the relationship drama they’ve both faced, they deserve to be happy together. The epic slow-burn romance they had all started in childhood has blossomed into something beautiful. As Jordan takes the next step in his football career and Layla expands her record label and other business pursuits, their relationship will be a solid foundation that grounds them when they need it most.

7 Olivia & Spencer Reuniting After Olivia’s absence and long-awaited return.

Now that Olivia (Samantha Logan) is back from London, Spencer and Olivia’s story on All American season 6 is bound to heat up on the heels of Spencer’s confession that he still loves her. Olivia is one of the most politically active characters on the show, but as a journalist, her desire to find the truth, no matter what, pushed the couple apart at the beginning of season 5. While they’ll always be in each other’s lives, how that relationship will evolve now that they’re back in the same place will be an important dramatic thrust for season 6.

All American: Why Did Olivia & Spencer Break Up?
Olivia and Spencer have a great love story on All American as friends who become something more, and there is a sad reason for their split.

6 Wrapping Up Coach Montes’ Story All American must treat his character’s departure with delicacy.

The actor who portrayed Coach Montes, Kamar de los Reyes, tragically passed away in late 2023, and the entire cast and crew of All American have expressed their sadness at this loss. For an actor to suddenly be taken from his work and his show in this way is always deeply affecting for a series. The creators must grapple with how best to prioritize sensitivity and care in discussing his absence. His character’s story will be wrapped up one way or another, but All American must balance the drama of the series with a tribute to the late actor.

5 Carrying On Billy’s Legacy Billy’s death still impacts the future of All American.

Season 5 shook things up with the enormous loss of Billy Baker (Taye Diggs), a constant and beloved coach and father on the series. Billy Baker didn’t die in real life, but that doesn’t make the loss of the character any easier to bear. While the accident that led to his death happened halfway through season 5, it’s still a constant source of tension and sadness for the characters. With season 6 approaching, enough time has passed for his children, Jordan and Olivia, and the other characters to move forward and honor his legacy.

4 Returning To Football Getting back to what made the show great in the first place.

The heart of All American is football and seeing the characters play out their dramas on the field when words don’t do the trick. Season 5 spent time exploring what life looked like outside the game and handling the life changes that each character faced. It seems that season 6 will bring back some equilibrium to the show, getting Spencer and Jordan back in the game as much as possible. The upcoming season promises to be more adult than ever before, so the difference between the young men playing as high schoolers and playing now should be prominent.

3 Spencer Committing To His NFL Dreams Grappling with the memory of his father and what he wants for himself.

Spencer’s journey to the NFL is what started the show, and though All American deviates a good deal away from Paysinger’s real life, it would be shocking if he didn’t make it to the big leagues. However, the trailer indicates that he’s been having doubts about his future and is questioning if this path to professional football is what he really wants. After all this time, it makes sense that Spencer will self-reflect, and this doubt will fuel his motivation once he reconnects with what made him love football in the first place. The trailer indicates that he’s been having doubts about his future and is questioning if this path to professional football is what he really wants.

2 Olivia Exploring Her Interests Outside Spencer Her journey to establish herself as a journalist and change the world.

Olivia and Spencer’s relationship is a major plot point in the show. However, that doesn’t mean that either character is solely defined by their relationship. Olivia has supported Spencer’s dreams of the NFL, and Spencer was there when Olivia was struggling with her sobriety. Now, Olivia has the chance to push herself even further outside their relationship. Returning from London will give her a new lease on life and a new perspective to influence her reporting. Elevating Olivia’s story arc and exploring her future is a logical next step for season 6.

6 Reasons All American’s Spencer & Olivia Will Get Back Together
Spencer and Olivia may not have gotten back together yet, but there are several reasons why they will get back together in All American.

1 Layla Opening Her Business Furthering her career past the record label and thriving.

Layla’s record label is an important part of her character, as is her love of music. Season 6 will involve her opening a new business to feature artists she works with. It will also serve as a place for her friends to hang out together outside college and the field. New characters will be introduced, as well as a new side of Layla that illustrates how independent she has become. Opening her own business is a great opportunity for Jordan to lend his support to Layla and create new storylines for the series.
