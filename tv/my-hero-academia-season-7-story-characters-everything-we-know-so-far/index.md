## Literature Review
Everything there is to know about My Hero Academia&#39;s seventh season, including the upcoming story, new characters, and more.




My Hero Academia&#39;s sixth season was one of the series&#39; best ever, met with resounding approval for its handling of the important Paranormal Liberation War and Dark Deku arcs. Anticipation for My Hero Academia season 7 is incredibly high as well, which will feature a new character teased at the end of season six as well as the build-up to and perhaps even the beginnings of the Final War arc. Here&#39;s everything fans should know about season 7 of My Hero Academia, at least as it stands now.





 Latest News on My Hero Academia Season 7 
          

My Hero Academia season 7 was announced on March 25, 2023, following the airing of the final episode of season 6. No details were revealed about it other than that it was entering production, meaning that there is no known release date for My Hero Academia season 7. On December 10, 2023, it was announced via X (formerly known as Twitter) that season 7 would have a release window of Spring 2024, although no more specific date has yet been established. Rumors suggest it may be aiming for an April release, but this should be taken with a grain of salt.



 The Story of My Hero Academia Season 7 
          




Assuming the anime follows the manga, My Hero Academia season 7 will open with what&#39;s known as the Star and Stripe arc, following the American character who was introduced at the tail end of the sixth season, Star and Stripe. From there, the story progresses into its last arc, the Final War arc, which chronicles the ultimate clash between the assembled hero and villain forces, deciding the fate of Japan and quite likely the world. The Final War arc is currently ongoing in the manga and has yet to conclude, so it&#39;s unknown whether season 7 will cover the Final War arc in full, or if it will only adapt a portion of the arc, with the rest left up to a potential season 8.



 The Characters of My Hero Academia Season 7 
          

Naturally, all of fans&#39; favorite students and pro heroes will be returning in season 7, and many have a significant role to play in the Final War. Most important, though, is the introduction of the new character seen at the end of season 6, Star and Stripe. Star and Stripe is America&#39;s #1 hero, who has gone against orders to head to Japan and lend aid to All Might, who was a hero of hers during his time in America. Star and Stripe is incredibly powerful, with a Quirk that&#39;s able to let her do nearly anything she can imagine, making her inclusion exciting.






 My Hero Academia Season 7 Release Date 
          

Historically, a season of My Hero Academia takes a little over a year of production time, with the new season debuting almost a year after the previous season ended. A release window of &#34;Spring 2024&#34; was announced but without any more specific date given. That would suggest a release date no earlier than April 2024, and likely a bit later than that. Because the Final War arc of the manga has yet to be concluded, it&#39;s also difficult to estimate the number of episodes that season 7 is planned to have. It will likely have at least as many as previous seasons, 25, but if there isn&#39;t enough content left for an eighth season, season 7 could be extended.
