Why The TVA Are Trying To Kill Deadpool In Deadpool 3

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1100 words 5 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Deadpool is courted by the TVA to become a hunter but quickly clashes with them, leading to violent confrontations. Deadpool’s previous time-traveling and universe-hopping antics in Deadpool 2 set up the TVA’s awareness of him. The presence of a comic book reference in the trailer suggests a connection to Avengers: Secret Wars and the involvement of Doctor Doom.

Literature Review

The Deadpool & Wolverine trailer showed Deadpool locked in a deadly struggle with the TVA, the MCU organization introduced in Loki season 1.

The full Deadpool & Wolverine trailer wasted no time planting Deadpool firmly in the MCU with Ryan Reynolds’ superhero seen coming into immediate conflict with the TVA from Loki.

After two Deadpool movies were released by Fox as part of the X-Men franchise, the Merc with a Mouth was brought under the Marvel Studios umbrella when Disney acquired Fox film assets in 2019, allowing Wade Wilson to join the MCU. Although the TVA might seem like a strange antagonist for Deadpool, it does make sense givenDeadpool’s previous antics of messing with timelines.

When they were introduced in Loki season 1, the TVA was originally tasked with tracking down rogue variants from across the multiverse who created branches from the Sacred Timeline. Deadpool 2 revolved heavily around time travel, and Deadpool did quite a few things that would have landed him on the TVA’s radar. The Loki season 2 ending showed that the TVA had changed into an organization that is meant to protect the multiverse. Though it seems like the TVA might be interested in recruiting Deadpool to become one of these hunters, the Deadpool & Wolverine trailer would suggest that things don’t work out so well.

Deadpool Is The TVA’s New Hunter

Near the beginning of the Deadpool & Wolverine trailer, Deadpool is seen being courted to join the TVA with the promise of becoming an MCU hero. This would seem like a natural fit if the TVA is seeking new hunters to go after Kang variants. A brief scene in Loki season 2, episode 6 showed that the TVA does keep tabs on Kang variants, and they said that Ant-Man and the Wasp “handled” a variant for them. Deadpool is an effective and powerful mercenary, making him a natural choice for hunting Kang.

    Every Actor Cast In Deadpool 3 (So Far)  Ryan Reynolds is joining the MCU as the Merc with a Mouth in Phase 6's Deadpool 3, and some of his supporting cast has already been revealed.

Deadpool initially seems interested in the offer, but it quickly becomes apparent that he and the TVA won’t see eye to eye. Clips of Deadpool brutally murdering TVA agents would suggest he won’t be a loyal hunter for very long. This isn’t too surprising, as Deadpool doesn’t seem like the type to work well as part of a strict organization like the TVA. Deadpool may have a different definition of what protecting the multiverse looks like, though it’s unclear what exactly causes the rift in the relationship between the Merc with a Mouth and the TVA.

Deadpool 2’s Ending Perfectly Sets Up The TVA’s Plan

How the TVA knows about Deadpool in the first place is pretty well set up by Deadpool 2’s ending, even though the movie didn’t intend it at the time. Deadpool 2 showed Wade Wilson time-travelingand making major changes to his timeline. This included bringing Vanessa back from the dead, and taking out the X-Men Origins: Wolverine version of himself that he wasn’t a fan of. Deadpool even seems to cross into different universes, traveling to shoot ‘Ryan Reynolds’ before he can star in the much-maligned Green Lantern.

Though most of these scenes are just for laughs, they also firmly establish why the TVA would know about Deadpool. Changing events on a timeline and even hopping universes are the exact types of shenanigans that would initially have had Deadpool’s universe to be pruned from the Sacred Timeline. Now, with Loki overseeing the multiverse, the TVA wouldn’t be looking to destroy Deadpool’s universe, but they may see him as a potential ally given his experience with time-traveling and hopping between worlds.

Is Deadpool 3 Setting Up A Secret Wars Shock?

If Deadpool decides to team up with variants from across the multiverse against the TVA, this could lead to Avengers: Secret Wars. The MCU’s Secret Wars is thought to include many variants from across the Marvel multiverse, and it could be possible that Deadpool has a hand in assembling at least some of them. This theory may have been lent some extra credibility thanks to a quick Easter egg near the end of the trailer.

  Deadpool & Wolverine   Release Date  July 26, 2024   Director  Shawn Levy   Cast Ryan Reynolds , Hugh Jackman , Morena Baccarin , Brianna Hildebrand , Jennifer Garner   Writers  Rhett Reese , Paul Wernick   Franchise(s)  Deadpool , Marvel Cinematic Universe

Just before Wolverine grabs Deadpool, there is a copy of Secret Wars #5 by Jonathan Hickman, Esad Ribić, and Ive Svorcinaon on the ground next to Deadpool. The Easter egg could hint that Deadpool & Wolverine is directly connected to the events of Avengers: Secret Wars. This could be especially true if Deadpool and Wolverine are fighting in The Void, which may serve as the battleground for Avengers: Secret Wars.

Of course, this comic may also be signaling the involvement of Doctor Doom in either Deadpool & Wolverine or Avengers: Secret Wars. Secret Wars #5 does focus heavily on Doctor Doom, so that connection would make sense. Of course, it’s hard to know with a Deadpool movie when something is serious or meant as a joke. Deadpool & Wolverine looks like it will have a similarly irreverent tone as previous Deadpool movies, so it will be interesting to see how seriously it takes any aspect of the MCU.
