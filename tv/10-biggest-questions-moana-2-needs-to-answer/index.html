10 Biggest Questions Moana 2 Needs To Answer

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1800 words 9 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Moana 2 can provide more details about Maui’s past, including why his human family abandoned him. The sequel can explain the significance of the chief’s necklace and how Moana’s ancestors were connected to the heart of Te Fiti. Moana 2 has the opportunity to explore the backstory of Gramma Tala and reveal what happened to her when she was younger.

Literature Review

Moana is finally getting a sequel 8 years later, which means a chance for the biggest unanswered questions from the first movie to be addressed.

Moana 2 is on the way, and the sequel will finally have the chance to answer the biggest question from the original movie. Released in 2016, Moana began the story of the hero of Motunui, who found Maui and restored the heart of Te Fiti. Now, eight years later, Disney has officially announced that Moana’s tale will continue into another film. This is a significant surprise, especially considering the live-action remake of Moana expected in 2025. Still, considering the potential for an even grander story, Moana 2 is welcome news. The original 2016 Moana movie ended with Te Fiti regaining her heart and restoring the gift of life to the world. This meant that Moana and her village could again explore the sea like their ancestors did, voyaging, discovering more islands, and having more adventures. Therefore, Moana 2, which will hit theaters on November 27th, 2024, could follow any number of stories. Seeing what Moana will do next is an exciting concept, but the added benefit of the sequel is a chance to fill in the cracks and curiosities left behind by the original film.

10 Why Did Maui’s Human Family Abandon Him? Moana 2 can include more details about Maui’s past.

Moana only grazed the surface of Maui’s origin story. The demigod of the wind and sea was born to humans and tossed into the ocean as an infant, where he was claimed by the gods and given his miraculous fishhook. However, Maui’s experience with rejection made him eager to earn humans’ approval, which got him into all kinds of trouble. The foundation of Maui’s story was enough for the first movie, but it would be interesting to learn more about what happened with the character’s family in Moana 2.

Moana 2 Shows Disney Is Finally Listening To Fans (In The Right Way)
It’s taken Disney a long time to release Moana 2, but the sequel’s recent update suggests the studio is finally listening to fans in the right way.

9 Why Was The Chief’s Necklace Made To Hold The Heart Of Te Fiti? Moana 2 can explain how the heart of Te Fiti was connected to Moana’s ancestors.

After the ocean gave baby Moana the heart of Te Fiti, Gramma Tala held onto it until her granddaughter was old enough to attempt her mission. She kept it in a shell necklace, which was later revealed to have been passed from chief to chief over centuries—up until Chief Tui, that is. The fact that this necklace was precisely the correct size and shape to hold the heart couldn’t be a coincidence, which has led to the theory that Moana’s family had been trying to return the heart of Te Fiti for generations. But what went wrong, and what did Chief Tui know of the matter?

8 Was Chief Tui Drawn To The Ocean For The Same Reason As Moana? Was Chief Tui originally selected to return the heart of Te Fiti?

Moana revealed that Chief Tui had been just as drawn to the ocean as Moana when he was young, but he put that longing aside when his friend was killed. This, combined with his apparent recognition of the heart of Te Fiti, seems to indicate that he had once been chosen by the ocean like Moana was. Of course, the original movie was never clear about this, meaning the door has been left open for Moana 2 to provide a more precise explanation.

7 What Was Gramma Tala Like When She Was Young? Moana 2 can dive into Gramma Tala’s past.

Gramma Tala was easily one of the best characters of Moana. She was quirky and spirited, and her love and belief in Moana were pivotal in the girl’s success. Still, the fact that Tala was such an interesting character makes it all the more frustrating that we know so little about her. It’s assumed that she was either chief herself or married to the chief before her son took over, and her knowledge of Maui, the heart of Te Fiti, the boats, and the ocean’s power indicates an exciting life full of magic. Perhaps Moana 2 can include some flashbacks to Gramma Tala’s past. The teaser for Moana 2 rules out a prequel, but flashbacks to the events before Moana could still be possible to create a larger world for the story.

6 Did Gramma Tala Die Because She Gave Moana The Heart? The heart may have kept Gramma Tala alive.

A common theory that developed after Moana is that Gramma Tala only died because she passed the heart of Te Fiti over to her granddaughter. Since the glowing green stone had the power to create life, it makes sense that the person who possessed it would gain something from that power. This would make Tala’s decision that it was time for Moana to carry out her destiny all the more impactful since it means she made a personal sacrifice to save the world. Of course, though moments in the film support this, it isn’t confirmed. So, Moana 2 could definitely answer this 8-year-old question.

5 What Happened To Tamatoa After Moana? Moana 2 should bring back Tamatoa.

Moana didn’t have a traditional villain, instead revealing that Te Ka was really just Te Fiti without her heart. Still, the movie threw in a character with that delicious Disney-villain flavor—Tamatoa. He was purely evil (he even ate his grandma) and had an excellent villain song, but Moana left his fate ambiguous since the last we see of him he was stuck on his back in the realm of monsters. Even if Moana2 only returned to Tamatoa in a post-credits scene (like in Moana), it would be great to find out what he has been up to since and whether he escaped his fate and collected more shinies.

4 How Could Mortals Use The Heart Of Te Fiti To Create Life? A new villain should try to use the heart of Te Fiti in Moana 2.

We know that Maui wanted to earn the love and appreciation of mortals all those years before Moana, which is why he stole the heart of Te Fiti. However, it’s never precisely clear what he expected them to do with it. Maui noted that the heart would give them the “power to create life itself,” but there is never any indication of how this could be done. Throughout Moana, all the thing ever did was glow green. If Moana 2 sees another villain steal the heart of Te Fiti, it would be interesting to see how they could actually put it to use.

3 Could The Ocean Still Help Moana After The First Movie? Will the ocean still be a “character” in Moana 2?

The ocean was a character all its own in Moana, so it’s hard to imagine a Moana 2 without the silent but sassy personality among the bunch.

The ocean was a character all its own in Moana, so it’s hard to imagine a Moana2 without the silent but sassy personality among the bunch. However, it’s never entirely clear if the ocean was only able to help Moana as it did because of her mission. With the heart of Te Fiti restored and Moana and her people traveling the seas, could the girl still interact with the ocean, or did this character go back to behaving like a regular body of water? It’s another question Moana 2 is sure to answer.

2 What Is Maui’s Role After Restoring The Heart Of Te Fiti Moana 2 can explore what Maui does for the world now that he restored the heart of Te Fiti.

As was said repeatedly throughout Moana, Maui was the demigod of the wind and sea. However, aside from giving the mortals gifts, it’s never clear what his actual duties were. After being trapped on an island for a thousand years, he had to return the heart of Te Fiti, but what then? It’s possible he went back to creating winds and pulling islands from the sea, but precisely how he goes about this would be fun to explore in Moana 2. Additionally, it would be interesting to see what other gods and demigods of the Pacific were up to.

1 Did Moana Take Over As Chief? Moana 2 can reveal Moana’s role in her community after the first movie’s ending.

The end of Moana sees a shell placed atop the stack of stones representing the chiefs of Motunui. Early in the movie, Chief Tui explains that when Moana is ready, she will place her own stone there. The shell seems to indicate that she was officially made the chief, and it would be her job to lead the island in their voyages. Still, this isn’t entirely clear. The shell might have just been a sign that they were leaving this tradition behind, and she may have become a lead Wayfinder while her father continued as chief (he was still young, after all). Moana 2 will have to clear this up.
