The Bachelor Season 28: Kelsey Toussant’s Jobs & Education

Kelsey’s ABC bio lists her career as an actress, and she has quite a few acting credits to her name. However, she also has some other jobs. She works as a realtor, and also owns her own clothing store called Toussant. According to Toussant’s official website, the Los Angeles-based boutique was established in 2020 by Kelsey. It features “clothes with personality.”

Toussant’s website states, “Toussant is for the woman who wears authenticity and confidence like a full coverage foundation.” Toussant’s mission statement is, “We hope to create one-of-a-kind designs that will one day be undeniably Toussant. Going from boutique to unique while always keeping you, our fashion-forward family, in mind!” Toussant sells dresses, sets, jumpers and rompers, tops, bottoms, and accessories, and has clothes for curvy and plus-sized women. Kelsey models some of the clothes on Toussant’s website.

Kelsey is also a trained actress, who has studied her craft. According to Kelsey’s official website, The Bachelor season 28 contestant has a B.A. in Theater from California State University of Northridge. She has trained in Killian’s Workshop’s Commercial Foundations Class, and with actor, director, and screenwriter Anthony Meindl’s Actor Workshop Foundations Class. Kelsey seems to be committed to learning as much as she can about acting.
