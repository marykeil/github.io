10 Questions The Walking Dead s Rick & Michonne Spinoff Must Answer

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1800 words 8 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Rick and Michonne’s return will boost The Walking Dead’s popularity once again, but it also leaves many unanswered questions. The Ones Who Live must clarify why the main survivors have never encountered the CRM despite their power in the new world. The spinoff must address the mysteries surrounding Michonne’s new group, Rick’s Japanese phone message, and the meaning of A and B in The Walking Dead.

Literature Review

The Ones Who Live will be one of The Walking Dead’s biggest projects in years, but Rick and Michonne’s spinoffs must answer some crucial questions.

The Ones Who Live offers plenty of excitement with Rick and Michonne returning to the franchise, but their spinoff show must answer some big questions. Andrew Lincoln’s Rick Grimes exiting The Walking Dead was one of the show’s biggest moments, and while the franchise had an uncertain future, his return will give the series some new momentum. The spinoff shows that followed the main series found their own level of success, but both Rick and Michonne returning will boost The Walking Dead’s popularity once again. While these characters re-entering the series will be huge, it also leaves many unanswered questions. With Rick and Michonne being gone for such a long time, their stories have massive gaps that must be filled. Not only have they been gone for years, but canonically, even longer has passed in the show, indicating how many mysteries their initial departures created. Although Rick and Michonne’s return creates a lot of questions, the CRM’s involvement with the spinoff leads to even more theories. They have been one of the most mysterious groups in the franchise, only appearing in the World Beyond spinoff, and have plenty of unanswered lore that The Ones Who Live must unveil. The Ones Who Live is set to premiere on February 25, 2024, on AMC and AMC+.

10 Why Haven’t Any Of The Main Survivors Discovered The CRM? The Main Survivors Seemingly Haven’t Encountered The CRM After 11 Seasons

Given how powerful the CRM are in The Walking Dead, it is a mystery that none of the main survivors are familiar with them. Rick’s main group has traveled pretty far throughout the series and encountered numerous other survivors, yet rarely anyone associated with the CRM. The closest connection the show’s key characters have is Jadis, but their overall lack of awareness regarding the CRM is odd considering how much control they have of the new world. The Ones Who Live should clarify why the CRM has managed to stay off the radar of the show’s main characters.

9 How Did Michonne Meet Her New Group? Michonne Is With A Group Of New Survivors In The Ones Who Live’s Trailer

The Ones Who Live trailer reveals Michonne has a new group, but it is unclear how she found them. Michonne’s story after the conclusion of The Walking Dead is completely unknown, meaning the spinoff will have to explain how she met this new group. Her latest allies seem like they will play a big role in the show, so getting to know them, their background, and their motives are important. The show will likely address the details of Michonne’s journey to find Rick, but revealing the details about the group helping her is essential to the spinoff’s story.

12 Things We’re Hoping To See In Rick Grimes’ Walking Dead Show, The Ones Who Live Rick Grimes’ Walking Dead spinoff will see the long-awaited return of Rick and Michonne and there are many things we’d love to see the show include.

8 Why Was Rick’s Phone Message Written In Japanese? Rick Wrote A Hopeful Message On His Phone In Japanese

Another mystery from The Walking Dead was regarding Michonne finding Rick’s phone with Japanese writing on it. The meaning of Rick’s phone message is revealed in The Ones Who Live trailer, with the text translating to “Believe a little longer.” While the trailer reveals the message’s meaning, it is still unclear why it was written in Japanese. The series has never indicated Rick can speak the language, so he has presumably learned Japanese or has an ally willing to help create an optimistic message for his friends to find. The origins of this message are something the spinoff must clarify.

7 What Does A & B Mean In The Walking Dead? The A And B Imagery Has Been Frequently Seen Throughout The Walking Dead

Throughout The Walking Dead, the letters A and B constantly appear, be it on buildings, shirts, or other environments. Even the two most important members of the Whisperers were named Alpha and Beta. With the imagery being constant, The Walking Dead’s A and B mystery has often been associated with the CRM. Jadis mentions trading people using the letters in World Beyond, all but confirming there is a CRM connection. There have been many theories suggesting what the letters actually mean, but Rick and Michonne’s spinoff can finally provide the answers given the CRM’s prominence in the show.

6 Why Haven’t The CRM Attacked Rick’s Group? The CRM Attacked Innocent Groups In The Walking Dead: World Beyond

Despite being such a menacing and intelligent faction, The CRM have not attacked Rick’s group in The Walking Dead, suggesting there could be deeper reasons for this. Part of it could be to have leverage over Rick, making him fall in line otherwise they’ll attack. The Ones Who Live looks like it will explore Rick and Major General Beale’s relationship, which could unveil the reason. The Commonwealth seems like another community that would be on the CRM’s radar, yet there hasn’t been a war, but learning more about the CRM could provide context as to why they have held back.

5 How Did The Events Of World Beyond Impact The CRM? The CRM Appears To Be Weaker After World Beyond

After World Beyond, the CRM will be weaker, and the impact of the show’s main characters should be addressed in Rick and Michonne’s spinoff. World Beyond’s protagonists successfully freed the CRM’s scientists from the research facility, resulting in many of the villains’ plans being thwarted. While they likely have more scientists and research going on elsewhere, losing such a big part of their research will make the CRM weaker and impact their future. Even if there are no cameos from World Beyond characters, the impact they had should be mentioned in The Ones Who Live, given how much they sacrificed.

4 Will The CRM Impact The Other Walking Dead Spinoffs? The CRM’s Influence Could Spread To Dead City Or Daryl Dixon

With the CRM looking like the next big villains of The Walking Dead, it is possible their impact spreads beyond The Ones Who Live. One cut detail hinted at The Walking Dead’s CRM connecting to Daryl Dixon’s villains, suggesting that Genet and the Pouvoir could be affiliated with the CRM. Since the Pouvoir does have a scientific focus and a thirst to lead France, the connection is plausible. Likewise, the CRM has facilities in New York where Dead City takes place, offering a loose link, and Rick and Michonne’s spinoff will hopefully shed more light on the CRM’s reach.

3 Why Is The CRM Keeping Rick Grimes Alive? Rick Grimes Seems To Be Important To The CRM

Rick Grimes being kept alive all these years by the CRM must have some significance, and the spinoff needs to address why the protagonist is so important to the group. Rick has some great survival instincts and amazing leadership skills, but the CRM likely has many soldiers with the same qualities. Perhaps Jadis plays a role in Rick’s survival, but there is certainly a mystery around why the group want him alive. This is one of the biggest questions The Ones Who Live must answer, as it will make Rick’s CRM role in The Walking Dead much clearer. 1:40

Rick Grimes’ Return Proves You Missed The Walking Dead’s Most Important Spinoff Judging by what’s now known about The Walking Dead: The Ones Who Live, the most important spinoff in The Walking Dead franchise went largely unseen.

2 What Is The CRM’s Overall Goal? There Is Still Some Uncertainty About The CRM’s True Motivations

While the CRM’s motive appears to be trying to find a cure to the outbreak, it doesn’t seem to stop there. Using humans to experiment on and wiping out communities they see as potential threats isn’t the attempt to restore humanity that they claim it is. It seems like on top of curing the zombie virus, the CRM also want control of the new world and the Civic Republic’s large community would suggest the military already have a strong start on this goal. The spinoff will reveal more about the CRM and potentially offer an insight into their true intentions.

1 What Happened To Rick After He Was Taken Away? Rick’s Post Season 9 Story Is Still Unknown

It is rare so much time passes in a TV series without the protagonist, yet Rick Grimes has been absent from The Walking Dead for over five years. Since he was taken in that helicopter after nearly dying, Rick’s story has been a mystery, one that must be answered in The Ones Who Live. While he obviously ends up with the CRM, the threats he encountered on the way should be revealed as well as his escape attempts. Rick’s post-season 9 story is The Walking Dead’s biggest unanswered question, but the spinoff can finally reveal what happened to Rick Grimes.

The Walking Dead: The Ones Who Live
TV-MA Action Drama Horror

Created by Scott M. Gimple and Danai Gurira, The Walking Dead: The Ones Who Live is a sequel spin-off series in The Walking Dead television franchise. The series picks up some time after Michonne departs from the original series, as she searches to be reunited with her lover, Rick Grimes. Meanwhile, Rick finds himself amid another war between the living and the dead.
