15 Best RomComs On Amazon Prime Right Now

SUMMARY

Amazon Prime Video offers a wide selection of romantic comedies, ranging from classic favorites to newer hits. The best rom-coms on Amazon Prime Video have a perfect balance of love and laughter, making them enjoyable for subscribers. Amazon Prime Video’s original romantic comedies stand out among the rest and deliver compelling love stories with a touch of humor.

Literature Review

With plenty of rom-coms on offer, films fans may not know where to start. These are the best romantic comedies on Amazon Prime.

The best romantic comedies on Amazon Prime Video represent some of the greatest movies that mix “aww” moments with laugh-out-loud mishaps. Amazon Prime Video has emerged as one of the best streaming services when it comes to movies and television, with a wide variety of options available across all genres. For those interested in compelling love stories that can make them laugh as well, there are many great rom-com options the platform has available right now, from old favorites to new hits. Amazon Prime Video’s original content has delivered some standout romantic comedies in the past few years, though the platform also has an extensive catalog of existing movies that include some of the best rom-coms of all time on any streaming platform. The best romantic comedies on Amazon Prime Video range from classic friends-to-lovers stories through to complicated high school romances and LGBTQ+ inclusive tales of love. However, the very best Amazon Prime Video rom-coms truly stand out among the rest for their perfect balance of love and laughs.

10 Best Rom-Coms Of 2023
2023 was a renaissance for the rom-com as filmmakers began to take the genre more seriously and include modern themes in the films.

15 I Want You Back (2022) Starring Charlie Day & Jenny Slate

I Want You Back

Release Date February 11, 2022 Director Jason Orley Cast Scott Eastwood , Mason Gooding , Gina Rodriguez , Manny Jacinto , Jenny Slate , Charlie Day Runtime 111minutes

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEOIn a chance meeting, two new friends, Peter (It’s Always Sunny In Philadelphia star Charlie Day) and Anne (Jenny Slate), learn they’ve both recently been dumped. After they both share their feelings about the sudden split-ups, they decide to help each other win back their exes by breaking up their former partners’ new relationships. I Want You Back is perfect for Amazon Prime Video subscribers who like ridiculous plans that produce outrageous results. It’s also satisfying for fans of the films that don’t go quite the way it’s expected to, ending with a bit of mystery as to how the characters’ lives will turn out.

The 30 Best Movies On Amazon Prime Right Now (February 2024)
In a vast landscape of streaming, Prime Video may very well have the best selection of movies around, and there are some great movies on the service.

14 Chasing Amy (1997) Starring Ben Affleck & Joey Lauren Adams

Chasing Amy

Release Date April 4, 1997 Director Kevin Smith Cast Ben Affleck , Joey Lauren Adams , Jason Lee , Dwight Ewell , Jason Mewes Writers Kevin Smith

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEO After Kevin Smith hit it big with Clerks and then struggled somewhat with the follow-up, Mallrats, he set his eyes on a more traditional rom-com. However, Smith didn’t take an easy topic as he chose to direct a movie about a straight man who falls in love with a gay woman. Chasing Amy is slightly problematic today, as Holden (Ben Affleck) tries everything he can to win Alyssa (Joey Lauren Adams) over despite her sexuality, but at its heart, that is the purpose, as the man realizes that isn’t how the world works. Smith saw his greatest early success with Chasing Amy, and it remains one of his top critically acclaimed releases. It received a Criterion Collection release and Smith won Best Screenplay at the Independent Spirit Awards for the script, while Jason Lee won for Best Supporting Actor.

13 The Sweetest Thing (2002) Starring Cameron Diaz, Christina Applegate, & Selma Blair

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEOThe set-up of The Sweetest Thing starts with several men being interviewed about a woman named Christina (Cameron Diaz), someone that they consider to be a player and a user of men. The movie then switches over to the life of Christina, who sets out with her friend Courtney to console Jane, who has just broken up with her boyfriend. However, while they are trying to help Jane recover, it is Christina who finds a new man (Thomas Jane) and this causes problems for her and her friends. The movie received a critical bashing in reviews, but it remains an underrated movie on Amazon Prime Video, as it has a lot of great moments as the three friends set out on a road trip. What results is a sex-positive female-led movie with a great cast.

The 30 Best TV Shows On Amazon Prime Video (February 2024)
Amazon Prime Video is one of the fastest-growing streaming services in the market, offering incredible original shows such as The Boys and Fleabag.

12 Coming 2 America (2021) Starring Eddie Murphy, Jermaine Fowler, & Nomzamo Mbatha

Coming 2 America

Release Date March 5, 2021 Director Craig Brewer Cast Teyana Taylor , Louie Anderson , James Earl Jones , Nomzamo Mbatha , Leslie Jones , John Amos , Tracy Morgan , Rotimi , Kiki Layne , Shari Headley , Eddie Murphy , Bella Murphy , Wesley Snipes , Jermaine Fowler , Arsenio Hall Writers Kenya Barris , Barry W. Blaustein , Eddie Murphy , David Sheffield

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEO Coming 2 America is the sequel to 1988 comedy Coming to America. It follows the new king of Zamunda (Eddie Murphy), who sets out to find the son he never knew existed, from his first trip to America. However, the romantic comedy here is not about Murphy’s Akeem. It is about his son, Lavelle, and his relationship with Mirembe, a royal groomer who falls in love with the king’s son. The movie then has Lavelle set out to prove he is worthy of her love. While not as successful as the original, Coming 2 America has plenty of laughs, including the barbershop friends getting back together again, and it actually has some smarter comedy than the original when it comes to breaking stereotypes. It also earned an Oscar nomination for Best Makeup and Hairstyling.

11 The Bounty Hunter (2010) Starring Jennifer Aniston & Gerard Butler

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEOJennifer Aniston was coming into her own as a romantic comedy star in 2010. During this time, she appeared in The Bounty Hunter that same year with Gerard Butler, who himself had been dabbling in the genre for a few years at that time. Aniston stars as a news reporter who was out on bail after assaulting a police officer. However, when she skips her court date to chase the lead on a possible murder cover-up, her ex-husband Milo is sent out to bring her in, something he is more than happy to do. While the movie received bad reviews, fans seemed to love it, and it ended up as a box office success, making $136.3 million on a $40 million budget (via Box Office Mojo). It also picked up a nomination at the Teen Choice Awards with Butler earning a nomination for Choice Movie Actor — Romantic Comedy.

10 Made Of Honor (2008) Starring Patrick Dempsey & Michelle Monaghan

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEOMade of Honor stars Patrick Dempsey, who had exploded in popularity thanks to his role as McDreramy in Grey’s Anatomy. Looking to capitalize on his new popularity, the movie casts him as a lifelong playboy who falls in love with his best friend - but it is too late because she is about to marry someone else. She asks him to be her maid of honor, which puts him in a very difficult situation. Michelle Monaghan stars as the bride-to-be and Kevin McKidd as her fiance, a wealthy Scottish Duke. Made of Honor picked up lukewarm reviews. However, credit went to Monaghan for her performance as well as that of Sydney Pollack in his final role before his death. Dempsey also showed comic timing, allowing him to be seen in a different light from his role in Grey’s Anatomy.

9 The Wedding Planner (2001) Starring Matthew McConaughey & Jennifer Lopez

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEO Matthew McConaughey was the biggest male romantic comedy star in 2001 and Jennifer Lopez was rising fast at the time. That made their pairing in 2001’s The Wedding Planner a big deal and the movie ended up as a breezy, yet familiar rom-com trope. Mary (Lopez) is a wedding planner who is hired to be the planner for a wealthy heiress, only to find out that her fiancé is a man who saved her from an accident that she fell for. This puts her in a tough spot, as her future in her company is on the line. While the movie shared a lot in common with the superior My Best Friend’s Wedding, it remains a fun watch. The performances are top-notch, and the movie is a quick watch and easy to enjoy for anyone who loves the genre.

8 The Map Of Tiny Perfect Things (2021) Starring Kyle Allen & Kathryn Newton

The Map of Tiny Perfect Things

Release Date February 12, 2021 Director Ian Samuels Cast Kyle Allen , Jermaine Harris , Cleo Fraser , Josh Hamilton , Kathryn Newton , Anna Mikami Writers Lev Grossman

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEO​​​​​​​ The Map of Tiny Perfect Things is a time-travel romantic comedy based on the novel by Lev Grossman (The Magicians). In the movie, Mark is a teenage boy who is stuck in a time loop where he just keeps repeating the same day over and over again. He has found a routine of helping people, but the one thing he can’t do is finally connect with a girl he likes. However, everything is shaken up when a new person shows up in the time loop named Margaret, and it turns out she is stuck in the same loop. It is here that Mark learns that this might not be his story at all, and he might be caught in Margaret’s time loop, and she has a very good reason for not wanting it to end. The Prime Video original romantic comedy received positive reviews and earned a nomination for Best Movie Made for Television at the Critics’ Choice Awards.

7 About Last Night (2014) Starring Kevin Hart, Michael Ealy, Reginal Hall, & Joy Bryant

About Last Night

Release Date February 14, 2014 Director Steve Pink Cast Kevin Hart , Michael Ealy , Regina Hall , Joy Bryant , Christopher McDonald , Adam Rodriguez Writers Leslye Headland , Tim Kazurinsky , Denise DeClue , David Mamet

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEO About Last Night is a 2014 remake of a 1980s romantic comedy. While the original movie starred Demi Moore and Rob Lowe, the remake has a predominantly Black cast but keeps the story mostly the same. In the movie, two couples decide to test out their relationship in the real world and realize they have a lot to learn, not only about each other but about themselves as well. Kevin Hart and Regina Hall carry the load when it comes to comedy, but the entire cast is in top form. The movie earned positive reviews, mostly praising the cast’s performances.

6 Couples Retreat (2009) Starring Vince Vaughn & Malin Akerman

Couples Retreat

Release Date September 19, 2009 Director Peter Billingsley Cast Vince Vaughn , Jason Bateman , Faizon Love , Jon Favreau , Malin Akerman , Kristen Bell Writers Jon Favreau , Vince Vaughn , Dana Fox

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEOCouples Retreat is an ensemble romantic comedy with Vince Vaughn in the main lead role. The movie sees several couples whose relationships are in various stages of distress head to a couple’s retreat on an island, hoping to rekindle their romance. The cast had several recognizable faces, including Jason Bateman, Jon Favreau, and Kristen Bell. It was also a huge box office success, making $171 million on a $60 million budget. While familiar and formulaic, it provides a lot of laughs and remains a fun watch.

5 Drinking Buddies (2013) Starring Oliva Wilde & Jake Johnson

Drinking Buddies

Release Date August 23, 2013 Director Joe Swanberg Cast Olivia Wilde , Jake Johnson , Anna Kendrick , Ron Livingston , Ti West , Jason Sudeikis Writers Joe Swanberg

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEO The story of friends discovering deeper feelings for each other is a classic rom-com trope and one that is still popular. However, Drinking Buddies finds a unique and effective way of playing around with the idea. Olivia Wilde and Jake Johnson star as friends who enjoy drinking and hanging out together, often seeming to have more chemistry than with their respective partners. Heavily improvised, the movie is strengthened by the strong cast that makes these characters and relationships feel authentic.

4 Red, White & Royal Blue (2023) Starring Nicholas Galitzine & Taylor Zakhar Perez

Red, White & Royal Blue

Release Date August 11, 2023 Director Matthew Lopez Cast Taylor Zakhar Perez , Nicholas Galitzine , Uma Thurman , Stephen Fry , Sarah Shahi , Ellie Bamber Runtime 121 Minutes

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEO Amazon Prime Video and other streaming platforms have allowed for a bigger representation of LGBTQ+ rom-coms in recent years. One of the latest that has gotten a lot of attention is Red White and Royal Blue. The movie follows the heir to the British throne and the son of the President of the United States who attempt to squash their public feud with each other only to discover feelings for each other. The interesting twist here makes Red White and Royal Blue a more topical rom-com than expected.

3 Top Five (2014) Starring Chris Rock & Rosario Dawson

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEODespite being one of the best comedians of all time, Chris Rock never seemed to find the right movie projects for his talent. However, writing and directing Top Five was all he needed to showcase his strengths as a storyteller as well as a comedian. Rock stars as a former comedian who has gravitated towards mainstream movies while dealing with his alcoholism. Leading up to his risky movie release and upcoming wedding, he is shadowed by a reporter (Rosario Dawson), forming a bond throughout the day. The movie is one of the great rom-coms about people falling in love over a day with hilarious cameos from Adam Sandler, DMX, and Jerry Seinfeld.

2 The Big Sick (2017) Starring Kumail Nunjiani & Zoe Kazan

The Big Sick

Release Date July 14, 2017 Director Michael Showalter Cast Zoe Kazan , Ray Romano , Kumail Nanjiani , Holly Hunter , Adeel Akhtar Runtime 120minutes

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEOOne of the best rom-coms of the past decade, The Big Sick follows Pakistan-born Kumail (Kumail Nanjiani) as he falls for a grad student, Emily (Zoe Kazan), but struggles with their conflicting cultures. When Emily suddenly falls ill, Kumail has to fight against family opinions to stick by her. This comedic romance film is an enticing watch for those who appreciate a plot that questions and explores expectations and biases about different cultures and interracial relationships. The movie was partially inspired by the real-life romance between Nanjiani and his wife, Emily Gordon, with the couple earning an Oscar nomination for their script.

25 Best Prime Video Original Comedy Movies
Amazon Prime has been behind some hilarious original comedy films from long-awaited sequels to ones that dive into both sci-fi and action.

1 Notting Hill (1999) Starring Hugh Grant & Julia Roberts

Notting Hill

Release Date May 13, 1999 Cast Julia Roberts , Hugh Grant Director Roger Michell Runtime 124minutes

WATCH NOW ON PRIME VIDEO Richard Curtis and Hugh Grant teamed again for what ended up being their biggest success together. Notting Hill stars Hugh Grant as a mild-mannered bookstore owner who has a chance encounter with a movie star (Julia Roberts), forming an unexpected romance. The movie serves as a modern and reversed take on a Cinderella story. These kinds of grounded fairy tales strike a chord with people and Notting Hill is one of the best examples of that. Its humor and charm led to it becoming the highest-grossing British movie of all time, while also scoring Golden Globe nominations for its two leads as well as Best Picture.
