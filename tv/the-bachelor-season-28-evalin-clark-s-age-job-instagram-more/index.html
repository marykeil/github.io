SUMMARY

Evalin Clark, a contestant on The Bachelor Season 28, is a 29-year-old nanny from San Antonio, Texas, who also runs a custom denim business called The Jeanie’s Creations. Evalin and Joey, the Bachelor, share the same goals of wanting to get married and start a family, making them potentially compatible. Evalin is a confident, outgoing woman who is looking for a husband who is adventurous, hardworking, and open to personal growth, qualities that align with Joey’s own personality.

Literature Review

The Bachelor S28 has 32 contestants competing for Joey Graziadei’s heart. Evalin Clark is one of the women who hopes to form a connection with him.

The Bachelor season 28 leading man Joey Graziadei is searching for his wife from among a group of 32 contestants, and Evalin Clark is one of those women. Joey, a 28-year-old tennis pro from Collegeville, Pennsylvania, got his start as the runner-up on Charity Lawson’s Bachelorette season. Although Joey and Charity fell in love, she felt an even deeper connection with Dotun Olubeko. She broke up with Joey to get engaged to Dotun. However, Joey later happily accepted the role of the Bachelor in order to continue his journey to find his wife.

Throughout The Bachelor season 28, Evalin has proven that she’s very serious about winning Joey’s heart. She first caught his attention on premiere night, when she arrived on a tennis umpire’s chair. However, she didn’t have the opportunity to speak to Joey during the night, and she cried because she worried that she wouldn’t receive a rose. During episode 2, Evalin became a Bachelor Nation legend when she jumped over a table in a wedding dress during the wedding group date game of musical chairs, in order to sit next to Joey at the bride and groom’s sweetheart table. Here’s what to know about Evalin.

The Bachelor Season 28: Evalin Clark’s Age

Evalin is 29 years old. She was born on June 18, 1994. Evalin’s zodiac sign is Gemini. Joey is also a Gemini, as his birthday is May 24, 1995. Not only are Evalin and Joey close in age, but Geminis are highly compatible with other Geminis. They could be a great match for each other based on their ages and zodiac signs.

The Bachelor Season 28: Evalin Clark’s Hometown & Family

According to her ABC bio, Evalin was born and raised in San Antonio, Texas, and she still lives there. She has a big family with eight siblings, and she’s ready to start a family of her own. Evalin and Joey have the same goals of wanting to get married and begin the next chapter of their lives. Perhaps they’ll fall in love and continue their stories together.

The Bachelor Season 28: Evalin Clark’s Job

Evalin is a nanny. She also has a custom denim business called The Jeanie’s Creations by Evalin Marie. The store offers customized jean jackets, sweatshirts, baby onesies, and more. On the official website for the company, Evalin wrote that while she was growing up, she could never pinpoint exactly what she wanted to do with her life, but “things that inspired creativity” always sparked her interest. Then she realized that she wanted to learn how to do embroidery, “and the rest is history.”

Through The Jeanie’s Creations, Evalin fulfills her desire to create by making unique and personal articles of clothing for people. This has brought her happiness. Evalin strives to design and create items that fully represent the people they’re for, and “together we imagine, create and inspire with items that we can wear.” The store’s website gives instructions about how to order customized items with accents such as rhinestones, rhinestone trim, flat studs, pearls, safety pins, or pointed studs.

On The Bachelor season 28 episode 2, Evalin gave Joey a custom jean jacket that she made for him. The jacket had a scene from a tennis court in Hawaii, and the words, “Eat Sleep Tennis Repeat,” embroidered on it. Inside the jacket was a rose, and the letters “4TRR,” which stood for “for the right reasons.”

Bachelor contestants often discuss whether their fellow competitors are on the show for the right reasons, and Evalin’s gesture was meant to show Joey that she was there for him. Joey loved the gift, and told her that it was one of the most thoughtful things that anyone had ever done. In her confessional, Evalin cried and said, “It was a big deal for me. I just gave him a piece of my heart in a way.”

The Bachelor Season 28: Evalin Clark’s Hobbies & Interests

In addition to her work and embroidery, Evalin has some hobbies and interests. Evalin’s ABC bio reveals that, while she still lives in San Antonio, she’s ready to see the world. She loves traveling. However, she says that she “isn’t really a bucket list kind of person.” Evalin is a big fan of the Broadway musical, Hamilton. She also shared that she really wants to go on a lunch date with The Bachelorette season 20 star Charity.

What Evalin Clark Is Looking For On The Bachelor Season 28

According to her ABC bio, Evalin hopes that her future husband is as adventurous is she is, hardworking, and “open to growth.” Evalin “is a bold, outgoing woman who isn’t afraid to speak her mind.” She’s confident and funny, and wants to find someone who can keep up with her “big personality.” Based on what Evalin is looking for, Joey is an ideal match for her. He is especially open to learning, growing, and becoming an even better version of himself. Joey allows the women to be their true selves, and accepts them for who they are.

The Bachelor Season 28: Evalin Clark’s Instagram

Evalin can be found on Instagram at @evalin.marie. Her Instagram bio states, “Life is full of adventures, it’s just a matter of which one you choose.” As of this writing, Evalin has over 4,000 followers. Her first post, which is from 2016, consists of a picture of her standing in front of the Eiffel Tower in Paris, France. Evalin also has many photos of herself from places such as California, Mexico, Italy, and her home state of Texas. Evalin also has a link to the Instagram page of her company, The Jeanie’s Creations by Evalin Marie, which can be found at @the.jeanie_.

Evalin’s most recent posts are about her time on The Bachelor season 28. She has a few posts dedicated to the wedding date, including a photograph of her musical chairs jump. On the Instagram page for The Jeanie’s Creations by Evalin, she shared a post about gifting Joey the jacket she made for him that included the clip from The Bachelor season 28 when she gave it to him. Evalin seems genuinely excited about her journey on the show, and is sure to post even more about it as the weeks continue.

Evalin is a strong woman who isn’t afraid to show her emotions. She would be a great match for Joey for many reasons, but especially because she is truly there for him. Evalin is already a Bachelor season 28 fan favorite. If she doesn’t end up getting engaged to Joey during the season finale, it’s almost certain that she’ll turn up on another Bachelor franchise show. It’s easy to imagine her looking for love on Bachelor in Paradise or starring as the next lead of The Bachelorette. Evalin is a wonderful woman who deserves to find the love that she’s looking for.
