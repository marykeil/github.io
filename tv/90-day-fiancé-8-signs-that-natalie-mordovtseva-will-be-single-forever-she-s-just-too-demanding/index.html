90 Day Fiancé 8 Signs That Natalie Mordovtseva Will Be Single Forever (She s Just Too Demanding)

SUMMARY

Natalie married her first husband for money and left him when he filed for bankruptcy. Natalie wanted a baby from her second husband, but he wasn’t ready to have kids. Natalie used Mike to come to the U.S. and is now dating Josh for his job and financial status.

Literature Review

90 Day Fiancé star Natalie Mordovtseva is too much in love with herself for her to ever love someone else. Her three failed marriages are a big hint.

90 Day Fiancé star Natalie Mordovtseva is not destined to find true love, even if she continues to date other men after Josh Weinstein. Natalie was first seen in 90 Day Fiancé season 7 with Mike Youngquist. Natalie was a model and actress in Ukraine with a degree in journalism and psychology. Mike thought Natalie was out of his league. However, Natalie had many demands from Mike before she came to the U.S. Mike was going to be Natalie’s third husband. She expected to live in a fancy neighborhood in America where she could revive her acting career and even try her luck in Hollywood. Once Natalie realized Mike was not going to fuel her fantasies and wanted her to do a regular 9-5 job, Natalie decided to leave Mike. Next came a model called Johnny, who Natalie slapped, and then she moved on to dating Josh, a man who was emotionally unavailable but perfect due to other reasons for Natalie. The Kyiv resident currently stars in 90 Day: The Single Life, where her future with Josh is in jeopardy. Natalie is choosing to leech off Josh as she figures out her new life in Los Angeles while she’s also trying to have a baby with Mike.

8 Natalie Left Her First Husband After His Bankruptcy Natalie Proved She Married The Man For Money

Natalie was married twice before Mike. Natalie never mentioned her first husband on 90 Day Fiancé. The husband she kept referring to during her segments was her second husband. To viewers, Natalie had only been married once before Mike. However, Natalie’s first wedding took place in her early 20’s. Natalie was married to a man named Zenon Pierides. Zenon was a Cypriot businessman. Natalie had moved to Cyprus after marrying him. It appears Natalie and Zenon’s marriage lasted for only two years. Natalie divorced him quickly and went back to Ukraine. Natalie may have only married Zenon for money, as she left him when he filed for bankruptcy.

7 Natalie Wanted A Baby From Her Second Husband Natalie Couldn’t Get What She Wanted From The Man

Natalie met her second ex-husband after she returned to Ukraine. This time around, Natalie went for a businessman all over again. Natalie’s second ex-husband used to be in the army before he became a “big businessman.” Not a lot about him has been revealed except for his name, which is Mr. Podyakov. Natalie and Mr. Podyakov remained married for eight years. She met Mike when she was going through a divorce with Mr. Podyakov. Natalie revealed the reason behind her ending her marriage in season 8. Natalie said that her clock had been ticking away, and her second ex was not ready to give her kids.

6 Natalie Admitted She Didn’t Love Mike Natalie Ruthlessly Broke Mike’s Heart On TV

Natalie had kept demanding Mike to give her a baby in season 8. Mike was in debt from having bought his Christmas tree farm back in Sequim and was not ready to be financially responsible for a baby yet. It does confirm that Natalie only decided to split with Mr. Podyakov because he was not ready to have children with her. She may have immediately started dating Mike because she wanted to fulfill her baby wish. Natalie was being honest when, during an interview with the 90 Day Fiance cameras, she couldn’t bring herself to say that she loved Mike. She’d acknowledged that it would “be a lie” to say that she loved him.

5 Natalie Used Mike To Come To The U.S. Natalie Has Been Called A Scammer By Mike’s Mom Trish

Mike’s mom, Trish Youngquist, was quick to spot Natalie’s true colors and even tried to stop Mike from marrying her. Trish had warned Mike that Natalie was a “Scammer.” She deduced that since their wedding, Natalie had been picking fights with Mike on purpose to find reasons to leave him. Trish saw Natalie doing the “most evil stuff.” Natalie being mean towards Mike confirmed Trish’s doubts that Natalie’s whole plan was to get over to America. To Natalie, it didn’t matter if it was Mike or some other man, Natalie would have left him eventually once she knew she was going to secure a Green Card.

4 Natalie Begged Mike Not To Divorce Her Natalie Is Selfish & Doesn’t Care About Giving Mike Closure

Natalie and Mike got married in April 2020. Natalie walked out of Mike’s house in December 2020. Natalie was hardly married to Mike for six months. However, Natalie made sure she was going to make Mike suffer by not divorcing him. Natalie was using Mike’s cards to sustain her fancy Florida lifestyle. When she met Josh, she expected him to pay her bills. Natalie wanted the best of both worlds and now that Mike has finally decided to divorce Natalie, it remains to be seen how she reacted to it. Josh was right when he refused to give her the status of his “girlfriend.” She’s been a married woman the whole time they’ve been talking.

3 Natalie Is Dating Josh With Ulterior Intentions Natalie Chose To Date Josh Because Of His Job & Financial Status

Natalie didn’t feel excited about dating Josh the first time she met him. She was put off by the fact that he had two kids from two different relationships. 90 Day Fiance’s Natalie, however, knew that Josh was the CEO of a modeling agency and could help her secure a career in the entertainment industry. She moved from Florida to California despite Josh not having accepted her officially as his girlfriend. Natalie also expected Josh to get her a place to stay and find her a different job until she could get modeling and acting assignments through him. Natalie knows Josh is successful and a decent person and is doing her best to manipulate him.

2 Natalie Also Wants Mike To Take Her Back Natalie Will Always Want To Get Back Together With Mike

While Natalie had moved out of Mike’s house and had even started dating other people like Johnny and Josh in Florida, she still didn’t want Mike to divorce her. Once things became serious with Josh, Natalie still remained married to Mike. Natalie shamelessly asked Mike for a second chance and told him that she loved him. She wasn’t going to divorce one man to be with another. Natalie wanted to keep Mike as her backup plan. Natalie’s marriage with Mike made her feel secure. She would always have the option to manipulate him into taking her back for as long as she needed him.

1 Natalie Is Only In Love With Herself Natalie Has Zero Feelings For Josh

Natalie knew she had to keep herself relevant after her split with Mike to get more 90 Day Fiancé screen time. Natalie could have decided to date Josh because that would have helped her continue her story. There are rumors that Josh is already in a relationship with someone else and is faking his love for Natalie. It’s not like that bothers Natalie since she’s getting what she wants, and that’s more time on TV, brand partnerships on social media, and perhaps a project or two, thanks to Josh himself. Natalie calls herself a “legendary woman” and she’ll never be able to love a man as much as she loves herself. 90 Day Fiancé airs Sundays at 8 p.m. EST on TLC.
