Vanderpump Rules Season 11 Episode 3 Recap Most Shocking Moments

2020-10-16 About 1400 words 7 minutes  - views   - comments

    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Tom Sandoval’s return to Vanderpump Rules has been filled with tension and difficulty in his relationship with Ariana. Tom’s behavior and inability to take responsibility for past actions is causing unnecessary conflict within the friend group. Lisa Vanderpump tries to help Tom navigate his relationships, but he becomes combative and resistant to her advice.

Literature Review

Vanderpump Rules season 11 episode 3 brought Tom Sandoval face to face with the friend group that imploded due to his affair with Raquel Leviss.

Vanderpump Rules season 11 episode 3 saw Tom Sandoval trying to get back into the swing of things apart from Ariana Madix, though his life and the friend group he helped to destroy has been changed entirely. Throughout Vanderpump Rules season 11’s first few episodes, Tom and Ariana’s relationship has been difficult to watch. The exes, who are still living under one roof, have decided to go no-contact and are communicating exclusively through Tom’s assistant, but are still dealing with tension from their breakup. While Ariana has moved on with a new man, Tom’s still trying to get his footing.

Tom’s behavior, which makes it clear he doesn’t understand what he did wrong during the drama of Scandoval, has been erratic after his return to Vanderpump Rules. The 40-something has been hopeful that he’d be able to apologize to his former friend group and move into a new phase of their relationship, but things haven’t been easy. In finally speaking to Lala Kent, Scheana Shay, and Katie Maloney for the first time since coming back to Los Angeles, Tom may be fully realizing that his life as he knew it is over, and the relationships he had no longer exist.

Tom Schwartz Was Irritated With How Tom Sandoval Handled James Sandoval Brought Up Decade Old Drama

At Tom’s birthday party, James Kennedy joined into the mix after a last-minute invitation left him confused on Tom’s intentions. After the pair got into a bit of a fight and James left the party in a huff, Tom explained to Tom Schwartz that he was annoyed with James for things that had happened over 10 years prior between the DJ and Tom’s ex Kristen Doute. Schwartz was irritated with the fact that instead of taking responsibility for his actions and apologizing for hurting James, Tom chose to push a decade-old narrative and create even more completely unnecessary conflict.

Tom Schwartz Left Tom’s Birthday Party For Emo Night The Group Clashed With Schwartz Over His Loyalties

After James left Tom’s party, Schwartz decided to wait a bit longer and then left himself. Having been invited to Emo Night by Scheana, Schwartz felt that he wanted to be supportive of everyone in the friend group. He shared with Tom that he’d be leaving for the club Ariana, Scheana, and Katie were at, and though Tom ribbed him about it a bit, he let Schwartz go. Unfortunately, Schwartz had timed the events poorly and wound up getting to Emo Night late, missing Scheana’s performance entirely. The group at Emo Night were irritated that Schwartz had been with Tom.

Tom Sandoval Took Raquel Leviss’ Silence Hard Raquel Was In Rehab On Tom’s Birthday

After the Vanderpump Rules season 10 reunion, Tom and Raquel continued their relationship despite her decision to enter a treatment facility in Arizona. Tom tried to support Raquel in her efforts to better her mental health, but he explained that things hadn’t been easy as he’d gotten back to LA and tried to reacclimate to his life. Raquel, who had extended her stay in the treatment facility after her initial stay was meant to be over, hadn’t called Tom for his birthday and it hit the Vanderpump Rules star hard. Tom explained it hurt not to have Raquel reach out.

Lisa Tried To Help Tom Navigate His Relationships He Was Combative With His Former Boss

Although Lisa Vanderpump is rarely on Vanderpump Rules at this point, she did meet with Tom at her defunct restaurant, PUMP, as she was watching he furniture be taken out and packed away. Lisa and Tom discussed where his life has landed, but the Vanderpump Rules season 11 star quickly got combative when Lisa tried to point out that he’d made his own bed and was refusing to lie in it. Tom fought with Lisa, snapping at her about the way others were treating him and refusing to listen. Lisa was hoping to help Tom, but found it difficult.

Katie And Scheana Tried To Repair Their Friendship The Pair Has Had Ups And Downs Through The Years

Although Katie and Scheana have had a rocky relationship in the past, the duo have been trying to get their friendship back on past and create a better future. Despite Katie having been a difficult friend in the past and Scheana having pushed Schwartz at Raquel after his divorce with Katie, the former friends have decided to put their differences aside. The dinner they shared made it clear that both women are hopeful they can move past the tough times, but it may be too little, too late.

Lisa Encouraged Tom Schwartz To Try To Get The Group Back Together She Invited Them To Her Restaurant Opening In Lake Tahoe

Lisa opened up to Schwartz about the worry she’s feeling surrounding Tom, as the two put their heads together to try and figure out how to establish some peace in the friend group. She shared she knows Tom is in the wrong but has concerns about him after expressing some dark thoughts. Lisa encouraged Schwartz to try to help reconnect the group by inviting everyone to her restaurant opening in Lake Tahoe, explaining that she would love to see the whole group together. While Schwartz wasn’t sure if he’d get them to say yes, he promised Lisa he’d try.

Tom Sandoval Went To SUR For See You Next Tuesday He Didn’t Get A Warm Reception At The Restaurant

While Tom wasn’t sure if he’d be welcome at the event, Tom went to SUR for James’ standing DJ gig, See You Next Tuesday, where he knew the rest of the group would also be in attendance. He was welcomed at the restaurant by a few of the servers and Schwartz, but the reception he got from the rest of the Vanderpump Rules season 11 cast was ice cold. Sitting at a table of his own with a friend, Tom struggled to keep himself calm as he noticed the way his former friends were rallying together without him.

Schwartz Invited The Group To Lake Tahoe, Calling Out Ariana Their Reaction Wasn’t Positive

At SUR, Schwartz sat down with the majority of the group and told them all about Lisa’s invitation to her restaurant opening in Lake Tahoe. While the group said it was possible, Schwartz explained that he’d be inviting Tom and things changed entirely. Ariana shared that she didn’t want to associate herself with Tom or Schwartz, so if either of them would be there, she wouldn’t be attending. Schwartz, who was simply sharing he wanted them to be in a better place, took her criticism hard. He questioned why it was Ariana making decisions for everyone in the group.

Tom Sandoval And Scheana Squared Off About Their Friendship Both Had Criticisms For Each Other About Their Post Scandoval Behavior

At the end of Vanderpump Rules season 11 episode 3, Tom and Scheana spoke for the first time after Scandoval ended their friendship entirely. Tom, who was hurt Scheana had brought him and Raquel up on a podcast presuming it was for clout, shared that he simply wanted to be able to speak to Scheana again. Scheana was too hurt by Tom having blocked her after she offered him support when his close friend passed away, and by Tom’s actions in general. The pair didn’t wind up in a good place, and left things on Vanderpump Rules unresolved.
