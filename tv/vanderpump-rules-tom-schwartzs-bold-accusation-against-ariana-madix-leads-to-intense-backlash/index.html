Vanderpump Rules Tom Schwartzs Bold Accusation Against Ariana Madix Leads To Intense Backlash

SUMMARY

Vanderpump Rules star Tom Schwartz faces backlash from fans for his mistreatment of Ariana Madix. Ariana cuts Schwartz out of her life entirely after he shows a lack of empathy and understanding. The rest of the cast, including Lala Kent and Katie Maloney, defends Ariana and criticizes Schwartz for his actions.

Literature Review

Vanderpump Rules’ Tom Schwartz spoke out about Ariana Madix’s attitude post-Scandoval, leading to some intense backlash from fans of the hit series.

Vanderpump Rules star Tom Schwartz made a mistake with Ariana Madix in a preview for an upcoming episode of the series, causing backlash amongst fans of the show. While Vanderpump Rules season 11 is just getting started, the series has been one of the most highly-anticipated of 2024 as fans have been eagerly waiting to see what happened in the direct aftermath of Scandoval. Ariana, who was cheated on by her longtime partner Tom Sandoval with the couple’s close friend Raquel Leviss, has been open about her disdain for Schwartz after the drama. Insistent that Schwartz was aware of Tom’s affair, she’s cut him out of her life entirely.

In a preview clip for the next episode of Vanderpump Rules, Tom is seen inviting the group up to Lake Tahoe to spend some time with Lisa Vanderpump, but Ariana isn’t happy about the idea. She explained that she didn’t want to see Schwartz or Tom, who was also invited, and Tom shared that he was confused as to why she felt like she was “the queen of the group” who could make decisions for everyone.

In the comments of Queens Of Bravo’s Instagram post featuring the clip, Ariana explained, “same guy who professes to miss my friendship. Ya right lmao gotta try and knock me down as soon as i remind him i dont **** with him just to give me another reminder i made the right choice for myself.” While Ariana is hurt by Schwartz’s lack of empathy and understanding, she didn’t realize her comment would lead to her co-star getting dragged on social media.

Vanderpump Rules Cast Attacks Tom Schwartz Over Tom Sandoval Invite

Though Schwartz is being attacked by fans, he’s was also getting some vitriol from the rest of the Vanderpump Rules cast in the preview clip. Schwartz was hopeful that the group would be able to come together and take a small trip to try and heal some of the issues they’ve had in the last year, but when Ariana wasn’t interested, Lala Kent and Schwartz’s ex-wife Katie Maloney came to her defense. “After everything that happened, how do you stay by his side?…on his end, whether you see this or not - and there’s going to be a day when you see it…he will devastate you," Lala told Schwartz.

Lala, Katie, Scheana Shay, and Ally Lewber all felt that inviting both Tom and Ariana to the same vacation spot was a mistake for Schwartz, and didn’t grasp why he couldn’t understand how it would be hurtful to Ariana. While Schwartz seems to understand things are delicate between his best friend and former friend, he doesn’t seem to be clear that Ariana has cut him out of the picture as well. While Lala was firm with Schwartz about the way she believes Tom has treated and will treat him in the future, it’s not clear if she reached the Vanderpump Rules season 11 pariah.

With Tom and Schwartz on one side while the rest of group is on the other, things have been difficult to navigate for the Vanderpump Rules season 11 cast. Though Ariana is adamant that she’s uninterested in spending any time with her ex or Schwartz, the pair are still part of the series and, in some ways, part of the friend group that makes up the cast. Though things will surely change throughout Vanderpump Rules season 11, it won’t be easy for Schwartz to mend fences with people he once held close.
