Single s Inferno Season 3 Contestants Who Left The Show Alone

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1100 words 5 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Single’s Inferno season 3 ended with four couples walking away hand-in-hand, but four contestants went home alone. Yun Ha-bin, once the most popular male contestant, left the Inferno without forming a strong bond with anyone. Yun Ha-jeong, caught in a love triangle, ended up alone when Gwan-hee chose another contestant.

Literature Review

Single’s Inferno included 12 singles. Though season 3 ended with four couples leaving hand-in-hand, some of the singles left the Inferno all alone.

The Netflix dating show Single’s Inferno season 3 ended with four couples walking away hand-in-hand, but there were several islanders who went home alone. Each season starts with 12 hopeful singles looking for love while living on a deserted island amid rough living conditions. They sleep in converted shipping container tiny homes and cook all their own food on an open fire outdoors while competing for a chance to spend time in Paradise, a luxury hotel. During the finale, singles pair up with the contestant of their choice to escape the inferno hand-in-hand.

The Single’s Inferno cast kept audiences guessing about who they’d end up with up until the very end. There were multiple love triangles and more than one contestant who wasn’t sure until the last moment who they would choose. During the season finale, four new couples were formed. Gwan-hee and Hye-seon, Min-kyu and Gyu-ri, Jin-seok and Min-young, and Min-woo and Si-eun. However, four of season 3’s singles weren’t able to couple up with anyone and had to leave the Inferno as single as the day they arrived.

Yun Ha-bin

At one point, 31-year-old professional actor Yun Ha-bin was the most popular male on the show. During a Single’s Inferno segment that involves contestants secretly giving candy to the men or women they were interested in, Ha-bin was the only contestant to receive a piece of candy from every single female. But his popularity waned quickly, and he was never able to form a strong bond with anyone. He was interested in 26-year-old office worker Yun Ha-jeong, but she had her heart set on cast mate Lee Gwan-hee, so Ha-bin left the Inferno alone.

Ha-bin continues to pursue his acting career and currently plays a minor role in the popular Netflix K-drama My Demon. His Instagram account features photos of Ha-bin traveling, hanging out with his friends and family, and even spending some quality time with his Single’s Inferno costars. Ha-bin and other cast members recently attended fellow cast mate Gwan-hee’s professional basketball game. Though he didn’t find love on the show, Ha-bin clearly built lifelong relationships with his Single’s Inferno season 3 cast mates. There is nothing to suggest that Ha-bin is dating anyone.

Yun Ha-Jeong

26-year-old Yun Ha-jeong works in the office of a medical clothing company, where she handles customer service and purchasing. She has done some modeling work, and she was runner-up for Miss Chunhyang 2021. She says that people describe her as a “fox” based on her looks. She also says she’s super popular and is extremely confident in her looks and personality. Ha-jeong was caught in one of season 3’s hottest love triangles. In the finale, she was one of the three women who chose Gwan-hee, but he chose Choi Hye-seon, so Ha-jeong ended up all alone.

Ha-jeong continues to work in the offices of a medical supplies company that makes scrubs. According to her Instagram posts, Ha-jeong also continues to enjoy modeling on the side. Unlike many of her Single’s Inferno cast mates, Ha-jeong never posts photos of herself hanging out with any of them, though she can often be seen in the photos that they post. She also doesn’t post anything that would indicate she’s dating anyone.

Son Won-Ik

In a surprising twist, the second episode revealed that there was a second Single’s Inferno on a different island with a whole other set of contestants. The first contestant introduced during the second episode was Son Won-ik, a real estate agent who sells luxury real estate and dabbles in modeling. He successfully matched up with Yu Si-eun during the fourth episode, and they spent the night together in paradise. But Si-eun only had eyes for Choi Min-woo, whom she chose during the Single’s Inferno season 3’s finale, sending Won-ik home alone.

According to his Instagram posts, Won-ik continues to work in the real estate sector while also doing a little bit of modeling. He continues to spend time with his Single’s Inferno cast mates. He recently attended a professional basketball game with several of them to watch Gwan-hee play. He hasn’t posted anything that would indicate he’s in a relationship, so he most likely continues to be as single.

Cho Min-Ji

26-year-old Cho Min-ji joined the series halfway through the season and caused quite a stir upon her arrival. The aspiring news anchor currently majors in Economics at Ewha Women’s University. Like several of the other female members of the cast, Min-ji was a former Miss Korea contestant. Upon arriving, she was allowed to choose two men to take to Paradise. After conversations with all the men, she chose Min-woo and Lee Jin-seok, but hit it off much better with Jin-seok, who was interested in An Min-Young. In the end, Min-ji chose Gwan-hee, but he chose Hye-seon, so Min-ji was sent home all alone.

Since leaving Single’s Inferno, Min-ji has continued to pursue her dream of becoming a news anchor. Min-ji’s Instagram account features pictures of her modeling and anchoring the news. She is involved in animal charities and posts to encourage pet adoption. She never posts photos of herself spending time with her fellow Single’s Inferno cast members. She also has never posted anything that would suggest that she’s in a relationship and appears to continue to be single.

Singles Inferno seasons 1-3 are currently streaming on Netflix.
