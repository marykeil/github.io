SUMMARY

“Mr. & Mrs. Smith” is a star-studded Amazon series inspired by the 2005 movie, featuring a fresh reinvention of the espionage narrative from the minds of Francesca Sloane and Donald Glover. The series follows the premise of the film, where two undercover spies must get legally married. However, Glover’s unique creative style promises a distinct and exciting adaptation. The ensemble cast includes Maya Erskine, Donald Glover, John Turturro, Paul Dano, Eiza Gonzalez, Parker Posey, and more. All 8 episodes will premiere on February 2, 2024, exclusively on Amazon Prime Video.

Literature Review

The star-studded cast of the new Amazon series Mr. & Mrs. Smith is jam-packed with A-list actors, adding to the hype of Donald Glover’s latest show.

The highly-anticipated Amazon series Mr. & Mrs. Smith features a star-studded ensemble cast. Taking inspiration from the notable 2005 movie of the same name starring Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt, Mr. & Mrs. Smith offers a fresh new reinvention of the espionage narrative from the creative minds of Francesca Sloane (Atlanta, Fargo) and Donald Glover (Atlanta, Swam). The new series is inspired by the Jane and John Smith characters from the 2005 film written by Simon Kinberg (Sherlock Holmes, X-Men: Days of Future Past).

All 8 episodes of Mr. & Mrs. Smith will premiere on Friday, February 2, 2024, exclusively on Amazon Prime Video. The series follows a similar premise to the 2005 film in which two strangers work for a covert spy agency but are required to get legally married to maintain their anonymity. The Mr. & Mrs. Smith adaptation is expected to be quite different from the 2005 film directed by Doug Liman (Edge of Tomorrow, American Made) due to Glover’s highly unique creative style and distinct aesthetic. 4 of the 8 episodes of Mr. & Mrs. Smith are directed by Atlanta alumni, with Emmy-winner Hiro Murai on board for two episodes, as well as Glover and Atlanta cinematographer Christian Sprenger both on for one apiece.

Maya Erskine

Actor: Maya Erskine, 36, will star in the lead role of Jane Smith in Mr. & Mrs. Smith. Erskine attended NYU and is best known for her role as Maya Ishii-Peters in the Hulu original comedy series PEN15, which is based on a younger version of herself in real life. Erskine also created PEN15 alongside co-actor and co-creator Anna Konkle and co-creator Sam Zvibleman. Erskine voiced the main character Mizu in the Netflix animated series Blue Eyed Samurai (2023) and appeared in three episodes of Obi-Wan Kenobi.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: Insecure (2016-2017), Wine Country (2019), BoJack Horseman (2020), Big Mouth (2020), PEN15 (2019-2021), Obi-Wan Kenobi (2022), Blue Eye Samurai (2023).

Character: Erskine will be portraying Jane Smith, wife of Donald Glover’s John Smith.

Donald Glover

Actor: Donald Glover, 40, will appear in the lead role of John Smith in the new Amazon series. Glover is a highly successful actor, writer, producer, comedian, and musical artist under the name of Childish Gambino. After attending NYU, Glover rose to prominence from his role on the NBC comedy series Community as Troy Barnes. He is also well known for creating and starring in his Emmy Award-winning series Atlanta as well as portraying Lando Carlssian in Solo: A Star Wars Story. Glover is set to reprise his role as Troy Barnes in the upcoming Community movie.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: Community (2009-2014), The Martian (2015), Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017), Solo: A Star Wars Story (2018), The Lion King (2019), Atlanta (2016-2022).

Character: Glover will appear in the lead role of John Smith opposite Erskine’s Jane Smith.

John Turturro

Actor: John Turturro, 66, is a highly acclaimed actor who has been featured in some of the greatest movies ever made. A longtime collaborator with The Coen Brothers, Turturro has appeared in some of their most iconic films such as The Big Lebowski and Barton Fink. Turturro recently starred in The Batman alongside Robert Pattinson as well as the acclaimed Apple series Severence alongside Adam Scott.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: Do the Right Thing (1989), Miller’s Crossing (1990), The Big Lebowski (1998), He Got Game (1998), Rounders (1998), Transformers (2007), The Night Of (2016), The Batman (2022), Severance (2022).

Character: Turturro will play Eric Shane in Mr. & Mrs. Smith.

Paul Dano

Actor: Paul Dano, 39, is a brilliant Emmy-nominated actor who has appeared in Academy Award-winning films such as There Will Be Blood, 12 Years a Slave, and Little Miss Sunshine. Dano notably starred in the recent biopic comedy movie Dumb Money based on the real-life Gamestop stock short squeeze. Dano also starred in the Best Picture nominee The Fablemans directed by Steven Spielberg and played The Riddler in 2022’s The Batman.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: The Sopranos (2002-2004), Little Miss Sunshine (2006), There Will Be Blood (2007), Prisoners (2013), 12 Years a Slave (2013), The Batman (2022), The Fabelmans (2022), Spaceman (2024).

Character: Dano will play a character named Hot Neighbor in Mr. & Mrs. Smith.

Eiza Gonzalez

Actor: Eiza González, 34, is a prominent Mexican actress known for her major roles in several action franchises, including Fast & Furious and Godzilla vs. Kong. González rose to prominence after starring in the 2017 box office hit Baby Driver as the character Darling. She stars as Madame M in the Fast & Furious spinoff movie Hobbs & Shaw and is set to appear in the new Netflix series 3 Body Problem from Game of Thrones creators D.B. Weiss and David Benioff.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: Baby Driver (2017), I Care a Lot (2020), Godzilla vs. Kong (2021), 3 Body Problem (2024), The Ministry of Ungentlemanly Warfare (2024).

Character: González’s character is not listed in the credits of Mr. & Mrs. Smith at the time of writing.

Alexander Skarsgård

Actor: Alexander Skarsgård, 47, is a Swedish actor and son of renowned actor Stellan Skarsgård. He is best known for his performances in the 2008 HBO miniseries Generation Kill as well as his recurring role in True Blood. Alexander Skarsgård recently appeared in the final two seasons of HBO’s celebrated drama series Succession and also starred in the HBO series Big Little Lies.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: Generation Kill (2008), True Blood (2008-2014), The Legend of Tarzan (2016), Big Little Lies (2017-2019), The Northman (2022), Succession (2021-2023).

Character: Skarsgård’s character is not listed in the credits of Mr. & Mrs. Smith at the time of writing.

Sarah Paulson

Actor: Sarah Paulson, 49, has appeared in dozens of notable films and television series including the recent Emmy Award-winning show The Bear. Paulson is a household name in the American Horror Story universe, having appeared in 95 of its 129 episodes across its various anthological seasons. She is an 8-time Emmy nominee and winner for her role as Marcia Clark in American Crime Story.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: Deadwood (2005), Mud (2012), 12 Years a Slave (2013), Carol (2015), The Post (2017), American Horror Story (2011-2021), American Crime Story (2016-2021), The Bear (2023).

Character: Paulson’s character is not listed in the credits of Mr. & Mrs. Smith at the time of writing.

Wagner Moura

Actor: Wagner Moura, 47, is a Brazilian actor best known for his leading role as Pablo Escobar in the hit Netflix series Narcos. Moura rose to prominence in Hollywood after starring in the 2013 sci-fi blockbuster Elysium alongside Matt Damon. He is set to star in the highly-anticipated A24 movie Civil War from filmmaker Alex Garland, which will be released in April 2024.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: Elysium (2013), Narcos (2015-2016), Narcos: Mexico (2018-2021), The Gray Man (2022), Puss in Boots: The Last Wish (2022), Civil War (2024).

Character: Moura’s character is not listed in the credits of Mr. & Mrs. Smith at the time of writing.

Parker Posey

Actor: Parker Posey, 55, is an experienced actress who became a household name during the 1990s from her work in several notable independent productions. Posey recently starred in Ari Aster’s 2023 film Beau Is Afraid as Elaine Bay and has made several appearances in prominent television series such as The Good Wife, Netflix’s Lost in Space, and HBO’s The Staircase. She will appear as a member of the highly-anticipated The White Lotus season 3 cast.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: Dazed and Confused (1993), subUrbia (1996), You’ve Got Mail (1998), Scream 3 (2000), Blade: Trinity (2004), Superman Returns (2006), Irrational Man (2015), Café Society (2016), Beau Is Afraid (2023).

Character: Posey’s character is not listed in the credits of Mr. & Mrs. Smith at the time of writing.

Ron Perlman

Actor: Ron Perlman, 73, is an incredibly experienced actor with more than 270 acting credits to his name. Perlman is arguably most recognized for his leading role in the Hellboy movies. Perlman began his acting career back in 1979 on the television series Ryan’s Hope. He rose to prominence from his leading role in a television adaptation of Beauty and the Beast, which ran from 1987 to 1990.

Notable Movies & TV Shows: The City of Lost Children (1995), Alien Resurrection (1997), Hellboy (2004), Hellboy II: The Golden Army (2008), Sons of Anarchy (2008-2013), Drive (2011), Don’t Look Up (2021).

Character: Perlman will play Toby Hellinger in Mr. & Mrs. Smith.

Sharon Hogan - Hogan is known for Pulling (2006-2009), Catastrophe (2015-2019), Game Night (2018), Bob’s Burgers (2019-2023), and Bad Sisters (2022). Her character is not listed in the credits of Mr. & Mrs. Smith at the time of writing.

Dave Attell - Attell is an actor and stand-up comedian known for Funny People (2009), Louie (2014), Trainwreck (2015), Crashing (2017-2019), and Bupkis (2023). His character is not listed in the credits of Mr. & Mrs. Smith at the time of writing.

Michaela Coel - Coel is known for Chewing Gum (2015-2017), Black Mirror (2016-2017), I May Destroy You Now (2020), and Black Panther: Wakanda Forever (2022). She will play a character named Bev in Mr. & Mrs. Smith.

Billy Campbell - Campbell is a Canadian actor known for The Rocketeer (1991), Enough (2002), Cardinal (2017-2020), and Something Undone (2021-2023). His character is not listed in the credits of Mr. & Mrs. Smith at the time of writing.

Úrsula Corberó - Corberó is a Spanish actress known for Money Heist (2017-2021), The Tree of Blood (2018), and Lift (2024). She will play a character named Rooney in Mr. & Mrs. Smith.

Tamara Torres - Torres is known for Orange is the New Black (2013-2019), Blue Valentine (2010), and Like Sunday Like Rain (2014). She will play a character named Marla in Mr. & Mrs. Smith.

Beverly Glover - Beverly Glover is Donald Glover’s mother, who will be making her acting debut in Mr. & Mrs. Smith.
