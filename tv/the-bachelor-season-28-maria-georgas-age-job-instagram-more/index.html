SUMMARY

Maria is a 29-year-old contestant on The Bachelor season 28 who wants to win Joey’s heart. Maria is from Kleinburg, Ontario, Canada and works as an executive assistant. She also has a background in acting, with a credit in the 2005 film The Pacifier. Maria has a black belt in Taekwondo karate, enjoys photography, and is “obsessed” with horror movies. She is looking for a long-term relationship.

Literature Review

The Bachelor season 28’s Maria Georgas is one of the 32 women competing for leading man Joey Graziadei’s heart. Here’s what to know about her.

The Bachelor season 28 features 32 women vying for Joey Graziadei’s heart, and one of them is Maria Georgas. Joey is a 28-year-old tennis pro originally from Collegeville, Pennsylvania, who’s currently living in Koloa, Hawaii. He got his start as Charity Lawson’s runner-up in The Bachelorette season 20. Joey and Charity had a strong connection, building their relationship from premiere night to the Fantasy Suites. He was shocked when she broke up with him to get engaged to Dotun Olubeko. However, Joey is now ready for a new start as the leading man of The Bachelor season 28.

The Bachelor season 28 features 32 women who will compete to get engaged to Joey. They include women from all over the United States and Canada. Joey already met one of the women, Lea Cayanan, during Charity’s “After the Final Rose” episode. She was given a mystery envelope, which she was instructed not to open until premiere night. In addition, sisters Allison and Lauren Hollinger will both appear on the show. As Joey’s journey begins, here’s what to know about Maria, one of the women who hopes to win his heart.

Maria Georgas’ Age

Maria is 29 years old. She was born on August 27, 1994. Maria’s zodiac sign is Virgo. Virgo women are known for being gentle and caring, as well as perfectionists. Joey was born on May 24, 1995, which makes him a Gemini. Although they’re different, if Virgos and Geminis work on their relationship, they can be a great match. In addition, Maria and Joey’s ages line up perfectly. Maria is one of the oldest contestants on The Bachelor season 28. Only five other contestants–Evalin Clark (29), Lexi Young (30), Kelsey Toussant (31), Madina Alam (31), and Samantha “Sam” Hale (31)–are age 29 or older.

Maria Georgas’ Hometown & Job

Maria is from Kleinburg, Ontaro, Canada. She works as an executive assistant. This means that Maria has to be very organized in her job and have good communication skills. Maria also has an IMDb page, which describes her as an actress. Her only acting credit listed is from the 2005 Vin Diesel film The Pacifier. Maria would’ve been about 11 years old when the movie came out, so she was a child actress. She even has a still shot from the movie on her Instagram page, in which she’s dressed as a Girl Scout in a scene with Vin and some other children.

Maria Georgas’ Hobbies & Interests

Maria is a well-rounded person, with many different hobbies and interests. According to her ABC bio, Maria has a black belt in Taekwondo karate. She also enjoys a big plate of spaghetti, and dreams of eating sushi in Japan. Another one of her interests is doing photo shoots with disposable cameras. In fact, one of Maria’s pinned Instagram posts is of her taking a picture of herself in a mirror with a disposable camera. In addition, Maria is “obsessed” with horror movies. It’ll be fun to see how Joey and Maria’s interests line up, and if they bond over any of them.

What Maria Is Looking For

Maria is a confident woman who is certain about what she wants her future to look like. Maria’s ABC bio describes her as “a bold, outgoing woman who isn’t afraid to speak her mind and go for what her heart wants.” She no longer wants short-term flings and “situationships,” but instead wants to settle down. Maria recently had a psychic reading that made her think that Joey could be her future husband. She’s looking for a man who’ll dive into her passions with her. Joey is also ready for marriage, so Maria’s psychic could be right, and they might end up leaving the show engaged.

Why Maria Considers Herself A Catch

In a handwritten note submitted to Bachelor Nation in which she completed the sentence, “I’m a catch because…,” Maria answered that she’s a loyal woman. She also again stated that she speaks her mind and isn’t afraid to go after what she wants. Maria wrote that she has a big heart, and when she loves someone, they’ll get all of her. She wants to take on life with that person, and be their biggest supporter.

Maria concluded with, “As long as you’re good to me … Believe I’ll be good to you.” All of these qualities make Maria an ideal Bachelor contestant because she’ll have to be assertive among the other women, but also supportive of Joey. If Joey can allow himself to believe that his relationship with Maria is real, they should have a great foundation for a relationship, based on what she said.

Maria Georgas’ Instagram

Maria can be found on Instagram at @maria.georgas. Her first post is from 2014. Maria’s Instagram page has many posts that feature her friends and her beloved dad. Maria posts many selfies of herself in various poses. She also posted some throwback pictures of herself from her childhood. Maria also has a post announcing that she’s a Bachelor season 28 contestant. It includes her promotional photograph and a video of her dancing with a tennis racket in honor of Joey. The caption reads, “It’s allll happening January 22nd on #the bachelor.” Maria also posted a collection of Instagram stories about The Bachelor season 28.

Maria’s Instagram account currently has 20.6K followers, which are certain to grow throughout The Bachelor season 28. They include Bachelor Nation members Ethan Kang (The Bachelorette season 19), Logan Palmer (The Bachelorette season 19, Bachelor in Paradise season 8), Kat Izzo (The Bachelor season 27, Bachelor in Paradise season 9), Greer Blitzer (The Bachelor season 27, Bachelor in Paradise season 9), and Christina Mandrell (The Bachelor season 27). It’ll be fun to see if Maria joins the ranks of these beloved Bachelor Nation members after her time on Joey’s Bachelor season.

Maria seems to be one of the most intriguing Bachelor season 28 contestants. With varied interests, she should be perfect for the exciting world of traveling and activities that makes up the show. Maria knows what she wants, and isn’t afraid to go after it, so she’ll probably try to catch Joey’s attention right away. In addition, Maria’s life goals seem to align with Joey’s. They both are ready for engagement and marriage, which is very important for a show such as The Bachelor. Hopefully, Maria and Joey found the love they’re searching for with each other and left the show engaged.
