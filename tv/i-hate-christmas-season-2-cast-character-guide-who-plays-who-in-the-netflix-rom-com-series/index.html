SUMMARY

Season 2 of I Hate Christmas premiered on December 7, a year after the first season, and brings more romance troubles for Gianna and her loved ones. Gianna, played by Pilar Fogliati, questions if she even wants a boyfriend after finding one in season 1, while her friends and family also experience romantic obstacles. The second season features a mostly returning cast, showcasing the fine acting and excellent chemistry that fans have come to expect from the Italian rom-com series.

Literature Review

I Hate Christmas season 2 is the second installment of the Italian-language holiday rom-com, and here’s who appears in this season’s cast.

I Hate Christmas season 2 sees the return of the lovable and funny cast of characters to the Italian rom-com series. Premiering on December 7, 2022, just in time for the holidays, I Hate Christmas stars Pilar Fogliati as Gianna, a nurse in Chioggia, Venice. Hapless in her love life, Gianna is sick of everyone giving her a hard time about not having a boyfriend. Season 1 sees her hatch a plan to get a boyfriend before Christmas. As is expected, Gianna’s search goes awry and season 2 once again finds her looking for love in Italy.

Season 2 premiered a year later, again on December 7. While it appears that all of Gianna’s problems have been fixed, things end up being worse than ever. Despite her success in finding a boyfriend in season 1, season 2 has her questioning if she even wants one. Gianna’s friends and family also all feel the sting that love can provide this season and, for once, she’s not the only one with romance troubles. Featuring a cast of mostly returning characters, I Hate Christmas season 2 sees more of the fine acting and excellent chemistry fans have come to expect.

Pilar Fogliati As Gianna

Pilar Fogliati returns to I Hate Christmas season 2 as Gianna, who is still struggling to determine what it is she wants out of a romantic relationship. One of those people who hate Christmas movies, Gianna dislikes the holidays and the guilt it brings. At least in season 2, she isn’t alone. Fogliati is an Italian actor who began her career in 2015 with a starring role as Elisabetta “Betta” Miraglia in Il Bosco.

She then went on to more main roles in TV series, most notably as Emma Giorgi in Un passo dal cielo about a forestry corps in Italy. In film, Fogliati appeared in Forever Young as Marika and Corro da te as Alessia. In 2023, she made her directorial debut with the anthology comedy Romantic Girls, in which she also stars.

Beatrice Arnera As Titti

Titti is one of Gianna’s best friends and, in season 1, was even more over the idea of love and romance than Gianna. However, season 2 actually sees Titti finding someone, though that person causes a rift between her and Gianna. Titti is played by Beatrice Arnera, an actor who has been appearing in Italian TV shows and movies since 2015 with her debut performance in Il paradiso delle signore as Claudia De Angelis.

She also costars in Romolo + Giuly: la guerra mondiale italiana as Giuly Copulati and Buongiorno, mamma! as Agata Scalzi. Arnera’s best-known film role comes in My Big Gay Italian Wedding, where she plays Camilla.

Fiorenza Pieri As Margherita

Margherita, played by Fiorenza Pieri, is Gianna’s older sister who has Gianna’s back when it comes to her parents asking about relationships. In season 2, Margherita is experiencing her own love troubles with her husband. Pieri has been acting since 2008, with some of her most notable film roles being Adelaide Santienna in Terror Take Away and Francesca in Beyond Love. Her performances in TV shows include the recurring roles of Suor Teresa in Che Dio ci aiuti, Marinella Fabbri in Blanca, and Caterina Nardini as Non mentire.

Massimo Rigo As Pietro

Gianna’s father Pietro is played by Massimo Rigo in I Hate Christmas. Pietro and his wife Marta were having marriage troubles in season 1, and in season 2 they announce they are getting separated, throwing Christmas plans into disarray. Rigo has appeared in a number of Italian movies and television shows.

He appears in the main cast of Fiori sopra l’inferno: I casi di Teresa Battaglia as Mayor Hans Knauss, is a recurring character on DOC - Nelle tue mani as Umberto Caruso, and appears in Non mi lasciare as Vincenzo Molli. His notable movie performances include Poliziotto in The Girl in the Fog, Vittorini in The Right Thing, and Zio Claudio in The Nest.

Sabrina Paravicini As Marta

Marta is Gianna’s mother and the source of many of her frustrations during the holidays in I Hate Christmas. In season 2, her marriage with Pietro begins to fall apart, causing even more stress for Gianna. Marta is played by Sabrina Paravicini, an Italian actor who had her start in 1991 on the TV series Cristina, l’Europa siamo noi as Susy in a supporting role.

Paravicini later went on to co-star in the first four seasons of the long-running series Un medico in famiglia as Jessica. The actor also has a main character role in the miniseries Follow Your Dreams as Antonella. In film, she plays Regina in Alice and the Land That Wonders, Cristina in Succede, and Sandra in Comunque mia.

I Hate Christmas’ Supporting Cast & Characters

Pierpaolo Spollon as Filippo - Filippo is Gianna’s stand-off neighbor and a new character in I Hate Christmas season 2. It turns out that Filippo is just a single dad who is too busy to spend too much time with others and when he does finally speak to Gianna, the two strike up a relationship. Pierpaolo Spollon stars in DOC - Nelle tue mani as Riccardo Bonvegna, Che Dio ci aiuti as Emiliano Stiffi, and Marco Allevi in L’allieva. His movie roles include Paolo in Che vuoi che sia and Martino Cecchin in A Holy Venetian Family.

Glen Blackhall as Umberto - Umberto is Gianna’s on-again, off-again boyfriend who is not as honest as he seems. Glen Blackhall, who plays Umberto, is best known for his television roles in Titanic the miniseries as Paolo Sandrini, Tutto può succedere as Marco Nardini, and Questo nostro amore as Luca. Blackhall’s movie performances include Raphael in Sin, Figlio Elia in Let Yourself Go, and Andrea in A Liberal Passion.

Simonetta Solder as The Caposala - Known only as “the Caposala” (“manager” in English), Gianna’s boss is a constant presence throughout I Hate Christmas, sometimes supportive, and sometimes not so much. Simonetta Solder, who plays the Caposala, plays Sophia in Love Wedding Repeat, Nina in One Kiss, and Maestra in Drifters.

Solder’s notable TV roles include Nora in The Red Band Society, Giulia in Come un delfino - La serie, and Mamma di Riccardo in DOC - Nelle tue mani. English audiences may best recognize Solder for her appearance as the wedding officiant for Lady Caroline Collingwood (Harriet Walter) and Peter Munion (Pip Torrens) in the Succession season 3 finale, “All the Bells Say”.

Federico Calistri as Giulio - Federico Calistri plays Giulo in I Hate Christmas. Giulio is Gianna’s brother who suddenly has his world thrown upside down when his wife is pregnant again, this time with twins. Calistri plays Ragazzo Punk in the miniseries Mi hanno sputato nel milkshake and Delmo in Inspector Coliandro. His film credits include Sante in Nina dei lupi, Ferruccio in The Beautiful Summer, and Deo in Because we dreamt of flying.

Giulia Gonella as Silvia - Silvia, played by Giulia Gonella in I Hate Christmas, is Giulio’s wife and the expectant mother of twins. Gonella has only had two other acting credits beyond this one. She plays Pamela Montanari in one episode of Don Matteo and appears in the short film Uroboro as Matilde.
