SUMMARY

“Obliterated” premiered with a star-studded cast and has quickly gained a significant viewership. The action drama-comedy revolves around an elite team from various branches of the U.S. Armed Forces working together to prevent a terrorist attack in Las Vegas. The cast includes familiar faces like Nick Zano and Shelley Hennig, who bring their own dynamic and chemistry to their respective roles as team leaders.

Literature Review

The Netflix television series Obliterated features a star-studded cast with familiar names, including Alyson Gorske. Who plays which character?

The first season of Obliterated premiered on November 30, 2023, and gained lots of attention thanks to its star-studded cast. The Netflix action drama-comedy series revolves around an elite team of members of the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA), the Navy SEALs, the Army, the Air Force, the Marine Corps, and the National Security Agency (NSA). The highly skilled individuals join forces to prevent a terrorist attack in Las Vegas. When they believe they have neutralized the threat, the characters spend a night out on the town. Unfortunately, the team learns the bomb is fake and has to fight through their intoxication to find the real thing.

The Obliterated cast includes Nick Zano as Chad McKnight, Shelley Hennig as Ava Winters, Terrence Terrell as Trunk, Alyson Gorske as Lana, C. Thomas Howell as Haggerty, Eugene Kim as Paul Yung, Paola Lázaro as Angela Gomez, Kimi Rutledge as Maya Lerner, and a few other supporting actors. Most of the characters make up the aforementioned elite team of officials from different branches of the United States Armed Forces. Together, they are a ragtag group of messy individuals trying their best to stop a terrorist network in Sin City.

Nick Zano As Chad McKnight

Nick Zano stars as one of the group’s leaders in Obliterated, Chad McKnight. Chad is a Navy SEAL who often butts heads with the other team head, Ava Winters, and is the object of multiple women’s affections in the Netflix series. Viewers will likely recognize Zano from his role as Nate Heywood/Steel in the CW Arrowverse show, DC’s Legends of Tomorrow.

The actor also had notable parts in the TV shows What I Like About You as Vince, Melrose Place as Drew Pragin, Happy Endings as Pete, 90210 as Preston Hillingsbrook, and 2 Broke Girls as Johnny. Zano’s most notable film role comes in The Final Destination as Hunt Wynorski.

Shelley Hennig As Ava Winters

Shelley Hennig plays Ava Winters, a CIA agent, in Obliterated. Ava is one of the people in charge of the elite team, and she is a bit uncompromising and headstrong as a leader. She and Chad have an interesting relationship (and wild chemistry). Before starring in Obliterated, Hennig portrayed Malia Tate in the MTV supernatural series Teen Wolf.

The actress also appeared in shows such as the long-running TV series Days of Our Lives as Stephanie Johnson, The Secret Circle as Diana Meade, and Dollface as Ramona. Her notable movie performances include Debbie Galardi in Ouija, Blaire Lily in Unfriended, and Carrie Grey in When We First Met.

Terrence Terrell As Trunk

Trunk, played by Terrence Terrell, is another Navy SEAL on the team in Obliterated. Aside from the Netflix series, Terrell has appeared in numerous other noteworthy television shows. The actor had smaller one-episode roles in series like Criminal Minds, Scorpion, Unsolved: The Murders of Tupac and the Notorious B.I.G., Shameless, Black-ish, and Batwoman. Terrell was also a series regular (as Eli Russell) in the CBS sitcom B Positive. Though he does not have a long list of film credits, he did play an orc in the unfairly maligned fantasy crime film Bright​​​​.

Alyson Gorske As Lana

Alyson Gorske’s Lana is the only main character in the Netflix action series who is not a trained employee of the United States Armed Forces. Instead, Lana is a civilian who gets mixed up in the case the team is working on in Las Vegas. Obliterated viewers might recognize Gorske from her roles in Devil’s Triangle as Sam Taylor, Meteor Moon as X-Caliber, Jungle Run as Amanda, and Amy’s F**k It List as Amy Taylor. Television will may know her best as the recurring character Rachel in the Jason Segal and Harrison Ford series Shrinking.

C. Thomas Howell As Haggerty

Haggerty has an affinity for explosives in Obliterated on Netflix and is played by C. Thomas Howell. Howell’s character’s role in the elite team is as a United States Army explosives technician, which comes in handy as they track down a bomb threat. The actor has appeared in various television shows and movies in his career, and some of his most notable film roles are as Ponyboy in The Outsiders and Thomas Chamberlain in Gettysburg. His best-known TV roles include George Foyet (or the Boston Reaper) in Criminal Minds, Dewey in Southland, and Roy in The Walking Dead.

Eugene Kim As Paul Yung

Eugene Kim’s Paul Yung works as the United States Air Force representation on the team in Netflix’s Obliterated. Paul is a trained pilot, so he operates in the air rather than on the ground. Aside from the Netflix show, Kim has appeared in many well-known series, such as General Hospital as Dr. Knox, I Think You Should Leave with Tim Robinson in a sketch that sees Tim Robinson trying to sneak a hotdog for lunch, and Brooklyn Nine-Nine as Dr. Midj. Yung’s most notable movie performance is a smaller part in The Lincoln Lawyer as Deputy D.A. Ernest Choi.

Paola Lázaro As Angela Gomez

Angela Gomez is the lone United States Marine in the group in Obliterated and is portrayed by Paola Lázaro. Gomez is an accomplished sniper, so she waits in the wings while other members of the elite team track down threats. Aside from being a part of the main cast of the Netflix television show, Lázaro is known for her role as Juanita “Princess” Sanchez in The Walking Dead. The actress also appeared in the shows Lethal Weapon as Luisa “The Gute” Gutierrez and Wu-Tang: An American Saga as Tonya. In film, she plays Cahya in Black Bear and Louisa in Pimp.

Kimi Rutledge As Maya Lerner

Kimi Rutledge stars as Maya Lerner, the final team member in Obliterated on Netflix. Lerner is the woman in the chair in the action series, as she is an NSA agent and a tech wiz. Viewers might recognize Rutledge from small roles in Shrill and Half Sisters, but Obliterated is her biggest role to date. Plus, Rutledge’s character is endearing in the show (as are most tech experts in procedurals).

Obliterated Supporting Cast & Characters

Carl Lumbly as Langdon - The iconic Carl Lumbly plays the Director of the CIA, Langdon, in Obliterated. Before appearing in the Netflix series, Lumbly starred in many shows and films. Most notably, the actor was Dr. Miles Hawkins in M.A.N.T.I.S., Detective Marcus Petrie in Cagney & Lacey, CIA Agent Marcus Dixon in Alias, and the voice of J’onn J’onnz/Martian Manhunter in a handful of DC animated projects. Most recently, Lumbly portrayed Isaiah Bradley in The Falcon and the Winter Soldier (a role he will reprise in Captain America: Brave New World in 2024).

David Costabile as Maddox - David Costabile stars as one of the Netflix television show’s villains — Maddox, a black-market operator. Viewers might recognize the prolific actor from his parts in Better Call Saul and Breaking Bad as chemist Gale Boetticher, Billions as Mike “Wags” Wagner, Flight of the Conchords as Doug, and Suits as Daniel Hardman. His film performances include the true-to-history Lincoln character James Ashley and Art Buchwald in The Post.

Costa Ronin as Ivan Koslov - Ivan Koslov, an arms dealer, is another antagonist in Obliterated and is portrayed by Costa Ronin. Before appearing in the action series, Ronin starred in shows like The Americans as Oleg Igorevich Burov and ​​​Homeland as Yevgeny Gromov. He briefly appears in Once Upon a Time in Hollywood as Wojciech Frykowski.

Lindsey Kraft as Yani - Lindsey Kraft plays Yani, a lounge singer, in the Netflix show. However, aside from Obliterated, Kraft is known for her roles in TV shows like Getting On as Marguerite Macaw, Grey’s Anatomy as Lisa Campbell, NCIS as Sarah Goode, The Ranch as Dr. Boyd, Grace and Frankie as Allison Giampietro-Smikowitz, and The Big Bang Theory as Marissa Johnson.
