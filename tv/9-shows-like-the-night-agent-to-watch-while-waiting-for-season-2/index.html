9 Shows Like The Night Agent To Watch While Waiting For Season 2

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1500 words 7 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

The Night Agent was a hit on Netflix, despite mixed reviews. It has an engaging storyline that appeals to a wide audience. Reacher, The Night Manager, Lupin, Slow Horses, and Tom Clancy’s Jack Ryan are similar shows worth checking out. These shows offer thrilling action, compelling characters, and intriguing government conspiracies that fans of The Night Agent will enjoy.

Literature Review

There’s still no official date for season 2 of The Night Agent, but there are a few similar shows to keep fans entertained until then.

Production on season 2 of The Night Agent has already begun, but since there’s no official release date announced, fans can spend the waiting period binging similar shows. The Night Agent is based on a novel of the same name and follows Peter Sutherland, a telephone operator at the White House. Peter ends up getting a phone call that places him in a big government conspiracy. Upon its release on Netflix, The Night Agent was an instant hit with audiences. It was among the most popular series of 2023 on the streaming service, despite less than extraordinary reviews from critics. The Night Agent’s storyline is engaging and quickly paced, inviting a wide range of audiences to enjoy the show. Season 1 is full of action, mystery, and drama and a few other similar series are worth checking out for fans waiting for The Night Agent’s second season.

The Night Agent: 10 Biggest Changes To The Original Book The Night Agent was a major success on Netflix, with season 2 in the works, but season 1 made some big changes to Matthew Quirk’s original novel.

9 Reacher (2022-Present) Streaming on Prime Video

Reacher
Drama Crime Action

Cast Malcolm Goodwin , Maria Sten , Willa Fitzgerald , Bruce McGill , Chris Webster , Alan Ritchson Seasons 2

Reacher takes fans on an exciting journey following the titular character. Reacher is based on a book series by Lee Child, following a man, Jack Reacher, who investigates local murders. Like The Night Agent, Reacher finds its lead character caught up in a government conspiracy and digging to uncover the notable figures involved with it. Due to Reacher’s former experience as a military police officer, he is primely equipped with combat skills, using them against others when necessary. This results in well-executed action sequences that build in the second season and add to the overall thrill of the series.

8 The Night Manager (2016) Streaming on Prime Video

The Night Manager

Cast Tom Hiddleston , Hugh Laurie , Elizabeth Debicki , Olivia Colman Seasons 1

The Night Manager is a series with one season, and a second season that is rumored to be in development, that gained much praise from audiences and critics. The series follows the night manager of a hotel, Jonathan, who also happens to be a former soldier. Similar to The Night Agent, Jonathan is thrust into action and crime after years of uneventful work. In The Night Agent, Peter sits by the phone awaiting an important call and in The Night Manager, Jonathan is persuaded to help bring down an arms dealer after having left risky jobs in his past.

7 Lupin (2021-Present) Streaming on Netflix

Lupin
Action Crime Drama Mystery

Cast Hervé Pierre , Vincent Londez , Fargass Assandé , Nicole Garcia , Antoine Gouy , Clotilde Hesme , Etan Simon , Soufiane Guerrab , Omar Sy , Ludivine Sagnier , Shirine Boutella Seasons 3

Lupin is a mystery thriller that follows Assane Diop in his conning of the wealthy Pellegrini family after his father’s death. The series has received acclaim over the years and was the most-watched, non-English series on Netflix at the time of its release. Omar Sy’s portrayal of Assane Diop is particularly praised by critics, and while some mystery shows fail to engage audiences with an unexpected plot, there’s no shortage of mystery in Lupin. The twists and additional puzzle pieces only continue to grow with each part of the series, including the final episode of Lupin’s season 3.

6 Slow Horses (2022-Present) Streaming on Apple TV+

Slow Horses
Thriller Drama

Cast Jonathan Pryce , Kristin Scott Thomas , Jack Lowden , Gary Oldman , Saskia Reeves , Chris Reilly , Rosalind Eleazar , Christopher Chung , Olivia Cooke Seasons 2

Slow Horses follows M15 agents who’ve been downgraded due to their lack of excellent work in the agency. Similar to the work experience of Peter in The Night Agent, the M15 agents’ tasks are often lackluster and mundane. That is until they are tasked with protecting England from danger. Differing from other thriller shows, Slow Horses doesn’t rely heavily on its action sequences, using them at realistic times, and instead, focuses on its characters. The ensemble cast is full of performances that neatly balance the drama and comedy needed for a series about the unconventional agents.

All 18 Slow Horses Episodes, Ranked Slow Horses is one of the best spy thrillers on TV, with very few dud episodes. However, despite its success, some are still better than others.

5 Tom Clancy’s Jack Ryan (2018-2023) Streaming on Prime Video

Tom Clancy’s Jack Ryan
Action Drama Thriller

Cast John Krasinski , Wendell Pierce , Abbie Cornish , Ali Suliman , Dina Shihabi , John Hoogenakker , Noomi Rapace , Jordi Molla , Francisco Denis , Cristina Umaña Seasons 3

There have been many portrayals over the years, but John Krasinski’s version of Jack Ryan is often noted as the best. This version of the character has more depth than previous ones due to the television series format, as it gives room for proper development. Krasinski stars in Tom Clancy’s Jack Ryan, a series about a CIA analyst who gets an upgrade as a field agent after discovering suspicious bank activity. The series is thrilling and includes compelling action sequences, with each season giving Jack a new government conspiracy or crime operation to untangle.

4 Citadel (2023-Present) Streaming on Prime Video

Citadel
Drama Sci-Fi Thriller

Cast Richard Madden , Priyanka Chopra Jonas , Stanley Tucci , Lesley Manville , Osy Ikhile , Ashleigh Cummings Seasons 1

Citadel follows two former spies, Mason and Nadia, as they piece their past back together to stop a rival agency, Manticore, from taking over the world. The season finale left audiences with a shocking ending and many questions for Citadel’s impending second season. Many thriller series tend to find themselves spending too much time on explanations or backstories, but Citadel, with only six episodes out so far, is paced quickly. In addition, the series includes many thrilling action sequences, helped out by its large budget, which makes Citadel one of the most expensive shows of all time.

3 Alias (2001-2006) Streaming on Prime Video and Disney+

Alias
Thriller

Cast Jennifer Garner , Michael Vartan , Bradley Cooper Seasons 5

Alias is about Sydney Bristow (Jennifer Garner) who is a CIA double agent attempting to conceal her work from loved ones. Sydney’s work is often dangerous as much of it includes secretive operations, such as the recovery of missing artifacts created by a famous Renaissance figure. Although some reviews of the series say it started to run out of steam by the end, Alias’ five seasons provided an engaging plot, impressive action sequences, and a performance by Garner that was continuously praised by critics. Garner earned a Golden Globe and SAG award for her performance in a television drama series.

2 The Americans (2013-2018) Streaming on Hulu

The Americans is a spy drama set in the ’80s and follows two KGB intelligence officers disguising themselves as an American couple. The performances and writing earned the acclaimed series numerous nominations and wins during its run, including two Peabody Awards. The Americans wonderfully balances the focus of the lead couples’ relationship and the action sequences expected of a spy series. The stakes set in each season never feel unrealistic or cheap, and it all pays off in a series finale that is noted as being one of the best series finales of the 2010s.

1 The Bodyguard (2018) Streaming on Netflix

Another Netflix series to watch while waiting for season 2 of The Night Agent is The Bodyguard, which follows David Budd, a police sergeant and veteran with PTSD. The series begins to set itself apart from others by not only dealing with outside threats such as government surveillance and terrorism, but also having its lead character struggling with PTSD. However, The Bodyguard’s first season is only six episodes long, therefore the series successfully balances heavier themes with an engaging pace and believable tension. The series was nominated for various awards and earned Richard Madden his first Golden Globe.
