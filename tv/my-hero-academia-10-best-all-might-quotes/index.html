My Hero Academia 10 Best All-Might Quotes

SUMMARY

All Might’s role as the Symbol of Peace and most inspirational hero makes his words carry more weight than anyone else in the series. With the classic “Just Believe In Yourself And You Can Become A Hero” All Might inspires Midoriya to believe in himself. “Nothing Is Nobler Than Self-Sacrifice” perfectly represents All Might’s ideals.

Literature Review

Because of his status as the Number One Hero and Symbol of Peace, My Hero Academia’s All-Might has delivered many inspiring quotes.

My Hero Academia is a wildly popular series featuring some wildly popular characters, but one stands out among all the rest: All Might. The “Symbol of Peace,” All Might is a powerful figure within the world of the series, and his words carry far more weight than those of just about anyone. All Might is the former holder of One For All and mentor to the series’ main protagonist, Izuku Midoriya. Because of his status as the Number One Hero and Symbol of Peace, he has delivered several inspiring quotes when giving advice and inspiration to Midoriya and other heroes.

10 “It’s Not Bad To Dream…” Season 1, Episode 2: “What It Takes to Be a Hero”

While this quote may seem degrading at first, it’s actually a solid piece of advice from the Number One Hero. With this quote, All Might is advising that one should dream, but also has to “consider what’s realistic.” Some things are just not possible. In the scene itself, All Might advises Midoriya to pick a more realistic goal than becoming a hero, as he believes that it’s impossible for someone without a quirk to become a hero. Still, this quote does have a good point in its message: to not dream so big that your dreams become unrealistic.

9 “Just Believe In Yourself And You Can Become A Hero.” Season 1, Episode 2: “What It Takes to Be a Hero”

One For All is a My Hero Academia Quirk with limitless potential that can be passed down through generations. And with this quote, All Might helps his successor understand that he too can be a hero. This is a pretty typical quote from heroes and can be seen everywhere in real life. The power of belief is strong, and after dashing Midoriya’s dreams earlier, All Might telling him that he can be a hero too, as long as he believes it is just the first step in the pair’s relationship, and he sets Midoriya down the path of inheriting One For All from All Might to become a hero, just like his idol.

10 Best My Hero Academia Ships, Ranked The My Hero Academia anime has been very action-focused lately, but much of the fanbase enjoys the romance aspect just as much.

8 “I Will Protect Your Son, Even If It’ll Cost Me My Life.” Season 3, Episode 12: “End of the Beginning, Beginning of the End”

After continuous attacks from the League of Villains, the students at UA are moved to live in dorms. Unfortunately, Midoriya’s mother does not want him to go back. All Might himself makes the pledge to Midoriya’s mother that he will protect him at all costs, even bowing down before her to show his dedication to his goal. This quote especially resonates with fans because, at this point, All Might has lost all his power, leaving him frail and weak. The fact that All Might is this weak yet still vows to protect Midoriya is enough to move Midoriya’s mother to allow All Might to continue training him.

7 “The Most Inflated Egos Are Often The Most Fragile.” Season 1, Episode 8: “Bakugo’s Start Line”

Bakugo is clearly one of the bravest characters in MHA, but with that bravery comes a massive ego, leading to this famous All Might quote. As All Might is fleeing the students following the end of his first class, he reflects on Bakugo and his performance, noting that his ego could be a problem. However, he knows that with a bit of counseling and support, he can do better. Sadly, Bakugo, at this point in the series, is not willing to accept help from anyone, as he wants to outshine All Might and become the Number One Hero all on his own.

6 “Nothing Is Nobler Than Self-Sacrifice.” Season 1, Episode 4: “Start Line”

Given his eventual sacrifice of his powers in his battle against All For One, All Might’s quote here about self-sacrifice really hits the mark. While this is a quote that’s largely said in his head, All Might makes this comment after watching Midoriya take on the big robot in the UA exam at the very start of the series. Realizing that this is the kind of self-sacrifice that any hero should be willing to make, All Might becomes more confident than ever that he has chosen the right successor for One For All, and it’s this move as well that gets Midoriya into UA in the end.

5 “I Smile … To Trick The Fear Inside Me.” My Hero Academia Manga, Chapter 1

One of the most iconic things about All Might is his constant smile despite nearly hopeless situations. But this quote makes everyone realize that there is real fear hiding behind that smile. Even though All Might has enough power in him to beat people like Homelander, he is still afraid of losing and not being able to save the people he swore to protect. Despite this fear, he keeps a happy smile on for the public so as not to show that the most powerful hero on the planet is afraid. This kind of quote tells audiences just what kind of man All Might is.

4 “Heroes Have Many Things To Protect. That’s Why We Can’t Lose.” Season 3, Episode 11: “One For All.”

It’s quotes like this one that show All Might’s determination to defeat the villains to save people, and it’s advice like this that makes him a great mentor. All Might understands that if heroes lose, then people die, potentially even people that the heroes care about. That’s why failure is not an option for the heroes. This quote also shows why All Might tries so hard to win against the villains he fights. He’s literally the strongest hero in existence, and if he loses, then there’s no one else who can win and save the day.

3 “You Can Always Come Out Ahead…” Season 1, Episode 8: “Bakugo’s Start Line.”

While All Might was still new to teaching at UA, he offered some much-needed advice to Bakugo. Following the first training bout pitting him against Midoriya, Bakugo is left stunned that his former friend was able to beat him. All Might offers the words, “You can always come out ahead by learning from the experience,” as an encouragement to Bakugo, basically telling him to learn from his defeat so that he can strive to become even better. It proves to be a basic starting point for Bakugo in his quest to become the Number One Hero.

10 Strangest My Hero Academia Quirks Quirks in My Hero Academia are superpower-like abilities that have no limitations. Due to this variety, there are many Quirks that stand out.

2 “Go Beyond! Plus Ultra!” Season 1, Episode 12: “All Might.”

Going beyond plus ultra is one of the quotes from All Might that Midoriya idolizes the most, making it one of his most well-known lines. The best use of this quote comes from All Might fighting the Nomu in the training facility. Fueled by a last-ditch burst of motivation, All Might utters this line to the Nomu before doing exactly what he said he would do: going beyond plus ultra. The final punch from All Might sends the Nomu flying out the roof of the arena and far, far away, putting an end to one of the best arcs in My Hero Academia.

1 “It Is Fine Now. Why? Because I Am Here.” Season 1, Episode 1: “Izuku Midoriya: Origin.”

This line is probably one of the most repeated phrases of All Might in the entire series, but that just continues to mark it as one of his best quotes. Saying this line to the public as he rushes in to save them from villains puts bystanders at ease, knowing that the Number One Hero is there to save the day and keep them all safe. Doing all this with a smile on his face is part of what makes All Might who he is, and this line not only demonstrates his charisma but also his intimidating presence to the villains he is about to fight. My Hero Academia
In My Hero Academia, some humans have superpowers called quirks. Izuku Midoriya, nicknamed Deku, is not one of them. Deku has always idolized heroes like the number one hero, All Might, and since he was a child, he has always wanted to be a hero. However, his lack of a quirk has always held him back, but a chance encounter with All Might after discovering a classmate in danger sets Deku on the path to becoming a true hero. My Hero Academia centers around Deku and a class of heroes-in-training at UA. This school shapes young quirk users into future heroes through fake rescue missions, combat training, and other hero-tempering tasks. With young Deku inheriting the “One-For-All” quirk, he will learn what it means to be a true hero while facing off with dastardly supervillains.
