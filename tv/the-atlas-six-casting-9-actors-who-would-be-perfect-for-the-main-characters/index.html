The Atlas Six Casting 9 Actors Who Would Be Perfect For The Main Characters

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1400 words 7 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Olivie Blake’s novel The Atlas Six became a TikTok sensation in 2021 and is being adapted into a television series by Amazon. Book readers have been speculating on the potential cast for the lead roles in The Atlas Six, with actors like India Eisley, Xolo Maridueña, and Khylin Rhambo being mentioned as ideal choices. The actors mentioned possess the necessary talent and physical appearance to bring the complex characters in The Atlas Six to life, such as Eisley’s experience in dramatic roles and Maridueña’s ability to portray honorable and kind characters.

Literature Review

There are many talented actors who would be perfect in the six lead roles and as other major characters in Amazon’s upcoming The Atlas Six adaptation.

Amazon is adapting The Atlas Six as a television series, and several talented actors would be perfect for the lead roles. The novel of the same name by Olivie Blake took BookTok by storm when it went viral on TikTok in 2021. This fantasy story tells the tale of six talented young magicians, or Medians, who become the six people chosen every 10 years to join the Alexandrian Society, a powerful secret society of magic users. Over the next year, the six will compete against each other in increasingly deadly ways to take the coveted opening. As the series is still in development, there’s no word yet on who will be cast in the upcoming show. Still, book readers have been speculating on who may take on the roles of the six Medians and the other major, complex characters in The Atlas Six. Many have made their own lists of who they hope to see on the small screen when the show premieres. A few actors in particular would be perfect in Amazon’s The Atlas Six adaptation.

9 Libby Rhodes - India Eisley Honorable Mentions: Mackenzie Foy, Isabelle Fuhrman

Eisley is known for her roles as Audrina in My Sweet Audrina and as Ashley in The Secret Life of the American Teenager. These dramatic roles mean that Eisley has the experience needed to play a character in a dark academia series like The Atlas Six. Libby is known for being shy, anxious, and brilliant. Eisley proved that she can pull off a character like Libby in her role as Audrina, a sensitive girl with a psychic connection to her deceased sister. Eisley also has the perfect physical appearance to play Libby as she’s youthful, a brunette, and has light eyes.

8 Nico de Varona - Xolo Maridueña Honorable Mention: Diego Tinoco, David Castro

Maridueña gained mainstream success with his roles as Jaime in Blue Beetle and Miguel in Cobra Kai. He’s also 22 and of Cuban descent, just like Nico, and has dark hair and dark eyes like Nico. Beyond his physical appearance, Maridueña’s roles as Jaime and Miguel showed that he is talented at playing characters who are honorable, hard-working, loyal, and kind. While Nico seems arrogant at first, he, in reality, has many of these qualities, meaning that Maridueña could be the perfect actor to portray Nico’s loyal and caring personality hiding underneath his apparent arrogance.

7 Tristan Caine - Khylin Rhambo Honorable Mention: Daniel Ezra, Jacob Latimore

Best known for his role as Mason in Teen Wolf, Rhambo’s looks make him the perfect Tristan, as well as being similar in age to the Atlas Six character. Tristan is cynical and paranoid, which may seem very different from Rhambo’s character of Mason, who’s loyal and kind-hearted. Despite this, Tristan is sensitive deep down, and he desires genuine connections with others. Considering this, and considering Rhambo’s experience also playing more serious characters, like Dink in Ender’s Game, Rhambo would be well-suited to capture both Tristan’s seriousness and his underlying sensitivity.

6 Parisa Kamali - Afsaneh Dehrouyeh Honorable Mention: Alia Shawkat, Aiysha Hart

Like Afsaneh Dehrouyeh, Parisa Kamali is an undeniable beauty and, like her, is also of Iranian descent. Beyond the surface looks, however, Dehrouyeh would be a good fit. She is known for her roles in Yellow and Our Kind of Love, dramatic short films that explore women’s experiences under the control of the Taliban in the former and with dating in traditional Afghan culture in the latter. Her familiarity with these types of roles and interest in portraying Middle Eastern women’s unique experiences means Dehrouyeh would be perfect as Parisa, a bold and intelligent woman who knows her worth and whose early life was heavily shaped by the traditional expectations placed upon her.

5 Callum Nova - Bradley James Honorable Mentions: Luke Eisner, Alexander Ludwig

Fans hoping for a Merlin revival and seeing Bradley James return to the role of Arthur could find a suitable replacement if he were to star in The Atlas Six. James’ familiarity with fantasy projects, including playing Varga in Underworld: Blood Wars, means he has the experience to star in a fantasy series like The Atlas Six. James’ Arthur could be arrogant at times, like Callum, and his success starring as the villainous, vampiric Varga means James could effectively depict Callum’s magical and manipulative behavior. Although he is older than Callum, James still has a very youthful appearance. He’s also tall with dirty blonde hair and blue eyes, just like Callum, which would help him physically inhabit the role.

4 Reina Mori - Lyrica Okano Honorable Mentions: Hayley Kiyoko, Sophie Oda

Best known for her role as Nico in Marvel’s Runaways, Okano shares Reina’s dark hair and eyes and is of Japanese descent, just like Reina. She is also about the same age as Reina. Not only does Okano fit Reina’s physical description, but her experience playing the withdrawn goth Nico means Okano can bring similar traits to Reina, who is quiet, withdrawn, and reluctant to accept her powers. Her experience wielding magic as Nico also means she could comfortably and confidently step into the fantasy world of The Atlas Six and wield Reina’s powers.

3 Gideon Drake - Alex Lange Honorable Mention: Ross Lynch, Logan Shroyer

Like Gideon, actor Alex Lange is 22 years old and shares his blonde hair and light eyes, meaning his physicality is already a match. Beyond this, his experience in drama shows like Code Black and Red Ruby means that he could confidently play a character in a dark academia series like The Atlas Six. Lange brought a certain sensitivity to the screen as Max and in his portrayal of his tender romance with Ariel in Code Black, meaning Lange could convincingly capture Gideon’s kind and loving personality.

2 Ezra Fowler - Fionn Whitehead Honorable Mentions: Finn Jones, Harry Collett

Being in his mid-twenties and having dark, curly hair, Whitehead perfectly matches Ezra’s physical description; while an actor matching a character’s physical looks isn’t the most important part of casting, it certainly helps those familiar with the books get on board. Not only does he have the physical going for him, but Whitehead has experience portraying emotionally complex, troubled, and even villainous characters, such as Stefan in Black Mirror: Bandersnatch and Zac in Voyagers. This means Whitehead would be perfectly suited to play Ezra, a character who can be cold and cruel at times and who has uncertain, shifting loyalties.

1 Atlas Blakely - Chiwetel Ejiofor Honorable Mentions: Adewale Akinnuoye-Agbaje, David Harewood

Atlas Blakely is the conniving Caretaker in Alexandria’s library, and it requires a talented, experienced actor to pull off the role. The distinguished British actor Chiwetel Ejiofor would be a great fit. He is best known for his roles as Solomon Northup in 12 Years a Slave and Baron Mordo in Doctor Strange. Being such an accomplished actor with experience in dramatic and fantastical roles like these, he could effectively take on a central, leading role in a dark fantasy series like The Atlas Six. His convincing portrayal as the villainous Mordo means Ejiofor could capture Atlas’ calculating, scheming personality. The Atlas Six
