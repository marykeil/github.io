# The Summer I Turned Pretty Season 3 Renewal, Cast, Story &amp; Everything We Know


## SUMMARY 


 

                        The Summer I Turned Pretty season 3 is confirmed by Amazon but no release date has been revealed.
            

                        The main cast of the series, including Lola Tung, Christopher Briney, Gavin Casalegno, and Jackie Chung, will likely return for season 3, along with some new additions.
            

                        If the series follows the novels, season 3 will focus on Belly graduating from high school, attending college, and making important decisions about her love triangle, providing a more mature storyline.
