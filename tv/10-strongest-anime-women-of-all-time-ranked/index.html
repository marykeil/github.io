10 Strongest Anime Women of All Time, Ranked

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 1600 words 8 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

S

Numerous anime series feature incredibly powerful female characters, often surpassing the strength of their male counterparts. Some female characters are powerful due to their overwhelming physical strength, or from possessing godlike abilities, like the famous magical girl powerhouse, Sailor Moon. Other female characters are written to be so powerful – like Makima and Najimi – that they seem impossible to truly defeat.

Literature Review

Anime is no stranger to strong female characters, with many of them possessing otherworldly abilities that rank them well above their opponents.

While most action-based anime, especially those classified as shonen, are known for their male characters, there’s certainly no shortage of powerful female characters. Whether it’s a standout character in a male-dominated anime or someone in a series with a predominantly female cast, numerous stories make their female characters incredibly powerful, and the inventiveness with which it happens is always great to see.

There are plenty of strong female characters in anime – some of which rival the abilities of some of the most famous characters in the industry. Beyond being good fighters, many female characters in anime possess abilities that are so powerful, – or broken – that they’re not only the strongest characters in their respective stories, but some of the strongest characters of all time.

With how common it is for male characters in fiction to get the most attention from fans, it’s always good to remember how strong some of the female characters can be, and that’s especially true for a few characters, in particular.

              12 Best Female Anime Characters of All Time, Ranked   Anime is filled with a plethora of great female characters, but the following 12 are some of the best-written women in all anime history.

Makima’s Control Devil Abilities Are Virtually Unmatched Chainsaw Man (2022)

While the anime has yet to cover the full extent of her abilities, Makima’s status as the Control Devil makes her one of the strongest characters in Chainsaw Man, with her damage transferring, brainwashing, and general physical abilities making her impossible to beat without satisfying specific conditions. Chainsaw Man, as a whole, doesn’t scale incredibly high in terms of power, but Makima’s strength level still makes her an incredibly powerful female character, and once the anime gets to the point where all of her abilities can finally be shown off, that will be clear to a much wider audience.

Watch On Crunchyroll

Tatsumaki Is One Of The Most Powerful Heroes In One-Punch Man One-Punch Man (2015)

Telekinesis is often depicted as a broken superpower in fiction, and one of the best showings of that comes from One-Punch Man’s Tatsumaki. Tatsumaki’s telekinesis has allowed her to fight some of the strongest characters and pull off amazing feats, most notably taking control of a meteor in season 1, and the manga takes things even further with how effortlessly Tatsumaki overwhelms Psykos both before and after she fuses with Orochi. Tatsumaki is likely weaker than the top S-Class hero, Blast, and she’s naturally weaker than Saitama, but that does nothing to detract from how powerful a character she is.

Watch On Hulu

Big Mom Was One Of One Piece’s Four Emperors For a Reason One Piece (1999)

Another notably powerful female character in anime is, of course, One Piece’s Big Mom. As one of the Four Emperors, Big Mom has a reputation for being one of the strongest pirates in the world, and sure enough, the versatility of Big Mom’s Soul-Soul Fruit, combined with her Armament and Conqueror’s Haki, made every fight with her one that pushed her opponents to the absolute brink. One Piece treats Big Mom as being roughly on the same level as Kaido, someone constantly heralded as one of the strongest characters in the series, and it’s easy to see why that is.

Watch On Crunchyroll

Hayate Yagami Is So Powerful That She Can Only Fight By Herself Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha Strikers (2007)

Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha is known for its wide assortment of powerful female characters, especially its protagonist, but no one is stronger than Hayate Yagami. With the power of the Book of Darkness combined with her own unnaturally high power, Hayate is one of the strongest mages in the Nanoha franchise to the point that she can only fight in situations with no risk of collateral damage. The biggest reason that Hayate isn’t a top-tier fighter is the long casting time for her strongest spells, but in terms of raw power, there are few people Hayate would ever lose to.

              10 Best Magical Girl Anime Fans Of Sailor Moon Need To Watch   Sailor Moon is one of the best magical girl anime, but there are many more: fans should check out shows like Cardcaptor Sakura and Madoka Magica.

Senjumaru Is One Of Bleach’s Strongest Soul Reapers For a Reason Bleach: Thousand-Year Blood War (2022)

Bleach is an anime with no shortage of top-tier fighters, and the female character who undoubtedly reigns supreme is Senjumaru Shutara. As a member of Squad Zero, Senjumaru is one of the strongest Soul Reapers in Bleach, and as shown in Bleach: Thousand-Year Blood War, Senjumaru’s Bankai, in addition to being powerful enough to destabilize the three realms of Bleach, allowed her to defeat Yhwach’s Schutzstaffel, single-handedly. The fact that Senjumaru was the one to do that suggests that she’s the most powerful member of Squad Zero after Ichibei, and that makes a big statement of how powerful she is.

Watch On Hulu

Kaguya Otsutsuki Was So Powerful That She Couldn’t Be Killed Naruto: Shippuden (2007)

Naruto is another anime with a lot of powerful fighters, and its strongest female character has to be Kaguya Otsutsuki. With Kaguya being the final villain of the original series, she’s stronger than all the villains who came before her, and with her wide assortment of ninjutsu, dojutsu, and overall physical power, Kaguya was so powerful that Team 7 could only manage to seal her away and not outright defeat her. A decade later, Kaguya is still used as a reference point for the strength of each new villain, and her incredible slew of feats makes it easy to see.

Watch On Crunchyroll

Najimi Ajimu Is A Purposefully Overpowered Anime Villain Medaka Box (2012)

Medaka Box is a series known for making characters ridiculously powerful, and the best example of that is Najimi Ajimu. While the Medaka Box anime never got to explore it, Najimi Ajimu has over 12 quadrillion abilities, with many of them blatantly overpowered abilities like atom splitting, power nullification, and reversing causality, and despite only showing off a fraction of her abilities, the only character who could defeat her was someone as blatantly overpowered as her. Najimi’s power is overwhelming to the point of parody, but it still makes her one of the strongest female characters in all of anime.

              10 Most Broken Shonen Anime Powers   Anime is no stranger to giving characters completely broken abilities, and these are the 10 powers that stand out as the most broken of them all.

Watch On HIDIVE

Kusu Could Very Easily Be One Of Dragon Ball’s Strongest Characters Dragon Ball Super (2015)

The Angels are some of the strongest characters in all of Dragon Ball, and the strongest is most likely Kusu of Universe 10. While the Angels don’t have a specific hierarchy of power, Kusu is the oldest of the Grand Priest’s children, so she’s likely the most powerful of her siblings and, naturally, one of the strongest characters in Dragon Ball. With how massive in scale the fights in Dragon Ball tend to get, that, in turn, would make Kusu one of the most powerful female characters in anime, and that says a lot about someone with so little screentime.

Watch On Crunchyroll

Sailor Moon Is Possibly The Strongest Character From 90s Anime Sailor Moon (1992) & Sailor Moon Crystal (2014)

While fighting isn’t usually the main focus, Sailor Moon features a lot of incredibly powerful characters, and its titular lead is the strongest of them all. Sailor Moon gets at least one new power-up with every story arc, and by the end of the series, Sailor Moon becomes so powerful that she can potentially destroy things on a universal scale at a minimum. The power-scaling of Sailor Moon is easily up there with other classic anime like Dragon Ball and Saint Seiya, if not outright surpassing it, and Sailor Moon, herself, stands out as the strongest character involved in that.

Watch On Netflix

Bernkastel Is One Of the Most Overpowered Characters In All Of Anime Umineko: When They Cry (2009)

Of all the female anime characters, the one that likely stands out as the strongest is Bernkastel from Umineko: When They Cry. While the anime didn’t get to show off her abilities, Bernkastel’s witch powers are strong enough to both destroy and weaponize entire universes. Her ultimate defeat only came about from very specific rules unique to the setting. Add in her ability to make the virtually impossible possible, and Bernkastel easily ranks among the strongest characters in Umineko: When They Cry, and as such, she can easily be seen as one of the strongest women in all of anime.
