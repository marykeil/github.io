SUMMARY

Kirsten Schoemaker is a 25-year-old from Nijeveen, Netherlands, who appeared on 90 Day Fiancé: The Other Way. She enjoys sharing her luxury lifestyle on Instagram, including photos of beautiful resorts, fashionable outfits, and dining experiences. Kirsten works as a Human Resources professional and also earns money through interactions with her fans on platforms like Cameo and Fanbasis.

Literature Review

There’s a lot to learn about 90 Day Fiancé: The Other Way season 5’s, Kirsten, apart from her relationship with Julio, who’s accusing her of cheating.

Get to know 90 Day Fiancé: The Other Way season 5’s Kirsten Schoemaker, including her age, birthday, job, and what she posts on Instagram. Kirsten is from Nijeveen in the Netherlands and was introduced in episode 1 by her now ex-boyfriend, Julio Moya, from Brentwood, New York. Julio had found Kirsten while he was scrolling through his Instagram Explore feed and met her in Amsterdam for the first time during the pandemic after three months. Despite never FaceTiming, Julio and Kirsten had to quarantine together for a week. Julio ended up spending two months in the Netherlands once they started dating.

Julio got used to life in the Netherlands and applied for residency. He was moving to the country within three months when Kirsten came to New York to visit him. Julio had not told his family about his decision to move, and once he did, his family convinced him not to go ahead. Julio broke up with Kirsten on a video call when she’d gone back home after failing to give her a timeline about when he could relocate. However, during Tell All, he said trust issues led to their split, as Julio accused Kirsten of cheating on him with a male friend.

Kirsten Schoemaker’s Age

Kirsten was 24 years old when she filmed 90 Day Fiancé: The Other Way season 5. According to Cameo, Kirsten is now 25 years old and celebrates her birthday on September 15, meaning her zodiac sign is Virgo. She is three years younger than Julio, who is 28 years old in 2023. He was born in 1995, while Kirsten was born in 1998. Kirsten shared a picture from her birthday celebrations this year, where she sat at a table sipping wine and wearing a classy blue denim dress.

What Does Kirsten Post About On Instagram?

Kirsten lives a life of luxury on social media. She can be seen posing at beautiful resorts and picturesque locations, wearing trendy yet effortlessly stylish clothes. She carries designer purses and enjoys fancy meals at the best restaurants. One of the reasons Julio fell for Kirsten is because they share similar interests. Julio, who has traveled to 28 countries so far, might have seen Kirsten’s Instagram highlights, where she shared snippets from her adventures in Mallorca, Tenerife, Sicily, Bodrum, Cinque Terre, and more. He could relate to her posting about her travels and how she loved to go out to eat.

What Does Kirsten Do For A Living?

Kirsten doesn’t talk about her job on Instagram. She seemingly wants to keep certain aspects of her life private and away from social media. She describes herself as a “TV personality” in her bio. Unlike most other young 90 Day Fiancé stars, Kirsten doesn’t seem to want to be an influencer, as she keeps her Instagram page private. Julio revealed what Kirsten does for work on 90 Day Fiancé: The Other Way and said she was working as a Human Resources professional. Kirsten also had accounts on Cameo and Fanbasis, where she earns money by interacting with her fans.

90 Day Fiancé: The Other Way airs Mondays at 8 p.m. EDT on TLC.
