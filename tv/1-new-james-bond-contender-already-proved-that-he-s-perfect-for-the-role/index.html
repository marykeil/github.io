1 New James Bond Contender Already Proved That He s Perfect For The Role

SUMMARY

The future of the James Bond franchise is uncertain, with speculation about who will play Bond in Bond 26. Jack Lowden, known for his role in the TV series Slow Horses, is a strong candidate to bring a fresh twist to the classic Bond character. Lowden’s character in Slow Horses, River Cartwright, exhibits traits and skills similar to Bond, making him well-prepared for the iconic role.

Literature Review

As anticipation builds for Bond 26, there is one contender for the leading role who’s already proven he has what it takes to carry on the franchise.

There is high anticipation surrounding the James Bond franchise, as viewers eagerly anticipate news of who will step into the role, and one contender has already proven he’s perfect for the job. Sean Connery portrayed the first version of Bond, and Daniel Craig has been the latest person to take on the position. His time as the sophisticated and suave spy, however, has come to an end following 2021’s No Time To Die. This has led to a natural curiosity about what is coming up for the classic series of films and who will be able to take on this huge role.

Currently, there is only speculation about who could play Bond in Bond 26, as nothing has been officially confirmed. The task of casting Bond is a tall one, as each of the previous actors has managed to bring something new to the franchise rather than tarnishing the previous movies’ legacies. Luckily, there is one very strong candidate for the position who would be able to breathe new life into Bond 26, while still bringing classic Bond to the big screen.

Slow Horses Proves Jack Lowden Would Be A Great Bond

Jack Lowden is a name that often pops up in the conversation surrounding the future of the James Bond franchise, and Slow Horses proves that he would be perfect to step into the leading role. Released in 2022, Slow Horses is a British spy thriller television series that is based on a series of novels by Mick Herron. It follows a dysfunctional team of British Intelligence agents and their obnoxious leader, Jackson Lamb. The group is tasked with navigating the world of espionage and trying to defend England from sinister forces.

Slow Horses is currently Apple TV’s longest-running TV show, and many have recognized it for being the perfect replacement for James Bond. It manages to hold on to the best parts of the spy genre, while also providing a fresh twist and including characters that are flawed yet likable. The characters and their connection to espionage feel reminiscent of the early appeal of James Bond, and the show’s rave reviews prove that audiences have been enjoying the charming and unapologetic characters, including Jack Lowden’s.

River Cartwright Is Perfect Preparation For A Role Like Bond

Jack Lowden brings River Cartwright to life, and the character is actually the perfect preparation for the actor to step into an iconic role like Bond. Cartwright is an up-and-coming MI5 agent with a sarcastic and determined attitude. He is willing to do whatever he takes to prove himself and is often seen choosing the harder path in order to do what is right. Many of his characteristics, especially his skill when it comes to spying, are similar to Bond’s, which means that Cartwright is already well prepared to take on the part.
