The Best Movies & Shows Like Anne With An E

Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Anne With An E, though canceled after three seasons, had an optimistic and gritty sense of optimism that resonated with fans. The show explored lesser-known aspects of Canadian history and introduced new characters and storylines not found in the original books. While period dramas like Anne With An E are few and far between, there are other movies and shows, like Derry Girls and The Personal History of David Copperfield, that offer similar storytelling styles.

Literature Review

Fans of Anne With An E should make sure to check out the other similarly great period dramas to help fill the void left by the show.

Netflix has specialized in shows like Anne With An E, which is based on Lucy Maud Montgomery’s Anne Of Green Gables books. The show was one of Netflix’s top critically acclaimed historical series but was canceled after three seasons. Though Anne’s story wouldn’t continue on the small screen, fans found comfort and similarities in other movies and shows like Anne with an E, including some Anne mentions in the show. Anne With An E is unique in its sense of optimism, even if it is grittier than the original story. It’s also unique in its exploration of lesser-known aspects of Canadian history, introducing characters and storylines that aren’t included in the books. While it’s not the only empowering, female-led period drama that’s suitable for all ages, period dramas are few and far between, especially ones that can match up with this series. However, there are options in movies, streaming series, and shows like Anne with an E for anyone looking for a story in the form and style of this Netflix historical series.

10 Best Adaptations Of Anne Of Green Gables The Anne of Green Gables novel and its sequels have received several film adaptations over the decades, with some ranking higher than others on IMDb.

20 Derry Girls (2018-2022) 3 Seasons, 19 Episodes

Derry Girls

Release Date January 4, 2018 Cast Saoirse-Monica Jackson , Louisa Harland Seasons 3 Writers Lisa McGee Network BBC

Streaming now on Netflix At first glance, it might not seem like Derry Girls has anything in common with shows like Anne With An E but that couldn’t be further from the truth though. Both series center on young women coming of age in a small town and grappling with feeling like they don’t fit in but finding solace in a select group of friends. In Derry Girls, that group is growing up in the 1990s Ireland instead of 19th century Canada. Anne and her friends frequently find themselves misunderstood by adults and challenging authority. In Derry Girls, those challenges just happen to be things like skipping school or going to a rock concert instead of wanting a dress with puffy sleeves.

19 The Other Boleyn Girl (2008) Scarlett Johansson As Anne Boleyn’s Sister Mary

The Other Boleyn Girl

Release Date February 29, 2008 Director Justin Chadwick Cast Natalie Portman , Scarlett Johansson , Eric Bana

Rent now on Apple TV & Prime Video

Released in 2008, Natalie Portman stars as Anne Boleyn in this adaptation of the Philippa Gregory novel. However, the movie deals with her lesser-known sister, Mary Boleyn, played by Scarlett Johanssen. In the movie, King Henry VIII falls in love with Mary first before Anne pushes her way in. The movie shows how Anne was able to manipulate her way into power when she won over King Henry VIII, leading to the eventual birth of Elizabeth I. It shares a bit in common with shows like Annie With an E, as they are both period dramas with strong, young female leads trying to find their place in the world.

18 The Personal History Of David Copperfield (2020) Dev Patel In The Charles Dickens’ Adaptation

Stream now on Hulu For anyone looking for a movie similar to shows like Anne with an E, but with a male lead, look no further than The Personal History of David Copperfield. Based on Charles Dickens’ tale, Dev Patel plays David Copperfield as he tells his story from childhood poverty to his rise and fall and everything in between. The movie received critical acclaim as it told the story of David’s rise from rags to riches, before suffering countless setbacks and losses but never giving up hope. This version of the story offers a light and fun retelling of the classic story.

17 The Favourite (2018) Olivia Coleman’s Oscar-Winning Turn As Queen Anne

The Favourite

Release Date November 23, 2018 Director Yorgos Lanthimos Cast Olivia Colman , Emma Stone , Nicholas Hoult , Mark Gatiss , Rachel Weisz Writers Tony McNamara , Deborah Davis Studio(s) Searchlight Pictures

Rent on Prime Video & Apple TV

The Favourite is a dark comedy set within a period drama. In The Favourite, Olivia Colman turns in an Oscar-winning performance as Queen Anne, following the death of her husband. Much like shows like Anne with an E, the main storyline follows women who seek friendship, but gain distrust. The comedy in The Favourite comes from the war of wills between these two women. Rachel Weisz stars as her trusted confidant Lady Sarah, who attempts to rule the country through Anne. Emma Stone is Abigail, Sarah’s impoverished cousin who arrives looking for work and ends up trying to gain a place in high society by taking her cousin’s place by any means necessary.

16 Mary Shelley (2017) Elle Fanning Stars As The Author Of Frankenstein

Stream now on AMC+

Mary Shelley wrote the horror novel, Frankenstein. In 2017, a historical drama arrived that tells the story of Shelley’s life, including her writing Frankenstein. The movie is different from shows like Anne With an E, as it is gothic-inspired and pays homage to the dark tales that Shelley wrote, but the historical aspects have a familiar feel. Also appearing are historical figures like Percy Shelley and Lord Byron. When they are all stranded in a house, Lord Byron suggests a contest to write a horror story, and Frankenstein is born.

Frankenstein: 10 Movies Inspired By The Book (That Aren’t Adaptations) The influence of Frankenstein is so great that these ten movies could be adaptations of Mary Shelly’s novel – even if they’re technically not.

15 Becoming Jane (2007) Anne Hathaway Stars As Jane Austen

Becoming Jane

Release Date March 2, 2007 Director Julian Jarrold Cast Anne Hathaway , James McAvoy , Julie Walters , James Cromwell , Maggie Smith , Anna Maxwell Martin

Stream now on Paramount+

Another biographical period drama about a classical author arrived in 2007 with Anne Hathaway starring as Jane Austen in Becoming Jane. This is a Regency Era romance that shows how her personal life influences the stories she tells, growing up as the daughter of a poor man, which prevents her from getting married. Much like shows like Anne With an E, this shows how a young woman’s determination can win people over and help her rise out of her status in life. While not always historically accurate, the movie did show how her situation could have led to the story Austen told in Pride & Prejudice.

14 The Duchess (2008) Keira Knightley Stars As The Duchess Of Devonshire

Stream now on Paramount+

The Duchess follows the life of the Duchess of Devonshire. Her reputation preceded her, thanks to her awkward living circumstances. After falling in love with a politician and befriending her husband’s mistress, Georgiana becomes the laughingstock of the aristocracy. The Duke’s mistress moves into their mansion, and Georgiana’s popularity begins to decline as she must focus on her issues. Much like shows like Anne With an E, this is about a woman who has to fit in with a society that never fully embraces her.

13 The Queen’s Gambit (2020) 1 Season, 7 Episodes

The Queen’s Gambit

Release Date October 23, 2020 Cast Anya Taylor-Joy , Moses Ingram , Marcin Dorocinski , Akemnji Ndifornyen , Jacob Fortune-Lloyd , Rebecca Root , Christiane Seidel , Harry Melling , Patrick Kennedy , Bill Camp , Isla Johnston , Thomas Brodie-Sangster , Chloe Pirrie , Marielle Heller Seasons 1 Writers Scott Frank Streaming Service(s) Netflix

Stream now on Netflix Though it’s set in the 1960s, The Queen’s Gambit is rooted in its time. Beth Harmon is, like Anne, an orphan. She learns to play chess at a young age but quickly masters the game. Beth is adopted as a teenager, and her adoptive mother, Alma, helps her get into tournaments. Beth tours the world, playing the best in the game - all of whom are men. She proves her gender is irrelevant, which is reflected in the portrayal of her darkest moments, making it a perfect option for fans looking for shows like Anne with an E.

The Queen’s Gambit Season 2 CAN Work (Not With Anya Taylor-Joy) Although Anya Taylor-Joy has poured water on the idea of a second season, Queen’s Gambit season 2 could actually work well without her involvement.

12 Marie Antoinette (2006) Kirsten Dunst Stars As Marie Antoinette

Available to rent now on Prime Video & Apple TV

Sofia Coppola’s Marie Antoinette is a colorful exploration of the young archduchess’s rise to power in France before the French Revolution. Though she’s not seen as a leader, she’s a misunderstood woman. The film focuses more on Marie Antoinette’s personality than on politics. She’s a teenager when she’s pushed into a life she’s not ready for, and she realizes too late she left her youth behind. While taking place in a prestigious home, it shares much in common with shows like Anne With an E thanks to the young woman trying to fit in.

11 Enola Holmes (2020) Millie Bobby Brown Stars As Sherlock Holmes’ Sister

Enola Holmes

Release Date September 23, 2020 Director Harry Bradbeer Cast Millie Bobby Brown , Helena Bonham Carter , Sam Claflin , Fiona Shaw , Henry Cavill , Louis Partridge , Burn Gorman , Adeel Akhtar , Susie Wokoma Writers Jack Thorne Studio(s) Netflix

Stream now on Netflix Like Anne, Enola Holmes is a young woman trying to make her way in a man’s world. Also, like Anne, she’s searching for her mother, though she’s not an orphan. Enola has quite a reputation because her brother is Sherlock Holmes. Enola feels oppressed by her brothers, so she leaves home to look for her mother. She gets into all sorts of adventures and even winds up tangled in the suffragette movement. Enola is someone Anne would look up to — and vice versa. Fans can look forward to more in Enola Holmes 3.

Anne With An E: 15 Questions The Series Left Unanswered Anne With An E may not return to the small screen… and if it doesn’t, fans will be left with plenty of questions.

10 Reign (2013-2017) 4 Seasons, 78 Episodes

Reign

Release Date October 17, 2013 Cast Adelaide Kane Seasons 4 Network The CW

Stream now on Prime Video Reign is a dramatized retelling of the life of Mary, Queen of Scots. Though most of Anne’s role models in shows like Anne With an E are female literary figures, female monarchs are also worthy of respect in shows like Anne with an E. From a young age, Mary grows up without any real family, but she finds her version of it at the French court. Mary struggles to feel respected by the men in power around her, but she doesn’t need their approval to lead with authority.

9 Mary Queen Of Scots (2018) Saoirse Ronan Stars As Mary, Queen Of Scots

Mary Queen of Scots

Release Date December 7, 2018 Director Josie Rourke Cast Margot Robbie , Jack Lowden , Guy Pearce , David Tennant , Joe Alwyn , Martin Compston , Saoirse Ronan , Brendan Coyle , Gemma Chan Writers Beau Willimon Studio(s) Universal Pictures , Focus Features

Available to rent now on Prime Video & Apple TV

The 2018 film following Mary, Queen of Scots is a darker and more historically accurate retelling of Mary’s life. The film begins later in Mary’s life than Reign does, and in this version, Mary’s hair is red like Anne’s. Of course, the film takes certain liberties when it comes to historical accuracy, but it explores Mary’s life in Scotland, which Reign doesn’t have a chance to do. This may appeal to Anne fans who are interested in Anne’s discovery that she’s Scottish - this, and the feminist message at the heart of this female-led royal biopic.

8 Great Expectations (1946) David Lean Adapts The Charles Dickens Novel

Stream now on Hulu Set in the 1860s, just before Anne’s time, Great Expectations is based on the novel by Charles Dickens. It follows Pip, a young boy at the start of the story, as he follows his dreams and comes of age. Like Anne, he’s living in hard times and must educate himself when he finds that his humble beginnings don’t provide him with the resources he needs to be successful. Along his journey, Pip meets many influential people, both good and bad, but doesn’t anticipate the consequences of his choices. It is a world where Anne would fit in.

7 Emma. (2020) Anya Taylor-Joy Stars In The Jane Austen Adaptation

Emma

Release Date February 21, 2020 Director Autumn de Wilde Cast Bill Nighy , Gemma Whelan , Rupert Graves , Mia Goth , Amber Anderson , Tanya Reynolds , Callum Turner , Chloe Pirrie , Josh O’Connor , Anya Taylor-Joy , Johnny Flynn , Miranda Hart , Suzy Bloom Writers Eleanor Catton Studio(s) Universal Pictures

Streaming now on Peacock+

The 2020 adaptation of Jane Austen’s Emma brings a lot of vibrancy and color to the screen. Austen is one of Anne’s favorite authors in shows like Anne With an E, and she would likely appreciate the comedic melodrama of Emma Woodhouse’s personal affairs. Set in the lush green hills of Regency-era England, the colorful costumes, eventful balls, and gossiping women will appeal to those who liked shows like Anne With An E. Emma is a self-proclaimed matchmaker - for anyone but herself. However, she’s so wrapped up in other people’s affairs that she doesn’t see the potential for romance in her own life.

6 Dickinson (2019-2021) 3 Seasons, 30 Episodes

Dickinson

Release Date November 1, 2019 Cast Anna Baryshnikov , Hailee Steinfeld , Wiz Khalifa , Jane Krakowski , Adrian Blake Enscoe , Toby Huss , Ella Hunt Seasons 3 Writers Alena Smith Streaming Service(s) Apple TV+

Stream now on Apple TV

Dickinson is a modern take on the troubled life of poet Emily Dickinson. The incongruous combination of the spunky, modern soundtrack and period-style costumes, settings, and set design makes for a fun viewing experience that allows viewers an insight into the mind of Emily Dickinson, a great example of shows like Anne with an E. Anne loves poetry, especially by female poets, but unfortunately, Dickinson remained largely undiscovered during her lifetime because of the constraints set upon her by society and her family.

5 Atonement (2007) Joe Wright Adapts The Ian McEwan Novel

Atonement

Release Date September 7, 2007 Director Joe Wright Cast James McAvoy , Keira Knightley , Saoirse Ronan , Romola Garai Writers Joe Wright Studio(s) StudioCanal

Rent now on Apple TV & Prime Video

Anne is often guilty of acting recklessly, and then spending a long time regretting her actions. This is the premise of the film adaptation of Ian McEwan’s Atonement, in which a young girl makes a grave mistake that she will regret for the rest of her life. Briony is the younger sister of Cecelia, and she has a passion for inventing stories - much like Anne. Cecelia shares a secret romance with Robbie, the family’s groundsman. Out of bitterness and innocent uncertainty, Briony falsely accuses him of a terrible crime, which puts an abrupt end to his relationship with Cecelia until their paths cross again during WWII.

4 Little Women (1994/2019) Two Different Adaptations Of The Classic Historical Novel

Little Women

Release Date December 25, 2019 Director Greta Gerwig Cast Emma Watson , Timothee Chalamet , Bob Odenkirk , Florence Pugh , Eliza Scanlen , Abby Quinn , Lilly Englert , Chris Cooper , James Norton , Sasha Frolova , Meryl Streep , Saoirse Ronan , Laura Dern , Louis Garrel Writers Greta Gerwig Studio(s) Sony

Streaming now on Starz (2019) and Paramount+ (1994)

Louisa May Alcott’s novel Little Women has been adapted many times since its publication in 1868, but two of the most well-known adaptations include Gillian Armstrong’s 1994 film and Greta Gerwig’s 2019 version. Both are respectable adaptations and remain true to the original story. The coming-of-age story follows the March sisters as they go through formative experiences and figure out who they want to be as they become women. The story is optimistic and full of youthful wonder, just like shows like Anne With An E.

3 Jane Eyre (2006/2011) Two Adaptations Of The Charlotte Brontë Classic

Jane Eyre

Release Date April 22, 2011 Director Cary Joji Fukunaga Cast Mia Wasikowska , Michael Fassbender , Jamie Bell , Su Elliot , Holliday Grainger , Tamzin Merchant , judi dench Writers Moira Buffini , Charlotte Brontë Studio(s) Focus Features , BBC Films , Ruby Films

Stream 2006 version on Hulu Anne loves to read whenever she can get the chance in Anne With an E, and one of her favorite books is Jane Eyre. She read the gothic novel in the orphanage and frequently quotes the governess’s wise thoughts. Jane Eyre goes to work at a mansion owned by Edward Rochester, a mysterious and wealthy man. Jane falls in love with him, but everything changes when she discovers that he has a dark secret. For fans of shows like Anne With an E, these are movies that show fans what Anne loved so much in her own life.

2 Pride & Prejudice (1995/2005) Two Adaptations Of The Jane Austen Novel

Pride & Prejudice

Release Date November 23, 2005 Director Joe Wright Cast Keira Knightley , Matthew Macfadyen , Brenda Blethyn , Rosamund Pike

Stream 1995 version now on Hulu One of the other books Anne frequently references is Jane Austen’s Pride And Prejudice. Elizabeth Bennett was an early champion of the feminist movement in literature, defying society’s expectations of what a woman should do with her life. Mrs. Bennett wishes for all her daughters to marry well (rich) and marry swiftly (young). When Kitty marries too hastily, the entire family’s reputation is threatened, putting Jane’s courtship at risk. Meanwhile, Elizabeth isn’t sure what to make of the seemingly snarky and arrogant Mr. Darcy.

1 Anne Of Green Gables (1985) The Movie Version Of Anne With An E

Buy now on Amazon If there’s any period drama that fans of shows like Anne With An E need to see, it’s the Kevin Sullivan adaptation of Anne Of Green Gables. It’s truer to the books than the Netflix series, focusing on Anne’s sense of wonder about the world, her friendship with Diana, and her relationship with Gilbert. The movie includes more characters from the books the series doesn’t include and manages to empower viewers through Anne’s self-driven character arc. This was a template for many shows like Anne with an E that came later.
