SUMMARY

Jasmine Pineda has double standards and hypocritically applies different rules to herself in relationships. Jasmine becomes cruel and attacks her partner’s masculinity when she is hurt in the relationship. Jasmine has a high sexual drive and is obsessed with physical intimacy, often discussing it in front of the cameras.

Literature Review

Jasmine Pineda could marry Gino Palazzolo on 90 Day Fiancé season 10, but she needs to change a lot to have a happy and healthy relationship.

Jasmine Pineda from 90 Day Fiancé season 10 is a walking red flag who may never find true love after Gino Palazzolo. The 37-year-old Panamanian woman debuted on the reality TV franchise in 2021. She joined 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days season 5, where she pursued her Michigan-based lover, Gino. Things seemed good for Jasmine and Gino in the initial phase of their relationship. They loved each other and had real chemistry, evident from their on-screen conversations and arguments. Unfortunately, they both also had major flaws and trust issues. These problems, in addition to Jasmine’s jealousy and short temper, didn’t let them tie the knot immediately.

Jasmine and Gino returned to 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days in season 6 in 2023. Once again, they struggled with personal differences and almost broke up due to Jasmine’s chaotic behavior. Gino didn’t give up on his relationship completely. He gave Jasmine another chance, and she reciprocated by apologizing to him and begging him to take her back. In late 2023, Jasmine and Gino debuted in 90 Day Fiancé season 10 to finally showcase their journey after their K-1 visa approval. The two reunited in the United States and kickstarted their new lives together in Michigan, hoping to tie the knot before the 90 days were over.

Jasmine Pineda Has Double Standards

Jasmine is a fit and fabulous middle-aged single mom. She has the looks to win anyone’s heart but lacks the personality to keep them interested in her. One of Jasmine’s biggest flaws is her hypocritical attitude. The mom of two has double standards when it comes to most things. In one of the episodes from 2022, Jasmine got angry at Gino for being respectful toward his ex and being nice to a waitress. Meanwhile, she didn’t apply the same rules to herself. Instead, she maintained a close connection with her ex-boyfriend, Dane, and even accepted $2000 from him to undergo plastic surgery.

Jasmine Pineda Becomes Cruel When She’s Hurt

Cruelty is common in toxic relationships. While Jasmine isn’t a bad woman, she becomes extremely cruel in moments of discomfort. One of the worst things the 90 Day Fiancé star has done is to hurt Gino by attacking his masculinity. In 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days season 6, she mocked her sex life with Gino and made him feel like he wasn’t man enough for her. The reality star also got extremely angry in a later episode and lied that she had cheated on Gino with her ex.

She claimed to have videos of her sexual encounter with Dane and belittled Gino by saying that her ex knew how to satisfy her.

Jasmine Pineda Has “Super High Sexual Drive”

Intimacy is extremely important in any romantic relationship. However, Jasmine is obsessed with getting physical with Gino and doesn’t have a filter. Most couples expect their partners to keep their bedroom talks within the bounds of their home. However, Jasmine likes to talk about sex in front of the cameras and showcase how desperate she is. In 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days season 5 Tell All, the mom of two said, “if you know that I love f****** with you. It’s so freaking good. I love it, Gino.” Jasmine added that she is a vegan but wants Gino’s “freaking meat.”

Jasmine Pineda Has Major Abandonment & Trust Issues

Jasmine’s jealousy was one of the first things viewers noticed about her. She was extremely insecure and wanted Gino to share everything with her. In one of the past episodes, she revealed that her history of abandonment and betrayals has caused her to be so jealous. However, she hasn’t yet done much to resolve that. The Panamanian reality star still has the same trust issues she had three years ago.

Jasmine would never be able to have a stable relationship with another man until she fixes herself first.

Most people want to have a relationship with someone they can be at peace with. Jasmine isn’t that person yet.

Jasmine Pineda Wants To Be Taken Care Of

Another one of Jasmine’s issues is that she takes things for granted. The 90 Day Fiancé star wants her significant other to take care of her. She expects them to pay for her and treat her like a Panamanian princess. Over the months, Jasmine expected Gino to get her new rings and got angry when he refused. She also got him to pay for her cosmetic procedures and much more. In one episode, Jasmine got Gino to spend money to get her an apartment. She used him to get a home next to her ex-boyfriend without feeling bad.

Jasmine also used her former lover and made him pay $2000 for her butt implants.

Jasmine Pineda Is Self-Centered

Most men would run away from Jasmine because she’s extremely self-centered. She only cares about her feelings over her partners. When Jasmine first landed in the United States, she immediately began complaining about the living situation and weather. Soon after, she argued with Gino about the wedding plans and forced him to have a wedding near a beach instead of in his hometown. She even told Gino not to invite his family to the wedding because her family couldn’t come. Jasmine acts as if only her opinion matters and reacts without caring for her partner’s feelings and expectations.

Jasmine Pineda Hates To Compromise

Jasmine can’t compromise for her significant other. Not only is she self-absorbed, but she’s also unwilling to do anything meaningful for her partners. In 90 Day Fiancé season 10, Jasmine made a big issue about the cold weather in Michigan. She acted like it was inhospitable and compared it to a frozen hell. Jasmine also didn’t compromise when she stayed at Gino’s house and got a minor allergic reaction. She made a bigger issue about it and acted like her skin rashes were Gino’s fault. The 90 Day Fiancé season 10 cast member can’t find a good partner until she learns to compromise a little.
