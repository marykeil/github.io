15 Best Romance Movies On Netflix Right Now

 Ella
2020-10-16 About 2500 words 12 minutes  - views   - comments  
Contents
    • SUMMARY
    • Literature Review

SUMMARY

Romance movies on Netflix provide a universal language of love that viewers can personally identify with. Whether following a formula or subverting expectations, romance movies on Netflix create timeless and satisfying love stories. From bittersweet tales to fantastical adventures, Netflix offers a variety of romance movies to suit different tastes and preferences.

Literature Review

From lighthearted comedies like Always Be My Maybe to devastating dramas like Marriage Story, there are plenty of great romance movies on Netflix.

From lighthearted comedies to devastating dramas (and everything in between), there are plenty of great romance movies on Netflix for streaming audiences to enjoy. The global audience of Netflix subscribers love a good romance movie because love is a universal language. Falling head over heels in love is a feeling that most can relate to. Whether it takes place in a different time like Lady Chatterley’s Lover, or a different culture like the animated A Whisker Away, viewers can always personally identify with a touching love story. Romance is one of the most formulaic genres, but that formula is a recipe for a great movie. Filmmakers can create a timeless cinematic love story by either following that formula with earnestness and a deeply personal touch or subverting that formula’s expectations with something entirely new. Whether fans are looking for a romance movie that will break their heart or one that will warm their heart, Netflix has the perfect film to satisfy their needs.

9 Best So-Bad-They’re-Good Romance Movies On Netflix Some of Netflix’s romantic comedies are great while others can be substandard. These movies, however, are so-bad-they’re-good.

15 20th Century Girl (2022) Starring Kim Yoo-jung And Byeon Woo-seok

This Korean romance is a bittersweet tale of first love. A high school student promises her best friend, who has to leave the country for heart surgery, that she’ll keep an eye on her crush and report back. The girl finds herself falling for the best friend of the boy she’s been watching, only to discover that she’s actually been watching the wrong boy all along, and the two girls actually have feelings for the same boy. The twist ending adds an element of realism to the story. Watch on Netflix

14 Love At First Sight (2023) Starring Haley Lu Richardson And Ben Hardy Ben Hardy and Haley Lu Richardson in Love at First Sight

Love At First Sight
PG-13

Runtime 91 Minutes Release Date September 15, 2023 Cast Haley Lu Richardson , Ben Hardy , Jameela Jamil , Rob Delaney , Sally Phillips , Dexter Fletcher

One of the newest Netflix original romance movies is inspired by the novel The Statistical Probability of Love At First Sight. In it, two people meet on a flight and have an instant connection, leading to a handful of brief adventures between the two and the question of whether an instant connection like that can be maintained. The chemistry of leads Haley Lu Richardson and Ben Hardy is what really helps make the movie charming and unforgettable. Watch on Netflix

13 A Whisker Away (2020) Featuring The Voices Of Mirai Shida And Natsuki Hanae

Anime movies have a way of making even the simplest love stories fantastical. In A Whisker Away, the fantasy elements are turned up high. A young woman with a crush on a classmate is given a magical mask that turns her into a cat temporarily. She finds that when she’s a cat, her classmate is much more appreciative of her, and she considers just turning into a cat forever. As the boy who initially ignored her comes to care about her as a human and a cat conspires to take the girl’s human face, however, her love story becomes even more complicated. Watch on Netflix

12 Plus One (2019) Starring Maya Erskine And Jack Quaid

Sometimes all it takes is a pair of very likable and charming leads to make a familiar rom-com premise feel fresh again. Such is the case with Plus One which is elevated by the fact that it stars Maya Erskine and Jack Quaid in the lead roles. The movie takes place during the busy wedding season with Erskine and Quaid playing best friends who decide to be each other’s designated dates for all the nuptials they have to attend. The “friends to lovers” trope is a well-worn formula in the rom-com genre, but one that audiences enjoy returning to for good reason. Though few people will be surprised when these two friends begin to develop feelings for each other, the humor, heart, and performances make it a relationship worth rooting for. Watch on Netflix

11 13 Going On 30 (2004) Starring Jennifer Garner And Mark Ruffalo

13 Going on 30
PG-13

Runtime 98minutes Cast Jennifer Garner , Mark Ruffalo , Judy Greer , Andy Serkis , Kathy Baker , Phil Reeves

13 Going On 30 might not have been an original concept - a 13-year-old living their adult life - but it is lightning in a bottle. That is, it’s got the perfect combination of elements to make it work. Jennifer Garner as Jenna, the little girl who suddenly gets to live out her future, and Mark Ruffalo as her former best friend and love interest. Judy Greer in a supporting role rounds out the main cast and ensures that the chemistry is perfect. Watch on Netflix

10 Holidate (2020) Starring Emma Roberts And Luke Bracey

Holidate
TV-MA

Runtime 104minutes Cast Emma Roberts , Frances Fisher , Kristin Chenoweth , Andrew Bachelor , Luke Bracey , Jessica Capshaw

Some of the best Netflix romance movies are also some of Netflix’s best Christmas movies. Holidate is one, although its premise means that it can be enjoyed on any holiday. A young woman tired of her family hassling her about not having a boyfriend makes a deal with a single guy: they’ll be each other’s “holidates” for as long as they’re both single so that their families leave them alone. Along the way, the two get to know one another and become friends, and eventually, more. The movie is self-aware in its use of rom-com tropes, which is part of what makes it so great. Watch on Netflix

9 Along For The Ride (2022) Starring Emma Pasarow And Belmont Cameli

Along for the Ride
pg-13

Director Sofia Alvarez Runtime 106 minutes Cast Kate Bosworth , Belmont Cameli , Ricardo Hurtado , Samia Finnerty , Dermot Mulroney , Emma Pasarow , Genevieve Hannelius , Andie MacDowell , Laura Kaiuki , Marcus Scribner , Paul Karmiyran

Inspired by Sarah Dessen’s novel, Along for the Ride isn’t just one of Netflix’s best romances, but also one of Netflix’s best coming-of-age movies. Auden falls in love while spending the summer with her father and making her first real friends her age. Auden has lived a sheltered life around the academic colleagues of her parents and has never really felt like she fit in with other kids. Spending a summer with a trio of teenage girls, learning to ride a bike, and falling in love, all help her get in touch with who she is. Watch on Netflix

8 Set It Up (2018) Starring Zoey Deutch And Glen Powell

Set It Up is a modern rom-com about two assistants who set their bosses up to give themselves less stress. While the plan works to their advantage, it also means the two of them spend a lot of time together. As they fall for one another though, it’s refreshing, because they’re able to do it without the romance traps that characters can fall into. They’re friends who share pizza and beer, get messy, and don’t hide their flaws. They are not the picture-perfect romantic couple, making them one of the best Netflix romance movie couples to watch. Watch on Netflix

7 Carol (2015) Starring Cate Blanchett And Rooney Mara

Carol
R

Director Todd Haynes Runtime 118minutes Cast Kyle Chandler , Jake Lacy , Sarah Paulson , Rooney Mara , Cate Blanchett , Cory Michael Smith

One of the best Netflix romances is not a modern-set one. Set in 1950s New York, Carol sees a wealthy socialite going through a bitter divorce and falling in love with a younger shopgirl. Blanchett received an Oscar nomination for Best Actress for her powerful work in the title role of Carol Aird, while Mara was nominated for Best Supporting Actress for playing her love interest, aspiring photographer Therese Belivet. Todd Haynes’ steady-handed direction gives Blanchett and Mara’s performances plenty of room to breathe, allowing the audience to bask in the characters’ forbidden romance. Watch on Netflix

6 Always Be My Maybe (2019) Starring Ali Wong And Randall Park

Always Be My Maybe
PG-13

Director Nahnatchka Khan Runtime 112 minutes Cast Karan Soni , Charlyne Yi , Randall Park , Vivian Bang , Daniel Dae Kim , Susan Park , Michelle Buteau , Casey Wilson , Lyrics Born , Keanu Reeves , James Saito , Ali Wong

Ali Wong and Randall Park star in Always Be My Maybe as childhood sweethearts who reconnect as adults and find they still have feelings for each other. The premise has been seen in many previous rom-coms, but Wong’s uniquely biting wit and Park’s unmistakably affable presence make Always Be My Maybe stand out from the crowd and provide a fresh take on the formula. Not only is Always Be My Maybe a wonderful love story, but it also has an unforgettable cameo appearance by Keanu Reeves as a ridiculous version of himself. It’s one of Netflix’s best original romance movies. Watch on Netflix

5 My Best Friend’s Wedding (1997) Starring Julia Roberts And Cameron Diaz

During the 1990s, Julia Roberts was the queen of romance. It seemed like every year she starred in at least one. While that’s not necessarily true, the lasting impression of movies like My Best Friend’s Wedding is what gave audiences that feeling. In it, Roberts is a woman who finds out the best friend she’s been in love with for years is getting married, so, she sets out to sabotage the union. The movie features a musical sequence, a bubbly Cameron Diaz, and a not-so-typical happy ending, making it perfect. Decades later, it remains one of Julia Roberts’ best movies. Watch on Netflix

4 Lady Chatterley’s Lover (2022) Starring Emma Corrin And Jack O’Connell

Lady Chatterley’s Lover
R

Runtime 103 minutes Cast Faye Marsay , Emma Corrin , Joely Richardson , Jack O’Connell , Ella Hunt , Nicholas Bishop

Emma Corrin gives a stunning lead performance as Lady Chatterley in Lady Chatterley’s Lover; an aristocratic woman who escapes from her loveless marriage by entering into a torrid affair with the handsome gamekeeper on her husband’s estate. Netflix’s 2022 version is the 10th film adaptation of D.H. Lawrence’s classic novel, but it’s arguably the best of the bunch. It’s well-acted by Corrin and her co-star Jack O’Connell, well-written by Life of Pi screenwriter David Magee, and director Laure de Clermont-Tonnerre doesn’t shy away from the moral complexity of the story’s themes. Watch on Netflix

3 To All The Boys I’ve Loved Before (2018) Starring Lana Condor And Noah Centineo

To All the Boys I’ve Loved Before
TV-14

Runtime 102 minutes Cast Lana Condor , Anna Cathcart , John Corbett , Janel Parrish , Israel Broussard , Noah Centineo

This movie is responsible for ushering in an era of Netflix teen romances. Based on Jenny Han’s YA novel, To All the Boys I’ve Loved Before follows shy high-schooler Lara Jean Covey, who secretly writes letters to her crushes and is horrified to learn that the letters have been mailed out. Condor’s charming, lovable performance has made Lara Jean one of the definitive teen rom-com heroines of her generation. To All the Boys I’ve Loved Before is everything that romance fans could want from an entry in the genre: sweet, funny, earnest, and it tells a great story. Watch on Netflix

2 Maestro (2023) Starring Bradley Cooper And Carey Mulligan

Maestro

Release Date December 20, 2023 Director Bradley Cooper Cast Bradley Cooper , Carey Mulligan , Maya Hawke , Sarah Silverman , Matt Bomer Runtime 156 Minutes

Following his critically acclaimed directorial debut with A Star is Born, Bradley Cooper wrote, directed, and starred in another story about a complicated musician and the powerful love of his life. Maestro tells the story of Leonard Bernstein (Cooper), one of the most celebrated American composers of all time, and his marriage to Felicia Montealegre (Carey Mulligan). Maestro is a unique love story that shows the different shapes romance can take. Bernstein was in relationships with men most of his life and Felicia understood this and struggled with finding a balance between raising a family and allowing him to embrace his sexuality. However, there was never a doubt how deeply in love they were. Cooper tells a touching and heartbreaking love story with a grand feel and amazing performances, especially from Mulligan. Maestro has been nominated for 7 Oscars, including Best Picture.

Watch on Netflix

1 Marriage Story (2019) Starring Scarlet Johansson And Adam Driver

Marriage Story
R

Runtime 136 minutes Cast Ray Liotta , Merritt Wever , Kyle Bornheimer , Alan Alda , Mickey Sumner , Adam Driver , Mark O’Brien , Julie Hagerty , Scarlett Johansson , Laura Dern

Scarlett Johansson and Adam Driver each give Oscar-nominated performances as two sides of a couple going through a difficult divorce in Marriage Story. Although Nicole and Charlie initially agree to split amicably, the legal proceedings quickly turn ugly. Every argument goes through lawyers, every tiny mistake is extrapolated in court as a sign of bad parenting, and every single emotion from their marriage is laid out on the table. Marriage Story is a raw, honest examination of what brings couples together in the first place, the challenges that each relationship faces, and the irreparable problems that eventually lead to a breakup. This is not a charming rom-com, but the dissolution of a relationship is just as compelling a watch and makes for one of Netflix’s best romance movies right now. Watch on Netflix
