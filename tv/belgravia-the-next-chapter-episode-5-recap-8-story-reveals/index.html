Belgravia The Next Chapter Episode 5 Recap 8 Story Reveals

Literature Review

The episode revealed further problems for Frederick, Clara, and James, and new information about Nell, Marquise d’Etagnac, and the Rochesters.

Much happened in Belgravia: The Next Chapter episode 5, chief of all the unexpected meddling by one character in Frederick and Clara’s affairs with the intent to sow discord. The episode continued many of the storylines previously introduced, including the mystery surrounding Nell’s past and Gorton’s blackmailing of Reverend Trenchard after their encounter at the bathhouse. With some Trenchard servants hoping to gain an education and others torn by their worries about their employers, Belgravia: The Next Chapter episode 5 continued to focus on them. The Rochesters also saw great things happen with Peter finally playing outside with his siblings. However, the biggest developments involved Clara’s newfound courage to defy social conventions by posing for a painting by Ellerby’s painter friend, Richard Dearden, and wearing a memorable gown at Marquise d’Etagnac’s ball. Clara and Frederick’s problems didn’t seem to decrease in Belgravia: The Next Chapter episode 5, and the Marquise d’Etagnac meddling didn’t help. Coupled with Frederick’s continued refusal to listen to Clara’s wishes, and Clara’s decision to stop trying to meet him halfway, since he always put himself in a position of superiority, times proved hard for the Trenchards, fully unable to regain the intimate connection they briefly shared.

8 Peter Meeting His Siblings Means The Duke Finally Started To Care For Him The Rochesters chose the most humane treatment for Peter by firing Moorcroft

Belgravia: The Next Chapter episode 4 already showed the Duke of Rochester connecting with his firstborn Peter, but episode 5’s reunion between Peter and his younger siblings truly proved the Duke’s change of heart. While the Duke didn’t trust Stephen Ellerby’s unusual treatment like the Duchess, he still went on with Ellerby’s suggestion to let Peter play outside with his siblings. Adding to Peter’s anticipation and his siblings’ happy reaction the dismissal of Dr. Moorcroft, Belgravia: The Next Chapter episode 5 wholly established a new path for the Rochesters and the care of their firstborn’s epilepsy.

7 The Marquise d’Etagnac’s Words To Clara & Frederick Make Her Intentions Dark She put ideas in Clara’s head & instructed Frederick to be more distant

The Marquise d’Etagnac was one of Clara’s first friends with the Duchess of Rochester, presenting herself as someone worried about Clara’s change in situation when she married a man older than her like Frederick. However, the developments in Belgravia: The Next Chapter episode 5 revealed the Marquise as someone with her own agenda, seemingly aiming to divide Frederick and Clara. Her impromptu visit to the Trenchard household might have looked like the Marquise trying to offer support to Clara, but her hints that Frederick spending time with business was potentially spent with mistresses while Clara stayed home seemed particularly cruel. However, what confirmed the Marquise’s agenda were her words to Frederick once he visited her to explain how Clara overstepped when she told the Marquise how happy Frederick was about the silver mine venture. Without Frederick attempting to ask for advice, she suggested he should stand his ground and that “life should be lived by his rules,” going against all the advice she had given Clara, to whom she had told to do what pleased her. While Belgravia: The Next Chapter episode 5 didn’t explain the Marquise’s reasons, her behavior still drove an even bigger wedge between Frederick and Clara.

6 James’ Scolding Of Emily Reminded Her To Be There For Nell, Not Judge Her Knowing her secret made Emily more understanding of & willing to help Nell

Among the most mysterious Belgravia: The Next Chapter characters, Nell finally shared a part of her secrets in episode 5. While Emily’s first reaction was to share her findings and assumptions with James, his stern admonishments to Emily to only be supportive of her and not judge Nell shook Emily, but also convinced her to come clean with her. This led Nell to tell Emily that she had a daughter but she had died, for which Nell blamed herself. While Emily initially judged Nell, knowing her past actually made her understand her and wish she could have helped Nell.

5 Belgravia: The Next Chapter Must Reveal Why The Marquise Wishes To Sow Discord She stoked Clara & Frederick’s problems by pulling them apart

The Marquise’s attempts to sow discord between Frederick and Clara were fruitful, as Clara’s dress at her ball led Frederick to accuse her of not “observing the proprieties.” Clara and Frederick’s problems were already many, as they hadn’t reconnected after she invited James to meet Frederick, but the Marquise’s advice aimed at sending them in opposite directions separated them even more. With the two not doing anything against the Marquise, the reason behind her plot against them is unknown, but Belgravia: The Next Chapter will have to reveal it, especially if it were to be connected to the Trenchard family.

4 The Reverend Receiving Another Letter Makes His Situation More Burdensome Gorton’s threats to out James will unlikely stop

Belgravia: The Next Chapter episode 5 started with Reverend James Trenchard being handed the note Gorton sent him to blackmail him into paying him so that he wouldn’t tell the bishop or James’ parish about his attending the bathhouse. Gorton promised he wouldn’t blackmail James again when they met, but the episode’s ending proved Gorton could do what he wanted, as he had all the power since James had much to lose. This put James in a very difficult situation where he could potentially be exploited for a long time and the second letter he received unfortunately hinted at that.

3 Clara May Become Interested In Ellerby If Frederick Continues To Ignore Her She clearly cares for Frederick but likes Ellerby’s friends & Ellerby may win her over

Despite their constant fighting, Clara and Frederick still had feelings for each other, and it was clear in Belgravia: The Next Chapter episode 5 when both defended the other against the Marquise’s accusations. Clara didn’t attend Ellerby’s friends’ parties because of Ellerby, but mostly because what they did fascinated her, and she wanted to pose for Richard. However, Ellerby’s unwavering support of her might eventually lead Clara to feel something more for him just like he does for her, especially with Frederick unable to express his feelings for her and willing to shut her out time and time again.

2 Frederick’s Refusal To Engage With Clara Will Be The End Of Them He tells her she’s his chief concern, but refuses to listen to her perspective

Frederick shut Clara out multiple times in Belgravia: The Next Chapter episode 5, including when she asked him the very vulnerable question about whether he worried more about their neighbors thinking they had problems than about the issues they clearly had. Despite dishing out a half-hearted excuse about her being his chief concern, Frederick continued not to answer truthfully to Clara’s questions, avoiding her inquiries about what he felt and how to move forward, closing himself off even more to her, thus risking their relationship’s future and the chance to have a marriage truly based on love between them.

1 Clara Being Identifiable In The Painting Causes Even More Drama Emily recognizing her can also lead Clara to finally have someone’s support

Potentially causing a bigger stir than Downton Abbey and The Golden Age’s characters ever did, Clara’s choice to pose for Richard might increase her troubles. Despite Richard promising he would keep the likeness to a minimum, Emily still managed to recognize her, making it possible for others in high society to do so. Although that could be a big problem because it would unveil Clara’s impropriety in choosing to model for an artist, it could also bring Emily back to her sister’s side, leading Clara to finally have someone’s staunch support in Belgravia: The Next Chapter.
